TAKE NOTE

#ArtistHotline

Hosted by the New York Foundation for the Arts (NYFA), #ArtistHotline is an initiative dedicated to creating an ongoing online conversation around the professional side of artistic practice. NYFA and SVA Career Development's Anna Ogier-Bloomer (MPS 2017 Digital Photography) will be answering questions about the work/life balance for artists, live on Twitter. Join in the conversation by using the hashtag #ArtistHotline. Tomorrow, December 20, 3:00 – 4:00pm.

Apply Today for Paris Impressionists: A Walking Tour

SVA Destinations presents a spring break trip to Paris from March 3 – 11, 2018. Rediscover the works of the impressionist masters. Explore Paris by walking in the footsteps of artists such as Degas, Monet, Van Gogh and Renoir. Visit the museums where their work is exhibited today. Learn how impressionists changed the course of art history. Limited spaces available. Click here to apply today. For more information, contact Aziza Gaines, program coordinator, at againes@sva.edu or 212.592.2070, or visit destinations.sva.edu.
