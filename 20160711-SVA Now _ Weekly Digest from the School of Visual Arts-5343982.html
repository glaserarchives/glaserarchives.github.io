SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts July 11 - 17
FEATURES

Before and After SVA: Enle Li

The 2015 Design alumnus talks about how he landed a job at Google, what attracted him to motion design, his favorite experience at SVA, and more.

Cover Stories: Andrew Solomon's Far from the Tree

Rex Bonomelli (BFA 1998 Graphic Design) and author Andrew Solomon discuss the cover for the National Book Critics Circle Award-winning Far from the Tree.

SVA Students Selected for Cartoon Network’s Inaugural Animation Jam

From July 9 - 11, BFA Animation students Matthew Hill, Alex Stanchfield and Alexa Swanson participated in Cartoon Network’s first-ever Animation Jam at the network's studios in Burbank, California.
IN THE PRESS
Photo by Hilary Swift

Bringing Art to the Living Room

The New York Times on artists who are subverting the traditional gallery model, with MFA Fine Arts alumni Sharona Eliassaf and Emily Weiner.

The Beauty of Pigeon Racing

In Vice, Carlos Jaramillo (BFA 2015 Photography) documents the unique Cuban sport and culture.

The Changing Web

Creative Boom interviews MFA Interaction Design faculty member Jeffrey Zeldman.

Lunchtime Lecture Series: Carolee Schneemann

MFA Art Practice presents the artist discussing her work. Tuesday, July 12, 12:30pm, 335 West 16th Street, Room 501H.
