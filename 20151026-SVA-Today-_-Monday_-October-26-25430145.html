Student Films Win at VIEW in Turin, Italy
Animation Magazine reports on the Italian conference, where 2015 MFA Computer Art alumni Maria Cecilia Pugliese's and Yijun Liu's Shift was the unanimous first prize jury selection. Ao Li's (MFA 2015 Computer Art) Olilo received an honorable mention.

6:00pm: "(Still) In Process" Reception
MPS Art Therapy presents an exhibition of an ongoing collaborative project initiated by women from the Tamil Nadu region of India in partnership with Sankalpa: Art Journeys in Auroville, India. The in-progress work is composed of various media, including textiles and found objects, and questions established power relationships in order to foster collective agency. By appointment only. Contact arttherapy@sva.edu to schedule a visit. Through Friday, November 13, MPS Art Therapy Project Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.
Press pick of the day
A Peek at a Drawing Table
Following her Kirkus interview with Jonathan Bean (MFA 2005 Illustration as Visual Essay), Julie Danielson takes an in-depth look at the process behind the illustrations in Bean's new book, This is My Home, This is My School.
NYC picks of the day
Art Brut in America
Featuring over 200 works from the collection of Jean Dubuffet, the exhibition is the first in the U.S. exploring the introduction of art brut to America. Admission is free. Through January 2016, American Folk Art Museum, 2 Lincoln Square.
New York City Marathon Pavilion Opening
In advance of the New York City Marathon on November 1, the TCS Runners Pavilion, an entertainment hub and welcome center for the race, is now open to the public. Through Saturday, October 31, 7:00am - 9:00pm daily, Tavern on the Green, 67 Central Park West.

Crimson Peak Screening
The Visual Effects Society presents a screening of director Guillermo Del Toro's Crimson Peak, starring Mia Wasikowska, Jessica Chastain, Tom Hiddleston and Charlie Hunnam. Admission is free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). Friday, October 30, 8:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
