SVA Appreciation Day

On the occasion of SVA’s 75th anniversary, Mark Levine, Manhattan Borough President, has proclaimed Tuesday, November 1, to be SVA Appreciation Day, noting SVA’s many contributions and value to the city.

NYC PICK

Día de los Muertos: Family Day at Green-Wood

A celebration of Día de Muertos (Day of the Dead)—a Mexican tradition of pre-Hispanic origin that celebrates the memory and presence of the deceased—through prayer, altars, music and dance. Free. Today, November 1, 3:30 – 6:00pm. The Green-Wood Cemetery, 500 25th Street, Brooklyn.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Support Following Seoul Tragedy

As we are learning more about the tragedy in Seoul, Korea at Halloween celebrations in the Itaewon area, we are thinking about members of the SVA community, and those with friends and loved ones there. If you feel like you would benefit from the support provided by Student Health and Counseling Services, remember to reach out at health@sva.edu. The International Student Office can be reached at iso@sva.edu.

10:00am | SVAlert Test

SVA periodically tests SVAlert, the College’s electronic notification system. There will be a test of the system conducted today, November 1, 10:00am.

Last Day | Call for Submissions: “Mixed Bag” Zine Exhibition

SVA Library West is seeking zine submissions for its debut exhibition, “Mixed Bag.” SVA students are invited to submit a unique zine by today, November 1, via the online form here. The most creative zines will be selected by Friday, November 4. Additionally, the library is offering $60 in Papercut funds to those selected to be in the show. An opening party will be held for the exhibition on Thursday, November 17, 6:00 – 8:00pm at SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Thursday | Memorial Service for Bruce Wands

A memorial service for the late MFA Computer Arts Chair Emeritus Bruce Wands will be held Thursday, November 3, 4:00pm, at Prout Funeral Home, 370 Bloomfield Avenue, Verona, New Jersey. All are welcome to join and share stories and remembrances about Bruce with everyone. For those unable to attend in person, a virtual service will also be available on Zoom. The Zoom call will open at 4:00pm and the service will begin around 4:15pm.


Additionally, work by Bruce Wands is now on view at “DREAMLANDS,” MFA Computer Arts’ fall exhibition.

Friday | Yoga for Artists: TLC for Hands, Wrists and Shoulders

Give your hands, wrists and shoulders a break with a rejuvenating stretch—they’ve been working so hard for you this semester! Join Student Health and Counseling Services and instructor Melissa Boo for a free, virtual and gentle yoga class. Beginner friendly. All you need is a flat surface and one to three “soft squares” (pillows, blankets, towels, etc.) RSVP required; register here. Open to all registered students, staff and faculty. Friday, November 4, 10:00am, online.

DOC NYC 2022 Documentary Festival

The DOC NYC documentary film festival returns to cinemas (including SVA Theatre) beginning Wednesday, November 9. SVA students and staff can use the code DOCNYC_PTNR_22 for $3 off select screenings throughout the festival. Visit docnyc.net for more information and tickets.
