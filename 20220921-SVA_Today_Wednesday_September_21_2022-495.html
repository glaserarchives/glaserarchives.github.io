After School Special Kicks off Tonight

Every day through Sunday, SVA Theatre will be showcasing the work of some of its most accomplished alumni in film, television, animation and visual effects. Read about the highlights at this year’s festival above.

EVENTS

2:00pm | Virtual Narcan Training with Nurse Abby

Join Nurse Abby Wohl from Student Health and Counseling Services for a virtual SVA staff and faculty Narcan training session to learn more about how to reduce risk and prevent an opioid overdose. As a Narcan trainee, you will receive your own Narcan kit. If you are unable to attend, additional trainings will be offered throughout the year. Today, September 21, 2:00 – 3:00pm, online.

5:00pm | The Curatorial Roundtable: Allison Glenn

MA Curatorial Practice presents Allison Glenn, senior curator at The Public Art Fund, to speak about two transformative exhibitions. Today, September 21, 5:00 – 6:00pm, online.

Today | After School Special 2022

SVA Theatre presents the College’s annual alumni film and animation festival. Events include screenings of the indie thriller Resurrection, the award-winning documentary Mayor, Disney hits Encanto, Lightyear and Turning Red; a screening of the best SVA thesis films of 2022 and an animation panel. SVA alumni will be present at Q&As throughout the week. All events are free and open to the public. Through Sunday, September 25. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

#SVAwesome

Scenes from Friday’s vernissage of “Enduring Heritage” at Saint-Paul de Mausole, Saint-Rémy-de-Provence. Hundreds gathered in the chapel and cloister for an evening of art, music, merriment and a glimpse of recent renovations to this historic site where Vincent van Gogh was once a patient. The “Enduring Heritage” exhibition of artworks by SVA alumni and clients of the Valetudo Institute is the latest of an 11-year collaboration begun by SVA Executive Vice President Anthony P. Rhodes (bottom, center) and Dr. Jean Marc Boulon, director of Valetudo (bottom, far left). For more information and images, click here. Photos: Gabriella Lincoln (BFA 2019 Photography).
