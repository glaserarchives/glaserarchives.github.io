The SVA RisoLAB Is Open!
The SVA RisoLAB is fully reopening this semester. Undergraduates interested in learning the Risograph print process can sign up for alumnus, faculty member and co-founder and manager of the RisoLAB Pan Terzis's Wednesday afternoon Riso printing class (VND-2134-A)—a few open spots remain. In this course, we will explore the possibilities that this dynamic medium offers through print design assignments, readings and critiques. Lab access is available in future semesters for a lab fee, and we also offer Continuing Education courses and MFA workshops. Visit our website for more info: risolab.sva.edu.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

Bystander Intervention: How to Be an Ally When You Witness Online Abuse
As more of our personal and professional lives go digital, online hate and harassment are on the rise. In this free, one-hour, interactive training, Hollaback! will give you the tools you need to intervene safely and effectively in online abuse using the 5Ds of bystander intervention, adapted for a digital space. Today, 2:00pm. Register here to join on Zoom.

Timothy Goodman and Mari Andrews: Discussion, Q&A and Reading
Join artist Timothy Goodman (BFA 2007 Graphic Design) and writer Mari Andrew as they discuss making art in the face of love, heartbreak, life and New York City. Taking place at Richard Taittinger Gallery, 154 Ludlow Street, where you can also see Goodman's first solo exhibition, "Too Young to Not Set My Life on Fire." Masks and proof of vaccination are required.

Lightbox Expo Featuring Nathan Fox
Chair of MFA Visual Narrative Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay) will be a featured speaker at the online LightBox Expo, which connects fans with the artists and creators behind films, animation, games, TV shows and illustrations, as well as an artwork marketplace. The expo began on Tuesday, September 7, and runs through Sunday, September 12. Get a $2 ticket now to access past and upcoming content and to see Fox's online session on Friday, September 10, 2:00 – 3:00pm ET.
