SVA Today | Your guide to everything
Tuesday, March 1, 2016
Tonight: The Return of SVA Filmmakers Dialogue
The film series returns tonight with a 6:00pm screening of City of Gold, a documentary that follows Pulitzer Prize-winning food critic Jonathan Gold as he explores the culinary culture of Los Angeles. Filmmaker Laura Gabbert and Gold will answer questions following the screening. The series will also screen the Norwegian thriller The Wave at 8:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
6:00pm: Eve Mosher
MFA Fine Arts presents faculty member, artist and interventionist Eve Mosher discussing her use of public works to raise awareness of issues such as the environment, public/private space use, history of place and the urban ecosystem. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
6:30pm: Alex Gallafent: Design Research, Journalism and Improv
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents IDEO design researcher Alex Gallafent discussing how the company thinks about its values when seeking insights from others, as well as his experience as a radio journalist for the BBC and his background in theater and improv. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
Press pick of the day
Graphic by Jessica Walsh.
33 Women Doing Amazing Things in Graphic Design
Canva, the design app, highlights the work of 33 female designers, including MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman and BFA Design faculty members Paula Scher and Jessica Walsh.
NYC pick of the day
History of Horror Films, Part I
Actor and filmmaker Elliot Passantino discusses the history of horror films, from Grand Guignol to the classic horror movies of the 1930s. Tickets are $8. 7:00pm, Morbid Anatomy Museum, 424 Third Avenue, Brooklyn.
Take Note
Discounted Tickets to PULSE
SVA students can purchase discounted $10 one-day passes to PULSE Contemporary Art Fair by using the code "DAYSVA." A valid student ID must be shown for entry. The fair is open Thursday, March 3, through Sunday, March 8, Metropolitan Pavilion, 125 West 18th Street.
Orientation Leader Information Sessions
The Student Engagement and Leadership Office presents information sessions for students interested in becoming orientation leaders. The sessions will be held on Monday, March 14, 10:00am and 3:00pm, and Tuesday, March 15, 6:30pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
