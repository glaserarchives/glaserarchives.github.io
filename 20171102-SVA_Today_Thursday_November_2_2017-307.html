SVA Today
Thursday, November 2, 2017

The Making of Professional Cuddler

A Q&A with Mika Orr (MPS 2017 Directing) about her thesis film Professional Cuddler, which is winning acclaim at various film festivals and screens again at The Big Apple Film Festival on Friday, November 3, at the SVA Theatre.

Graduate Information Sessions

The following graduate programs are hosting information sessions tonight, where you will have the opportunity to tour the facilities, learn about the program and meet with the chair, faculty, department staff, current students and alumni.
MFA Art Writing: 6:00 – 8:00pm, 132 West 21st Street, 6th floor
MPS Digital Photography: 6:00 – 8:30pm, 132 West 21st Street, Room 203E
MPS Fashion Photography: 6:30 – 8:00pm, 214 East 21st Street, Room 307A
PRESS PICK
Photo by Zackary Drucker and Rhys Ernst

Where Photos Can Take You

Aperture shares the greatest journeys that photographers, including Zackary Drucker (BFA 2005 Photography) and Justine Kurland (BFA 1996 Photography), have taken through their photographs.
NYC PICKS

"Hidden in Plain Sight"

An exhibition, curated by Sarah Sze (MFA 1997 Fine Arts), featuring a new series of portrait-oriented paintings by Anne Neely. Reception tonight, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Saturday, December 16. CUE Art Foundation, 137 West 25th Street, ground floor.

El Museo del Barrio

Visit this museum of Latino, Caribbean and Latin American art before it closes on Sunday, November 5, for renovations scheduled to last until summer 2018. Exhibitions on view here. Suggested admission $5 – $9. El Museo del Barrio, 1230 Fifth Avenue.
TAKE NOTE

Artist in Residence: Xuan Chen

SVA's Visible Futures Lab presents abstract visual artist Xuan Chen, who will discuss her fall residency proposal and her past work. Tomorrow, November 3, 6:00 – 7:00pm. Visible Futures Lab, 132 West 21st Street, 7th floor.

Anna Ogier-Bloomer

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by artist, photographer and faculty member Anna Ogier-Bloomer (MPS 2017 Digital Photography). Tuesday, November 7, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Thor: Ragnarok

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Thor: Ragnarok, starring Chris Hemsworth and Cate Blanchett. Admission is free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Tuesday November 7, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

DOC NYC: What's Your POV?

As part of DOC NYC PRO, MFA Social Documentary Film co-presents a panel, moderated by department director of operations Rose Vincelli Gustine, about how to bring a great story to life. Panelists are Nanfu Wang, Jennifer Brea, and Drew Xanthopoulos. Must have a DOC NYC PRO pass to attend; purchase here. Thursday, November 9, 10:30am. Cinepolis Chelsea, 260 West 23rd Street.

Alex Fattal: Suenos, with Michael Taussig

MFA Art Writing presents a screening of Suenos, an experimental film by anthropologist and filmmaker Alex Fattal, followed by a conversation between Fattal and author Michael Taussig. Thursday, November 9, 6:30pm. 132 West 21st Street, 6th floor.

Paul Pfeiffer

BFA Fine Arts presents a talk by artist Paul Pfeiffer, who uses moving images, sound, sculptural objects and architectural installations to explore how images shape perceptions of ourselves and the world. Thursday, November 9, 7:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.
