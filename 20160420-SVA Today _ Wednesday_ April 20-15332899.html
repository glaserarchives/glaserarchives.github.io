SVA Today
Wednesday, April 20, 2016
An image from 4th Base by Kristine Windfeldt.
Five Innovative New Design Projects
Graduating MFA Design students showcase their thesis projects in the department's exhibition "Venture 16," which opens Saturday, April 23, at the SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street, and features an innovative medical marijuana device, a new platform for sharing stories, and more.
1:30pm: Smart Classroom Training
SVA Audio Visual Services presents an information session on how to use the College's "smart" classroom technology. Topics include classroom equipment, trouble-shooting techniques and technical support information. RSVP to rbarton@sva.edu. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 311.
6:30pm: Day Night Day Night
BFA Animation and BFA Film present Day Night Day Night (2006), a thriller about a young woman who plans to detonate a bomb in Times Square. Director Julia Loktev and editor Michael Taylor will answer questions following the screening. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 502.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Raft, by Alexis Rockman
Bridging Art and Science
The New York Times interviews Alexis Rockman (BFA 1985 Fine Arts), whose work focuses on scientific themes such as climate change, species extinction and geology.
NYC PICKS OF THE DAY
Luigi Ballerini and Tom Huhn
Luigi Ballerini discusses his latest book of poetry, Cephalonia, with BFA Visual & Critical Studies Chair Tom Huhn. Admission is free. 6:00pm, Italian Cultural Institute of New York, 686 Park Avenue.
Cornelia Parker: "Transitional Object (PsychoBarn)"
British artist Cornelia Parker's site-specific installation on the Met's Roof Garden is inspired by painter Edward Hopper and filmmaker Alfred Hitchcock. Suggested student admission is $12, $25 general public. Through Monday, October 31, Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Avenue.

TAKE NOTE
Fresh Meat
Browse, barter or buy SVA students’ self-published titles at this annual comics fair hosted by Cartoon Allies, the College’s organization for all students interested in comics and cartooning. Friday, April 29, 6:00 - 9:00pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
2016 All-Staff Art Show Call for Submissions
SVA staff are invited to participate in the fourth annual All-Staff Art Show. Applications can be found at sva.edu/asas2016 and require an image of the work, its description and dimensions. No work should be larger than 24 x 24". The deadline is Wednesday, June 1.
