NYC PICK

“Creating Justice: The Art of Judge Kim and the Kids’ Court”

A group exhibition of work by the team behind the new children’s book series, Judge Kim and the Kids’ Court, including Joseph Illidge (BFA 1990 Media Arts), Christopher Jordan (BFA 1993 Illustration), Shawn Martinbrough (BFA 1993 Illustration) and Milo Stone (BFA 1996 Illustration). Student admission: $10. Through Saturday, March 18. Society of Illustrators, 128 East 63rd Street.

MA Curatorial Practice presents a lecture by artist and theorist Hito Steyerl in which she excavates the connection between visual literacy, documentation and the analytical capabilities of computer vision. Wednesday, January 18, 12:00 – 1:00pm, online.

#SVAwesome

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services presents the 2023 HOPE Art Competition. The creator of the winning piece will win a prize of $500 and all of the finalist’s work will be showcased in the SVA Flatiron Windows exhibition space at 133/141 West 21st Street. This year’s theme is a suicide prevention campaign because together, we can find hope. The artwork will serve as a reminder to SVA students that #YouAreNotAlone and #YourLifeMatters. You can find more information here, along with submission guidelines. The submission deadline is Monday, February 6.
