11:00am | Warner Music Group Internship Information Session and Interviews

SVA Career Development invites students to learn about Warner Music Group’s available internships in departments such as creative, A&R, marketing, publicity, studio services, sales and design. 11:00am – noon. 136 West 21st Street, Room 409F. SVA Library and the International Student Office present the second of four origami workshops for students, staff and faculty members to celebrate diversity at SVA. Light refreshments will be served. Free. 6:00 – 9:00pm. 132 West 21st Street, Room 631F.

Bob’s Burgers Screening and Q&A with John Roberts

He’ll talk about breaking into comedy, gaining attention on YouTube with his viral hits and how he co-wrote a script and took it from pitch to pilot. Free with SVA ID; RSVP here. Wednesday, March 1, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Digital Archives Trials of Life and Time Magazines

SVA Library has just begun trials of the digital magazine archives of Life and Time. EBSCO’s magazine archives are fully searchable digital versions of the magazines' backfiles, including full-text, cover-to-cover content with article and advertisement indexing of issues from the early 1900s until 2000. These trials are accessible via the library's A-Z Databases page. They will expire on Monday, March 20.

Call for Applications: RisoLAB Artist-in-Residence

MFA Visual Narrative’s RisoLAB artist-in-residence program is now open for applications for this summer, providing visual storytellers with workspace and full access to the RisoLAB. Multi-media artists from any area, including graphic novels, zines, picture books, graphic design and photography, are welcome. Risograph training and support will be provided. Applications are due by Friday, March 24 at noon.
