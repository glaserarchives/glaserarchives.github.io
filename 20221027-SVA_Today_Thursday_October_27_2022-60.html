“Masked Vigilantes on Silent Motorbikes”

A new exhibition at Poster House shows how artists are using posters as a medium, featuring work by Michael De Feo (BFA 1995 Graphic Design), Nicholas J. Fraser (MFA 2008 Fine Arts) and KAWS (BFA 1996 Illustration).

EVENTS

9:00am | MFA Design for Social Innovation Virtual Open House

Join MFA Design for Social Innovation at an online open house to learn about the exciting work students, alumni and faculty are doing and see what an MFA in social design can do for you. Today, October 27, 9:00 – 10:30am, online.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Associate Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

5:45pm | Dracula Screening

SVA Library West presents a screening of Dracula (1992) as part of their “vampire week” movie series. Screenings continue throughout the week with Trouble Every Day (2001), tomorrow, October 28, 4:00pm. Tonight, October 27, 5:45pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

6:00pm | “DREAMLANDS” Reception

An exhibition of animations, moving images, prints and films that explore dreams, nightmares and the spaces in between by MFA Computer Arts students and alumni. Register here. The exhibition runs today, October 27 – Saturday, November 19. Thursday, October 27, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

8:00pm | VASA Halloween Party

Enjoy a spooky night of dancing, arcade games, desserts and prizes at the VASA Halloween Party! Wear a costume and walk the runway in the annual costume contest or check out the ghostly goodies guessing game for a chance to win the entire jar of treats. SVA’s Horror Society will be raffling off a Blu-Ray boxed set of the Evil Dead franchise. Tickets are on sale here for $15. Today, October 27, 8:00pm – 12:00am. Dave & Buster’s, 234 West 42nd Street.
