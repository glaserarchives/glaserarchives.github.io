9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Tuesday from 9:30 – 11:00am ET, SVA's International Student Office will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Note: Next Tuesday, May 25, will be the last weekly session.

Individual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week. To schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.
