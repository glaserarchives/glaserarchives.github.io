New Resources: Art & Architecture ePortal and Interaction of Color Interactive Site

The SVA Libraries have just subscribed to the Art & Architecture ePortal and the Interaction of Color interactive site from Yale University Press. The A&AePortal contains 300 e-book titles across 25 art historical subject areas and 60,000 associated images. Interaction of Color is the complete digital, interactive edition of Josef Albers’ seminal work of the same name. With an additional 60 plates from the 1963 Complete Edition, this new digital edition is also packed with elegant and innovative features that help users explore the book’s essential concepts and experiment with color the way Albers intended. Both can be found as distinct resources on the libraries’ A-Z Databases page.
