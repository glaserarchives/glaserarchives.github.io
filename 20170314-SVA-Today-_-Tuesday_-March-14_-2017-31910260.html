Database Trial: ACI Scholarly Blog Index

SVA Library has started a trial of the ACI Scholarly Blog Index, an editorially selected and curated collection of scholarly blogs. The collection includes over 10,000 blogs and more than two million blog posts and continues to grow as more blogs are added monthly. Content spans a full range of Library of Congress Classifications and includes authors' credentials, as well as their social media connections. Features include tools for social media sharing, citation management, export and save, discussion, bookmarking and list creation. Lists allow researchers to better organize relevant blog posts around a topic, class, etc. Users can create their own reading lists or assigned reading lists for students. This trial runs until May 1, 2017. Access it via the Library's A-Z Databases page.
