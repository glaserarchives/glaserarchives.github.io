Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 9:30 – 11:00am, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

5:30pm | MA/MAT Art Education Information Session
MA/MAT Art Education faculty and alumni will present an overview of the program, explaining coursework and answering any questions about requirements, portfolios, student teaching, schedules, teacher certification and the job market for K-12 art teachers. Register here to join on Zoom. 5:30 – 7:00pm.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Sign Up Now | AAPI Faculty/Staff Forum Tomorrow
Members of the Asian and Asian American communities continue to face harassment, discrimination and physical violence. On Friday, October 15, 11:00am – 12:30pm ET, faculty and staff who self-identify as AAPI are invited to join this virtual forum to connect on these or other topics for peer support. Some of you may have participated in April; new members are welcome. If you are interested in participating, please fill out this Google Form to register. Zoom information will follow.

If you are unable to participate, you can still fill out the Google Form to indicate your interest in future opportunities and to be included in future correspondence.
