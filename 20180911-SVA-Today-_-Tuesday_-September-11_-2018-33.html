EVENTS

11:00am | Sign Up for a New York Public Library Card and Register to Vote

An NYPL librarian will be on hand to sign up SVA students, faculty and staff and answer questions. The SVA Library staff will also assist those who wish to register to vote in New York or their home district. 11:00am – 2:00pm. SVA Library, 380 Second Avenue, 2nd floor.

Visible Futures Lab Equipment Demos

Starting today, SVA's Visible Futures Lab is hosting a series of equipment demonstrations illustrating how to use laser cutters, 2D and 3D printers, and more. Sign up for a time slot here. Through Sunday, September 16. Visible Futures Lab, 132 West 21st Street, 7th floor.

TAKE NOTE

Mental Health First Aid for Students

Student Health and Counseling Services is proud to host Mental Health First Aid training for SVA students. This eight-hour training teaches the skills to provide initial support when someone experiences a mental health crisis. Find more info about the class here. Register here. Open to SVA students only. Friday, September 14, 9:00am – 5:00pm. Soc Doc Theater, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Bob Milgrom Memorial and Tribute

SVA Humanities and Sciences will hold a memorial and tribute to the department's former co-chair, Bob Milgrom, who passed away in April. Please feel free to read a personal statement and tell stories about him at the event. Refreshments will be served. RSVP to Laurie Johenning at ljohenning@sva.edu. Friday, September 21, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Apply for the Cultural Partner Program

The International Student Organization welcomes undergraduate students to apply for this two-semester long and membership-based program, featuring NYC culture trips, a monthly discussion series and the opportunity to participate in and lead a community service project. Apply here by Wednesday, September 26. Email cpp@sva.edu with any questions.
