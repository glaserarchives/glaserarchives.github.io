EVENTS

9:00am | Curatorial Roundtable: Ingrid Haug Erstad

MA Curatorial Practice presents Ingrid Haug Erstad, director of Bergen Assembly, a triennial for contemporary art that takes place in the city of Bergen, Norway. Today, February 22, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.

2:00pm | Still I Rise: Mental Health Risk and Resiliency in the BIPOC Community

In honor of Black History Month, SVA Student Health and Counseling Services will be hosting an event focused on mental-health stigma and resilience in the BIPOC community. Join SVA therapists Jamie Kao and Rebecca Amoah to connect with each other and have a cup of hot cocoa! Today, February 22, 2:00 – 3:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:00pm | Paris Impressionists: A Walking Tour 2023 Info Session

Attend this session on how you can experience Paris with SVA Destinations this summer—walk in the footsteps of Van Gogh and Monet while earning art history credits. Register here for a Zoom link. Tonight, February 22, 6:00pm, online. Questions? Email Aziza Gaines, program coordinator, at againes@sva.edu.

Last Chance | HOPE Art Competition Exhibition

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services present “Together, We Can Find HOPE,” the annual HOPE art competition exhibition of campaign posters created by SVA students. Closes today, February 22. SVA Flatiron Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.
