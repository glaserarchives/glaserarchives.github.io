ANNOUNCEMENTS

Thursday | cardboard: a meditation on medium
SVA CP Projects Space presents “cardboard: a meditation on medium,” an exhibition curated by MA Curatorial Practice student Caroline Taylor Shehan featuring artists who elevate cardboard from substrate to subject. Thursday, March 24 – Wednesday, March 30. Reception Thursday, March 24, 7:00pm. MA Curatorial Practice, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

Friday | Titmouse 5 Second Night
SVA Theatre presents this annual event, for which Titmouse animators create and screen whatever goodies have been floating around their minds all year. Titmouse, founded by SVA alumni Chris and Shannon Prynoski, employs many SVA graduates, and several animators/artists will be in attendance. Friday, March 25, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. Tickets $5 – $15; purchase here.
