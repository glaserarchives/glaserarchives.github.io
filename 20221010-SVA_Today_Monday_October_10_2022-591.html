ANNOUNCEMENTS

Additional Museum Benefit for Faculty and Staff: SVA Library Is Now Circulating Corporate Membership Cards

SVA Library has corporate membership passes to the Museum of Modern Art and the Whitney Museum of American Art to circulate to SVA faculty and staff. These passes can be checked out from the circulation desk for one week at a time. Benefits include: free admission and expedited entry to the museums for the cardholder and additional guests, as well as discounts at the museum shops and﻿ at select cafes in the museums. The membership passes are available at the main library (380 Second Avenue, 2nd floor); to check them out from SVA Library West (133/141 West 21st Street, lower level), please contact library staff here in advance.
