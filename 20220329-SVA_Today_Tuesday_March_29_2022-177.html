SVA at the Oscars
A selection of this year’s 2022 Oscar winners featured SVA talent behind the scenes, including Cruella, Dune and Encanto.
Event

7:00pm | Aundre Larrow
MPS Digital Photography presents a talk with Jamaican-born, Brooklyn-based photographer and Adobe Creative Resident Aundre Larrow. Larrow has worked on the set of W. Kamau Bell’s TV series United Shades of America, and seeks to see the fundamental truth in each human being, regardless of background, culture or upbringing. Tonight, 7:00pm ET, on Zoom.
SVA IN THE PRESS
Ina Jang

Nudes From a Female-Identifying Perspective
BuzzFeed spoke with photographer Ina Jang (MPS 2012 Fashion Photography; BFA 2010 Photography) about “Nude,” an exhibition currently on view at Fotografiska featuring work by Jang and faculty member Elinor Carucci.
NYC PICK

Another Kind of Knowledge
The Architecture and Design Film Festival presents a screening of Another Kind of Knowledge, a documentary focused on the wonderful, but little-known, work of Danish architect Dorte Mandrup. Mandrup herself will be present for a conversation to introduce the film. Tickets are free with discount code SVA. Tonight, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

This Weekend | MFA Visual Narrative and RisoLAB at MoCCA
New York’s largest comics and cartoon festival returns this weekend and MFA Visual Narrative and the RisoLAB will be there at Table A94. Student and alumni work will be available for purchase and Riso demos will take place throughout the weekend by artists in residence. Saturday, April 2 – Sunday, April 3. Admission is $18 per day, $25 for weekend tickets. Metropolitan Pavilion, 125 West 18th Street.

Next Week | Mindy Seu
MFA Products of Design presents a talk by designer and gatherer Mindy Seu. Gathering, in both its material and social form, serves as the intentional aggregation of resources for a specific community. In this lecture, Seu will discuss tools, indexes and learning trails used in the practice of gathering. Monday, April 4, 2:30pm. MFA Products of Design, 136 West 21st Street, 7th floor. To virtually attend any of MFA Products of Design’s Spring 2022 Lecture Series events, email productsofdesign@sva.edu.
#SVAwesome
How to Survive a Pandemic, edited by Adam Evans (MFA 2020 Social Documentary Film) with archival assistance and research by Jamie Deradorian-Delia (MFA 2020 Social Documentary Film), takes an inside look at the historic, multinational race to research, develop, regulate, and roll out COVID-19 vaccines in the war against the coronavirus pandemic. The film premieres tonight, 9:00pm ET, on HBO. Via HBO.
