Commencement 2016
Although May 18 dawned gray and cloudy in New York City, a little inclement weather couldn’t dim the excitement of over 1,000 students poised to walk across the stage at Radio City Music Hall and receive their diplomas at SVA’s 41st formal commencement ceremony. Read the full story with slideshow.
Photograph from the 2015 All-Staff Art Show.
2016 All-Staff Art Show June 1 Submission Deadline
SVA staff are invited to participate in the 4th annual All-Staff Art Show. Applications can be found at sva.edu/asas2016 and requires an image of the work, description and dimensions. All work should be no larger than 24" x 24".
SVA Today Summer Edition Begins Monday
SVA Today will go weekly for the summer months, beginning Monday, May 23. The weekly summer edition will include all of the features of SVA Today. The daily edition will resume on the first day of the fall 2016 semester.
Digital Short Story Show
An exhibition of work by MFA Visual Narrative students. From Saturday, May 21, through Saturday, June 11, at SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

NYC PICK OF THE DAY
Decade of Dance Parade
Check out this annual dance parade and festival, which kicks off at 21st Street and Broadway and makes its way down to Tompkins Square Park. All-ages dance lessons and a dance party follow, with live performances. Saturday, May 21, 1:00pm.
New York on Location: Street Fair and Celebration
Museum of the Moving Image, Theatrical Teamsters Local 817, I.A.T.S.E. Local 52 and Kaufman Astoria Studios present a free day-long, family-friendly street fair that offers a behind-the-scenes look at film production in New York. Sunday, May 22, 11:00am - 5:00pm.
TAKE NOTE
Dreaming Public Art
Madison Square Park Conservancy and SVA present Dreaming Public Art, a symposium featuring a keynote conversation with artist Martin Puryear and Columbia University art historian Dr. Kellie Jones. Tuesday, May 24, 7:30 - 10:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
