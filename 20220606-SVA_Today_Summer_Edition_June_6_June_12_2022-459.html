Events This Week

Tuesday | All-Staff Art Show
The 10th annual exhibition of works in various media by College staff. Tuesday, June 7 – Wednesday, June 22. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Wednesday | The Power of Story
MFA Visual Narrative presents the first talk in their Storyteller’s Series featuring faculty member Jonathan Gottschall, a distinguished literary scholar and winner of the Storytelling World Resource Award. Thursday | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour Thursday, June 9, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

A group exhibition of sculpture, photography and mixed-media work by four artists and alumni of SVA’s artist residency programs. Through Thursday, June 9. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Reporting a Positive COVID-19 Test
In order for the College to operate safely and effectively during the coronavirus pandemic, all students, staff and faculty who test positive for COVID-19 must report it to SVA Student Health and Counseling Services. Students must email student-covid-alert@sva.edu; resident students must also email reslife@sva.edu. Staff and faculty must email employee-covid-alert@sva.edu.

To read more about SVA’s COVID-19 reporting and contact-tracing process, visit the COVID-19 Reporting, Tracing and Isolating Protocols section of SVA’s Managing COVID-19 webpage.

Next Week | The Storyteller as Illustrator
MFA Visual Narrative presents the next installment of Storyteller’s Series, featuring Feifei Ruan (MFA 2015 Visual Narrative), a Chinese illustrator and visual storyteller. Feifei was the Gold winner of the Promax Awards 2019, the Gold Medal winner of the SOI MoCCA Award 2018, the Silver winner of the CLIO Awards 2020 and the Bronze Cube winner of the ADC Awards 2019. Attendees will need to show proof of COVID-19 vaccination and booster and remain masked. This event is co-hosted by SVA Library West. Wednesday, June 15, 6:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street.
In Memoriam
Former BFA Animation and BFA Film chair, filmmaker and Marine Corps veteran Reeves D. Lehmann (BFA 1977 Animation) died last month after a long illness. Lehmann, a longtime SVA faculty member, was instrumental in advancing the College’s animation and film programs, and for many years oversaw the Dustys Festival, an annual screening, industry networking event and awards ceremony for graduating students in his departments, named after College founder (and fellow veteran) Silas H. Rhodes’s military nickname. To read Lehmann’s full obituary, click here. Photograph by Nir Arieli (BFA 2012 Photography).
