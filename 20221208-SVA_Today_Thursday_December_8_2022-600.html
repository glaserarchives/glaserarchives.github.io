EVENTS

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Associate Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

12:00pm and 9:00pm | MFA Visual Narrative Application Q&As

MFA Visual Narrative presents two virtual Q&As with Chair Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay) about the program and application process. Today, December 8, 12:00 – 1:00pm and 9:00 – 10:00pm, online.

1:00pm | MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism Online Info Session

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents an online info session with faculty and alumni about its intensive one-year MA program investigating the contexts and consequences of design. Today, December 8, 1:00pm, online.

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

4:00pm | 2022 VASA Holiday Bazaar

Get ready for the most wonderful time of the year at the annual VASA Holiday Bazaar! Student vendors will be selling prints, crochet, jewelry, apparel and more. Today, December 8, and tomorrow, December 9, 4:00 – 7:00pm. Open to SVA students, faculty and staff only. SVA ID and Cleared4 are required for entry. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

5:30pm | MA/MAT Art Education Information Session

MA/MAT Art Education presents an overview of the program, explaining coursework and answering any questions about the requirements, portfolios and student teaching. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer or fall 2023 admission. Tonight, December 8, 5:30 – 7:00pm, online.

6:30pm | MFA Products of Design Virtual Open House and Info Session

MFA Products of Design presents a virtual open house and info session featuring presentations with faculty, alumni and current students followed by a virtual reception. Tonight, December 8, 6:30 – 8:30pm, online.

Be an SVA Holiday Angel

SVA’s annual Holiday Angels program returns to make children’s wishes come true in one of three ways:

  • •Participate by directly purchasing a gift on Amazon for a child connected to Abraham House.
  • •Bring a newly purchased toy to SVA Student Affairs, 340 East 24th Street, to be donated locally to Toys for Tots.
  • •Make a credit card contribution to fund the Toys for Tots or Abraham House gift drive. Contributions are accepted through Friday, December 16.

For more information, call 212.592.2898 or email akrumm@sva.edu. To learn more about Abraham House, visit abrahamhouse.org. To learn more about Toys for Tots, visit toysfortots.org. Credit card donations appreciated; cash not accepted.
