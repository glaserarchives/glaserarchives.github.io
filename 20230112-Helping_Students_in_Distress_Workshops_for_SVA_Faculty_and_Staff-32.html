In-person and virtual offerings for January 2023

Helping Students in Distress Workshops for SVA Faculty and Staff

Helping Students in Distress is a workshop designed for SVA faculty and Staff to go over best practices when working with distressed students, review important terminology and practice interventions that can help during a stressful situation. Join Caryn Leonard-Wilde from Disability Resources and Arielle Kempler and Rebecca Amoah from Student Health and Counseling Services for a 3-hour in-person workshop and 45 min question and answer to learn more about how you can best support your students. Two virtual options will also be offered. Please only RSVP to one of these events. There are only 25 seats for each training. 

In-person 
  • Monday, January 23rd from 9:30 am - 1:00 pm, RSVP here

Virtual
  • Tuesday, January 24th from 1:00 pm - 4:30 pm
  • Friday, January 27th from 9:30 am - 1:00 pm
Please RSVP for either date here
