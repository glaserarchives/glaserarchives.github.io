EVENTS

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30 pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

1:00pm | VASA Student Senator Application and Information Session

Students are invited to apply to be a VASA Student Senator. Senators represent their major in interdepartmental collaborations and events. Get involved on campus and organize events for your peers; apply here. Attend an info session today, January 12, at 1:00pm at SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street. Contact senators@sva.edu with any questions.

5:30pm | MA/MAT Art Education Information Session

MA/MAT Art Education faculty and alumni will present an overview of the program, explaining coursework and answering any questions about the application requirements, foundations in education and internships. Tonight, January 12, 5:30 – 7:00pm, online via Gather.

6:00pm | “Dread/Dream” Reception

An exhibition of work by artists from MFA Fine Arts class of 2020, whose in-person thesis show was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Through Monday, January 30. Reception: Tonight, January 12, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

6:00pm | RisoLAB Continuing Education Information Session

Learn about riso printing courses offered during the spring semester at SVA, hosted by SVA RisoLAB co-founder Panayiotis Terzis (MFA 2015 Fine Arts; BFA 2006 Illustration). Tonight, January 12, 6:00 – 7:00pm, online.

6:30pm | Online Winter Drawing Marathon 2023

SVA Continuing Education and BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents a virtual drawing marathon with faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts). Tonight, January 12, 6:30 – 9:00pm, online.

7:00pm | Visual Narrative Information Session

SVA Continuing Education presents an information session for the CE Visual Narrative program. Tonight, January 12, 7:00 – 8:00pm, online.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Next Week | The 12th Annual New York Game Awards

The New York Videogame Critics Circle invites SVA students to attend the New York Game Awards, hosted by former Nintendo America president Reggie Fils-Aimé. The ceremony celebrates the best games of 2022 and features musical performances, comedy from Daily Show comedians and a special award presented to the CEO of Microsoft Gaming, Phil Spencer. To attend, please email to mediaowl@aol.com from your SVA email address ASAP, as these free tickets are limited. Tuesday, January 17, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | Lunar New Year Celebration Gathering 2023

Celebrate the Lunar New Year with SVA’s International Student Office and Student Health and Counseling Services. Enjoy dumplings and other celebratory food and make an origami rabbit—all are welcome! Friday, January 20, 3:00 – 4:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Call for Submissions: Lunar New Year Poster Competition 2023

​​The International Student Office is calling for poster submissions with the theme of Lunar New Year or this coming year’s zodiac animal, the Rabbit. All submitted posters will be showcased on Instagram, @sva.iso, and a winner will receive a $100 gift certificate from the SVA Campus Store. To participate, students must complete this form and email a poster as a JPEG to Chisato Takeyama at ctakeyama@sva.edu by Tuesday, January 17. Open to all students.

Helping Students in Distress Workshop

Join Student Health and Counseling Services and Disability Resources for a three-hour workshop, Helping Students in Distress. SVA faculty and staff will go over best practices when working with distressed students, review important terminology and practice interventions that can help during a stressful situation. The first workshop will take place on Monday, January 23, 9:30am – 1:00pm. Location TBD. Sign up here. The following two workshops will be virtual and will take place on Tuesday, January 24, 1:00 – 4:30pm and Friday, January 27, 9:30am – 1:00pm. Sign up here.
