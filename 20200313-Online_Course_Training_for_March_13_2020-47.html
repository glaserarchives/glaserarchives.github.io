Dear faculty,

"Transitioning to online" training sessions will be available today March 13, 2020 at:

               11:00 am

               1:00 pm

               3:00 pm

Simply click this link (links to an external site) to join with the ZOOM teleconferencing tool. You'll be prompted to download the app but can also simply join through your web browser.

Additionally, an OLT staff member will be available at the same link for Q&A whenever training is not in session. OLT can also be reached by phone (212-592-2313) or email (olt@sva.edu) Monday through Friday from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm.

Lastly, OLT has created an open discussion board for all faculty.  Please use this to post any question related to transition your course online. We strongly encourage you to share helpful feedback with other faculty to help them through this process. We want to maintain a positive and constructive dialogue and hearing your thoughts will help us improve the support we are offering.

A recorded version of the training session will be available in the faculty knowledge base on Canvas by this weekend.
