SVA alumnus Cal Peternell's cookbook Almonds, Anchovies, and Pancetta: A Vegetarian Cookbook, Kind Of is on The New York Times' list of the 19 best cookbooks of fall 2018.
NYC PICK

Archtober

A month-long celebration of architecture and design, featuring daily building tours and lectures by design experts. Schedule here. Through Wednesday, October 31.
TAKE NOTE

Geography and the Economic Imagination of Development

From research fieldwork notes in Ecuador, Maria Garcia aims to topple conventional thinking about globalism and reckon with the ways we can reclaim our ideas of place and space from the neoliberal and capitalist imagination. Talk presented by BFA Visual & Critical Studies. Monday, October 15, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

NewFest 2018

New York City's annual LGBT film festival returns to SVA Theatre from Wednesday, October 24 – Tuesday, October 30. The SVA community can access a limited amount of $5 tickets to the following screenings with code SVANF18 or by clicking the links below:

Dykes, Camera, Action! (by MFA Social Documentary Film alumnus Caroline Berler): Saturday, October 27, 6:00pm
Mapplethorpe: Saturday, October 27, 8:30pm
High Art (on 35mm film, with a Q&A with Lisa Cholodenko and Patricia Clarkson): Sunday, October 28, 5:00pm
Hallokween Shorts: Monday, October 29, 6:30pm

Each screening is followed by a Q&A. Visit newfest.org for more information and a full schedule.
