SVA Alumnus Ray Billingsley Named "Cartoonist of the Year"
Ray Billingsley (BFA 1979 Cartooning), creator of the syndicated comic strip Curtis, has won the National Cartoonists Society's Reuben Award for Outstanding Cartoonist of the Year! Click here to read our Q&A with Billingsley, from the fall/winter 2020 Visual Arts Journal.
Events

In and Out of Time
BFA Fine Arts presents an exhibition of work by 31 of its students and alumni, curated by program chair Suzanne Anker. On view through Friday, November 5. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 9:30 – 11:00am, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

4:00pm | Practice Lecture Series: Sarah G. Sharp
MFA Art Practice presents a talk by multidisciplinary artist Sarah G. Sharp, whose work explores land, identity, craft and new technologies. Register here.

4:00pm | MobiusLab Student Club Meeting
The new MobiusLab student club is hosting its second meeting, open to all students. Come dressed 1980s-style and meet and collaborate with students from other programs. 4:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Trauma, Home and DEI
Dr. Jarvis Watson, director of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at SVA, will host a virtual, interactive event discussing trauma, home and DEI work in the context of SVA community art from the Photoville exhibition "These Years." Register here.

Image from Shattered, the thesis project of BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects students Nelson Mai, Tiantian Zhang and Kaitlin Yu.

SVA Student Kaitlin Yu Awarded ASIFA-Hollywood Scholarship
Congratulations to fourth-year BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects student Kaitlin Yu, one of the nine undergraduate and graduate students chosen to receive a 2021 – 2022 ASIFA-Hollywood Animation Educators Forum Scholarship! The funding will be used for Yu's thesis project with fellow students Nelson Mai and Tiantian Zhang. Via Animation Magazine.

Little Island Park
Beat the weekend crowds and check out Manhattan's new "floating" park before the fall chill sets in. 6:00am – 11:30pm. Pier 55, Hudson River Park at West 13th Street.

16mm Cartoon Carnival: Scary Town
SVA faculty member Tommy José Stathe presents two unique programs of spooky animated oddities from the 1920s through the 1950s, projected from real film and hosted by roving Brooklyn arts organization Rubulad. $15 per program; location address will be shared upon ticket purchase. Sunday, October 24, 3:00pm and 6:00pm.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Save the Date: All-Staff Meeting
It's alive! The SVA All-Staff Meeting returns this month to the SVA Theatre. After months of remote work, come catch up with your colleagues, hear what's happening around the College and get a little taste of Halloween on Thursday, October 28.

To attend in person, your SVA ID is required and you must have completed the Cleared4 process at least 2 hours prior to the start of the meeting.

For those unable to attend in person, the meeting will be live-streamed. Pre-register now to receive your link to the program. Doors open at 3:30pm. Program begins at 4:00pm.

"iPadtober" Returns to the SVA Campus Store
