Thursday | Afghanistan: 20 Years of War
Award-winning journalist Anand Gopal will discuss why the war’s effects did not live up to the false promises of American political leaders and possible implications for the future of America’s military footprint on the globe. Presented by the SVA Honors Program and BFA Visual & Critical Studies. Thursday, October 7, 6:00 – 7:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C. Register here.

Calling UX/UI Designers: Sign up for the Designpreneurs Hackathon!
Are you looking to build a UX/UI case study portfolio? Do you want to collaborate with a business entrepreneur to create an innovative UX/UI solution? Join the Designpreneurs Hackathon, in partnership with NYU Stern School of Business. At this virtual hackathon, start-up designers from SVA will team up with start-up entrepreneurs from NYU to build the best start-up design over one weekend. Learn more and apply here.

Notes: You must be able to dedicate 100% of your time on Friday, October 29, 5:00 – 9:00pm; Saturday, October 30, 9:00am – 9:00pm; and Saturday, October 31, 9:00am – 3:00pm. The application is only open for candidates who have at least one UX/UI case study portfolio.
