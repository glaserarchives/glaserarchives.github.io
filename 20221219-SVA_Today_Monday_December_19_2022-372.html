EVENTS

10:00am | SVA Graduate Admissions Applications Q&A

The School of Visual Arts invites all prospective graduate students to meet Graduate Admissions staff and learn about the application process. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer 2023 or fall 2023 admission. Today, December 19, 10:00 – 11:00am, online.

4:00pm | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 4:00pm. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

SVA IN THE PRESS

LG Wonder Box Showcase 2022

BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects student Chloe Ko has won third place in the inaugural LG Wonderbox Showcase for her artwork, Giant Present, which will be featured on a digital billboard in Times Square through the holiday season. Via Sfgate.

NYC PICK

Lighting of the World’s Largest Menorah: Manhattan

The “world’s largest” 36-foot menorah will be lit each night this week through Sunday, December 25, outside the east entrance of Central Park. Today, December 19, 5:30pm. Fifth Avenue and 59th Street at Grand Army Plaza near Central Park.
