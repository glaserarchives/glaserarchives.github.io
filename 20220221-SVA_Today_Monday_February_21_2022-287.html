Alumnus Wins Spark Animation Grant
The Latino Film Institute has named Anthony Gallego (BFA 2020 Animation) a recipient of the new LXiA Spark Animation Grant. Sponsored by Netflix, the $10,000 award will support the development and production of Gallego's short film Yuri, which deals with the subjects of death and grief. Read more in Animation Magazine.

in town and online

Seneca Village Walking Tour
Before Central Park was built, Seneca Village, located in the area that is now the West 80s, was home to the largest population of land-owning Black Americans in pre-Civil War-era New York. Honor Black History Month by taking an hourlong self-guided tour to learn about the community before it was displaced by the building of the park. Presented by the Central Park Conservancy.
