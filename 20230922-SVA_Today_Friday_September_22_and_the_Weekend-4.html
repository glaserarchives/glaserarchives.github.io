NYC PICKS

The Keepers

A durational still performative group action by artist and SVA faculty member Ed Woodham that aims to bring attention to the demolition of the Penn Station neighborhood’s historic buildings. Today, September 22, 6:30 – 11:30am. This performance will take place across three different locations around the Penn Station neighborhood; The Church of St. John the Baptist, Gimbel’s Skybridge and the demolished Hotel Pennsylvania.

Musical Creation Based on Chilean Folklore: Works of Carlos Isamitt Alarcón

A hybrid lecture/performance by musicologist Freddy Chávez Cancino, pianist Elizabeth Mendieta and vocalists Belem Abraham and Claudia Leyton who will share musical examples from Isamitt’s folkloric collections and discuss how these materials became the basis for new pedagogies of visual and musical arts. Saturday, September 23, 2:00pm. New York Public Library for the Performing Arts–Bruno Walter Auditorium, 111 Amsterdam Avenue between West 64th and 65th Streets.

New York Latino Film Festival Block Party

The New York Latino Film Festival presents a free block party celebration featuring live music, lechon a la varita from renowned chef Manolo Lopez, games and a free film screening of Nueba Yol. Sunday, September 24, 2:00 – 10:00pm. Quisqueya Plaza, 238-224 Dyckman Street.
