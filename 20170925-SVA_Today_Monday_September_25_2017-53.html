BFA Fine Arts student Ellis Edwards talks about her first year at SVA, why she abandoned mechanical engineering to pursue art, where she'd like to be in four years and more.

NYC PICKS

I Write Banned Books

In celebration and defiance during Banned Books Week 2017, PEN America in partnership with Strand Books invites authors whose books have been banned or challenged because of their cover art and content to discuss their experiences. Tickets $15; purchase here. 7:00pm. Strand Bookstore, 828 Broadway.

"Making | Breaking: New Arrivals"

An exhibition of more than 40 recent contemporary acquisitions for the museum’s permanent collection, as well as sketches, prototypes and videos illuminating the designers’ groundbreaking design thinking. Free with SVA ID. Through Sunday, November 19. Cooper Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museum, 2 East 91st Street.
