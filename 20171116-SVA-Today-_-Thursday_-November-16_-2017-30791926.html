"Stephen Shore" Member Previews

As corporate members at The Museum of Modern Art, SVA faculty and staff can visit the museum during its Member Preview Days to see "Stephen Shore" before it opens to the public. Show your SVA ID at the admission desk to get your tickets. Today, 10:30am – 5:30pm, tomorrow and Saturday, 10:30am – 9:00pm. The Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53rd Street.

Museum of the City of New York Free Admission

In celebration of the one-year anniversary of its landmark exhibition "New York at Its Core: 400 Years of NYC History," The Museum of the City of New York is offering free admission through Sunday, November 19. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.

An exhibition of work by seven Brazilian artists who articulate notions of fragmentation and proliferation in their work. Curated by Tatiane Schilaro Santa Rosa (MFA 2015 Art Criticism and Writing). Through Friday, December 8. B[x] Gallery, 203 Harrison Place, 3rd floor, Brooklyn.

Winter Weather Closing Notifications

SVAlert, SVA's electronic notification system, is the College's primary means of announcing unscheduled closings to the SVA community. With winter approaching, please take this opportunity to verify that your SVAlert contact information is correct and up to date by logging on to MySVA and clicking the SVAlert icon in the upper right.

Unscheduled closings are also announced via alerts on sva.edu and recorded messages on SVA's main phone line, 212.592.2000. If you learn from a major media outlet that New York City public schools have closed or are closing due to weather, assume that SVA will also close.
