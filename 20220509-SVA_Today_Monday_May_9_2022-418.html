Events

1:00pm | MFA Design for Social Innovation Thesis Show 2022
The MFA Design for Social Innovation class of 2022 presents their thesis projects focused on making change within their communities and the world. Today, 1:00pm; register here. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

7:00pm | The Monster Inside Me Screening
The Monster Inside Me is a new documentary that explores the impacts of chronic illness on relationships. The director follows his wife and other patients as they receive a Lyme disease diagnosis and experience psychological symptoms that harm their relationships with significant others, family and friends. Free for SVA students, faculty and staff; SVA ID required. Today, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Captain America at Bed Bath & Beyond
Sculptor David Cortes (BFA 1996 Animation) was commissioned by Marvel to design a statue of Captain America for the 75th anniversary of the superhero’s introduction. The work is on display at Liberty View Industrial Plaza in Industry City, Brooklyn. Via Untappedcities.
NYC PICK

An Evening with Ariel René Jackson
Ariel René Jackson presents the New York premiere of their latest moving image work, A Welcoming Place, along with the single-channel videos Bentonville Forecast: In the Square (2019) and Descendance (2021). Student tickets: $8. Today, May 9, 7:00pm. Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53 Street.

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a special advance screening of Montana Story followed by a Q&A with directors Scott McGehee and David Siegel. Admission is limited to SVA community members and their guests. Read the SVA Theatre COVID-19 protocols here. Tuesday, May 10, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Sound Utility Master Class for Filmmakers

In Hollywood), author of Behind the Sound Cart: A Veteran’s Guide to Sound on the Set. Free for all students and faculty with registration. Sunday, May 22, 2:00pm. Academy for Careers in Television & Film, 1-50 51st Avenue, Long Island City.

All Staff Art Show
SVA staff are invited to participate in the 10th annual All Staff Art Show. Last year, over 50 works by talented staff were featured in the exhibition. Grab a piece from your collection or create something entirely new; all submissions are welcome. The deadline for submissions is Wednesday, May 25.
