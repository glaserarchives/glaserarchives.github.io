Creative Arts Therapies for the Military Population 
Friday, March 16, 2018
133 West 21st Street, Room 101C
6:30pm-8:00pm

Melissa Walker will discuss the The Healing Arts Program, the National Intrepid Center for Excellence, and Walter Reed National Military Medical Center, as well as their subsequent partnership with the National Endowment for the Arts.  Research outcomes and expansion efforts will be shared.

Gifts from the Exiles
CATs of Color 
Exhibition: March 9-March 30
Reception: March 30, 5:00-6:30pm
132 West 21st Street, 5th Floor Gallery

Lecture and Artist Panel: 
March 30, 6:45-9:30pm
133 West 21st Street, Room 101C

Creative Art Therapists (CATs) of Color explore the role of altruism in a diverse society.  Through their multidisciplinary exhibition they challenged themselves to think socially; to explore what cultural values allow them to care for others and, in turn, allow others to care for them.
