Peter Ash Lee for Rolling Stone

Events

Last Chance | BFA Photography and Video Senior Show
BFA Photography and Video presents a virtual thesis show, providing a dynamic and interactive experience with its class of 2022’s final projects. Closes today, May 27, online.

Last Chance | “Tales of the City”
BFA Cartooning and BFA Illustration present work by third-year students. Closes today, May 27, online.

Last Chance | “Ancestral Echoes: A Decade of Bio Art”
An exhibition of work by BFA Fine Arts students, alumni and visiting artists, curated by department chair Suzanne Anker and Bio Art Laboratory Manager Tarah Rhoda (MFA 2020 Fine Arts; BFA 2010 Fine Arts). Closes tomorrow, May 28. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
SVA IN THE PRESS

Showing Young Children the Beauty Found in Nature
Judith Gale Mont (BFA 2016 Fine Arts) was interviewed by the Baltimore Jewish Times about her work as art director at Chizuk Amuno Congregation’s Goldsmith Early Childhood Center in Baltimore.
NYC PICKS

Fleet Week 2022
Explore a variety of displays, activities and demos from the U.S. Marine Corps, U.S. Coast Guard and USO. Through Monday, May 30. Fleet Week will conclude on Monday with the Museum’s annual Memorial Day ceremony. Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum, Pier 86, West 46th Street.

Trudy and Mona: LIVE AND IN THE FLESH!!!
A live talk-show performance by theater artist and SVA staff member Khristal Curtis, running nightly through Monday, May 30. Tickets: $15 – $45 sliding scale. The Brick Theater, 579 Metropolitan Avenue, Brooklyn.

Hester Street Fair
An open-air market featuring a special curation of small and local businesses. Tomorrow, May 28, and Sunday, May 29, 11:00am – 6:00pm. Pier 17, 89 South Street.

“Earthseed”
A solo exhibition of recent work by Bronx Museum of Art artist-in-residence Tiffany Smith (MFA 2015 Photography, Video and Related Media). Free. Through Sunday, June 26. Bronx Museum of Art, 1040 Grand Concourse, the Bronx.
