News and events from the School of Visual Arts
SVA Now
News and events from the School of Visual Arts
June 12 - 18, 2017

SVA Takes Hollywood

A recap of this year's SVA Premieres student film showcase in Hollywood, which included screenings, studio visits and networking with SVA alumni in the industry.

Invisible Women Athletes

BFA Advertising students Jack Welles and Olivia Zorechak created an award-winning ad with Everlast that draws awareness to the lack of coverage female athletes receive.

Animating Monsters

Max Colson (BFA 2016 Animation) talks about his job animating monsters, the networking opportunities SVA provided him with, the importance of self-promotion and more.
IN THE PRESS

SVA + Women in Animation

Variety on SVA's partnership with Women in Animation, which aims to encourage students to connect with its global community of animation professionals that empower and support women.

Photographing a "Beautiful Boy"

The New York Times on Lissa Rivera’s (MFA 2009 Photography, Video and Related Media) "Beautiful Boy," a photo series that explores gender roles. On view at ClampArt Gallery in New York.

From Film to Furniture

The New York Times Style Magazine on Dozie Kanu (BFA 2016 Film), whose love of Kubrick films now influences his "striking" furniture designs.
EVENTS

Jade Doskow

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by architectural and landscape photographer Jade Doskow (MFA 2008 Photography, Video and Related Media), who is known for her rigorously composed and eerily poetic images that examine the intersection of people, nature and time. Tuesday, June 13, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 418F.

SVA Short Film Fest

MPS Directing presents a film festival of 13 thought provoking, tearjerking, smile-inducing films from the the department's class of 2017. RSVP here. Thursday, June 15, 4:00 – 10:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
