June Newsletter
Inner Worlds
Exhibition June 5-22
5th Floor Project Space
Opening Reception: 
Thursday, June 15, 1:30-3pm
Friday, June 16, 1:30-3pm

Inner Worlds: A Celebration of Individuality features children and adolescents attending Happy Hour 4 Kids, a full time therapeutic center.  Children created their pieces in individual and group art therapy, collaborating with the art therapist.  Through building, drawing, and painting, students are exploring their identity, building self-esteem and confidence as their artwork comes to fruition.

Viewings by appointment only.  
Contact arttherapy@sva.edu to schedule.
