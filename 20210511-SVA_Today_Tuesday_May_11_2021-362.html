MFA Computer Arts Thesis Presentations
Taking place today, May 11, and tomorrow, May 12, at 8:00pm ET, MFA Computer Arts' 2021 virtual thesis presentations will include short talks and a screening of graduating students' work. The Tuesday presentations will highlight 2D-animation and motion-graphics projects; Wednesday will feature experimental art and 3D-animation projects. See both events live on mfaca.sva.edu/live.

Support COVID-19 Relief Efforts in India
As India continues to fight a devastating wave of COVID-19 infections, all those who are able to support relief efforts are encouraged to do so. Click here for a list of NGOs and fundraisers providing aid to those in need.
Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Tuesday from 9:30 – 11:00am ET, SVA's International Student Office will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week. To schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Diane Meyer
Diane Meyer is a Los Angeles-based artist whose work explores history, memory and the photograph as a physical object. Join Meyer's talk on Zoom. Presented by MPS Digital Photography as part of its i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. 7:00 – 8:30pm ET. Join on Zoom.

Kicking Off Today: Senior Week '21 ft. Shuga Cain from RuPaul's Drag Race!
Celebrate graduation with VASA at Senior Week from today through Friday, May 14! Open to all graduating students. Get tickets here.

The featured event is Drag Queen Bingo, which will be hosted by RuPaul's Drag Race contestant Shuga Cain on Thursday, May 13, 6:00pm ET. Shuga Cain hosts a special bingo night filled with prizes and surprises! Play bingo, win prizes and get the Drag Race tea! Tickets only $5.
