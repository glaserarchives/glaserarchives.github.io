EVENTS

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-in Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to the “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

5:30pm | La Bodega Hispanic Heritage Welcome Meeting

Kick off the celebration of Hispanic Heritage with La Bodega—the best place to get connected to the Latin American community on campus—during a night of games and a movie screening. Vengan, vamos a festejar! Tonight, September 28, 5:30 – 8:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Stay tuned to the club’s Instagram, @labodegasva, for updates!

6:00pm | Charles Traub: “Skid Row” Reception

BFA Visual & Critical Studies and the SVA Flatiron Project Space present large-scale portraits by MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Chair Charles Traub of denizens of the “Skid Row” neighborhoods of New York and Chicago. Through Thursday, October 26. Reception: Tonight, September 28, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

6:00pm | “Exploded Views” Reception

A joint exhibition of short films, prints and puppets by 25 students and alumni from BFA 3D Animation and Visual Effects and BFA Animation, curated by faculty member James Bascara (BFA 2011 Illustration) and BFA Animation Director of Operations Angelica Vergel. Reception: Tonight, September 28, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Monday, October 2. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

7:00pm | Women in Film Welcome Event

Connect with the SVA Women in Film club again at its welcome event—meet the team, participate in fun games and get to know people from all years and majors! Tonight, September 28, 7:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 603. Open to anyone and everyone.

7:30pm | The Creator Screening

SVA Theatre presents a screening of The Creator, directed by Gareth Edwards. Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Tonight, September 28, 7:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | The Algorithmic State: Artificial Politics 3.0

MA Curatorial Practice presents a panel of artists and scientists to explore how artificial intelligence and the emerging concept of artificial consciousness shape our understanding of truth and autonomy in today’s world. Thursday, October 5, 12:00 – 1:00pm, online.

Next Week | Visiting Artist Lecture: Suzaan Boettger

BFA Fine Arts presents a visiting artist lecture with art historian and critic Suzaan Boettger, who will discuss paintings she discovered and deciphered in research for her new book, Inside the Spiral: The Passions of Robert Smithson. Thursday, October 5, 7:00 – 8:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.
