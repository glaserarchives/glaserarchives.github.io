Free Admission to Cooper Hewitt for National Design Week

National Design Week celebrates the power of design in our everyday world. Through Sunday, October 23, Cooper Hewitt’s galleries will be free to enter, and talks, studio visits, tours and more will welcome all to engage with design. Reserve your free admission tickets in advance here.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Tomorrow | Virtual Narcan Training with Nurse Abby

Join Nurse Abby Wohl from Student Health and Counseling Services for a virtual SVA staff and faculty Narcan training session to learn more about how to reduce risk and prevent an opioid overdose. As a Narcan trainee, you will receive your own Narcan kit. If you are unable to attend, additional training will be offered throughout the year. Tomorrow, October 18, 2:00 – 3:00pm, online.

Tomorrow | Film Workshop #1

SVA’s Women in Film club presents their first of many film workshops. This week’s workshop will focus on collaborative screenwriting and how to get into a creative headspace. Everyone is welcome; no experience is needed. Tomorrow, October 18, 8:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 501.

Thursday | Coloring and Cocoa

Join SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and de-stress before midterms over free hot cocoa and cookies. Take them to go or stop in and do some fun coloring sheets. Thursday, October 20, 2:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Thursday | MA/MAT Art Education Information Session

MA/MAT Art Education presents an overview of the program, explaining coursework and answering any questions about the requirements, portfolios and student teaching. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer or fall 2023 admission. Thursday, October 20, 5:30 – 7:00pm, online.

NY Phil at the New Geffen Hall Discount

Use code EARTH for 35% off. Wednesday, November 16 – Saturday, November 19; see dates and times here. David Geffen Hall, 10 Lincoln Center Plaza.
