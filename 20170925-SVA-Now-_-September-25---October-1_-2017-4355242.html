SVA Now
News and events from the School of Visual Arts
September 25 - October 1, 2017

SVA Subway Series Hall of Fame: George Tscherny

Introducing a new video series in which notable designers and College faculty reflect on the posters they have created for SVA. First up is design veteran George Tscherny, who designed the first SVA poster in 1956, as well as SVA's logo.

Christoph Niemann on Creativity

In advance of "The Masters Series: Christoph Niemann," the renowned illustrator and graphic designer selects and comments on some of his work. The exhibition opens on Saturday, September 30, at the SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

My First Year: Ellis Edwards

BFA Fine Arts student Ellis Edwards talks about her first year at SVA, why she abandoned mechanical engineering to pursue art, where she'd like to be in four years, and more.
IN THE PRESS

MythBusters Returns

Popular Mechanics releases the first trailer for the reboot of the popular science-reality show MythBusters, which will be co-hosted by Jon Lung (MFA 2016 Products of Design).

Dreams of Drawing Hellboy

The Washington Post on how Shawn Martinbrough (BFA 1993 Illustration), a veteran comic book illustrator, got to fulfill his longtime dream of drawing one of his favorite characters, Hellboy.

Surviving Hurricane Irma

Surfer on what it was like to experience Hurricane Irma on Barbuda, featuring a first-hand account and photographs by Mohammid Walbrook (BFA 2016 Photography and Video).
EVENTS

Jenna Pirog

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by Jenna Pirog, the virtual reality editor at The New York Times Magazine, as part of the i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tuesday, September 26, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

On Assignment

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents three program alumni—photographers Ryan Koopmans and Ryan Pfluger, and Jacqueline Bates, award-winning director of photography for The California Sunday Magazine—who will discuss their work and decision-making process. A Q&A will follow. RSVP here. Tuesday, September 26, 7:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.
