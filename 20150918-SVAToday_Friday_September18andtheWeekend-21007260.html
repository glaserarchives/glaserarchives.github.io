NYC Weekend Picks
NY Art Book Fair
The NY Art Book Fair is the world’s premier event for artists’ books, catalogs, monographs, periodicals and zines. The fair features over 370 booksellers, antiquarians, artists, institutions and independent publishers from 28 countries, including SVA. Through Sunday, September 20, MoMA PS1, 22-25 Jackson Avenue, Queens.
Last Chance: Photoville
Don't miss this free photo event built from re-purposed shipping containers and including "Objects and Subjects in 2015" by recent BFA Photography alumni and “Hey Sailor! What’s New In Town?,” a project by Kathryn Mussalem (MPS 2015 Digital Photography). Through Sunday, September 20, at The Uplands of Pier 5, Brooklyn Bridge Park.

Participating neighborhood galleries extend hours and host opening receptions.Tonight, 7:00 - 10:00pm, various locations.
Brooklyn Book Festival
The Brooklyn Book Festival is the largest free literary event in New York City, presenting an array of national and international literary stars and emerging authors. Sunday, September 20, 10:00am - 6:00pm, Brooklyn Borough Hall and Plaza, 209 Joralemon Street, Brooklyn.
Take Note
Until Oct. 11: Library Offers Three New Databases
The SVA Library has trial memberships to Flipster, the International Bibliography of Theatre & Dance and Literary Reference Center Plus. Flipster provides digital versions of popular magazines. The International Bibliography of Theatre & Dance is the definitive research tool for the study of theaterand the performing arts. Literary Reference Center Plus is a full-text database that provides information on thousands of authors and their works. Through October 11, available online.
Sept. 19: International Information Session in Rio de Janeiro
Do you have friends or family in Brazil who are interested in a career in the arts? SVA's director of international outreach, Adam Rogers, will be in Rio to discuss the College and its programs to prospective students. Saturday, September 19, 3:00 - 6:00pm, Windsor Atlantica Hotel, Av. Atlântica, 1020, Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro.
Sept. 20: 67th Emmy Awards Screening
Cheer on nominated SVA alumni and their shows during a live broadcast of the 67th annual Emmy Awards, hosted by Andy Samberg, on the big screen. Part of SVA’s After School Special 2015: Alumni Film & Animation Festival. Sunday, September 20, 8:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
