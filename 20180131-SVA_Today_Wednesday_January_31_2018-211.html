EVENTS

11:00am | SVA Library West Opening

SVA Library staff welcomes the College community to the soft opening of its newly renovated SVA Library West branch. Eat cookies, tour the space and learn about library services, including the new video game room. Today and Friday, February 2, 11:00am – 4:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

6:30pm | "Year One" Reception

Redux Pictures and BFA Photography and Video present a visual reflection of the first year of the Trump presidency, featuring work from Redux Pictures, VII, NOOR and The New York Times. 6:30 – 9:00pm. On view through Friday, February 9. 214 East 21st Street, 2nd-floor gallery.

Jackie Winsor, Linnea Kniaz

TAKE NOTE

Visiting Critic: Yasmil Raymond

BFA Fine Arts presents a talk by visiting art critic and curator Yasmil Raymond on her curatorial practice. Tuesday, February 6, 7:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.

SVA Masters Workshop: Design Rome

This type, typography and design workshop, taking place from May 27 – June 10, will increase your knowledge and appreciation of classical Italian inscription and contemporary Italian graphic design. Apply here. For more information, visit destinations.sva.edu.
