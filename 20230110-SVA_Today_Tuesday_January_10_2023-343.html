January Events and Exhibitions

This month’s calendar is filled with artist and curator talks, an exhibition of BFA Interior Design: Built Environments student work and the annual MFA Illustration as Visual Essay “Book Show.”

EVENTS

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Nancy A. Scherl

MPS Digital Photography presents Nancy A. Scherl (MFA 2000 Photography and Related Media), a fine-art photographer specializing in portraiture. Tonight, January 10, 7:00 – 8:30pm. RSVP for location.

7:00pm | Daredoodles With Tom Motley

SVA Continuing Education presents a free comics drawing workshop with faculty member Tom Motley. Tonight, January 10, 7:00 – 8:30pm, online.

Ongoing Exhibitions

  • •“The Book Show,” through Saturday, January 14. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
  • •“An Infrastructure for Restorative Justice,” through Saturday, January 14. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.
  • •“Crisp: The Artist Residency Project Fall 2022 Group Exhibition,” through Tuesday, January 31, online.

SVA IN THE PRESS

Justine Kurland

The Best Photography Books of 2022

Justine Kurland’s (BFA 1996 Photography) SCUMB Manifesto was named one of The Guardian’s photobooks of the year for 2022.

NYC AND ONLINE PICK

Someone Talked! WWII Espionage Posters

A conversation between military poster expert Mel Meehan and Poster House’s guest curator Tim Medland on the exhibition “With My Little Eye: Warnings from the Homefront.” Free. Tonight, January 10, 6:00 – 7:00pm, online.

ANNOUNCEMENT

Tomorrow | Curatorial Roundtable: Michael Connor

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with Michael Connor, co-executive director of Rhizome. Wednesday, January 11, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.
