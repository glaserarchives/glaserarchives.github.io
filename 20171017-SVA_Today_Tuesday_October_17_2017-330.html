SVA Today
Tuesday, October 17, 2017

Allan Wexler, "Absurd Thinking, Between Art and Design"

MFA Art Writing and MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism present Allan Wexler, whose lecture coincides with the publication of his new monograph, Absurd Thinking, Between Art and Design. RSVP here. Tonight, 6:30 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
EVENTS

2:30pm | Yearbook Photo Shoot

SVA Yearbook is shooting portraits for the 2018 edition. All fourth-year students are encouraged to dress how they like, stop by and have their photo taken. The yearbook is given to students at commencement for free. 2:30 – 7:30pm, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

6:30pm | Tsibi Geva

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by faculty member Tsibi Geva, one of Israel's most prominent artists. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
PRESS PICK

Paula's Poster for Pasadena Playhouse

The New York Times interviews BFA Design faculty member Paula Scher about her poster design for Pasadena Playhouse's production of Our Town.
NYC PICKS

"Platform: Creative Musings on Mass Transit"

An evening of site-specific works of art inspired by the experience of taking mass transit in New York City. Tickets free with promo code SVA100; purchase here. 6:00pm. New York Transit Museum, subway station at the corner of Boerum Place and Schermerhorn Street, Brooklyn.

"Calder: Hypermobility"

An exhibition that focuses on the extraordinary breadth of movement and sound in the work of Alexander Calder. Free with SVA ID. Through Monday, October 23. The Whitney Museum of American Art, 99 Gansevoort Street.
TAKE NOTE

MAT Art Education Open House

Join MAT Art Education to learn about the field of art education and the program from faculty, current students and alumni. Register here. Thursday, October 19, 5:30 – 7:30pm. 132 West 21st Street, 4th floor.
