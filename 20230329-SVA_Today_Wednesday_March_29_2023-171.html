EVENTS

4:00pm | The Bound Book: A Three-Day Bookbinding Workshop

Learn several bookbinding techniques with artist Althea James at SVA Library West. Bring your own projects or use the provided array of fun, eccentric supplies. Today’s workshop will focus on different spine stitches. Learn how to do accordion binding in tomorrow’s workshop and join on Friday for a free, experimental day. Today, March 29, 4:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

6:00pm | Conversation With the Bronx-wide Coalition and “The Bronx-Wide Plan”

MFA Design for Social Innovation is hosting a public lecture by the Bronx-wide Coalition on their Bronx-wide planning process to create a community-led, long-term economic development plan for the borough grounded in racial justice and economic democracy. Tonight, March 29, 6:00 – 9:00pm. MFA Design for Social Innovation, 136 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

7:00pm | Austin Film Festival in New York

Join the Austin Film Festival and the Writers Guild of America East for a panel on creating entertainment based on true crime stories, featuring screenwriters Hilary Bettis (The Dropout), Becky Mode (Fleishman Is in Trouble) and Robert Siegel (Welcome to Chippendales). Free with RSVP; register here. Tonight, March 29, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Masks are encouraged but not required in all SVA buildings and all SVA students, faculty and staff must still complete their daily health attestation via Cleared4 before entering any campus building.
