Events

12:00 | MFA Visual Narrative Application Q&A
The MFA Visual Narrative program empowers the next generation of storytellers with the skills and expertise to change the world through story. If you are interested in applying, drop in to chat with department chair Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay), who will be available to answer any questions about the program and the application process. Applications are due Friday, February 11, for second-round reviews, with acceptances continuing on a rolling basis for a June 2022 start as space remains available. Today, 12:00 – 1:00pm ET, and Thursday, February 3, 8:00 – 9:00pm ET, online. Register for your preferred session at the links.

1:00pm | VASA Elections Info Session
Do you want to build a team to become the VASA leadership next year? To qualify to run in the upcoming College-wide election, interested candidates must attend one information session. For more information, email activities@sva.edu. Today, 1:00pm, SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, and Friday, February 11, 3:30pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

3:00pm | Taxes 101: The Basics of Income and Sales Tax for Creative Individuals
Led by Minerva Financial Arts founder and Arts & Numbers author Elaine Grogan Luttrull, this workshop will review federal and state income tax basics for creative individuals and discuss common deductions claimed by creative entrepreneurs. We’ll also review common sales tax requirements as well as the obligation to investigate the laws in other states where artists make sales. Presented by SVA Career Development. Today, 3:00 – 5:00pm ET, on Zoom. Register here.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Campus Fire Drills
SVA Facilities Management and Environmental Health and Safety will be conducting fire drills as per New York City compliance this week. A representative from Global Security will walk through each location with a member of SVA Security and Facilities Management. The fire drills will begin on the top floors and proceed down until all floors have completed the drill. A short briefing will take place on each floor during the walk-through; depending on building occupancy, some locations will have a briefing in the lobby. We apologize in advance for any inconvenience that this may cause and will do our best to minimize any disruption to your day.

Fab Friday with Mike Dupree
VFX artist Mike Dupree has created stunning visual effects for a wide range of media from television to commercials, feature films, game cinematics and music videos. You might recognise his work from the opening title sequence of Netflix's Patriot Act with Hasan Minaj, Epic Games's Gears of War 5 and HBO's Game of Thrones. With over 10 years of experience in this field, Mike has amassed a diverse range of skills that informs his ability to solve problems creatively and efficiently. Mike will be joining us to share how he started, show us some dazzling work and impart some creative problem solving wisdom. Presented by BFA Advertising and BFA Design. Friday, February 4, 6:30pm ET, on Zoom.

SVA Community Fridge Lunar New Year Food Share
The SVA Community Fridge will have packaged food and fruits to celebrate the Lunar New Year and will be calling for Lunar New Year-related food donations (no frozen food, please) through Friday, February 11. You can also donate any amount to help fund the fridge. Click here to contribute and write "community fridge" in the comment box to ensure that your donation is directed to the project.
