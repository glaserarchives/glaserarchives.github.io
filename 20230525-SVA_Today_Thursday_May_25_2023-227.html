SVA faculty member Kelsy Postlethwait (MPS 2018 Branding) writes about the concept of “dynamic duos” in the context of the creative world for Print.

A cabaret folk-pop performance by award-winning playwright Ethan Lipton and his three-piece orchestra. Free. Tonight, May 25, 7:30pm. Online and in-person at David Rubenstein Atrium, 61 West 62nd Street.
