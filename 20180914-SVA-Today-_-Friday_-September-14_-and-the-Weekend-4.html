SVA Today
Friday, September 14, and the Weekend

Don't Miss The After School Special

Last chance to catch some screenings at SVA's annual film and animation festival. This weekend, see SVA Premieres, Ferdinand, The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie, Black Panther and Coco. Screenings are followed by Q&As with industry professionals; click here for more info. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
EVENTS

Saturday | Diverse Toons

BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects and SimplyRobotix present a panel discussion on diversity in animation featuring animator Matt Munn, director Pilar Newton-Katz and department faculty member and modeler Andre Rodriguez, and moderated by Monique Henry-Hudson (BFA 2011 Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects). RSVP here. Tomorrow, September 15, 2:00 – 4:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 301C.

Visible Futures Lab Equipment Demos

SVA's Visible Futures Lab is hosting a series of equipment demonstrations illustrating how to use laser cutters, 2D and 3D printers, and more. Sign up for a time slot here. Through Sunday, September 16. Visible Futures Lab, 132 West 21st Street, 7th floor.
PRESS PICK

"Fade to Black"

Blouin Artinfo shares images from Gary Simmons' (BFA 1988 Fine Arts) exhibition "Fade to Black," which is on view at the California African American Museum in Los Angeles.
NYC PICKS

"Totem," a Solo Show by MOR

A solo exhibition presented by stencil artist MOR that centers around her depiction of various symbols that she has familiarized herself with through her vast works in stencil art. Free. RSVP here. Through Sunday, September 16. Con Artist Collective & Gallery, 119 Ludlow Street.

Mad. Sq. Park Kids Fest

A free family-friendly festival featuring a diverse lineup of performing arts programming. Saturday, September 15, 11:00am. Madison Square Park.

Jean-Luc Godard's Sympathy for the Devil

An exclusive New York theatrical run of the newly restored collaboration between The Rolling Stones and director Jean-Luc Godard, Sympathy for the Devil (One Plus One). Free with SVA ID. Through Wednesday, September 19. The Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53rd Street.
TAKE NOTE

Free Risograph Workshop

Join Zen and the Art of Riso, a workshop held by Stewart Wilson from the artist-in-residence program at MFA Visual Narrative's RisoLab. Limited seats, no experience needed. Free but must register here. Monday, September 17, 6:00 – 9:00pm. RisoLab, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.

i3 Lecture: Laura Barisonzi

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by sports and fitness photographer Laura Barisonzi. Tuesday, September 18, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

MFA Social Documentary Film Thesis Showcase

MFA Social Documentary Film presents its eighth annual thesis showcase, featuring previews of documentary shorts and in-progress features by the program's class of 2018. Filmmakers will be present, and Q&As will follow each screening. RSVP and see the screening schedule here. Thursday, September 20 – Saturday, September 22. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
