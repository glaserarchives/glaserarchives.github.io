“Marshall Had a Divine Power”
For Print, MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller wrote a tribute to the late artist, longtime SVA faculty member and MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Chair Marshall Arisman.

Correction to Yesterday’s SVA Today
Yesterday’s SVA Today incorrectly named the MFA program founded and chaired for 38 years by Marshall Arisman—it is MFA Illustration as Visual Essay. We apologize for and regret the error.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am ET. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.
