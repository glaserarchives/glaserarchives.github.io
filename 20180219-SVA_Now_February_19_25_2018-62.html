EVENTS

Artist Talk with Liu Shiyuan

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents Beijing-based artist and program alumnus Liu Shiyuan, who will discuss her work in anticipation of her solo show "Isolated Above, Connected Down" at Tanya Bonakdar Gallery, New York. RSVP here. Tuesday, February 20, 7:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

Shannon Mattern, "Ether/Ore: An Atlas of Urban Media"

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents a talk by Shannon Mattern, associate professor of media studies at The New School, who will discuss topics in her new book Code and Clay, Data and Dirt: 5000 Years of Urban Media. RSVP here. Tuesday, February 20, 6:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

Network Aesthetics

MA Curatorial Practice presents Patrick Jagoda, David Joselit, Steven Henry Madoff and Stephanie Owens, who will discuss their own work as researchers and producers in the expanding field of "network aesthetics," which explores artistic production and what it means to live, work and communicate under the sign of algorithmic, networked life. Thursday, February 22, 7:00pm. CP Project Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

"One of a Kind: The Dresses of Madison Avenue"

An exhibition of fashion ensembles repurposing everyday objects created by Kevin O'Callaghan's 3D Design students. Reception: Wednesday, February 28, 6:00pm. On view through Friday, March 16. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
