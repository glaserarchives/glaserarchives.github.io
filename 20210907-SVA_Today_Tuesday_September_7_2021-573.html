Exhibitions on View
Check out these exhibitions in-person at the SVA Galleries!

"SVA: Making Art More Than Ever," an exhibition of projections of multidisciplinary works by students and recent graduates. To give a historic contrast to these all-new works, the exhibition will also include a selection of popular SVA subway posters from the past six decades. Wednesday, August 18 – Thursday, September 9. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

"The Art That Hope Inspired," an exhibition of slideshows that display juried multidisciplinary works by 59 SVA students and recent alumni. Through Tuesday, September 21, via monitors in the SVA Flatiron Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.

"Murals of Hope," an exhibition of uplifting student murals created last winter. Through Tuesday, September 21. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street. Click here to watch a video about the mural project by Yijia Zeng (MFA 2021 Social Documentary Film).

"Welcome Back SVA," an exhibition of animation reels and posters by BFA Animation students and printed works by BFA Illustration students. Through Tuesday, September 21. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Sophia Dawson's "Correspondence"
The Guardian speaks with artist and SVA faculty member and alumnus Sophia Dawson (BFA 2010 Fine Arts) about the artwork on view in her exhibition at the SVA Flatiron Project Space, featuring correspondence and ephemera from jailed activists.

International Student Office (ISO) Appointments and Drop-in Hours
Book a virtual appointment: Starting today, September 7, ISO will be using a new webpage where students can schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor at svaiso.youcanbook.me. Find step-by-step instructions here.

Virtual drop-in hours: Every Thursday from 9:30 – 11:00am, ISO will offer virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor virtually without an appointment. Sign up here.

In addition to the virtual appointments and drop-in advising, students can also contact ISO by email at iso@sva.edu. This account is monitored during normal business hours, Monday – Friday, 9:00am – 5:00pm.
