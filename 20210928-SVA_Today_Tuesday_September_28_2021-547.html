Tomorrow | H&S Talks: It's OK to Not Be OK
We can all use help asking for help. Learn the who, what, why and how of reaching out when you need it most. Hosted by SVA Humanities and Sciences (H&S) in collaboration with Student Health and Counseling Services and featuring Jeanne Villani, licensed psychologist and H&S faculty member. Tune into the discussion tomorrow, September 29, 11:00am. Fill out this form to receive a Zoom link. Reach out to Susan Kim, skim60@sva.edu, with any questions.

VASA Halloween Events for SVA Students This Month
Six Flags Fright Fest with VASA
Join VASA at Six Flags for Fright Fest on Saturday, October 9. Ticket sales start tomorrow, September 29, 10:00am. Purchase here; $25 each for students only, limit one ticket per person. SVA ID required. Departure at 10:30am from 133/141 West 21st Street and return at 10:30pm. Tickets sell out quickly so be sure to catch them before they're gone!

VASA Halloween Party at Dave & Busters
Join VASA's annual Halloween party at Dave & Buster's in Times Square on Thursday, October 28, at 8:00pm. There will be arcade games, a costume contest, prizes and dancing! Tickets are $10 before Thursday, October 14, and $15 after; purchase here. Open to SVA students only (SVA ID required for entry). Face masks will be required and no food is permitted, so eat beforehand.

Call for Submissions: Visual Opinion Student Magazine
Submit your artwork to the Visual Opinion today and get paid! VO is SVA's student-run magazine. The next edition's theme is "exposed." Deadline: Tuesday, October 5, 9:00am. Email your submission to vo@sva.edu with your name, title of the piece, and your year and major. Please note that only students residing in the United States can submit.
