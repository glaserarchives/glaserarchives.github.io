EVENTS

5:00pm | The Artists Roundtable: Carrie Mae Weems (New York)

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with artist Carrie Mae Weems, whose practice investigates family relationships, cultural identity, sexism, class, political systems and the consequences of power in her work. (Weems, who has taught at SVA, was also the College’s 2016 commencement speaker; see her remarks here.) Tonight, September 12, 5:00 – 6:00pm, online.

6:00pm | “Leigh Behnke: Time Travelers and Ghost Ships” Reception

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents “Time Travelers and Ghost Ships,” recent paintings by artist and SVA faculty member Leigh Behnke, as its first exhibition of the 2023 – 2024 academic year. Reception tonight, September 12, 6:00 – 8:00pm. On view through Friday, September 15. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

6:30pm | RisoLAB and Visual Narrative Info Session

MFA Visual Narrative and the SVA RisoLAB are offering a wide range of continuing education classes for the fall 2023 semester—attend one of the two online information sessions this week to hear about the Risograph printing courses available. Tonight, September 12, and Thursday, September 14, 6:30 – 8:30pm, online.

6:30pm | Thesis Screening 2023

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents its 2023 thesis screening, featuring lens- and screen-based media by three alumni from the class of 2023. Tonight, September 12, 6:30 – 8:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Rayon Richards

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk with Brooklyn-based portrait and interior design photographer Rayon Richards (BFA 2004 Photography). Tuesday, September 12, 7:00 – 8:30pm. RSVP for location.

SVA Flu Vaccine Clinic

To keep the community healthy, SVA Human Resources and Student Health and Counseling Services are partnering with Adirondack Health to bring a flu-vaccine clinic to campus later this month and in October, for a total of three days. This clinic will be available to all students, staff and faculty.


Please use the links associated with each date to sign up for one 15-minute time slot for a vaccine. Adirondack Health accepts most commercial insurance plans, including Cigna.


Appointments will be available on:

Tuesday, September 26, 3:30 – 6:00pm, 132 West 21st Street, Room 1220E; register here.


Tuesday, October 10, 8:00 – 10:00am, 340 East 24th Street, lower-level board room; register here.


Wednesday, October 11, 1:00 – 3:30pm, 340 East 24th Street, lower-level board room; register here.

Women in Entertainment—Sessions From New York

Women in Entertainment presents a half-day summit bringing together professionals across TV, film and theater to discuss issues that are changing the field and how to rise above them. This event will explore the evolving landscape of storytelling, content strategies and project development in New York City’s entertainment industry. Gain valuable insights from top New York City-based creatives and discover the keys to breaking into the creative scene. Wednesday, September 27, 9:30am – 2:00pm. A limited number of free tickets are available to SVA students, faculty and staff with the code “SVA2023.”

Book a New Museum Tour for Your Class

For the fall 2023 semester, SVA’s New Museum University Level membership allows select opportunities for free self-guided tours. Faculty are encouraged to take advantage of these tours for their classes. Please contact Miranda Pierce at mpierce1@sva.edu for details.
