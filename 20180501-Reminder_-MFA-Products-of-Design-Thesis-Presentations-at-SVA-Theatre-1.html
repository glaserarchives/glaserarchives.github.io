ABOUT THE PROGRAM
The MFA in Products of Design is an immersive, two-year graduate program that creates exceptional practitioners for leadership in the shifting terrain of design. We educate heads, hearts and hands to reinvent systems and catalyze positive change. Graduates emerge with confidence, methods, experience and strong professional networks. They gain the skills necessary to excel at top design firms and progressive organizations, create ingenious enterprises of their own, and become lifelong advocates for the power of design.
