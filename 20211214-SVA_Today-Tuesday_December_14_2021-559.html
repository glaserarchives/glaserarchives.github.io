My Art Portfolio: Then and Now
This edition of our YouTube series My Art Portfolio features Grace Toscano (BFA 2021 Illustration), who showcases the progression of her art portfolio from late high school to her senior year at SVA. Check out more of Toscano’s work via her Instagram (@iswimmi) and her website.
Events

11:00am | Stress Less for Finals: Yoga for Artists
Take a self-care break with Student Health and Counseling Services and prioritize mind–body health by participating in a free, gentle yoga class and learning stretches to help release stress and tension. Today, 11:00am – 12:00pm ET, online. RSVP here.

12:00pm | MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism Alumni Panel and Info Session
Join an all-star lineup of MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism alumni beaming in from around the world for our virtual panel and info session. Come hear about what our alumni have been up to since graduating and learn how our two-semester program can help you achieve your personal and professional goals. This year, we’re waiving application fees for event attendees so there’s no reason not to attend and apply! Today, 12:00 – 2:00pm ET, online. Register here.

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Polly Irungu
Presented by MPS Digital Photography, the latest i3 Photo Lecture features Polly Irungu, a multimedia journalist, self-taught photographer and founder of Black Women Photographers. Tonight, 7:00 – 8:30pm ET, online. Via Zoom.
