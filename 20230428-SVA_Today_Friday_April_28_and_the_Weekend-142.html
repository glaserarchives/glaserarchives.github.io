NYC PICKS

Black Web Fest 2023

A two-day festival that brings together Black creators from around the world to share their work, learn new skills and network. Tickets: $28.52. Today, April 28, 1:00 – 8:00pm, and tomorrow, April 29, 1:00 – 8:00pm. Heyground Harlem, 461 West 126th Street.

“From Depero To Rotella”: An Exclusive Guided Tour With Paul Shaw

A guided tour of the Center for Italian Modern Art’s current exhibition, “From Depero to Rotella: Italian commercial posters between advertising and art through the lens of typographic design,” with SVA faculty member Paul Shaw, noted calligrapher and design historian with an expertise in modernist typography. Free for students. Tonight, April 28, 6:00pm. Center for Italian Modern Art, 421 Broome Street, 4th floor.

16mm Cartoon Carnival #109: Tech Time

SVA faculty member Tommy José Stathes hosts a screening of animated cartoons themed around then-new technologies, fantasies about future technology, robotics and inventiveness in general from the 1920s through the 1950s. Tickets $15. Sunday, April 30, 5:00pm. 389 Melrose Street, Brooklyn.
