SVA Today | Your guide to everything
Monday, October 5, 2015
Three SVA Alumni Named 2015 ADC Young Guns
Christopher Brand (BFA 2007 Graphic Design), Daniel Zender (MFA 2014 Illustration as Visual Essay) and Zipeng Zhu (BFA 2013 Design) were among the 19 recipients of this year's award, which honors outstanding advertising and graphic design work by creative professionals under the age of 30.
Date Change: PMDA Ceremony
The PMDA ceremony scheduled to take place tonight has been postponed. The event will now take place on Tuesday, November 10. The event time and location remain the same.
10:30am: Pixar Visits SVA
Join Pixar's University Relations team as they discuss the animation studio's internship program and what they look for when reviewing student reels. Presented by SVA Career Development. RSVP required. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
Opening: "Appalachian Travelogue"
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of work in various media by alumnus George Rue (BFA 2014 Visual & Critical Studies), which documents the artist’s travels along the Appalachian Trail. Curated by faculty member Peter Hristoff. Reception on Friday, October 9, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Through Thursday, October 29, VCS Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

"Swimming With Sharks" by Katherine Bernhardt. Photo: Jason Mandella/Venus Over Manhattan
Great Women Bad-Boy Artists
In New York, critic Jerry Saltz reflects on "bad-boy" female artists, including Katherine Bernhardt (MFA 2000 Fine Arts).
NYC pick of the day
Edgar Allan Poe and His Ghostly Neighbors of Greenwich Village
Learn about the spooky history of such Greenwich Village sites as the New York University Library and "the most haunted area in America," Washington Square Park. Tickets $15 for students and seniors, $20 general admission. 7:30pm, 85 West Third Street.
Take Note
CINE Celebrates and CINE PitchFest
CINE presents CINE Celebrates, a new awards event honoring film and television professionals, and CINE PitchFest, a chance for five indie producers to pitch their projects to executives in only five minutes. Meet industry insiders and learn firsthand how projects are pitched. Free for the first 25 SVA students who register. $25 student tickets for CINE Celebrates; $10 student tickets for CINE PitchFest. General admission ticket prices vary. Wednesday, October 7, 6:00pm, and Thursday, October 8, 1:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
To Park or Not to Park?
MFA Interaction Design presents Nikki Sylianteng (MFA 2013 Interaction Design) discussing her guerrilla parking sign project. Wednesday, October 7, 6:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.
"The Martian" (2015) Screening
SVA and the Visual Effects Society present a 3D screening of The Martian (2015), directed by Ridley Scott and starring Matt Damon, Jessica Chastain and Chiwetel Ejiofor. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). Thursday, October 8, 8:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
MFA Design Information Session
MFA Design presents an open house and information session featuring faculty, staff, students and alumni. RSVP required. Saturday, October 10, 1:00 - 4:00pm, 380 Second Avenue, 5th floor.
MPS Art Therapy Information Session
MPS Art Therapy presents an information session featuring faculty, students and alumni. RSVP required. Saturday, October 17, 12:00 - 2:00pm, 132 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.
