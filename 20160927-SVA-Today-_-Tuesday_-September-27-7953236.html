EVENTS

7:00pm: Gail Albert Halaban

Photographer Gail Albert Halaban explores the tension between public and private life—what is seen by all, and what is hidden. Her latest series, "Vis à Vis," is set in Paris, where she photographs the interior worlds of apartments and courtyards from the outside looking in. Presented by MPS Digital Photography. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

TAKE NOTE

Call for Submissions: VASA Halloween Poster Contest

VASA is accepting submissions for its Halloween Party Poster Contest. The winning poster will be used to advertise this year's Halloween party, and the winner will receive two free tickets. The deadline is Thursday, September 29, 5:00pm. The winning poster will be announced Monday, October 3. Write vasa@sva.edu with the subject line “Halloween Contest” for poster specifications and details.

Flipster Database Trial

The SVA Library now offers trial access to Flipster, a digital newsstand with over 1,100 magazines. The database is accessible from the library's A-Z Databases page. Download the Flipster app from the iTunes store or Google Play to download magazines to read offline. The trial expires Saturday, October 22.
