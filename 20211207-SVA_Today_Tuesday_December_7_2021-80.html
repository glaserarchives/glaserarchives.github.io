SVA Today
Tuesday, December 7, 2021

SVA ♥ Milton
“SVA ♥ Milton” is a multimedia exhibition that places the enduring influence of the late designer and faculty member Milton Glaser in a real-world context and celebrates his legacy as a designer and teacher through photographs, audio and original work from the Milton Glaser Design Study Center and Archives at SVA. Through Saturday, January 15. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street, and SVA Flatiron Project Space and Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Misfits
MFA Fine Arts presents an exhibition of work by Anna Amato; MFA Fine Arts students Yirui Jia, Ailyn Lee and Se Young Yim; and Tingleguts (MFA 2021 Fine Arts). Curated by Marianna Peragallo (MFA 2019 Fine Arts). Through Thursday, January 6. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street. Register here.

Last Chance: “Your presence is the education I want”
Ma Curatorial Practice presents “Your presence is the education I want,” featuring works by Eunwoo Nam and Zi Yi Wang and curated by MA Curatorial Practice student Sophia Park. This exhibition addresses collecting as a practice to explore the physical manifestations of memory for those living in diaspora. Through tomorrow, December 8. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.
