Correction: We Love Decay: Call for Performance Proposals

Note: The Friday, February 23, issue of SVA Today spelled the contact email address incorrectly.

BFA Visual & Critical Studies seeks proposals for its second annual performance incubator, taking place from April 15 – 20 in the SVA Flatiron Project Space. Previous projects have included short-term installation, video projection, participatory artworks and durational performances, ranging from 15 minutes to 12 hours. Include a summary of the project with a description of time and technical requirements. Proposals from individuals outside of the SVA community are welcome. Email proposals to everhagen@sva.edu by Wednesday, February 28.

Contest: Create a Site-Specific Artwork for SVA Library

SVA Library’s Visual Resources Center seeks an artist to create a site-specific artwork or installation made out of the center’s collection of old slides, which will be permanently on display in the library. Proposals must not interfere with the functionality of the space. You will work with SVA Library staff to determine ideas and a timeline for completion. The winning submission will receive a $500 gift card to Blick Art Materials to use on the installation. If interested, email Lorraine Gerety, lgerety@sva.edu, and Tessa Morefield, tmorefield@sva.edu.
