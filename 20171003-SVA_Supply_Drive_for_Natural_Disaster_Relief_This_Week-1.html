Good morning,

In recent weeks, we have watched with alarm and sadness as earthquakes hit Mexico and hurricanes struck the Texas Gulf Coast, the Caribbean, Puerto Rico and Florida, leaving widespread suffering, loss and destruction in their wakes.

Our deepest sympathy goes to all who are feeling the impact of these disasters and threats, including those who have loved ones, homes and other connections in the affected regions. 

Please join Student Affairs and VASA in filling up the SVA Student Center with much needed supplies!

 

This week, Wednesday – Friday, 10:00am – 10:00pm, please stop by the Student Center, 215 East 23rd Street, to drop off supplies for the victims in the natural disasters in Mexico, Puerto Rico and the Caribbean.

 

The following supplies are needed:

  • Bottled water
  • Diapers and baby wipes
  • Medical supplies (bandages, Aspirin, alcohol, hand sanitizer)
  • Toothpaste/toothbrushes
  • Deodorant
  • Soap/body wash
  • Feminine hygiene products
  • Non-perishable/canned food such as
    • Beans, legumes
    • Canned meats
    • Canned vegetables
    • Canned fruits
    • Boxed milk
  • Batteries
  • Heavy duty trash bags
  • Portable lamps
  • Disinfectant wipes
  • Water purifying tablets

Additionally, for those who are interested and able to help those impacted by these natural disasters, you can help relief efforts by making a donation to one of the below reputable organizations:

United Way

Unicef

Red Cross


Thank you.
