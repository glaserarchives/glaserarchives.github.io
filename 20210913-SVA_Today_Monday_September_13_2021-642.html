SVA IN THE PRESS

I ❤️ New York More Than Ever
For Quartz, Anne Quito (MFA 2014 Design Criticism) writes about how, after the terrorist attacks on September 11, 2001, late design legend and SVA faculty member Milton Glaser used his "I ❤️ NY" logo to create a new symbol of hope and strength for New York City that persists to this day.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Support Haiti Relief
Last month, the nation of Haiti was struck by a devastating earthquake. Dr. Jarvis Watson, SVA's director of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion, writes about Haiti's history and presents a list of organizations through which individuals can support relief efforts in the country.

New Course for International Students: English for Professional Communication
In this six-week online course, students will learn to write formal emails, participate in job and internship interviews, make small talk to network like a pro, and much more. Fluency and pronunciation will be an important part of this course as students develop communicative confidence.

Students can contact their academic advisor to sign up. For more information about this course, please contact Helene Rubinstein, associate chair of English as a Second Language, at hrubinstein@sva.edu.

There are two sessions of this course, taking place via Zoom, Monday, September 27 – Monday, November 1: Mondays, 9:00 – 11:00am ET (EVD0341A), and Mondays, 12:00 – 2:00pm (EVD0341B).
