Events

Ongoing | SVA ♥ Milton
SVA presents “SVA ♥ Milton,” an exhibition celebrating the legendary life and work of the late designer and longtime faculty member Milton Glaser. Due to COVID-19 precautions, the exhibition is currently open only to SVA students, faculty and staff. Through Saturday, February 5. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street, and SVA Flatiron Project Space and Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.
SVA IN THE PRESS

Communication Arts on the SVA Alumni-Founded Champions Design
Champions Design founders Bobby C. Martin Jr. (MFA 2003 Design), Jennifer Kinon (MFA 2003 Design) and lead strategist Carina Sandoval (MPS 2018 Branding) have been featured in Communication Arts, describing how the chemistry between the two founders first blossomed during their time at SVA.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

John Chamberlain: Stance, Rhythm, and Tilt
Gagosian presents “John Chamberlain: Stance, Rhythm, and Tilt,” an exhibition of sculptures by sculptor John Chamberlain gathered over a 60-year period curated by art historian Susan Davidson. Through Saturday, September 5. Gagosian Gallery, 522 West 21st Street.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

COVID-19 Reporting and Vaccine Booster Mandate
If you've tested positive for COVID-19, you must report your status to SVA as soon as possible. Students should email student-covid-alert@sva.edu; staff should email employee-covid-alert@sva.edu.

SVA also requires the vaccine booster for those who are eligible; proof of the booster must be uploaded to Cleared4 by Monday, January 24. Click here for more information about SVA's COVID-19 policies and protocols.

New Public Speaking Skills Course for International Students
A six-week online Public Speaking Skills course (EVD0340A) is now being offered to international students. Mondays, 12:10 – 2:10pm, on Zoom. Email Helene Rubinstein with any questions.
