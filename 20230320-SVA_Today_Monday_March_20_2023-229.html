The Monacelli Press

Milton Glaser: POP

A new book by three longtime SVA community members and upcoming exhibition at the SVA Gramercy Gallery celebrate the late designer, illustrator and SVA faculty member Milton Glaser’s “groovy” period.

EVENTS

12:00pm | Bill Plympton Presents SLIDE

MFA Computer Arts presents a special test screening of Academy Award-nominated animator Bill Plympton’s (1969 Cartooning) upcoming feature film, SLIDE, an animated Western about a slide guitar-playing cowboy. Today, March 20, 12:00 – 2:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Alex Kitnick on “Normal Art”

BFA Visual & Critical Studies and the SVA Honors Program present art historian Alex Kitnick to discuss what “normal art” is and if it is something we want to make. Tonight, March 20, 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

SVA IN THE PRESS

Zhiyu You

Blending Chinese Mythology, Western Imagery and Societal Pressures

Zhiyu You (BFA 2022 Illustration) is featured in Creative Boom for her artwork that illustrates the plight of women.

NYC AND ONLINE PICK

Meredith Broussard: Bias Is More Than a Glitch

Data journalist and educator Meredith Broussard discusses how neutrality in tech is a myth and why algorithms need to be held accountable for confronting race, gender, and ability biases in tech. Stavros Niarchos Foundation Library, Seventh Floor Event Center, 455 Fifth Avenue.

“Third World Mixtapes: The Infrastructure of Feeling”

A solo exhibition of work by Shellyne Rodriguez (BFA 2011 Visual & Critical Studies) stewarding the stories of people that have shaped her lived experience. Through Saturday, April 22. P·P·O·W Gallery, 390 Broadway, second floor.

Last Day to Submit Applications for Student Center Staff

The SVA Student Center is accepting staff applications for the 2023 – 2024 academic year. Students who are excited about the community and eager to participate on campus are encouraged to apply. Become part of the team and work alongside peers and SVA Student Affairs in this exciting experience! Position details can be found here. Apply here by 12:00pm today, March 20. Questions? Final Call for Submissions: Visual Opinion Graduation Issue

All seniors graduating this 2022 – 2023 academic year are invited to submit their work forVisual Opinion’s annual graduation issue—paintings, drawings, photography, sculptures, designs, film stills, poems, etc. The deadline is today, March 20. Submit your best artwork to vo@sva.edu in a high resolution format of 300+ dpi. More information is available on Instagram @visualopinion.

Friday | Yoga for Artists: Anxiety and Muscle Tension Relief

Take a break with SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and yoga instructor Melissa Boo. Learn yoga poses that will help reduce anxiety and provide tension relief. Rest and restore in a free, beginner-friendly, virtual yoga class. Open to the entire SVA community—students, staff and faculty. RSVP required; register here. Friday, March 24, 10:00 – 10:45am, online.

Friday | Gartic Phone Drawing Game

Show off your artistic skills and enjoy delicious donuts with VASA while playing Gartic Phone, the game of telephone—with drawing! Students are required to bring an iPad/tablet and smart pen. Friday, March 24, 1:30 – 3:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street.
