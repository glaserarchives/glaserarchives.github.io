SVA Today
Thursday, October 19, 2017

SVA's Emmy Winner

A Q&A with David Osit (MFA 2011 Social Documentary Film), who took home the Emmy Award for Outstanding Arts and Culture Documentary for his film Thank You for Playing.
EVENTS

8:15am | Yearbook Photo Shoot

SVA Yearbook is shooting portraits for the 2018 edition. All fourth-year students are encouraged to dress how they like, stop by and have their photo taken. The yearbook is given to students at commencement for free. 8:15am – 12:25pm. 209 East 23rd Street, ground floor.

5:00pm | VASA Student Senator Information Session

Visual Arts Student Association (VASA) invites students to attend an information session to learn how to become your department's Student Senator. Email vasa@sva.edu with any questions. Apply here. 5:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

5:30pm | MAT Art Education Open House

Join MAT Art Education to learn about the field of art education and the program from faculty, current students and alumni. Register here. 5:30 – 7:30pm. 132 West 21st Street, 4th floor.
PRESS PICK

"This Fantasy Is Over"

ArtNet News on how artist and fashion photographer Collier Schorr (BFA 1985 Communication Arts) has called on agents, magazines and designers to stand up to harassment.
NYC PICKS

Robert Lazzarini and Noah Becker

A conversation between contemporary artist Robert Lazzarini (BFA 1990 Fine Arts) and writer Noah Becker on the occasion of Lazzarini’s recent exhibition, "Inflorescence," on view at De Buck Gallery through Thursday, October 26. 6:00pm. De Buck Gallery, 545 West 23rd Street.

Judith Hopf Opening Reception

The NYU Institute of Fine Arts Great Hall Exhibition Series presents an exhibition featuring films and brick sculptures by Berlin-based artist Judith Hopf. 6:30pm. Through January 2018. NYU Institute of Fine Arts, 1 East 78th Street.
TAKE NOTE

Eli Schmidt

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by photographer Eli Schmidt, whose work is a fusion of fashion, documentary and editorial sensibilities, as part of its i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tuesday, October 24, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Floyd Norman

An evening with legendary Disney animator Floyd Norman, featuring a screening of the 2016 award-winning documentary about him, Floyd Norman: An Animated Life, followed by a Q&A with Norman himself. RSVPs are neither required nor accepted. Doors open for SVA ID holders at 5:30pm; if seats remain, they will be released to the public at 6:00pm. Saturday, October 28, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
