TAKE NOTE

Announcing the Spring 2016 Dean's List

A total of 1,732 students from SVA's 11 undergraduate majors have been named to the latest dean's list, which is now available on MySVA. To be included, students must successfully complete 12 credits of coursework, without any grades of incomplete, and earn a term GPA of at least 3.5. Congratulations to the honorees!
