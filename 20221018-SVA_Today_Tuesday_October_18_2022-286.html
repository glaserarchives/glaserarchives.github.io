NYC PICK

Picture Book Bohemia: A Children’s Book Walking Tour of Greenwich Village

MFA Visual Narrative faculty member Leonard Marcus will lead two walking tours of Greenwich Village, a creative bohemia for children’s book authors and writers. On this two-hour tour, see where the author of Goodnight Moon got her start as a writer, where Maurice Sendak and Eric Carle produced their most famous books and the ghostly building where American children’s book publishing began. Advance registration is required; register by email at lm@leonardmarcus.com. The tours will begin on the northeast corner of Fifth Avenue and West 12th Street. $10 for students, $30 for non-students. Saturday, October 22, 10:00am – 12:00pm, and Saturday, October 29, 10:00am – 12:00pm.
