Events

Ongoing | SVA ♥ Milton
SVA presents “SVA ♥ Milton,” an exhibition celebrating the legendary life and work of the late designer and longtime faculty member Milton Glaser. Due to COVID-19 precautions, the exhibition is currently open only to SVA students, faculty and staff. Through Saturday, February 5. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | Narratives, Stories, Algorithms: Rethinking Independence in Digital Times
Presented by MA Curatorial Practice, “Narratives, Stories, Algorithms: Rethinking Independence in Digital Times,” is a new exhibition curated by Xiao Zong (MA 2021 Curatorial Practice) that questions ways in which we receive information, form opinions and draw conclusions in our digital era of fragmented information, fabricated truths and malleable narratives. Through Thursday, January 27. Pfizer Building, 630 Flushing Avenue, 5th floor.

The Booster Bus Is Coming!
Need a vaccine booster? All eligible SVA students, faculty and staff members in need of a COVID-19 vaccine booster can get one at a “booster bus,” provided by Student Health and Counseling Services, on Tuesday or Friday of next week. Be sure to bring proof of your prior vaccination and a photo ID (i.e., government-issued driver's license or passport). For questions about booster eligibility, click here.

Remember to upload your booster verification into Cleared4 by Monday, January 24. The bus will be parked in front of the SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street, on Tuesday, January 18, and Friday, January 21, 9:00am – 6:00pm. No appointments required.

Call for Submissions: Reflecting on the Legacy of Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.
In celebration of Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.'s birthday, Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at SVA is inviting submissions of work reflecting on Dr. King’s legacy and life’s work. This call is open to all students, faculty and staff; examples of work include written pieces, paintings, videos, photos, illustrations, etc. Select work may be included in programming and on social media for SVA’s observance of Black History month in February. For more information or to submit your work, click here.

Virtual Support Space for International Students: New Year’s Resolutions
Do you have a New Year's resolution that you want to share? Join Holly from Student Health and Counseling Services and Will from the International Student Office for a Spring 2022 Meet and Greet Virtual Support Space for international students. There will be resources offered as well as opportunities to connect with others, share your resolutions and an open forum for international students to ask any questions. Friday, January 21, 2:00pm ET, on Zoom.
