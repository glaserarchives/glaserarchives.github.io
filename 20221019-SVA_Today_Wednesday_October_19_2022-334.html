MA Curatorial Practice presents Amy Cheng, co-founder and curator of TheCube Project Space in Taipei, to discuss radio as a tool for artistic freedom. Today, October 19, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.

MPS Art Therapy presents a virtual information session featuring a panel of current students, alumni, faculty and staff who will provide insight into the interdisciplinary graduate program and the application process. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer or fall 2023 admission. Tonight, October 19, 5:00 – 6:00pm, online.

“The Artists’ Experience: From Brotherman to Batman”
