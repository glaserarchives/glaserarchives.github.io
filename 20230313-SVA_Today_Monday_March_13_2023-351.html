“Mentors” 2023 Q&A

EVENTS

10:30am | Student Leadership Series: Scrub the Stress Away

Join Student Engagement and Leadership and Student Health and Counseling Services to make your own sugar scrubs and have a conversation about stress management at the Student Center! Materials are provided. Today, March 13, 10:30am. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Questions? Email activities@sva.edu.

11:00am | 2023 VASA Election Candidate Q&A

Meet this year’s Visual Arts Student Association candidates during a Q&A session where they will discuss their platforms and answer students’ questions. Today, March 13, 11:00am, via Zoom here. Another Q&A will be held tomorrow, March 14, 11:00am, via Zoom.

6:00pm | “Mentors 2023” Reception

BFA Photography and Video presents an exhibition of artwork by students inspired by their working relationships with world-renowned artists, curators, editors and directors from the global arts community. Today, March 13, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Last Chance | “Material Mythologies”

A juried exhibition of drawing, painting, photography and sculpture by 12 students. Closes today, March 13. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Last Chance | “rupture and repair”

An exhibition of multimedia artworks by MPS Art Therapy students, collaborators from their internship sites and community members. Closes today, March 13. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

NYC AND ONLINE PICK

Ethics in Public Art Talk

A panel of artists and activists discuss the intersections between public art and issues of equity, representation and social justice. Free. Online and in-person; while in-person registration for this event has sold out, a limited number of standby tickets will be available at the event tonight. Tonight, March 13, 6:30pm. Online and at Stavros Niarchos Foundation Library, 7th Floor Event Center, 455 Fifth Avenue.
