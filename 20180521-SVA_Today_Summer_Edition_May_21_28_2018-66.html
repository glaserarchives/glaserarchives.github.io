SVA Subway Series Win Graphis Poster Awards

Four SVA Subway Series campaigns created by SVA’s Acting Chairman of the Board Milton Glaser and BFA Illustration faculty Chris Buzelli, Edel Rodriguez and Yuko Shimizu (MFA 2003 Illustration as Visual Essay) have won high honors in the 2019 Graphis Poster Annual.

THIS WEEK

Monday | Matthew Connors and Sam Contis in Conversation with Steel Stillman

With artist and writer Steel Stillman, artists Matthew Connors and Sam Contis will discuss their recent bodies of work, which are currently featured in "Being: New Photography 2018" at The Museum of Modern Art. Monday, May 21, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Tuesday | Trembling Mountain with Kesang Tseten

MFA Social Documentary Film presents a screening of the documentary Trembling Mountain with the film's director Kesang Tseten. Free; RSVP to Amitabh Joshi at ajoshi@mfasocdoc.com. Tuesday, May 22, 6:30pm. SocDoc Auditorium, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Wednesday | Continuing Education Information Sessions

Learn about Continuing Education's course offerings. Information sessions will take place Wednesday, May 23, 6:30pm.

Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects: 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 301C

Interior Design: 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 1104C

Thursday | "Catalysts in the Morning Dew" Reception

A juried exhibition featuring illustrations, paintings, photographs and mixed-media collages that are inspired by socio-political themes throughout the world. Thursday, May 24, 6:00pm. Through Tuesday, June 5. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Sunday | Solo: A Star Wars Story

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Solo: A Star Wars Story, starring Alden Ehrenreich, followed by a live simulcast Q&A with the film's visual effects artists. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Sunday, May 27, 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Georgia O'Keeffe: Visions of Hawai'i

Hawaiian flora and 15 of pioneering modernist Georgia O'Keeffe's paintings are on display together in this exhibition. Tickets $10 – $28; purchase here. Through October 2018. New York Botanical Garden, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.

Freelance Isn't Free

Celebrate the first anniversary of the Freelance Isn’t Free Act (Local Law 140) with a special Q&A with local creative, technical, government and business leaders to review the enhanced protections for freelance workers, freelancer rights and remedies for wage theft violations. Free and open to the public. RSVPs not accepted or required. Wednesday, May 24, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Mad. Sq. Art Annual Symposium: Removing Public Art

The role of public art has been at the center of the controversy over Confederate and other historic monuments and contemporary art. Madison Square Park Conservancy and SVA present a symposium where artists, cultural leaders and scholars will lead a conversation on historic and contemporary monuments and current solutions. RSVP to symposium@madisonsquarepark.org. Friday, June 1, 9:00am – noon. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
