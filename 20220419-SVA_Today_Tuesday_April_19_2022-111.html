ANNOUNCEMENTS

Friday | Earth Day Walkout
Join the Indigenous Student Union for an Earth Day school walkout. Students are invited to walk out of class and attend a series of intergenerational indigenous teach-ins featuring climate activists from Abya Yala (Mexico, Central and South America). Learn about the climate crisis, intergenerational organizing, United Nations Permanent Forum on Indigenous Issues (UNPFII) and how to stay connected and involved within your communities. The teach-in will be held at 209 East 23rd Street in the 3rd floor amphitheater. Open to SVA students, faculty, and alumni only. Visitors are asked to bring their Eventbrite registration with them to the event. Everyone must remain masked, except where otherwise noted. RSVP here. Space is limited. Friday, April 22 at 1:00pm. Those who cannot attend in-person, or who are outside the SVA community, are welcome to join the conversation via Zoom. Meeting ID: 819 8917 3695.

Friday | Give and Take with SVA

As part of Earth Week, VASA invites the SVA community to join them for Give and Take with SVA, an opportunity to declutter and drop off any unused art materials at the SVA Student Center. In addition to this event, free bamboo cutlery will also be available all week long at the SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Next Week | BFA Animation Alumni Talk with Adam Rosette
Join BFA Animation for a Twitch livestream with Adam Rosette (BFA 2007 Animation), head of story at Dreamworks and story artist on The Bad Guys, moderated by faculty member Chrissy Fellmeth (BFA 2007 Animation), storyboard artist, director and animator. Wednesday, April 27, 6:30pm ET, online.

Distinguished Staff Award Nominations
Each year at the All-Staff Meeting, SVA recognizes outstanding staff contributions to the College with the presentation of two Distinguished Staff Awards. Each award includes a cash prize of $5,000. It’s up to you to nominate one of your administrative-staff colleagues to receive this award. Award information, a list of eligible employees and the nomination form are available at staffawards.sva.edu. Nominations are due at 5:00pm on Friday, May 6.
