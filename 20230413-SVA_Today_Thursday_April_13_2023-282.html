ANNOUNCEMENTS

Next Week | Carmen Screening and Director Q&A

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a preview screening of Carmen, the feature directorial debut of acclaimed choreographer Benjamin Millepied starring Melissa Barrera, Oscar nominee Paul Mescal and Rossy de Palma. Millepied’s reimagining of Carmen tells its story through an experimental dreamscape featuring an original score by Nicholas Britell and original songs by Britell, Tracy ‘the D.O.C.’ Curry, Taura Stinson and Julieta Venegas. There will be a Q&A with Millepied following the screening. Registration is required and attendees must present their SVA ID at the door (non-SVA guests are permitted if accompanied by an attendee with an SVA ID); RSVP here. Watch the trailer here. Monday, April 17, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | Self-love in the Time of Finals

Take a cozy cocoa break and de-stress from finals with Holly Fu and Rebecca Amoah from SVA Student Health and Counseling Services. Write yourself a love letter and reflect on how far you’ve come this semester while enjoying hot cocoa and cookies. Hot cocoa, cookies and stationery will be provided. Thursday, April 20, 2:30 – 4:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.
