Pop-Up Magazine in NYC 10/30

An MFAVN Endorsed Event!

The MFA Visual Narrative program is proud to partner with Pop-Up Magazine, the live storytelling event heralded by the New York Times as “a sensation.”


We urge all of you to attend - we have found it to be a sparkling evening of contemporary storytelling. An eclectic mix of writers, radio producers, filmmakers, and photographers brings a night of brand-new, true, multimedia stories to the stage, with a live score by their house band Magik*Magik Orchestra. The Fall Issue coming to New York includes Kurt Andersen (Studio 360, New York Times Bestselling Author), Aparna Nancherla (Crashing, Late Night with Seth Meyers), Sean Rameswaram (Radiolab’s More Perfect), and more. 
You can also submit your own ideas for consideration for a future performance, with the development supported by Pop Up. Check that out here.

Oct. 30, 7:30pm
David Geffen Hall at Lincoln Center
Tickets start at $36
Buy Tickets: https://www.popupmagazine.com/
Performances in October and November also in SF, LA, Portland, DC and the Twin Cities.
