Two-Week Summer Documentary Workshop
Apply by April 1!

We’re launching our first ever summer intensive workshop, June 11-22 at our department office in NYC. "The Art of the Documentary Interview” is a hands-on, collaborative, two-week ‘Mini-SocDoc’ where emerging filmmakers will have the chance to polish their skills as directors while learning from our experienced faculty.

Direct, film & edit two interview-based documentaries on industry-standard equipment, and get the skills you need to push your next projects forward. Undergrads: get 2 studio credits for the workshop!

Applications due April 1. Click here for more info and to apply.

 

SocDoc at Spring Film Festivals

Spring is Festival Season & we are zipping around North America this month with films from our prolific faculty and alumni. SocDocers are attending many festivals – we hope to see you around!
 
Tribeca Film Festival premieres the latest from SocDoc faculty Michael Epstein: House Two; and Hot Docs, North America's largest documentary festival, premieres two alumni films: Sasha Friedlander's Grit and Yuqi Kang's A Little Wisdom (Yuqi & her crew pictured above at SXSW) as well as several faculty films. Plus our films at New Directors/New Films, Full Frame and more! Read all about it!

 
 

🤔Thinking About Applying to SocDoc?


Although the Fall 2018 deadline has passed, we are still accepting applicants on a rolling admission basis. We are looking for the next generation of non-fiction storytellers! Prior professional experience is not necessary, but you must have a passion for filmmaking, strong story ideas, and a desire to fully immerse yourself in the industry and art of documentary filmmaking. If this sounds like you, please get in touch for more info, a tour, and to apply.
