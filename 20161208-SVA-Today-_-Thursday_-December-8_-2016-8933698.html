MPS Digital Photography Helps Help-Portrait

Earlier this month, MPS Digital Photography students, faculty and alumni participated in Help-Portrait, a global event in which photo professionals offer their services free of charge to under-served communities.

One-on-one meetings with department faculty and staff. Be prepared with a written statement and a digital presentation of your current and most accomplished work, whether video or photography. 1:00 – 3:00pm; RSVP here. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

6:00pm | Workshop for Bystander Empowerment

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and the College's Title IX Office have partnered with the Domestic & Other Violence Emergencies (DOVE) program from New York Presbyterian Hospital to host See Something, Say Whaaat?, a workshop to develop skills and understanding of how every person can intervene in instances of violence and assault. All are encouraged to attend. Light refreshments will be served. 6:00pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Holiday Angel Program

SVA Student Affairs, The Hudson Guild Community Center and Abraham House are providing gifts for those in need through SVA's annual Holiday Angels Program. To view a list of children and their wishes, click here. Confirm your selection of a child to be sponsored by calling 212.592.2140, writing akrumm@sva.edu or visiting Student Affairs, 340 East 24th Street, ground floor. Cash donations accepted in person only. The Hudson Guild also collects items for seniors, including socks, scarves, gloves, hats, new and lightly used coats, board games and books (multiple languages accepted). All gifts are due to Student Affairs Thursday, December 15, 4:00pm.
