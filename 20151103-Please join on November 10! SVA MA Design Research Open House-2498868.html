SVA MA Design Research, Writing & Criticism
Open House/Information Session

Tuesday, November 10, 2015
6:00 – 8:30 PM
136 West 21 Street, 2nd Floor, New York

Register to attend


Please join us on Tuesday, November 10, in the SVA Department of Design Research, Writing & Criticism, for an evening of insights and behind-the-scenes access into the world of design. 

Don't miss out on this once-a-year opportunity to hear firsthand from program faculty, students, and alumni about the ways in which they engage with design research and writing, and how they put it into practice in the workplace.  

Following the evening's presentations, prospective students will have the opportunity to discuss their own goals for graduate study, tour the department, and continue conversations over drinks and refreshments.

6:00 p.m. Doors open.  
6:30 p.m. Presentations by program chair, faculty, alumni, and students.
7:30 p.m. Q&A, drinks and refreshments

Visit our website for more information about the program, curriculum, and faculty

The SVA MA in Design Research, Writing & Criticism is a rigorous one-year program, well suited to established professionals as well as graduates wishing to continue their studies at an advanced level. With a two-semester timeframe, the SVA MA in Design Research provides intensive instruction in the tools and techniques for researching, analyzing, and interpreting design and its cultural and environmental implications. 

We are now accepting applications for Fall 2016 and are pleased to announce that each successful candidate will be granted a significant scholarship. Please contact us for further details.
