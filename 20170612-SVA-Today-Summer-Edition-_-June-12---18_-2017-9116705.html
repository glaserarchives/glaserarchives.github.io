TAKE NOTE

Words 86 Submission Deadline Extended

Words 86 seeks poems, stories, personal essays, short film scripts and one-act plays. Submit by email to lphillips@sva.edu with subject heading: "Submission to Words 86." All submissions should include the writer’s name, address, email and phone number. Deadline is Thursday, June 15.

Routine Systems Maintenance This Weekend

SVA Administrative and Network Services performs routine systems maintenance on the third weekend of each month, to help ensure that critical software and hardware platforms serving the community continue to operate reliably. Systems that routinely undergo maintenance include MySVA, MyServices, Web Advisor, Web Budget, Colleague, SVA Wireless Networks, SVA Wired Networks and the Cisco Phone System. Maintenance is generally performed late Saturday evening into early Sunday morning; service on these systems may be interrupted during these hours. Any questions can be directed to the SVA Helpdesk at 212.592.2400 or helpdesk@sva.edu.

Yoga and Meditation for Wellness and Creativity

Student Health and Counseling Services presents this workshop on basic yoga and meditation skills to promote well-being and stress reduction. Art Therapy Studio Space, 132 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

New Subscription: The Chronicle of Higher Ed

SVA Library has begun a site subscription to The Chronicle of Higher Education, the top destination for news, advice, and jobs for people in academe. The subscription gives the SVA community access to all premium features of the Chronicle online, including the latest articles. To access premium features of the subscription, visit the Chronicle from the library's A-Z Databases page.

Now Circulating: MoMA Corporate Pass

SVA Library has a corporate membership pass to The Museum of Modern Art to circulate to SVA faculty and staff. This pass can be checked out from the library circulation desk for one week at a time. Benefits include: free admission and priority entrance for the cardholder and up to four guests, and up to three additional guest tickets for $5 each; member early hour access to select exhibitions from 9:30 – 10:30am daily; free tickets to MoMA film programs on the day of your visit; discounts on parking, shopping, hotels and more.
