EVENTS

10:00am | SVA Graduate Admissions International Applicant Q&A

Meet International Student Office and Graduate Admissions staff and learn about the application process. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer or fall 2023 admission. Today, December 9, 10:00 – 11:00am, online.

4:00pm | 2022 VASA Holiday Bazaar

Get ready for the most wonderful time of the year at the annual VASA Holiday Bazaar! Student vendors will be selling prints, crochet, jewelry, apparel and more. Today, December 9, 4:00 – 7:00pm. Open to SVA students, faculty and staff only. SVA ID and Cleared4 are required for entry. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

7:00pm | BFA Animation Thesis Animatic Screening

BFA Animation presents a night of thesis animatic screenings! All current SVA students are invited to meet the program’s fourth-year students, check out their thesis works in progress and get a behind-the-scenes look at the animation thesis process. No RSVP needed. Tonight, December 9, 7:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 610C.

Saturday | Holiday Mixer

Join BFA Photography and Video Student Senator Shane Singh to meet fellow students, make new friends and learn about other majors at SVA. Saturday, December 10, 3:00 – 6:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 612.

Last Chance | The Masters Series: Lynsey Addario

SVA presents an exhibition of work by Lynsey Addario, acclaimed photographer, MacArthur Genius Grant and Pulitzer Prize recipient, for its 32nd annual Masters Series Award and Exhibition. Closes tomorrow, December 10. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

SVA IN THE PRESS

Dinh Quang Lê at National Gallery Singapore

Dinh Quang Lê’s (MFA 1992 Photography and Related Media) work is included in an exhibition surveying 150 years of photography in Southeast Asia at National Gallery Singapore. Via The Strait Times.

NYC PICKS

Indigenous Storytelling

Join Indigenous storyteller Perry Ground for an interactive telling of traditional native stories at the Environmental Education Center. Saturday, December 10, 11:00am. Environmental Education Center, 19 Washington Street, Brooklyn.

Coney Island Yo-Yo Fling 2022

East Coast yo-yoers come together for a day of competitions and performances. Tickets: $15. Saturday, December 10, 11:00am – 6:00pm. Coney Island USA, 1208 Surf Avenue, Brooklyn.

New York Jewish Book Festival

The Museum of Jewish Heritage’s first-ever New York Jewish Book Festival, featuring talks, panels and author signings. Free. Sunday, December 11, 10:00am. Museum of Jewish Heritage – A Living Memorial to the Holocaust, 36 Battery Place.

Your Voices

A Kinetic sculpture by Es Devlin installed at Lincoln Center, featuring accompanying performances by multilingual singing groups throughout the month. Free. Saturday, December 9, and Sunday, December 10, 6:00pm. Josie Robertson Plaza, 10 Lincoln Center Plaza.
