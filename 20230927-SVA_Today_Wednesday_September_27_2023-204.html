9:00am | The Curatorial Roundtable: Kit Hammonds (Mexico City)

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with K﻿it Hammonds, chief curator at Museo Jumex, one of Mexico’s major contemporary art collections and exhibition spaces. Today, September 27, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.

6:00pm | SVA Graduate Admissions Preview Night

SVA invites all prospective graduate students to meet Graduate Admissions staff and learn about the graduate programs and application process. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer 2024 or fall 2024 admission. Tonight, September 27, 6:00 – 8:00pm, online.

6:30pm | Jump-Start a Career in Film and TV Production

Center for Communication presents a panel of producers from Made in NY productions—including those from BET, Netflix and Paramount—who will discuss the skill set that production assistants need to make a great impression in their first job. Free and open to the public with RSVP; register here. Tonight, September 27, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
