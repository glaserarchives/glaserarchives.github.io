SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts May 16 - 22, 2016
FEATURES
Must See: Video Goes Viral
BFA Design student Kurt Rauffer talks about his widely shared James Bond-inspired title sequence for The Empire Strikes Back, which has 800,000 views and counting on Vimeo.
Documentary Follows Blind Soccer Players in China
MFA Social Documentary Film student and 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winner Anqi Cai talks about her thesis film, Blind Soccer, which follows a group of blind soccer players in China as they train for a national tournament.
Goodbye Earth, Hello Othen
BFA Photography and Video student and 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winner Tayler Smith talks about her Instagram project Othen, a fictional planet that offers a safe space for humans of all identifications.
A Guide (and App) to the Latest Social Innovations
Here’s a closer look at four thesis projects from MFA Design for Social Innovation's class of 2016, as well as a new app that features the graduating students' work.
BioArt Project Examines Religion and Identity
BFA Fine Arts student and 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winner Andrew Joseph Cziraki discusses his Bornless One project, which analyzes holy water from churches in New York City in an attempt to find connections between science, religion and the identities of local communities.
IN THE PRESS
Photo by Inez and Vinoodh
The Most Brilliant Couple in Town
Sarah Sze (MFA 1997 Fine Arts) and her husband, the oncologist and author Siddhartha Mukherjee, are profiled in the May issue of Vogue.
Going Minimal
Business Insider asked MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller and MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman their opinions on the new Instagram logo.
The True Meaning of Seeing
BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects student Karis Oh's thesis project, The Moment, was recently named a Vimeo "Staff Pick" and featured at Short of the Week.
Opposites Attract
Print features posters by SVA students from the Corporate Identity and Multimedia honors course taught by faculty members Ivan Chermayeff, Tom Geismar and Sagi Haviv.
EVENTS
Class of 2015 commencement
Watch SVA's 2016 Commencement Live!
This year's exercises, to be held at Radio City Music Hall, begin at 10:00am, Wednesday, May 18. Famed photographer Carrie Mae Weems will give the address. The event will be streamed live at sva.edu/commencement.
MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Thesis Exhibition
Reception Monday, May 16, 6:00pm. Exhibition runs through Thursday, May 19, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
MFA IxD Festival
Graduating MFA Interaction Design students will present work that connects ideas to the human experience, with prototypes available for play and exploration. The event will stream live here. Tuesday, May 17, 10:00am - 3:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
SVA Short Film Festival
MPS Directing presents a screening of thesis projects by graduating students. Thursday, May 19, 6:00 - 9:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
