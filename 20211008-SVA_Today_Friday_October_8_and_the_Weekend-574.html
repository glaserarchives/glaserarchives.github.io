3:30pm | FAB FRIDAY Talk with Posture Media
FAB FRIDAY by BFA Advertising and BFA Design is a weekly guest-lecture series set to inspire and educate with great design. This week's guests are the co-founders of Posture Media, Winter Mendelson and SVA alumnus Asher Torres. Join on Zoom today at 3:30pm. (Note: The Zoom link has been updated and is different than the one that was included in Wednesday's SVA Today.)

In their presentation, "Posture Media in the Making: Surviving an Industry Pivot from Editorial to Marketing," they will share their personal career journey starting with launching an indie magazine that ultimately transitioned into a creative agency. They will also share their experience as nonbinary and transgender creatives in the advertising and marketing industries, and where they think the future of the industry is headed. Email bfaadvdes@sva.edu with any questions.
SVA IN THE PRESS

Alumni Films on Netflix
Two documentaries made by SVA alumni will debut on Netflix this fall: Convergence: Courage in a Crisis, co-directed by Sara Khaki (MFA Social Documentary Film) tells the story of nine people around the world who responded to challenges brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic (via Realscreen); and Procession, produced by Bennett Elliott (BFA 2010 Film and Video) and shot by Rob Kolodny (BFA 2010 Film and Video), centers on six men who survived childhood sexual assault at the hands of Catholic clergy (via The Hollywood Reporter).
