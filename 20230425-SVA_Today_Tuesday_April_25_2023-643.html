“Like an Exploded iPhone”

The Guardian writes about how “Time Lapse,” an exhibition of work by Sarah Sze (MFA 1997 Fine Arts) at the Guggenheim, translates the digital age’s overload of images into kaleidoscope installations.

EVENTS

5:00pm | VASA Finals Fair: Play-Doh Night!

Join VASA for Play-Doh night—make fun creations out of Play-Doh while enjoying nostalgic candy and a movie screening. Tonight, April 25, 5:00 – 7:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Polite Society Screening

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a screening of Polite Society, the feature directorial debut of director Nida Manzoor, starring Priya Kansara and Ritu Arya. Polite Society follows martial artist-in-training Ria Khan, who believes she must save her older sister from her impending marriage, pulling off the most ambitious of all wedding heists in the name of independence and sisterhood. Watch the trailer here. Registration is required and attendees must present their SVA ID at the door (non-SVA guests are permitted if accompanied by an attendee with an SVA ID); RSVP here; seating is limited. Tonight, April 25, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Global Design Graduate Show

Submissions are open for the world’s largest online art and design undergrad/postgrad initiative, The Global Design Graduate Show. Share your creative work alongside your peers from around the world and get it judged by industry leaders. The Global Design Graduate Show is open to all undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate art and design disciplines for those that have graduated between September 2022 – August 2023. Free to apply. The application deadline is Thursday, August 31. Learn more and apply here.
