SVA Today
Monday, April 25, 2016
Photo from last year's All-Staff Art Show reception
Submit Now to the All-Staff Art Show!
SVA staff are invited to participate in the fourth annual All-Staff Art Show. Applications can be found at sva.edu/asas2016 and require an image of the work, its description and dimensions. No work should be larger than 24 x 24". The deadline is Wednesday, June 1.
Opening: "rheo-"
An exhibition that investigates identity and language in the Internet age. Participating artists include Charles Atlas, Andrew Birk, Cindy Sherman, Manuel Solano and Ryan Trecartin. Curated by MA Curatorial Practice fellow Kayla Fanelli. By appointment only. Contact macp@sva.edu to schedule a viewing. Charles Atlas film screening tonight at 7:00pm. Through Friday, April 29, CP Projects Space 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.
6:00pm: BFA Fine Arts Open Studios Reception
Last chance to view work by BFA Fine Arts students in theirstudios. Reception tonight, 6:00 - 9:00pm, 335 West 16 Street, lower level - 5th floor.

Photo by Eric Ruane
Art Imitating Life
James Madison University's blog interviews graphic design professor Sophia Glock (MFA 2008 Illustration as Visual Essay) about her process as a comics artist.
NYC PICK OF THE DAY
Crime Stories: Photography and Foul Play
An exhibition of crime-related photography, including forensic evidence, mugshots and more. Suggested student admission is $12, $25 general public. Through Sunday, July 31, Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Avenue.
TAKE NOTE
Relaxation Techniques
Student Health and Counseling Services presents Christine Gilchrist and Stella Kim sharing relaxation techniques for relieving stress, such as breathing exercises and essential oil use. Open to SVA students. Tuesday, April 26, 3:00 - 4:30pm, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.
MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Thesis Exhibition
An exhibition of thesis projects by graduating students. Curated by faculty member David Sandlin. Reception Monday, May 16, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Saturday, April 30, through Thursday, May 19, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
