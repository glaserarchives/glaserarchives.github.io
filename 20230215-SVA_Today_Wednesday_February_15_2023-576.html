SVA’s New Subway Posters Honor Marshall Arisman

The subway posters, created by the late artist and MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Chair Marshall Arisman and his widow, writer Dee Ito, feature previously unseen drawings of monkeys, one of Arisman’s favorite subjects.
