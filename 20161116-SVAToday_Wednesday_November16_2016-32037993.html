Free Risograph Color Mixing Workshop

This free workshop, led by RisoLAB artist-in-residence and cartoonist Bjorn Miner, will teach the basics of "analog" color-mixing and introduce attendees to printing with the Risograph, a digital silk-screen machine. No experience necessary. Registration required; sign up here. Friday, November 18, 6:30 - 9:30pm, RisoLAB, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.
