SVA Now
June 18 – 24, 2018
News and events from the School of Visual Arts

Ride the Pride Train

SVA community members and creators of the guerrilla Pride Train campaign talk about their intentions and the reactions to their bold efforts.

Baseball's Ugliest Uniforms

A Q&A with graphic designer, professional sports branding expert and writer Todd Radom (BFA 1986 Media Arts) about Winning Ugly, his recent book and affectionate tribute to Major League Baseball's ugliest uniforms.
IN THE PRESS

On Lorna Simpson Collages

The Atlantic on how Lorna Simpson (BFA 1982 Photography) "maps the complex galaxies of black women's hair" in her new collage series, made from magazine clippings, watercolor and images of geological formations.

A Chat with Milton Glaser

Ad Age interviews SVA's Acting Chairman of the Board Milton Glaser about New York City, design and advertising.

Jeans and Leather

Vogue Italia shares "Jeans and Leather," a fashion video directed by Collier Schorr (BFA 1985 Communication Arts).
