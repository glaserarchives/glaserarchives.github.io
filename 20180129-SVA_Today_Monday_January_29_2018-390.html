EVENT

11:00am | SVA Library West Opening

SVA Library staff welcomes the College community to the soft opening of its newly renovated SVA Library West branch. Eat cookies, tour the space and learn about library services, including the new video game room. Today, Wednesday, January 31, and Friday, February 2, 11:00am – 4:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

6:00pm | Out of Overwhelm: Mindfulness for Creative People

MFA Fine Arts and Student Health and Counseling Services present a workshop that will teach you mindfulness practices that can help you let go of thoughts that can get in the way of your artistic practice. Space is limited and RSVPs are required. 6:00 – 7:30pm. MPS Art Therapy Gallery Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.
PRESS PICK

New York's Public Art Show

Artnet News on New York's public exhibition featuring covers designed by 50 artists, including one by MFA Fine Arts faculty member Marilyn Minter, in honor of the magazine's 50th anniversary.
NYC PICK

An Evening of Maria Lassnig Films

The world premiere of a series of experimental films artist Maria Lassnig made in New York City in the 1970s. Tickets free with SVA ID, but must be picked up in person. 7:00pm. The Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53rd Street.

Amy Sillman: Mostly Drawing

An exhibition of new work by artist Amy Sillman (BFA 1979 Fine Arts). Through Saturday, March 3. Gladstone Gallery, 130 East 64th Street.
TAKE NOTE

Dog Therapy

Student Health and Counseling Services presents dog therapy with New York Therapy Animals. Tomorrow, January 30, 3:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Library, 380 Second Avenue, 2nd floor.

Information Session: Applying for Leadership Positions on Campus

Interested in becoming a resident assistant, orientation leader or a VASA leader? Join Student Leadership Series for an information session to learn how to apply for these positions. Thursday, January 31, 6:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Information Session: SVA in Taos, New Mexico

Join SVA Destinations to learn about the course Writing in the Land of Enchantment in Taos, New Mexico, which will take place from June 2 – 18. Thursday, February 1, 6:30 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Self-Care for Creatives

MPS Art Therapy presents this workshop, where you will learn to prevent creative burnout and discover new ways of incorporating creativity. RSVP to Val Sereno at vsereno@sva.edu. Friday, February 2, 2:00 – 3:00pm. 132 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
