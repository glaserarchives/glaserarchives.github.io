SVA Today
Thursday, May 12, 2016
A Social Innovation App
MFA Design for Social Innovation’s 2016 graduating class recently presented their thesis projects at the SVA Theatre. Here’s a closer look at four, with descriptions by the designers themselves, as well as a new app that features the work of all of this year's graduating DSI students.
From Homage to Viral Video
BFA Design student Kurt Rauffer's James Bond-inspired title sequence for The Empire Strikes Back has over 700,000 views on Vimeo and was featured in Entertainment Weekly, The Hollywood Reporter, Popular Mechanics, and more. We spoke to Rauffer about the attention his work has received this week.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
The True Meaning of Seeing
BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects student Karis Oh's thesis project, The Moment, was recently named a Vimeo "Staff Pick" and featured at Short of the Week.
NYC PICKS OF THE DAY
Noah Becker: "Fun Drugs"
An exhibition of paintings and text that reference science fiction, pop culture, cartoons, children's programming and horror films. Reception tonight, 7:00pm. Through Wednesday, June 1, Amy Li Projects, 166 Mott Street.
DA Video Club Screening
DA Video Club, the directing duo of BFA Photography and Video students Desiree Kong and Agusta Yr, hosts a screening of their work. A party with DJs will follow. Attendees must be 21 or older; admission is $2. 8:00pm, Hopes & Fears, 200 Morgan Avenue, 2nd floor, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE
Facade Work Continues at 205, 209 East 23rd Street
Select facade demolition and removal are currently taking place on the rear, east side and west side walls and will continue for approximately one more week, weather permitting. Select facade demolition and removal of the front wall will begin next week and continue for approximately two weeks, weather permitting. During this period there will be loud construction noise Monday through Friday, 7:30am to 3:30pm, with occasional days until 5:30pm, and 9:00am to 5:00pm on Saturdays. In the front of 205 there is a container for debris removal that will be present throughout the project's duration. Periodic updates will be provided in SVA Today in the coming weeks. For questions, contact Richard Riccio, executive director of Facilities, 212.592.2156.
Movie Ticket Sales Suspended
Due to the Student Engagement and Leadership Office's impending summer move to SVA's new residence hall, the final day for sales of discounted Regal Cinemas tickets is Monday, May 16, until further notice. Tickets can be purchased Friday, May 13, and Monday, May 16, 9:00am - 5:00pm, at the Student Engagement and Leadership Office, George Washington Residence, 23 Lexington Avenue, mezzanine. Contact activities@sva.edu for more information.
The Project Class: "Adaptations"
BFA Design presents a screening of "Adaptations," a series of 12 short films made by students in the Project Class, taught by MPS Directing Chair Bob Giraldi, faculty member Jason Cicci and Patti Greaney. Monday, May 16, 6:00 - 9:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
