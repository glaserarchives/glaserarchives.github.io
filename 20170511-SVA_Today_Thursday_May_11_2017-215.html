SVA Today

Commencement 2017

A rundown of the 2017 Commencement exercises, where 1,700 students gathered at Radio City Music Hall to receive their diplomas and hear from keynote speaker Gloria Steinam.
EVENTS

6:00pm | "Cognitive Dissidence" Reception

An exhibition of work by artists in the MFA Fine Arts class of 2017, curated by Dan Cameron. 6:00 – 9:00pm. Ray Smith Studio, 261 Bond Street, ground floor, Brooklyn.

6:30pm | CE Fine Arts Information Session

SVA Continuing Education presents an evening with fine arts faculty, where you can see their work and their students' work and let them answer your questions on how to begin working in the fine arts. 6:30 – 8:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 602C.

6:30pm | CE Visual Narrative Information Session

An evening with Continuing Education Visual Narrative faculty, where you will see their work and their students' work and ask questions about the classes. 6:30 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.
PRESS PICK

The Heart of Steven Universe

Slate praises Rebecca Sugar's (BFA 2009 Animation) animated show Steven Universe and discusses why it is so appealing to such a variety of demographics.
NYC PICK

Empire of Smoke: The Legacy of Tobacco

A special event held in conjunction with the exhibition "Beatrice Glow: Spice Roots/Routes," with an introduction by the curators, a performance and lecture by Beatrice Glow and a lecture by historian Gunja SenGupta. Free; RSVP here. 6:30pm. New York University Institute of Fine Arts, 1 East 78th Street.
TAKE NOTE

Alien: Covenant Screening

The Visual Effects Society presents a screening of Alien: Covenant, featuring Michael Fassbender and Katherine Waterston and directed by Ridley Scott. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Saturday, May 20, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
