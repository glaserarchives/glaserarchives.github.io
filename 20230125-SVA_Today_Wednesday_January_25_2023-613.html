ANNOUNCEMENTS

COVID-19 Updates

Masks are still mandatory in all SVA buildings because seven-day community case (transmission) rates are currently not at or below the levels of 100 per 100,000 people in New York City, or 1,200 per day; and seven per 5,500 people at SVA, or one per day. Read more about how transmission rates are calculated.

  • •The current seven-day NYC case (transmission) rate can be found here.
  • •The current SVA seven-day community case (transmission) rate is 12.

If you test positive, upload the results of any COVID-19 test to Cleared4 via the link in your daily symptom checker. Look for an email from studenthealthalert@sva.edu or employee-health-alert@sva.edu. Begin quarantining for at least five days.

Snow Days ❄️

When severe weather is predicted, SVA will make a determination to close on a case-by-case basis and communicate updates via SVAlert and on sva.edu. On SVA snow days, there will be no remote or in-person undergraduate, graduate and continuing education classes, the libraries will be closed and only essential personnel who can safely travel to the College will be required to come to campus.
