Trolleys to Mexico

Performance in the Park: "Pink Trash"

Artist Maren Hassinger restages her 1982 performance Pink Trash, which explores ideas of public space and ethical citizenship by drawing attention to the ways New Yorkers engage with their local environment. Preceding the performance is a tour of "We Wanted a Revolution: Black Radical Women, 1965 – 1985." Receive a discount on tickets by using the code BKMCORP17. Sunday, July 23, 1:00pm. Brooklyn Museum, 200 Eastern Parkway, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

New Digital Magazine Subscription: Flipster

SVA Library has subscribed to Flipster, a digital magazine solution for reading complete digital surrogates of magazines online and via a mobile app. It's available to SVA students, faculty and staff. The library subscribes to 35 different magazines through Flipster, including Art in America, Computer Arts, Juxtapoz, The New Yorker, Wired and more.

Routine Systems Maintenance this Weekend

SVA Administrative and Network Services performs routine systems maintenance on the third weekend of each month, to help ensure that critical software and hardware platforms serving the community continue to operate reliably. Systemsthat routinely undergo maintenance include MySVA, MyServices, Web Advisor, Web Budget, Colleague, SVA Wireless Networks, SVA Wired Networks and the Cisco Phone System. Maintenance is generally performed late Saturday evening into early Sunday morning; service on these systems may be interrupted during these hours. Any questions can be directed to the SVA Helpdesk at 212.592.2400 or helpdesk@sva.edu.
