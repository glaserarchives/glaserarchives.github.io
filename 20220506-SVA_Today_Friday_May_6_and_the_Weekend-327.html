John Waters to Headline 2020 and 2021 Make-up Commencement
Filmmaker, artist and author John Waters has been announced as the keynote speaker for the in-person ceremony celebrating the SVA classes of 2020 and 2021, to be held at Radio City Music Hall on Monday, June 27. Waters previously served as keynote speaker for SVA’s virtual commencement exercises in 2020; read more about that speech here.

SVA IN THE PRESS
Artour Toulinov

Remembering Marcus Leatherdale
The New York Times wrote about the life and work of downtown Manhattan portraitist Marcus Leatherdale (1978 Media Arts), whose subjects included Andy Warhol, Madonna and Keith Haring (1979 Fine Arts), and who passed away last month at 69.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Last Chance | Distinguished Staff Award Nominations
Each year at the All-Staff Meeting, SVA recognizes outstanding staff contributions to the College with the presentation of two Distinguished Staff Awards. Each award includes a cash prize of $5,000. It’s up to you to nominate one of your administrative-staff colleagues to receive this award. Award information, a list of eligible employees and the nomination form are available at staffawards.sva.edu. Nominations are due today, 5:00pm ET.

Next Week | MA/MAT Art Education Thesis Presentations
The MA/MAT Art Education class of 2022 presents their thesis projects addressing research questions through academic inquiry and engagement with art-making. Tuesday, May 10, 11:00am, online.

Next Week | Montana Story Screening
SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a special advance screening of Montana Story followed by a Q&A with directors Scott McGehee and David Siegel. Admission is limited to the SVA community and their guests. Read the SVA Theatre COVID protocols here. Tuesday, May 10, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
