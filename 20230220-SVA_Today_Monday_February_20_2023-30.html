SVA Masking Policy Update

Starting today, February 20, masks are encouraged but not required in SVA buildings. Daily health attestations via the Cleared4 app will still be required, so that the College can continue to effectively track COVID-19 case numbers within the community.


While wearing a mask on campus is no longer mandated, all SVA community members will be expected to respect anyone’s choice to continue doing so.

#SVAwesome

Adebunmi Gbadebo (BFA 2017 Fine Arts), In Memory of Pirecie McCory, Born 1843, Died June 10 1933, In Thy Place to Stand, HFS, 2021, True Blue Cemetery soil, South Carolina Gold rice. Gbadebo made the work using soil from the land that was once cleared by her ancestors. She describes this practice as “a way to commemorate what they endured and as a way to activate the land, using it and shaping it. The making of the work has been a practice of healing and a practice of care for their memories and what remains of their physical bodies, it’s in the soil.” Gbadebo’s exhibition “Remains” is on view at the Claire Oliver Gallery, 2288 Adam Clayton Powell Jr. Boulevard, through Saturday, March 11. Via Claire Oliver Gallery.
