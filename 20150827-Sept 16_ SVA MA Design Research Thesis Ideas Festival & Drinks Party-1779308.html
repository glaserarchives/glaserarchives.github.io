Dear Beth
 
Please save the date of Wednesday, September 16 for a Thesis Ideas Festival and Drinks Party in honor of the SVA MA Design Research Class of 2016. We will kick off the evening at 4:00 p.m. by hearing from incoming students, fresh off a three-day thesis workshop, who will introduce themselves via brief overviews of their thesis topics. Afterwards, we will continue the conversation with department faculty and alumni over cocktails and refreshments.
 
4:00–6:30 p.m. Students each give 5-minute presentations with images of their thesis ideas. Faculty and invited guests respond. 

6:30–8:30 p.m. Drinks party in main space.


We really hope you can make it for some, or all, of the evening. It’s always a pleasure to have you with us!

Best wishes,

Molly Heintz and Alice Twemlow
