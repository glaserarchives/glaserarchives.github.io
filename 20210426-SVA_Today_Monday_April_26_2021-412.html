Student Leadership at SVA
Every year, the Office of Student Affairs, in collaboration with Student Engagement and Leadership and Residence Life, organize an initiative to recognize the outstanding work of SVA student leaders. This year, student leadership has been highlighted through a series of blogs, all of which can be viewed here. Each blog highlights a different topic of student leadership at SVA and will be featured throughout the week in SVA Today. Up first:

Orientation Leader Stars
This blog highlights the work of the students who served as Orientation Leaders, and who work diligently each semester to welcome our newest SVA students as they join our awesome community. Click here to view the Orientation Leader Stars blog.

Final Steven Universe Anti-Racism PSA
Cartoon Network has launched its final anti-racism PSA, focused on how to be an ally and developed by Steven Universe creator Rebecca Sugar (BFA 2009 Animation) and OK K.O.! Let’s Be Heroes creator Ian Jones-Quartey (BFA 2006 Animation). Via Animation Magazine.

KUSAMA: Cosmic Nature
Come see outdoor installations of contemporary Japanese artist Yayoi Kusama’s multifaceted art, including monumental and immersive floral sculptures. Through October 2021. Purchase timed tickets here. New York Botanical Garden, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.
