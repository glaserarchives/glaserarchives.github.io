🎙️ Get To Know: Emily Goodman, Registered Nurse—Student Health & Counseling Services

Congratulations on your new position as registered nurse in Student Health & Counseling Services! How did you get your start in the field?

Thank you! I have been a nurse for almost 30 years but began working as a school nurse about five years ago. Before that, I worked in hospitals, provided disease management services and assumed various administrative positions in healthcare. I loved the positive and growth-oriented atmosphere of working in a school and found supporting student health to be vitally important in education.


What are some of the ways you support SVA students in your role?

In my role, I am available to consult with students about their medical concerns and identify appropriate care options and providers. I also have many projects on the front burner, including an on-campus flu vaccine clinic in the fall; Naloxone dispenser certification for the SVA community; educational events and Instagram Live sessions on topics such as improving sleep habits, back pain and sexual health; and a free live bi-weekly yoga class for SVA community members.


Anything else you would like to add?

I identify as a creative thinker and artist. I have a BFA in dance from an art school. I have vivid memories of what it felt like to be both passionate and driven in terms of work but also isolated and scared. I wish to meet students where they are.

Purna Venugopalan

🤯 Free Courses and Tutorials

LinkedIn Learning’s online classes and tutorials provide opportunities to brush up on your old skills and explore entirely new ones.


Mindful Working: 11 Ways to Improve How You Work

The practices in this course are designed to support and deepen your ability to navigate the workday. Here, you can reconnect with your natural resilience, intelligence and kindness.


Finding Your Idea Hook

A great hook answers, “What is this a story about?” But knowing how to create a solid hook can be challenging. In this course, join instructor Stefan Mumaw as he explains exactly what a hook is, what types there are, the characteristics of good hooks and strategies for developing effective ones.


Agile Rebranding

In this course, Wes Kao teaches startup-inspired frameworks and strategies for companies of all sizes to do rebranding in easy, repeatable and iterative steps. Discover how to prioritize moving fast, identify your rebranding goals more precisely, and translate your vision into public-facing copy and visuals.
