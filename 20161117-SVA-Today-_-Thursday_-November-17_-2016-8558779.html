An SVA Graduate's Dream of a Transnational Trolly Comes True

NationSwell on Peter Svarzbein (MFA 2011 Photography, Video and Related Media), who is overseeing construction of a trolly between his hometown of El Paso, Texas, and Juarez, Mexico—a project he first conceived as a conceptual artwork.
NYC PICKS

From Holograms to HoloLens

An interactive tour of Holographic Studios, the world’s oldest gallery of holography, with Dr. Laser (a.k.a. holography pioneer Jason Sapan). $20 early-bird, $35 general admission. 6:00 – 8:00pm, Holographic Studios, 240 East 26th Street.

Steve McCurry Book Signing

The award-winning photographer Steve McCurry signs copies of his books, including his latest, On Reading. 7:00 – 8:30pm. The Strand, 828 Broadway, 2nd floor.

Post-Election Support

A supportive space to discuss your responses to the election. There will be several "micro-talks" by mental health practitioners and allied professionals followed by a Q&A and larger breakout discussion. 8:00 - 11:00pm, Judson Memorial Church, 239 Thompson Street.

Union Square Holiday Market

Gifts and artwork for all ages, as well as a variety of food and drink. Through December 24, hours vary. Union Square, East 14th Street at Union Square West.
TAKE NOTE

Veterans Coalition of the Arts Panel Discussion

VASA presents a panel discussion of faculty member Camillo Mac Bica’s book Worthy of Gratitude: Why Veterans May Not Want to Be Thanked for their Service in War, sponsored by the Veterans Coalition of the Arts. The panel will feature SVA students and faculty, including Bica himself. Tomorrow, November 18, 2:00 – 3:00pm, 133 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

SVA Destinations: Writing in Taos, New Mexico, Information Session

SVA Destinations presents an information session about its two-week program, Writing in the Land of Enchantment in Taos, NM. For more information about SVA Destinations, visit destinations.sva.edu. Tomorrow, November 18, 4:00 – 5:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 409F.

SVA at PULSE Miami Beach

SVA Galleries is showing the work of nine recent graduates from five departments at this year's PULSE Miami Beach art fair, and a limited number of PULSE day passes are available. To receive a pass, which admits two to the fair, contact extrelations@sva.edu no later than Tuesday, November 22. If you have work appearing in any of the Miami fairs and would like it promoted by SVA's social media accounts, send the information to extrelations@sva.edu.
