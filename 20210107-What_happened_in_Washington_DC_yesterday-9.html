To the SVA Community,


Yesterday we all witnessed what happens when protesters incited by their titular leader become a mob and in so doing, as the Mayor of Washington DC has correctly pointed out, engage in acts of domestic terrorism. It is clear from the planning that occurred on social media prior to yesterday that some were intent on disrupting by force the proceedings then occurring in the Capitol building. That attempt failed and failed in full view of the nation and the world.

 

Although this was a shameful exhibition by those who participated, it seems likely that if we are all vigilant and ensure that law enforcement does its job this time, those who perpetrated these crimes, all of which were recorded, are brought to justice through the courts. This would be a fitting end to the current administration and its aiders and abettors.

 

But I would be remiss if I did not bring to your attention the disparity in the police response to those who occupied the Capitol building and were allowed to leave without consequence, so far, and those peaceful demonstrators who were violently removed from Lafayette Park and other Black Lives Matter protestors last summer.

 

In a few days I know we all hope that the country will return to what it has traditionally been: a polity based upon norms, facts, and the rule of law.

 

Please stay safe and well during these difficult and trying times.


David Rhodes
