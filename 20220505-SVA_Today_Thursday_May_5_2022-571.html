ANNOUNCEMENT

Next Week | The Monster Inside Me Screening
The Monster Inside Me is a new documentary that explores the impacts of chronic illness on relationships. The director follows his wife and other patients as they receive a Lyme disease diagnosis and experience psychological symptoms that harm their relationships with significant others, family and friends. Free for SVA students, faculty and staff; SVA ID required. Monday, May 9, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
