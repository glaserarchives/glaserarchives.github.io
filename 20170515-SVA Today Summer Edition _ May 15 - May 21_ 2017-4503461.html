SVA partnered with the Madison Avenue BID for a public art exhibition titled "A Moment in Time." Under the mentorship and supervision of 3D Design chair Kevin O'Callaghan, students created monumental six-foot tall watch-themed sculptures commemorating a specific moment.

Routine Systems Maintenance This Weekend

SVA Administrative and Network Services performs routine systems maintenance on the third weekend of each month, to help ensure that critical software and hardware platforms serving the community continue to operate reliably. Systems that routinely undergo maintenance include MySVA, MyServices, Web Advisor, Web Budget, Colleague, SVA Wireless Networks, SVA Wired Networks and the Cisco Phone System. Maintenance is generally performed late Saturday evening into early Sunday morning; service on these systems may be interrupted during these hours. Any questions can be directed to the SVA Helpdesk at 212.592.2400 or helpdesk@sva.edu.

Madison Square Park Conservancy Annual Symposium on Public Art

Distinguished leaders in the museum and contemporary art fields, prominent artists and thinkers will gather for dynamic conversation and presentations. Panelists include artists Joshua Bennett, Josiah McElheny, Simone Leigh, Iván Navarro and Erwin Redl. Free with registration; email symposium@madisonsquarepark.org. Friday, June 2, 9:00am – noon. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
