From the latest SVA Style, here is a list of offbeat New York City stores that only stock a single item or category of merchandise.
EVENTS

Noon | Lunar New Year Gathering

Celebrate the Lunar New Year with SVA's International Student Office and the Chinese Student Club. Enjoy Korean, Filipino and Japanese food and Chinese tea and decorate your own paper lantern. Noon – 2:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Curator Risa Puleo

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by Risa Puleo, the curator of "Monarchs: Brown and Native Contemporary Artists in the Path of the Butterfly," on view at Bemis Center for Contemporary Arts in Omaha, Nebraska. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

6:30pm | Shannon Mattern, "Ether/Ore: An Atlas of Urban Media"

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents a talk by Shannon Mattern, associate professor of media studies at The New School, who will discuss topics in her new book Code and Clay, Data and Dirt: 5000 Years of Urban Media. RSVP here. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

7:00pm | Artist Talk: Liu Shiyuan

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents Beijing-based artist and program alumnus Liu Shiyuan, who will discuss her work in anticipation of her solo show "Isolated Above, Connected Down" at Tanya Bonakdar Gallery, New York. RSVP here. 7:00 – 8:30pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

7:00pm | Jamie Herman

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by digital retoucher Jamie Herman, as part of its i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

NYC PICK

Correspondence

An exhibition of portraits by Sophie Dawson (BFA 2010 Fine Arts) of people who were imprisoned for crimes related to their activism in the Black Panther and Black Liberation movements in the 1960s and '70s. Through Saturday, February 24. Okay Space, 281 North Seventh Street, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

Movie Night: Hidden Figures

SVA International Student Office's Cultural Partner Program presents a screening of Hidden Figures. RSVP here. Friday, February 23, 8:30pm. 24th Street Residence, 340 East 24th Street, media room.
