Good Morning,

 

I write to inform you that Joo Chung, a faculty member in the BFA Illustration Department, will no longer be teaching at SVA.  Professor Chung was a long-standing member of SVA’s community, having worked with hundreds of students who valued his skills and expertise.  However, last week, numerous complaints of alarming and never-before reported misconduct were raised, making his continued tenure at SVA untenable.  Effective immediately, Professor Chung has been removed from SVA.  All classes he is currently teaching will be reassigned to other SVA faculty as will the classes he is scheduled to teach in the Fall 2020 semester.  I encourage any student with concerns regarding Professor Chung to reach-out to SVA’s Title IX Office or to their academic advisor regarding their class schedule.

 

David Rhodes
