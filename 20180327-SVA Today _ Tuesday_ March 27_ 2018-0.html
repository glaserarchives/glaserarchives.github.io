SVA Today
Tuesday, March 27, 2018

"Candid Yet Thoughtful, Frank Yet Deliberate"

Multidisciplinary, feminist-focused artist AnnaLiisa Ariosa-Benston (MFA 2016 Fine Arts) discusses her methods and intentions in her art practice.
EVENTS

5:00pm | BFA Advertising and BFA Design Spring Exhibition Reception

BFA Advertising and BFA Design present an exhibition of ads, campaigns, designs, motion graphics and 3D work by the programs' third- and fourth-year students. 5:00 – 8:00pm. Through Tuesday, March 27. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Legacy Russell, "Glitch Feminism"

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by Legacy Russell, the founding theorist behind Glitch Feminism, a cultural manifesto and movement that aims to use the digital as a means of resisting the hegemony of the corporeal. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

7:00pm | Victor Sira

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by Venezuela-born artist and photographer Victor Sira. 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

8:00pm | Outside In with Lynn Shelton

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue invites you to join director Lynn Shelton (MFA 1995 Photography and Related Media) for a screening of her latest film, Outside In, starring Edie Falco and Jay Duplass. A Q&A with Shelton will follow. Free with SVA ID (one guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). 6:15pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

A Call for Digital Protection

MFA Interaction Design faculty member Paul Ford on how it is time for a digital protection agency, for Bloomberg Businessweek.
NYC PICK

David Guttenfelder, A Rare Look: North Korea to Cuba

A talk by National Geographic photographer David Guttenfelder, whose talent for crossing long-closed borders has led to some of the publication's most revealing geopolitical photo essays. Purchase discounted tickets here using the code "EXPLORE." 8:00pm. The Town Hall, 123 West 43rd Street.

Email Christine Gilchrist at cgilchrist@sva.edu with any questions. Thursday, March 29, noon – 2:00pm. MPS Art Therapy Gallery Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

Discounted Tickets: Brooklyn Nets and School of Rock on Broadway

Student Engagement and Leadership is selling $20 tickets to Brooklyn Nets vs. Detroit Pistons, on Sunday, April 1, 6:00pm (limit two tickets, open to SVA students, faculty and staff), and School of Rock on Broadway, Sunday, April 8, 2:00pm (limit one ticket, SVA students only). SVA ID required. Tickets on sale weekdays, 2:00 – 4:00pm, at the 24th Street Residence, 340 East 24th Street, ground floor.
