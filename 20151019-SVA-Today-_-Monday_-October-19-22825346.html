SVA Today | Your guide to everything
Monday, October 19, 2015
SVA Library Launches New Website
The new SVA Library website features a completely updated design with illustrations by Donica Ida (MFA 2014 Design). Information about library collections, services and spaces is easily accessible via the main header links and the four animated buttons beneath the search box. For questions and comments, contact library@sva.edu.
6:30pm: Mohamed Gohar Screens The Night of Counting Years
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents the Egyptian broadcaster and producer screening the classic 1969 Egyptian movie and discussing journalistic and media responsibility and the use of art in promoting social stability. With artist and SVA faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts). 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
Press pick of the day
Tim Griffin to Receive French Honor
Artforum reports that the MA Curatorial Practice faculty member and executive director and chief curator of The Kitchen will be awarded the insignia of chevalier of the Order of Arts and Letters by the French government. The award recognizes eminent artists and writers, as well as people who have contributed significantly to furthering the arts in France and throughout the world.
NYC pick of the day
Jacob A. Riis: Revealing New York's Other Half
The first major retrospective of the pioneering photographer and social reformer's work since 1947. Admission is $10 for students and seniors; $14 general admission; free for ages 20 and under. Through March 2016. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.
Take Note
Discounted AMC Movie Ticket Sales Suspended on Two Upcoming Fridays
Discounted AMC movie ticket sales will be suspended on Friday, October 23, and Friday, October 30. As a reminder, tickets can be purchased at the Student Engagement and Leadership Office (formerly Student Activities) on the mezzanine level of the George Washington Residence, 23 Lexington Avenue, on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 2:00 - 5:00pm.
ICG Emerging Cinematographer Awards
The International Cinematographers Guild presents its annual ceremony, which recognizes and screens the films of those who received the year's Artistic Achievement in Cinematography Awards. Admission is free for SVA students and faculty. Use code "ECA15" to register. Sunday, October 25, 3:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
