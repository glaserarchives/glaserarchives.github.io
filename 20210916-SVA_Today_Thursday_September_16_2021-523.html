Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 9:30 – 11:00am, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour this Thursday, September 16, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

12:00pm | SVA Club Fair
Clubs are a fantastic way to make friends, discover new interests and get involved on campus. Explore some of SVA's 23 clubs at the club fair, today, 12:00 – 2:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Clubs that will be present today: Asian American Student Union, Cartoon Allies, Concept and Vis-dev Club, Criterion Club, Figure Drawing Club, Horror Society, LGBTQ+ SVA, Mobius Lab, SVA Arthouse, the SVA Paddock and Women in Animation.

Additional clubs will be at the Student Center on Friday, September 17, 3:00 – 5:00pm. Questions? Email clubs@sva.edu.

6:00pm | 11th Annual SocDoc Thesis Showcase
The MFA Social Documentary Film class of 2021 will share their work-in-progress thesis films, which inspire and amaze—especially as they completed their films through the global pandemic. Screenings will occur online with select showings in-person on Thursday, September 16, and Friday, September 17, at SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. View the full schedule here and RSVP here to receive streaming links and to attend any in-person screenings or online Q&As.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Moe's Cafes at SVA Are Open for In-Person Dining!
Hungry between classes? Dine in-person or on-the-go at the two Moe's Cafe locations on SVA campus, where you can get breakfast, lunch and dinner during the week! Moe has bagels, coffee, sandwiches, burgers, fries, vegetarian options, and more at student-friendly prices.

East side: 209 East 23rd Street, 2nd floor
West side:133/141 West 21st Street, lower level
Monday – Thursday, 8:00am – 7:00pm; Friday, 8:00am – 4:00pm

Tomorrow | Virtual Support Space for International Students: Readjusting to Campus Life and Managing Pandemic Stress
Join the first Virtual Support Space for International Students of the academic year hosted by Cong (Holly) Fu from Student Health and Counseling Services and William Park from International Student Office tomorrow, September 17, 2:00 – 3:00pm. For this month's session, Amy Zhu, a psychiatric nurse practitioner from Graymatters, will join to discuss how to readjust to campus life and manage pandemic stress. RSVP here.

Tuesday | Mid-Autumn Festival Gathering 2021
Join this year's in-person Mid-Autumn Festival gathering! There will be a screening of Netflix's Over the Moon and a paper lantern-making activity. A mooncake recipe will be distributed and there will be an opportunity to win a hand-pressured mooncake mould! Tuesday, September 21, 4:30 – 6:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level. Capacity is limited, so RSVP here to save your spot.

Organized by 24th Street Residence fall resident assistants, the International Student Office and Student Health and Counseling Services.
