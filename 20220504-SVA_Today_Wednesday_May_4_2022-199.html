ANNOUNCEMENTS

Distinguished Staff Award Nominations
Each year at the All-Staff Meeting, SVA recognizes outstanding staff contributions to the College with the presentation of two Distinguished Staff Awards. Each award includes a cash prize of $5,000. It’s up to you to nominate one of your administrative-staff colleagues to receive this award. Award information, a list of eligible employees and the nomination form are available at staffawards.sva.edu. Nominations are due Friday, May 6, 5:00pm.
