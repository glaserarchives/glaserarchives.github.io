The New York Botanical Garden's annual holiday train show is officially returning for its 30th iteration, featuring the garden's own buildings and structures along with its traditional presentation of city landmark replicas. ANNOUNCEMENTS

COVID-19 Testing for Anime NYC Attendees
News media reported yesterday that a person who attended the Anime NYC Convention, held at the Javits Center in Manhattan late last month, has tested positive for the Omicron variant of COVID-19. As an effort to contain the virus and keep the community safe, New York State Governor Kathy Hochul and New York City Mayor Bill de Blasio have urged all who attended the convention to get tested for COVID-19.
