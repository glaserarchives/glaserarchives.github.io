EVENTS

9:00am | The Curatorial Roundtable: Udo Kittelmann (Berlin)

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with Udo Kittelmann, former director of the National Gallery in Berlin and of several other major museums. Today, September 6, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.

Opening Today | “Exploded View”

A joint exhibition of short films, prints and puppets by 25 students and alumni from BFA 3D Animation and Visual Effects and BFA Animation, curated by faculty member James Bascara (BFA 2011 Illustration) and BFA Animation director of operations Angelica Vergel. Reception: Thursday, September 28, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Wednesday, September 6 – Monday, October 2. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Opening Today | “ILLO COMX Preview”

An exhibition of highlights by 125 BFA Comics and BFA Illustration graduates from the class of 2023, curated by the departments’ chair, Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Wednesday, September 6 – Monday, October 2. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Deadline Today: Apply to Be a VASA Staffer Today!

VASA is hiring staff for the 2023 – 2024 academic year! Being a staff member is a paid, on-campus position that gives you an opportunity to work alongside VASA in planning events, creating promotional materials and more. Learn more and apply here. The deadline to apply is today, September 6, 11:59pm.

Next Week | RisoLAB and Visual Narrative Info Session A

MFA Visual Narrative and the SVA RisoLAB are offering a wide range of continuing education classes for the fall 2023 semester—attend an online information session to hear about the Risograph printing courses available. Tuesday, September 12, 6:30 – 8:30pm, online.

SVA Cartoon Allies and MFA Visual Narrative at SPX

SVA Cartoon Allies and MFA Visual Narrative are partnering on a table at the Small Press Expo in Bethesda, Maryland, Saturday, September 9 – Sunday, September 10. Small Press Expo is the premier event for indie comics, cartooning and graphic novels. Drop by Table G2 to meet SVA students and browse and purchase student and alumni comics! And drop in on MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay) at Table W38-40.

Women in Entertainment—Sessions From New York

Women in Entertainment presents a half-day summit, bringing together professionals across TV, film and theater to discuss issues that are changing the field and how to rise above them. This event will explore the evolving landscape of storytelling, content strategies and project development in New York City’s entertainment industry. Gain valuable insights from top New York City-based creatives and discover the keys to breaking into the creative scene. Wednesday, September 27, 9:30am – 2:00pm. A limited number of free tickets are available to SVA students, faculty and staff with the code “SVA2023.”
