New Trial Resource: Art & Architecture ePortal

The SVA Libraries have begun a trial of the Art & Architecture ePortal from Yale University Press. With over 300 titles across 25 subject areas and 60,000 associated images, the A&A ePortal is an essential e-book resource for faculty and students. The platform features authoritative scholarship in the history of art, architecture, decorative arts, photography and design, and also provides invaluable content in related disciplines, such as African American studies, religious studies, women’s studies, environmental studies and visual culture. This trial will run until Thursday, October 13.
