EVENTS

9:00am | Mental Health First Aid Training

Student Health and Counseling Services presents Mental Health First Aid training for SVA staff and faculty. This eight-hour training teaches the skills to provide initial support when someone experiences a mental-health crisis. More info about the class here. Register here. 9:00am – 5:00pm, Soc Doc Theater, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

6:30pm | MFA Social Documentary Film Thesis Showcase

MFA Social Documentary Film presents its eighth annual thesis showcase, with previews of documentary shorts and in-progress features by the program's class of 2018. Filmmakers will be present, and Q&As will follow each screening. RSVP and see the screening schedule here. Tonight, 6:30 – 10:30pm, and tomorrow, noon – 11:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Saturday | The Predator and White Boy Rick

SVA Theatre presents two screenings on Saturday, September 22: The Predator, starring Sterling K. Brown, at 5:00pm, and White Boy Rick, starring Matthew McConaughey, at 8:00pm. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Sunday | The House with a Clock in Its Walls and The Sisters Brothers

SVA Theatre presents two screenings on Sunday, September 23: The House with a Clock in Its Walls, starring Jack Black and Cate Blanchett, at 5:00pm, and The Sisters Brothers, starring Joaquin Phoenix and John C. Reilly, at 8:00pm. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
