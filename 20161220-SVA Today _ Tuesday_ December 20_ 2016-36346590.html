December "Hot Five"

The top five most-discussed projects from SVA Portfolios this December include a film festival poster series, animated logos and more.

6:30pm | Passengers 3D Screening

SVA Theatre presents a 3D screening of Passengers, directed by Morten Tyldum, who studied at SVA, and starring Jennifer Lawrence and Chris Pratt. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Taking on Phantasmagoria

Flaunt interviews Wyatt Mills (BFA 2013 Fine Arts) about how his surreal pieces reflect the "internal drama" that people experience.
NYC PICKS

Sleep Walkers/Zoo Mantras

Dancer, artist and writer Simone Forti will perform her piece Sleep Walkers/Zoo Mantras for the first time in New York since the 1970s. Free admission on first-come, first-served basis. 6:30pm. International Studio & Curatorial Program, 1040 Metropolitan Avenue, Brooklyn.

"Muhammad Ali, LeRoy Neiman and the Art of Boxing"

An exhibition that celebrates Muhammad Ali using the art of LeRoy Neiman, one of the most popular artists of boxing. Through March 26. Ticket prices vary; purchase here. New-York Historical Society, 170 Central Park West.

2016 Annual Party Photos on MySVA

The photographs from the 2016 annual party, a '70s-themed extravaganza held last Friday at Guastavino's, are now available for viewing on MySVA. Enjoy!

Student Club Applications

If you are interested in creating a club or renewing your existing club at SVA for the spring 2017 semester, please complete the application before Friday, January 20. Note that all club leaders must have a GPA of at least 3.0.
