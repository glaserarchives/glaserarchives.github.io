Wildlife Along the Israel-Palestine Border

Recent MFA Photography, Video and Related Media graduate and 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winner Netta Laufer on her "25FT" project, which examines wildlife along the Israel-Palestine border as a way to consider human conflict.

Scharf on Haring, Basquiat, NYC and SVA [Video]

THIS WEEK

Monday (6/20): Strike a Pose

The SVA Theatre, NewFest and NYC Pride present an advance screening of Strike a Pose, a documentary that follows seven male dancers on Madonna's 1990 Blonde Ambition tour. Dancers from the film will be on hand for a post-screening talk and party. $5 for SVA students, faculty and staff with code "SVAPRIDE"; $25 general admission. 7:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Tuesday (6/21): Cat Del Buono

MPS Digital Photography presents the 2008 MFA Photography, Video and Related Media alumnus discussing her work, which incorporates performance, interactive video and humor. 7:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Tuesday (6/21): Ken Burns In:Pictures

SVA and The Modern School of Film present the documentary filmmaker discussing and screening clips from Buster Keaton's The General (1926), Carol Reed's The Third Man (1949) and Tom McCarthy's The Station Agent (2003), all of which have influenced his understanding of the American experience. $5 for SVA students, $10 for faculty and staff (two ticket limit and one non-SVA guest permitted), $20 general admission, $60 VIP reserved seating. 7:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Wednesday (6/22): "MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Thesis Exhibition" Reception

6:00 - 8:00pm. Exhibition runs through Friday, July 1, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Saturday (6/25): Constellations of Memory

An exhibition of thesis projects by the MAT Art Education class of 2016. Reception Friday, July 8, 4:00 - 6:00pm. Through Thursday, July 14, SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Sunday (6/26): MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Thesis Screening

2:00 - 6:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Sunday (6/26): Independence Day: Resurgence

The Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Independence Day: Resurgence. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). 7:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
