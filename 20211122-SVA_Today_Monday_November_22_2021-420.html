Trial Access: Artforum Magazine Archives
The SVA Library has just begun trial access to the Artforum magazine archives, the leading magazine for coverage of international contemporary art, from its launch in 1962 to 2020. Spanning six decades of reporting on art in all media, Artforum offers features, reviews, and interviews relating to artists, exhibitions, publications, and other art world events and trends. This trial will expire at the end of the fall semester.

Visual Opinion Interns

Send your resume, portfolio/website and a statement of interest to vo@sva.edu with the subject line “VO Intern Application.” Deadline to apply is Tuesday, November 30, 9:00am ET.
