#SVAwesome

Last week, SVA staff from New York City touched down in Seoul for a packed schedule of alumni and cultural engagement culminating in a dinner and reception co-hosted by Alumni Affairs and Development and the Korean Alumni Association. Other highlights of the week included a visit to SVA’s Seocho Gallery to see an exhibition of work by MFA Photography, Video and Related Media alumni Ok Hyun Ahn (2003), Jungsuk Park and Jeongmee Yoon (2006); a viewing of Minseop Yoon’s (MFA 2013 Fine Art) site-specific work Dance for the Night, at Amore Pacific Museum of Art; a stop at Tak Hoon Kim’s (MFA 2005 Computer Art; BFA 1997 Animation) Tak Toon studio; studio visits with Myoungduck Seo (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay) and Heewon Seo (MFA 2012 Fine Arts); and dinner with Korean Alumni Association cabinet members.
