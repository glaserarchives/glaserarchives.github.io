EVENTS

Ongoing | “The Masters Series: Lynsey Addario”

An exhibition of work by Lynsey Addario, acclaimed photojournalist, MacArthur Genius Grant and Pulitzer Prize recipient, as part of the 32nd annual Masters Series Award and Exhibition. Curated by Maya Benton and Perri Hofmann. Through Saturday, October 29. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Ongoing | “The Health Poster Project”

An exhibition of posters by 24 students from BFA Illustration, MFA Illustration as Visual Essay and the 2022 Summer Illustration Residency, curated by BFA Illustration and BFA Comics Chair Viktor Koen. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “MPS Fashion Photography Class of 2022”

MPS Fashion Photography presents an exhibition of works by the class of 2022. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “Dwelling in Motion”

A juried exhibition of works that analyze the constantly growing relationship between habitat and inhabitant. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Ongoing | “A Sticker History Exhibition”

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents “A Sticker History Exhibition: A Snapshot from 100 Years of Design Told Through Adhesive Materials,” an exhibition of stickers by various cultural creators from diverse backgrounds and eras curated by DB Burkeman and Stuck-Up. Through Thursday, September 29. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

NYC PICK

Latinx & Latin American Identities & Representation in the Visual Arts

This year’s ArtTable Annual Leadership Series focuses on Latinx and Latin American identities and representation with a distinguished panel of speakers. SVA students are offered a $10 ticket with discount code “ATxSVA”; learn more and register here. Tomorrow, September 7, 5:30pm. Christie’s New York, 20 Rockefeller Plaza.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Free Museum Admission with SVA ID Is Back!

As a benefit to students, faculty and staff, SVA maintains memberships at various museums. With your SVA ID, you can enjoy free and discounted access to the Brooklyn Museum, Museum of Modern Art, MoMA PS1, New Museum of Contemporary Art and Whitney Museum of American Art. For more details, visit the MyID dashboard > Department Resources Page > Museum Benefits, or click here. Contact Miranda Pierce at mpierce1@sva.edu or ext. 2305 with any questions.

Yoga for Artists: Preparing Body and Mind for New Beginnings

Start the semester right with a free, beginner-friendly virtual yoga class. Explore ways to prepare your body and mind for the fall semester with Student Health and Counseling Services and instructor Melissa Boo. All you need is a flat surface and 1 – 3 soft squares (pillows, blankets or towels). Open to the entire SVA Community. RSVP required; register here. Friday, September 9, 10:00 – 10:45am, online.

After School Special 2022

SVA Theatre presents the College’s annual alumni film and animation festival. Events include screenings of the indie thriller Resurrection, the award-winning documentary Mayor, Disney hits Encanto, Lightyear and Turning Red; a screening of the best SVA thesis films of 2022 and an animation panel. SVA alumni will be present at Q&As throughout the week. All events are free and open to the public. Wednesday, September 21 – Sunday, September 25. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
