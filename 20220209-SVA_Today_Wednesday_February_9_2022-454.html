SVA Today
Wednesday, February 9, 2022

My Art Portfolio: Then and Now
This edition of our YouTube series My Art Portfolio features fourth-year student Kyra Husbands (BFA Visual & Critical Studies), who showcases the progression of her art portfolio from late high school to today. Husbands's work can also be seen in the current "The Way We See Us" Black History Month student exhibition, via her Instagram (@k.y.ra) and on her website.

The Dream Cave
Curated by MA Curatorial Practice student Wanhang Chao, this exhibition attempts to create a space as absurd and yet unified as Alice in Wonderland, featuring works by artists Mosa (Zijun Zhao) and Yichi Zhang. Wednesday, February 9 – Wednesday, February, 16, at the MA Curatorial Practice Space. 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

All Week | SVA Community Fridge Lunar New Year Food Share
The SVA Community Fridge, in collaboration with the SVA International Student Office, will be offering packaged food and fruits to celebrate the Lunar New Year and is calling for Lunar New Year-related food donations (no frozen food, please) through Friday, February 11. You can also donate any amount to help fund the fridge. Click here to contribute and write "community fridge" in the comment box to ensure that your donation is directed to the project. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Student Center hours are Monday – Friday, 10:00am – 10:00pm; Saturday and Sunday, 12:00pm – 6:00pm.

Friday | Fab Friday with Anna Crider
Fab Friday is a weekly design inspiration guest lecture series hosted by the BFA Design and BFA Advertising departments. This week's guest is Anna Crider, a partner at Entro, an New York City-based environmental design agency. Friday, February 11, 3:30pm ET, via Zoom.

Friday | Final VASA Elections Info Session
Do you want to build a team to become the VASA leadership next year? To qualify to run in the upcoming college-wide election, interested candidates must attend one information session. For more information, email activities@sva.edu. Friday, February 11, 3:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Friday | Lunar New Year Community Crafts
Join the SVA Asian American Student Union this Friday to honor the Lunar New Year holiday and make some crafts for the community! Use your stellar artistic skills to decorate brown paper bags for Heart of Dinner, an AAPI nonprofit providing groceries to East Asian elders in New York City, and create beautiful postcards for the Ain't I A Woman campaign, which is protesting 24-hour workdays for local home-care workers. Friday, February 11, 4:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street.

For those unable to make it, blank postcards will be available at the SVA Student Center, Friday, February 11 – Friday, February 18.

Friday | VASA Fest
Come to VASA Fest and meet your representatives in the Visual Arts Student Association, SVA's student government organization; learn all there is to know about joining clubs; and find out about upcoming VASA programs and elections. If you are a student and interested in running for a student government position, the final VASA election info session will be held at 3:30pm. Friday, February 11, 3:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Saturday | Junior Jammies 2022
Come watch the amazing group films created by each of BFA Animation's Advanced Animation Workshop classes throughout the fall 2021 semester. Enjoy a great selection of films and chat with the directors and coordinators of each film afterward live. Saturday, February 12, 12:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
