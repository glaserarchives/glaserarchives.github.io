SVA Today
July 5 – 10, 2022
Joseph Sinnott

2020 and 2021 Make-up Commencement
Graduates from the SVA classes of 2020 and 2021 reunited in-person for a make-up commencement ceremony at Radio City Music Hall last week, with renowned filmmaker, artist and author John Waters delivering the keynote address.
Events

Today | Practice Lecture Series: Shayna Strype
MFA Art Practice presents a talk by its 2022 artist in residence, Shayna Strype, an interdisciplinary artist working in film, animation, live performance and puppetry. Tuesday, July 5, 12:30 – 2:00pm. MFA Art Practice, 335 West 16th Street, 5th floor.

Tomorrow | “And Then . . . 2022”
A thesis exhibition of visual stories by five graduates of the MFA Visual Narrative class of 2022. Reception: Friday, July 22, 6:00 – 8:00pm; RSVP here. Wednesday, July 6 – Monday, July 25. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | Thesis Exhibition 2022
MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents its 2022 thesis exhibition of lens- and screen-based work by 11 students. Through Monday, July 11. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
SVA IN THE PRESS
Collier Schorr

Collier Schorr for Interview
Collier Schorr (BFA 1985 Communication Arts) photographed Anne Hathaway for Interview’s June 2022 cover story.

“Black Melancholia” at Bard
Ja’Tovia Gary (MFA 2014 Social Documentary Film) and Lorna Simpson (BFA 1982 Photography) are featured in “Black Melancholia,” an exhibition currently on view at Hessel Museum of Art at Bard College in Annandale-on-Hudson, New York. Via Vogue.

Rich Tu in Forbes
Rich Tu (MFA 2009 Illustration as Visual Essay) is interviewed by Forbes about the intersection of art, education and cultural awareness.

“2 Minutes With . . . Camilo de Galofre”
Camilo de Galofre (BFA 2013 Advertising) talks about his background, his creative inspirations and recent work he’s admired for Muse by Clio.

Rosemarie Albanese Looks for the Humanity in New York City Trash
Rosemarie Albanese (MPS 2022 Branding) is featured in Print for her series of “trash poems.”
NYC PICKS

“A Mimetic Theory of Desire”
A group exhibition featuring work by Jamie “Trey” Abdella (BFA 2016 Illustration). Through Friday, July 29. David Lewis Gallery, 57 Walker Street.

“Sequential Synergy: The Art of Comics”
An exhibition of contemporary comic book art celebrating the diverse ways comics artists and cartoonists utilize the real estate of the blank page, featuring work by Gina Minichino (BFA 1990 Media Arts). On view Thursday, July 7 – Friday, July 29, 2022. Opening reception: Saturday, July 9, 3:00 – 6:00pm. 440 Gallery, 440 Sixth Avenue, Brooklyn.

Movies With a View: Before Sunrise
The Movies With a View series returns to Brooklyn Bridge Park with a screening of Before Sunrise. Food and drink will be available at Smorgasburg at the Movies along the Pier 1 promenade. Thursday, July 7, 6:00 – 10:00pm. Free. Harbor View Lawn, 80 Furman Street, Brooklyn.

BRIC Celebrate Brooklyn: Crumb
BRIC Celebrate Brooklyn continues with a concert by Crumb, featuring an opening performance by Slauson Malone 1. Saturday, July 9, doors at 6:30pm, show at 7:30pm. Free; RSVP here. Lena Horne Bandshell, Ninth Street and Prospect Park West, Brooklyn.
