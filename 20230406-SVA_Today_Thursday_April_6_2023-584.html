Career Development Virtual Office Hours This Month

Every Thursday in April, 10:00 – 11:00am, SVA Career Development will host its virtual office hour. A career counselor will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form on Thursday mornings to access a Zoom link to join.
