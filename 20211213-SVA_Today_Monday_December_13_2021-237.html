SVA Today
Monday, December 13, 2021

Meet Alumni Scholarship Award Winner Sanika Phawde
Sanika Phawde (MFA 2021 Illustration as Visual Essay), a drawing and imaging professor at the Parsons School of Design, discusses her Alumni Scholarship Award-winning thesis project, Taste Your Words Before You Spit Them Out, a graphic novel that explores how certain cultures with a history of migration and colonial traumas used food as a communicative tool.

IN TOWN AND ONLINE

Living Histories: Queer Views and Old Masters
The Frick Collection presents “Living Histories: Queer Views and Old Masters,” an exhibition featuring the work of artists Doron Langberg, Salman Toor, Jenna Gribbon and Toyin Ojih Odutola. Each artist's work puts particular emphasis on issues of gender and queer identity that were typically excluded from narratives of early modern European art. Through Wednesday, August 31, 2022. The Frick Collection, 1 East 70th Street.
