Events

Last Chance | “Other Visions”
An exhibition of multidisciplinary work exploring hidden structures and unique ways of seeing through language, identity and light. Closes today, August 22. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Tomorrow | Beast Screening
SVA Theatre presents a screening of Beast, starring Idris Elba. Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Visit svatheatre.com for SVA Theatre’s current COVID-19 guidelines, including information on masks and vaccination requirements. Tuesday, August 23, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Thursday | 2022 All-Staff Meeting
Celebrate 75 years of SVA at the 2022 All-Staff Meeting! To ensure everyone’s safety, your SVA ID is required and you must have completed the Cleared4 process at least two hours prior to the start of the meeting in order to enter the theater. SVA ID is required for check-in. Masks are mandatory when indoors. For those unable to attend in person, the meeting will be live-streamed. Pre-register here to receive your link to the program. Thursday, August 25, 8:00am breakfast, program starts at 9:00am. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “Dwelling in Motion”
A juried exhibition of works that analyze the constantly growing relationship between habitat and inhabitant. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Sketch Night at Frick Madison

Charlie Parker Jazz Fest
The city’s annual salute to the late saxophonist Charlie Parker returns for its 30th year, featuring a three-day celebration of live jazz in the neighborhoods near where Parker worked and lived. Free. Friday, August 26 – Sunday, August 28. Locations vary, learn more here.

Brooklyn Public Library Vinyl Collection
Starting this month, the Brooklyn Public Library invites guests to explore its vinyl collection located at the Central Library in the New & Noteworthy room. Listening stations and a variety of records available for browsing and borrowing. Brooklyn Public Library, 10 Grand Army Plaza, Brooklyn.
ANNOUNCEMENT

After School Special 2022
SVA Theatre presents the College’s annual alumni film and animation festival. Events include screenings of the indie thriller Resurrection, the award-winning documentary Mayor, Disney hits Encanto, Lightyear and Turning Red; a screening of the best SVA thesis films of 2022 and an animation panel. SVA alumni will be present at Q&A’s throughout the week. All events are free and open to the public. Wednesday, September 21 – Sunday, September 25. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
In Memoriam
Dennis Gillyard, an SVA staff member for 34 years, died last month at the age of 76. Gillyard first joined the College as a mailroom clerk, working in that role from 1981 to 1987. He returned in 1994, working in Resource Management for nine years and then Mail Processing/Office Services, where he worked for 19 years before retiring in April of this year.
