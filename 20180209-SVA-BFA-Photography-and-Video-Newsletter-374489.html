Swann Galleries Hosts Pop-up of Original 1870s Portrait Studio

Visitors are welcome to visit the pop-up portrait studio at Swann Galleries which features an original handpainted backdrop from a 19th Century tintypist or ambrotypist's studio. All are encouraged to bring their own cameras.

The preview hours are February 10, 12-5PM, February 12 to 14, 12-5PM, and February 15, 10AM-12PM.
