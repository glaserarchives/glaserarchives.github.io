SVA IN THE PRESS

SVA Surrounds The Mic
MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman is hosting a podcast, called The Mic, for NYCXDesign. The latest episode features BFA Design faculty member Paula Scher and alumnus Julian Alexander (BFA 1998 Graphic Design).

Celebrating Seymour Chwast
David Plunkert pitched an idea to legendary designer (and onetime SVA faculty member) Seymour Chwast about mounting a poster show in Baltimore’s Spur Gallery to help promote Chwast’s forthcoming book, Poster Man. MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller takes a closer look for Print.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

Machine Hallucination: NYC
“Machine Hallucination: NYC” is a uniquely immersive artistic experience by artist Refik Anadol that incorporates artificial intelligence and advanced technology in a 30-minute visual presentation of AI re-imaginings of New York City. Through January 2, 2022. Artechouse, 439 West 15th Street. Purchase tickets here.
