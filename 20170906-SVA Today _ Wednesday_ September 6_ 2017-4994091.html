International Students Workshop: Adjusting to Life in NYC

Student Health and Counseling Services presents a workshop for international students interested in learning about adjustment and ways to be successful in the transition to living and learning in New York City. New Colourbox Key for 2017

SVA Library provides access to the Colourbox stock image database for all current students, faculty and staff. Colourbox contains millions of royalty-free images, vector illustrations and videos. Each fall, a new access key is released for our education license. The fall 2017 key, which is valid until September 1, 2018, is 241395657896, and is also posted under the "Library" tab on MySVA.

SVA at Photoville

BFA Photography and Video and MPS Digital Photography present their own exhibitions of work at Photoville, the annual photography show housed in shipping containers located in DUMBO’s Brooklyn Bridge Park. Wednesday, September 13 – Sunday, September 24. Brooklyn Bridge Plaza, Water Street at New Dock Street, Brooklyn.
