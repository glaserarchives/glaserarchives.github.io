COVID-19 Updates

Masks are still mandatory in all SVA buildings because seven-day community case (transmission) rates are currently not at or below the levels of 100 per 100,000 people in New York City, or 1,200 per day; and 7 per 5,500 people at SVA, or 1 per day. Read more about how transmission rates are calculated.

  • •The current seven-day NYC case (transmission) rate can be found here.
  • •The current SVA seven-day community case (transmission) rate is 19.

If you test positive, upload the results of any COVID-19 test to Cleared4 via the link in your daily symptom checker. Look for an email from studenthealthalert@sva.edu or employee-health-alert@sva.edu. Begin quarantining for at least five days.


See this page for more information on SVA’s COVID-19 policies.
