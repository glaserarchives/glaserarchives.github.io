Get your application in soon to make the next round of reviews to increase your chance at being awarded scholarship funds! We will continue rolling admissions, but funds have already started to be offered this month to accepted applicants.

We Put Story First

When words and images come together, they can create powerful visual narratives — stories that can touch hearts, change minds and even change the way we see our world - story first. The MFA Visual Narrative program at SVA responds to the needs of today’s evolving creative markets by merging the artist and author. It’s an evolutionary process that puts story first and gives you the tools you need to craft your own stories and to help others craft theirs.

SVA's MFA Visual Narrative program features:
• Low Residency Model- three summers at SVA in NYC and four semesters online 
• Rolling Admissions  
• Free summer 2018 SVA housing grants for those who reside outside NYC 
• Acceptance of all Bachelor’s degrees or equivalents  
• Ability to audit up to four additional SVA classes tuition-free
