Events

1:00pm | SVA in L.A. Information Session
Hosted by Sal Petrosino, director of special projects and events–recruitment, the SVA in L.A. program will hold an information session to discuss this year’s trip, including scheduling, accommodations and potential guests. Today, 1:00 – 2:00pm ET. SVA Amphitheater, 209 East 23rd Street, third floor. For those who cannot attend the session in person, register here to attend via Zoom.

6:00pm | Opening Night: "Your presence is the education I want"
Ma Curatorial Practice's CP Projects Space presents the opening night of “Your presence is the education I want,” featuring works by Eunwoo Nam and Zi Yi Wang and curated by MA Curatorial Practice student Sophia Park. This exhibition addresses collecting as a practice to explore the physical manifestations of memory for those living in diaspora. Tonight, 6:00 – 7:00pm. Register here.

Julia Rothman in The New York Times
BFA Illustration faculty member Julia Rothman's latest article with Shaina Feinberg, "Their Holiday Parties Are Just Getting Restarted," is featured in The New York Times.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

Jónsi's Obsidian
Inspired by the recent eruption of the Fagradalsfjall volcano in Iceland, “Jónsi's Obsidian,” by interdisciplinary artist and musician Jónsi, consists of two sound installations and sculptural works meant to infuse the senses, including ambient sounds, mechanically generated frequencies, samples from nature and more. Through Friday, December 17. Tanya Bonakdar Gallery, 521 West 21st Street.
