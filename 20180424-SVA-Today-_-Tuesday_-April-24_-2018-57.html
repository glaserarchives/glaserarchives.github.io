TAKE NOTE

Submit Your Distinguished Staff Award Nominations

Each year at the All-Staff Meeting, SVA recognizes outstanding staff contributions to the College with the presentation of two Distinguished Staff Awards. The nomination form, a list of eligible staff members and further details about the award are available at staffawards.sva.edu. Nomination deadline: Friday, May 11, 5:00pm.

Proficiency Exam Dates 2018 – 2019

The SVA Writing Resource Center has published the dates for upcoming proficiency exams. Students who matriculated in fall 2017 will not be able to register for Humanities and Sciences electives until they pass the exam. Current students who have not yet completed the requirement are urged to take the exam in August. Click here for the full schedule.

Biomimicry for Social Innovation: Think Like an Ecosystem

Biomimicry NYC, friends of MFA Design for Social Innovation, are hosting Think Like an Ecosystem, a three-day workshop that will teach you how to apply ecosystem intelligence to organizations and social innovation efforts and inspire new ways of thinking and collaborating in New York City's backyard. Register here. June 12 – 14. New York Botanical Gardens, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.
