Elevator Repairs

The elevators at 132 West 21st Street will be out of service for repairs from 11:00pm – 7:00am starting tonight, February 14, 11:00pm, through Thursday, February 16. Service will be restored each morning at 7:00am for normal business hours until the repairs are completed. As a secondary option, passengers may use the elevator at 136 West 21st Street and cross over on the seventh floor.

Family Day at SVA Theatre

SVA Theatre and New York International Children’s Film Festival presents a day of short films as well as arts and crafts for kids and their families. All free and open to the public! Thirteen short films will play on loop beginning at 10:00am and MA/MAT Art Education students will be in the lobby with fun arts and crafts projects for kids. Visit at any time between 10:00am – 2:00pm. While kids and adults of all ages are welcome, the films and activities are geared towards children ages three to eight. All attendees must be masked while inside the building. RSVPs are welcome, but not necessary. Saturday, February 25, 10:00am – 2:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Submission Deadline Extended: Honoring Turkish Heritage

Deadline extended! SVA’s International Student Office calls for submissions of artwork that relates to Turkish culture or artwork from students with Turkish backgrounds. Open to all students! The extended deadline for submissions is Tuesday, February 28. Submissions will be displayed in the ISO reception area at 340 East 24th Street as part of an exhibition running mid-March through the end of April. Access the submission requirements and form here. Contact Chisato Takeyama, ctakeyama@sva.edu, with any questions or to schedule an artwork drop-off at ISO.
