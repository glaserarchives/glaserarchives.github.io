June Newsletter
#Connected
Counseling in Schools 
Exhibition: June 2-14
Opening Reception: June 6, 5-8pm
Panel Discussion: June 11, 5:30-7pm
132 West 21st Street, 5th Floor Gallery

A collaborative art exhibition exploring youth responses to technology, digital media, and relationships.

Art therapists and art therapy interns will participate in an interactive panel discussion addressing the collaborative art exhibition #Connected, clinical creative arts therapy in schools, and the therapeutic exhibition process.  Read more in our blog post.

Contact arttherapy@sva.edu to RSVP for the panel discussion and make an appointment for additional viewing hours.
The Kindness Experience
Happy Hour 4 Kids
Exhibition: June 16-30
Reception: June 19 1:30-3pm & June 20, 2:30-3pm
132 West 21st Street, 5th Floor Gallery

The Kindness Experience features children and adolescents attending Happy Hour 4 Kids, a full time therapeutic center.  Children practiced an act of self-care and kindness by creating artwork for themselves and then practiced kindness towards a peer by choosing someone at random to create artwork for.

Contact arttherapy@sva.edu to make an appointment for additional viewing hours.
