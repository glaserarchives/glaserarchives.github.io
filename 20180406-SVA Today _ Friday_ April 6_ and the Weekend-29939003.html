Spring Fashion Spotlight

SVA Style presents the spring ensembles of some of the most stylish people at SVA.
EVENTS

1:00pm | Mindful Joint 2018: Contemporary Korean Art in Conversations

BFA Fine Arts presents day two of this two-day symposium that brings together artists, writers and curators from South Korea and the U.S. to break the generalized perception of Korea and understand the complexities of artists’ practices and cultural backgrounds. RSVP here. 1:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Fine Arts Building, 335 West 16th Street, ground floor.

Sunday | Ready Player One

SVA Theatre presents a 3D screening of Steven Spielberg's Ready Player One. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Sunday, April 8, 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Stories from Seoul

In her TEDx Talk, children's book author and illustrator Yangsook Choi (MFA 1995 Illustration as Visual Essay) discusses how growing up in Seoul, South Korea, made her a great storyteller.
NYC PICKS

Frick First Fridays

Free access to the museum's permanent collection, special exhibitions, talks and performances on the first Friday of every month. Tonight, 6:00 – 9:00pm. The Frick Collection, 1 East 70th Street.

A Chilling Make-Believe: Alexis Rockman on Grant Wood

Artist Alexis Rockman (BFA 1985 Fine Arts) will discuss the romanticized and ambivalent view of a pre-industrial rural world in Grant Wood’s landscape paintings, which are on view at the museum. Tickets $8 – $10; purchase here. Tonight, 6:30pm. Whitney Museum of American Art, 99 Gansevoort Street.

MoCCA Arts Festival

The Society of Illustrators presents this alternative comic and cartoon fest celebrating emerging creators and legends of the industry and featuring events with SVA faculty including Steven Brodner, Nathan Fox, Steven Heller, Bill Kartalopulos, Gary Panter and Matt Rota, and alumni including Mark Newgarden, Nate Powell, Cecilia Ruiz and Dash Shaw. MFA Visual Narrative will also host a student sales table and a Risograph demo area at tables 299-305 on the second floor. Tickets $7 per day; purchase here. Tomorrow, April 7 – Sunday, April 8. Metropolitan West, 639 West 46th Street.
TAKE NOTE
