Present Tense: New Prints, 2000 – 2005
International Print Center New York presents an exhibition of works originally shown by the institution in the first five years of its New Prints program, as part of IPCNY's 20th anniversary celebrations. Participating artists include SVA alumnus Willie Cole (BFA 1976 Media Arts) and faculty Dread Scott and James Siena. Through Saturday, December 18; 508 West 26th Street, 5A. Reservations recommended for in-person viewing; click here for more information and a 3D virtual tour of the show.
