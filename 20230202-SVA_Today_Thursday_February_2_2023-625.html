February Events and Exhibitions

EVENTS

12:00pm | RA Selection Information Session

Interested in being a resident assistant (RA) this summer or next academic year? Attend one of the mandatory virtual information sessions to learn more about the position and receive the application materials. For the information session, attendees must be on video, signed on singularly (one candidate per device) and be present for all content. In order to apply you must attend one information session. Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

Last Call for Submissions: Honoring Turkish Heritage

SVA’s International Student Office calls for submissions of artwork that relates to Turkish culture or artwork from students with Turkish backgrounds. Open to all students! The deadline for submissions is today, February 2. Submissions will be displayed in the ISO reception area at 340 East 24th Street as part of an exhibition running Monday, February 6, through Friday, March 31. Access the submission requirements and form here. Contact Chisato Takeyama, ctakeyama@sva.edu, with any questions or to schedule an artwork drop-off at ISO.
