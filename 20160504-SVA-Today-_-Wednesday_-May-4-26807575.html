2016 Jeonju International Photography Festival
BFA Photography and Video faculty member Eric Weeks (BFA 1987 Photography) has been named the executive director of this year's festival, and will curate the main exhibition, "FACT/FICTION," featuring Victoria Hely-Hutchinson (BFA 2009 Photography), faculty member Stacy Renee Morrison and Orit Raff (BFA 1996 Photography). The festival takes place in Jeonju, South Korea, Saturday, May 14, through Sunday, May 22.
