Events

11:00am | Yoga for Artists: TLC for Hands, Wrists and Shoulders
Join Student Health and Counseling Services and instructor Melissa Boo for a free yoga class and learn stretches for hands, wrists and shoulders that help release tension, improve flexibility and increase range of motion. The best way for artists and creatives to avoid injury and pain is through prevention! Open to the entire SVA community. Today, 11:00am – 12:00pm ET, online. RSVP required.

4:30pm | Faculty Book Club
President, founder, and lead artist of RAE Comics T.J. Sterling will be joining us as co-facilitator with MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay). We will be discussing the short documentary Drawn Together: Comics, Diversity, and Stereotypes and the comic Your Black Friend, by Ben Passmore. Today, 4:30pm on Zoom.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Thursday | ISO Meet and Greet and I-20 Travel Signature
Come meet the ISO staff next week at SVA Library West! This event will be a chance for international students on campus to meet the International Student Office (ISO) team and fellow international students studying in NYC. The ISO staff will also be providing travel signatures for international students who plan to travel outside the U.S for Thanksgiving and winter break. As a current international student, you must have a valid travel signature on page 2 of your I-20 for your re-entry to the U.S. Make sure to bring your printed I-20 and passport to receive a travel signature! Thursday, November 18, 3:00 pm – 5:00 pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Thursday | MFA DSI Virtual Open House
MFA Design for Social Innovation offers students the unique opportunity to study design practices that directly respond to complex, global challenges. Join this virtual open house to connect with amazing and diverse program faculty, students and alumni, learn about their exciting work and see what an MFA in DSI can do for you. Thursday, November 18, 9:30 – 11:00am ET, on Zoom. Register here.

Friday | SVA Language Exchange Social
Join the first SVA Language Exchange Social! Holly from Student Health and Counseling Services and Will from the International Student Office will host an hour of fun activities and opportunities to connect with others through language learning and speaking. Friday, November 19, 2:00 – 3:00pm ET, on Zoom. Reservations required; register here.

Visual Opinion InternsInterns

Send your resume, portfolio/website and a statement of interest to vo@sva.edu with the subject line “VO Intern Application.” Deadline to apply is Tuesday, November 30, 9:00am ET.
