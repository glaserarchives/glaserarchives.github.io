IN THE PRESS

Products of Design at the MoMA Design Store

Core77 explores the ongoing SVA MFA Products of Design collaboration with MoMA Wholesale. Be sure to check out the window displays at the MoMA Design Stores in Midtown and Soho, which feature the products released to date.

Peanuts Murals in NYC

The New York Times on how Peanuts Worldwide commissioned seven artists, including Kenny Scharf (BFA 1981 Fine Arts), to create murals of their interpretation of classic Peanuts characters in Hudson Square.

Works by Paula Scher

Design Observer interviews BFA Design faculty member Paula Scher about her upcoming monograph, Paula Scher: Works.
EVENTS

Poetic Algorithms

A group of works created by first-year MFA Computer Arts students exploring the possibilities of technology and its impact on the way we see the world. Tuesday, April 10 – Thursday, April 12. Reception: Thursday, April 12, 6:00pm. Highline Loft, 508 West 26th Street, Loft 5G.

Kathy Shorr

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by Kathy Shorr (BFA 1988 Photography), who photographed survivors of gun violence for her most recent project, "Shot." Tuesday, April 10, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

MFA Design Venture 18: Design and Discovery

MFA Design presents its thesis forum, where the 12 program graduates and design entrepreneurs will premiere their unique products. RSVP here. Wednesday, April 11, 6:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

The Haves and Have Nots

BFA Cartooning and BFA Illustration presents an exhibition of work by third-year students exploring themes of social injustice and class struggle. Reception: Wednesday, April 11, 6:00pm. Through Saturday, April 21. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
