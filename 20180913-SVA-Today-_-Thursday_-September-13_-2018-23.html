6:00pm | "Fifth Floor" Reception

BFA Visual & Critical Studies and BFA Fine Arts present an exhibition celebrating printmaking, curated by SVA faculty member and alumnus Shannon Broder. 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Friday, September 28. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

6:30pm | Laura Parnes' Tour Without End Screening

As part of its Scheimpflug lecture series, MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a screening of Laura Parnes’ comedy/documentary film Tour Without End, followed by a conversation between Parnes, writer and musician Johanna Fateman and film critic Amy Taubin. 6:30 – 9:00pm SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street

7:30pm | The Greatest Showman

As part of the After School Special, SVA presents a screening of The Greatest Showman, followed by a Q&A with BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects alumni who worked on the film’s visual effects—Jasmine Carruthers, Mina Choe and Elana Lederman. 7:30pm. RSVP here. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Photoville: "The Color Of..."

MPS Digital Photography alumni, faculty and staff are participating in Photoville, the annual photography show housed in shipping containers located in DUMBO's Brooklyn Bridge Park, with a show dedicated to the theme of color. Through Sunday, September 23 (closed September 17 – 19). Hours of operation here. Brooklyn Bridge Plaza, Water Street at New Dock Street, Brooklyn.

Talking Our Way to the Future: A Free Online Master Class

MFA Design for Social Innovation presents the first free webinar in its series "Master Classes in Social Design: Lessons from The Intergalactic Design Guide." Learn how to use words that define, and then bring about, the future we want. Register here. Thursday, September 20, noon.

Mental Health First Aid Training for Faculty and Staff

Student Health and Counseling Services presents Mental Health First Aid training for SVA staff and faculty. This eight-hour training teaches the skills to provide initial support when someone experiences a mental health crisis. More info about the class here. Register here. Friday, September 21, 9:00am – 5:00pm, Soc Doc Theater, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Free Museum Admission with SVA ID

As a benefit to students, faculty and staff, SVA maintains memberships at various museums. With your SVA ID, you can enjoy free access to the Brooklyn Museum, Cooper-Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museum, Museum of Modern Art, MoMA PS1, New Museum of Contemporary Art and Whitney Museum of American Art. For more details, visit MySVA and click "SVA Museum Benefits" in the Quick Links section. Contact Miranda Pierce at mpierce1@sva.edu or ext. 2305 with any questions.
