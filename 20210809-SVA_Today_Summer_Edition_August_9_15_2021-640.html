EVENTS THIS WEEK

Thursday | Advocating for Your Career in the U.S.: Workplace Communication for International Students
This workshop for international students explores the communication style used in the U.S. with a focus on the U.S. work environment, with perspectives from SVA's International Student Office, Career Development and an immigration attorney. Learn more and register here. Thursday, August 12, 10:00 – 11:30am ET.

Thursday | Career Development Virtual Office Hour at Noon
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour this Thursday, August 12, 12:00pm ET, after the Advocating for Your Career in the U.S. workshop. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

Next Week | Internship-for-Credit Information Session for Fall 2021
Learn how you can qualify for the SVA Internship-for-Credit Program this fall. SVA Career Development and the International Student Office will cover the online application system, student enrollment criteria, the responsibilities of both students and employers, how to find and apply for an internship, special requirements for international students, and deadlines and procedures for the fall semester. Create a Video/Animation, Win $500
SVA Theatre seeks a 10 – 20 second pre-roll video (animated or live action) to play before film screenings shown during this September's After School Special 2021: SVA's Alumni Film & Animation Festival. Screenings will be free and open to the public and will feature Q&As with prominent alumni.

Set the video to music that you have created yourself, own the copyright for, have obtained a license for, or is in the public domain, and please note its source when submitting. Download logos for the festival, SVA and SVA Theatre here. Examples of past winning videos can be viewed here and here.

Upload submissions to Vimeo or YouTube, and send an email with a link to the video, your name, graduation year and program major to Adam Natale, director of SVA Theatre, at anatale@sva.edu, by Friday, August 27, 5:00pm ET. Questions may also be directed to Natale. Final video format should be a digital file (e.g., QuickTime ProRes). The winner will receive $500 and an invitation to the festival's VIP reception.

Get the New GoSVA Mobile App
The entirely redesigned GoSVA mobile app is available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and Google Play Store for Android. This new mobile app provides on-the-go access to all SVA essential systems and information for students, faculty, staff and visiting guests. If you have the prior version of the GoSVA app installed on your device please uninstall it before installing this new version. More information and links to the app stores can be found at go.sva.edu. An installation guide is available at technology.sva.edu.
