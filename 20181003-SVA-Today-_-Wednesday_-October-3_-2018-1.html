SVA Today
Wednesday, October 3, 2018

Preview: SVA at Comic-Con

Comic-Con returns to New York tomorrow, and SVA alumni will take part in a number of panel discussions and presentations over the course of the weekend, from talks on diversity and representation to celebrations of famed comics careers.
EVENT

10:00am | Yearbook Photo Shoot

SVA Yearbook is shooting portraits for the 2019 edition. All graduating students are encouraged to dress how they like, stop by and have their photo taken. The yearbook is given to students at commencement for free. 10:00am – 2:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 309A.
PRESS PICK

Nourishing the Senses

Interior Design on Nourishing the Senses: Restaurant Architecture of Bentel & Bentel, a book that highlights two decades of local jobs by BFA Interior Design Chair Carol Bentel and partner Peter Bentel.
NYC PICK

The Best American Comics 2018

Panelists including MFA Visual Narrative faculty member Bill Kartalopoulos, Julia Gfrörer, Julian Glander, Kevin Hooyman and Julia Jacquette talk about this year in comics. Tickets $15 – $25; purchase here. 7:00pm. Strand Bookstore, 828 Broadway.
TAKE NOTE

Curators of the Sharjah Biennial 14: Leaving the Echo Chamber

MA Curatorial Practice hosts a panel featuring presentations and discussions with the Sharjah Biennial 14 curators, Zoe Butt, Omar Kholeif and Claire Tancons, and the Sharjah Art Foundation President and Director Hoor Al Qasimi, followed by a Q&A moderated by faculty member Brian Kuan Wood. Wednesday, October 10, 7:00pm. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

The 20th Animation Show of Shows

BFA Animation, BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects, and MFA Computer Arts present this international festival of the best in animated short films. RSVP here. Monday, October 15, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
