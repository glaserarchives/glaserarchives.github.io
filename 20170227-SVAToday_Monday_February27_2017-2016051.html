TAKE NOTE

Young Joon Kwak and Gordon Hall

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by the artists, who will collaborate on a public discussion in an experimental format, stemming from the artists' shared interest in proposing alternative ways of being bodies in relation to objects with open-ended functionality. Tomorrow, February 28, 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Cliff Kuang: The Untold Story of Design Thinking

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents award-winning design journalist Cliff Kuang, who will read a chapter from his book User Friendly, and reveal a largely forgotten history involving the terrors of Communism and the hippie idealism of 1960s California. RSVP here.Tomorrow, February 28, 6:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

Let’s Talk: Visual Arts and Activism

SVA Alumni Affairs and Development presents a panel discussion with five alumni who are working to inspire social or political change through their art, design or film projects. A reception in the theater lobby will follow the discussion. RSVP required. Tomorrow, February 28, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Seeing the Light

MPS Digital Photography presents this presentation by professional photographer Paul Bernstein, who will raise your ability to see the light, select the correct photographic tools and utilize the proper lighting techniques to create images. Tomorrow, February 28, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Applying for Arts Opportunities

SVA Career Development will cover best practices for researching, assessing and applying for arts opportunities. Wednesday, March 1, 12:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Orientation Leader Application and Information Sessions

Orientation leader applications are now available online. For more information, attend an information session on Tuesday, March 14, 6:30pm, or Wednesday, March 15, 3:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
