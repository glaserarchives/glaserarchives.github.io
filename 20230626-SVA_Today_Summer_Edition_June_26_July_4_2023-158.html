SVA IN THE PRESS

“A Flood, a Boxcutter and a Son’s Pursuit to Keep His Father’s Legacy”

After a warehouse flood, late SVA faculty member Nachume Miller’s (BFA 1975 Fine Arts) final works were recovered by his son, Danny Miller, and compiled into a monograph, Nachume Miller: Behind the Painting. An exhibition of Miller’s work, “Nachume Miller: Suns & Illusions,” is on view at David Benrimon Fine Art through Monday, July 10.

“Oceanus” Turns a Critical Eye on Maritime Industries

Alexis Rockman’s (BFA 1985 Fine Arts) exhibition at Mystic Seaport Museum exploring seafaring history and the underwater worlds it impacts is featured in The Art Newspaper.

Skittles Packaging Redesign for Pride

Zipeng Zhu (BFA 2013 Design) is one of two Brooklyn-based creators who were chosen to redesign Skittles packaging for Pride Month this year.

“Reframing Symbolic Images Through a Feminist Lens”

Artist Emily Weiner (MFA 2011 Fine Arts) talks about her use of symbolism and ornamentation in an interview with A Women’s Thing.

Ten Interior Design Projects by SVA Students

Dezeen features 10 senior thesis interior design projects by 2023 BFA Interior Design: Built Environments graduates, including an aquatic research center designed to raise awareness of freshwater ecosystems and a healing center designed to promote well-being through exposure to white noise.
