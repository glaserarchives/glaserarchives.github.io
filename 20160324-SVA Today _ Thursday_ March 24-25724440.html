SVA Today
Thursday, March 24, 2016
Artist Carrie Mae Weems to Speak at Commencement
SVA is pleased to announce visionary photographer and MFA Art Practice faculty member Carrie Mae Weems as this year's commencement speaker. The convocation will take place on Wednesday, May 18, 10:00am, at Radio City Music Hall.
6:00pm: "Mentors" Reception
BFA Photography and Video presents an exhibition of student work inspired by mentorships with leading arts figures. Curated by BFA Photography and Video Chair Stephen Frailey. Through Friday, April 1, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
6:00pm: "The Never Ending Project 1.0" Reception
BFA Interior Design presents an exhibition about the department's participation in last year's Never Ending Project, a weeklong design challenge that brought together three teams from three different continents. Through Monday, April 4, VCS Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
A still from Taking the Plunge.
The Next Pixar?
MoviePilot hails Taking the Plunge, the thesis project by 2015 BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects alumni Thaddaeus Andreades, Elizabeth Ku-Herrero, Nicholas Manfredi and Marie Raoult, for its Pixar-like quality. Click through to watch the film.
NYC PICKS OF THE DAY
A Democracy of Imagery
An exhibition featuring work by 84 photographers from the 1860s through 2002. Through Saturday, April 30, Howard Greenberg Gallery, 41 East 57th Street.
Shirley Jaffe: "Works on Paper"
An exhibition of recent mixed-media work by the abstract painter. Through Saturday, April 30, Tibor de Nagy Gallery, 724 Fifth Avenue.
TAKE NOTE
Orientation Leader Application
The Student Engagement and Leadership Office is accepting applications from undergraduate students for orientation leader positions. The deadline is Monday, March 28. For questions, contact activities@sva.edu.
Richard Tuschman
MPS Digital Photography presents photographer Richard Tuschman, best known for his “Hopper Meditations" series, discussing his practice and his current project, Once Upon A Time in Kazimierz, an open-ended novella told in photographs. Tuesday, March 29, 7:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
IMAGE OF THE DAY
Visual Opinion, SVA's student-run magazine, is accepting submissions by students of the class of 2016 for its graduation issue. The deadline is Wednesday, March 30.
