SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts July 5 - 10
FEATURES

Before and After SVA: Yasaman Alipour

The 2015 BFA Photography alumnus talks about tapping into her visual heritage, transforming herself creatively, how she landed an art director position at The Brooklyn Rail, and more.

Cover Stories: Rebecca Schiff's The Bed Moved

Janet Hansen (BFA 2007 Graphic Design) and author Rebecca Schiff discuss the cover for Schiff's short story collection.

Maria Paulino Wins Top Boxing Photo Award

The assistant to the CIO in SVA's Administrative and Network Services talks about winning top honors in the Boxing Writers Association of America’s photo contest.

Summer Exhibition Roundup

Read more about current SVA alumni exhibitions on view in NYC, which explore everything from civilization vs. the wild to celestial bodies to the power of paper.
IN THE PRESS

The Surreal Side of Nostalgia

Hi-Fructose on the work of MFA Illustration as Visual Essay student Nicole Rifkin.

The Contradictions of Adolescence

Vice on the work of Cooper Winterson (BFA 2016 Photography and Video).
EVENTS

Lunchtime Lecture: Tom Sachs

MFA Art Practice presents sculptor Tom Sachs discussing his work, which comments on consumer culture. Tuesday, July 5, 12:30pm, 335 West 16th Street, Room 501H

Threaded

An exhibition of work by SVA Summer Residency program alumni Alice Quaresma and Jayoung Yoon. Reception Thursday, July 7, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Tuesday, July 5, through Friday, July 29, SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

And Then...

An exhibition of thesis projects by members of the MFA Visual Narrative class of 2016, curated by faculty members Jonathon Rosen and Ed Valentine. Reception Friday, July 22, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Saturday, July 9, through Tuesday, July 26, SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
