TAKE NOTE

Facade Work Continues at 205, 209 East 23rd Street

The project continues on schedule, weather permitting. Ninety percent of the facade work has been completed for the east side and 80% on the north (back) of the building. Removal of bricks on the west side begins on today, July 5 and will continue for eight days, weather permitting. During this removal period there will be loud construction noise associated with the project Monday through Friday between the hours of 7:30am and 3:30pm with occasional days until 5:30pm and 9:00am to 5:00pm on Saturdays. Periodic updates will be provided in SVA Today in the coming weeks. For questions, please contact Richard Riccio, executive director of Facilities, 212.592.2156.

Gallery Name Changes at 133/141 West 21st Street

The VCS Gallery at 133/141 West 21st Street has been renamed the SVA Flatiron Project Space. The corridor facing 21st Street, formerly called the SVA Flatiron Project Space, is now SVA Flatiron Windows.
