Sunday | SVA @ NYC Pride March
Nearly 100 SVA community members will be participating in the annual NYC Pride March this weekend! Look out for the special SVA Pride banner—created by BFA Design student Morgan Dyer—and show up along the route to celebrate Pride with your fellow students, staff and faculty!

The march begins at 25th Street and Fifth Avenue and proceeds along a route that passes the Stonewall National Monument and the New York City AIDS Memorial. Click here for a march map—and if you can’t make it to the march, follow the SVA Instagram, @svanyc, for coverage. Sunday, June 26, 12:00pm.

Make-up Commencement
The make-up commencement ceremony for the classes of 2020 and 2021 will be held Monday, June 27, 1:00pm, at Radio City Music Hall, with filmmaker, artist and author John Waters as keynote speaker. The event will be live-streamed on sva.edu.

Now Available: The Digital Artforum Archive
The SVA Library now subscribes to the digital Artforum archive, which covers the issues published from its launch in 1962 to 2020. Artforum (later Artforum International) is the leading magazine for coverage of international contemporary art, featuring reporting, reviews and interviews relating to artists, exhibitions, publications and art-world events and trends.

New Kanopy Login Procedure
Kanopy, a streaming service provided by the SVA Library, recently changed its login procedure. If you’ve encountered an error when trying to access it, check out the quick how-to guide with login instructions. Contact the SVA Library with any questions at library@sva.edu.

As a Narcan trainee, you will receive your own Narcan kit. Register here for either date, Tuesday, June 28, 2:00pm, or Wednesday, June 29, 2:00pm.
