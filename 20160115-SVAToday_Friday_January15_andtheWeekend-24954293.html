Take Note
Flu Prevention Tips
Student Health and Counseling Services has created a helpful list of tips for preventing the spread of flu this season. Selected advice includes regular hand washing, cleaning frequently touched surfaces and receiving an annual seasonal flu vaccine (either via shot or nasal spray). For more information, including locations with the vaccine available, visit sva.edu/flu.
New Video Database Trials
The SVA Library now offers trial access to three video streaming databases: Docuseek 2, Infobase Films on Demand and Psychotherapy.net. The videos in these collections cover psychotherapy and counseling, arts and humanities, news and archival footage, documentary film, education and social issues, and more. The trials end on Sunday, February 14. To access the databases, visit library.sva.edu.
