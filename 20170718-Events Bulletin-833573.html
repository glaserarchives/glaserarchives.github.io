MFA Visual Narrative Events
Thesis Artist Talks, Tonight! 7/18
Please join the MFA Visual Narrative program as our thesis students proudly present their eight cutting-edge multi-media storytelling thesis projects. The exhibition, "And Then", reflects the uniquely interdisciplinary nature of this program, where creative writing is taught alongside visual arts to harness the power of story.
Thesis Reception 7/21
After an intense two years of late nights and hard work, it’s time for a party! Please join us on Friday for the MFA Visual Narrative Thesis Reception "And Then . . . " where you can get lost in the stories these students have brought to life through their physical installations.

Print Slam 7/27
As Part of our Open Studios event, our RisoLAB will host an exhibition and sale of work made this past summer semester. This includes students from our CE classes, our Artists-in-Residence and more. Come support these artists and check out the Lab for yourself!

Open Studios 7/27
Work from first and second year students reflects the interdisciplinary nature of our program, including narrative short films, visual maps of The High Line, stop-animation pieces, and more. Since class is only in session at SVA during the summer, this event also provides an unusual opportunity to meet current students and faculty and tour our Risograph facility and studio space.
