10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am ET. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

Every Thursday, 12:30 – 2:00pm ET, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).

Wearing Masks: The Performance of Identity in Contemporary Art
On the occasion of the exhibition Gillian Wearing: Wearing Masks, the Guggenheim presents a virtual panel of artists to discuss how they each explore the central theme of identity performance within their creative practices. Today, 2:00pm ET, online.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Tomorrow | Earth Day Walkout
Join the Indigenous Student Union for an Earth Day school walkout. Students are invited to walk out of class and attend a series of intergenerational Indigenous teach-ins featuring climate activists from Abya Yala (Mexico, Central and South America). Learn about the climate crisis, intergenerational organizing, United Nations Permanent Forum on Indigenous Issues (UNPFII) and how to stay connected and involved within your communities. The teach-in will be held at 209 East 23rd Street in the 3rd floor amphitheater. Open to SVA students, faculty, and alumni only. Visitors are asked to bring their Eventbrite registration with them to the event. Everyone must remain masked, except where otherwise noted. RSVP here. Space is limited. Tomorrow, April 22, 1:00pm. Those who cannot attend in-person, or who are outside the SVA community, are welcome to join the conversation via Zoom. Meeting ID: 819 8917 3695.

Tomorrow | Give and Take with SVA
As part of Earth Week, VASA invites the SVA community to join them for Give and Take with SVA, an opportunity to declutter and drop off any unused art materials at the SVA Student Center. Exchange or give away your art supplies to peers from the hours of 4:00 – 7:00pm. In addition to this event, free bamboo cutlery will also be available at the SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
