New York Film Festival: Free Live Talks

SVA Career Development Director and alumnus Angie Wojak will moderate a panel at the New York Film Festival, "Costume Design and the Creative Process: From Film to Television." Free tickets to this panel and all other NYFF Live talks (schedule here) will be distributed on a first-come, first-served basis starting one hour prior to the talks at the Elinor Bunin Munroe Film Center box office, 144 West 65th Street. Limit one ticket per person. For those unable to attend, video from these events will be available online at filmlinc.org.

MoMA Opening: "Charles White: A Retrospective"

On Tuesday, October 2, at 7:00pm, the Museum of Modern Art celebrates the opening of Charles White's first major museum survey in over 30 years. SVA has received limited invitations to the event for faculty and staff. Each invitation admits two. RSVP is required; do so here, with promo code SVAWHITE and company School of Visual Arts. You will receive a confirmation email. Say your name at check-in for entry for you and one guest. Contact MoMA at corporate_membership@moma.org or 212.708.9840 with any questions. The Museum of Modern Art, 18 West 54th Street.

Register to Vote!

Over the next two weeks, SVA Student Affairs is holding a three-day voter registration drive, as well as hosting a program on the importance of voting and civic engagement. All are encouraged to visit one of the drive locations to ensure that they are registered in time to participate in November's elections. New York State's voter registration deadline is Friday, October 12.

The registration drive dates are Thursday, October 4; Tuesday, October 9; and Friday, October 12. Why Voting Matters, an informal one-hour program, will take place on Thursday, October 4, 6:30pm, in the SVA Student Center.

To register online, click here. And for more information on voter registration, click here.

Thursday, October 4 – Monday, November 19. Viewing hours by appointment; contact arttherapy@sva.edu to schedule. Reception: Thursday, October 11, 5:00 – 8:00pm. MPS Art Therapy Gallery Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.
