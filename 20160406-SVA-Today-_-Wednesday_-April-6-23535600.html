Announcing the Dusty Film and Animation Festival 2016 Alumni Honorees
Animator and historian Tom Sito (BFA 1977 Animation) and Academy Award-winning production sound mixer Mark Ulano (1975 Film and Video) will be honored at the 27th annual Dusty Film & Animation Festival with Outstanding Alumni Awards. The festival will take place Saturday, May 7, through Tuesday, May 10, at the SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
6:00pm: PhotoViz with Nicholas Felton
MFA Interaction Design presents data visualization artist Nicholas Felton discussing PhotoViz and the crossover between photography and data visualization. 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

Why Portraiture?
A lecture on the role portraits have in shaping how Western individuals and societies see themselves, from antiquity to today. Admission is free, but seating is first-come, first-served. 6:00pm, The Frick Collection, 1 East 70th Street.

All-Staff Art Show Call for Submissions
The fourth annual All-Staff Art Show is accepting submissions for this summer's exhibition, which opens on Monday, July 18. All administrative staff are eligible to submit one work for the show. Video should be less than two minutes long and all other work should not exceed 24 x 24" on the wall or floor. To apply, click here. The deadline is Wednesday, June 1.
