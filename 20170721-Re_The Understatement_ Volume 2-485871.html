Hello,


We would like to share with you an early preview of The Understatement, Volume 2, which we hope will continue the conversation. You’ll find articles from the Measured speakers, an overview of the templates they used, and a bit about the progress we’ve made on other fronts.


We’ve been developing plans to create The Measured Manual, an online resource of tools, information and cases for measuring the effectiveness of social design, and are talking to lots of people to understand how to make it most useful. If you’d like to weigh in on plans, we’d love to hear from you. Just email us here and we’ll make a time to talk.


Thanks again for your interest and we look forward to hearing from you.
