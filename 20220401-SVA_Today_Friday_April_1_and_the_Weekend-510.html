Events

1:00pm | Business Club Networking

Join the SVA Business Club for an afternoon of networking. It's a great opportunity for graduating students—come by and make connections for future careers, projects and more. Today, 1:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:30pm | You Can Sit With Us, Let’s Chat Jobs
BFA Advertising and BFA Design’s Fab Friday series wraps up for this academic year with Sally Chung, BFA Design faculty member and hiring manager at BCG Digital Ventures. Chung will be joined by Ciara Cornette, a recruiter for top-notch talent in the world of big name companies with clients like Sweetgreen, Warby Parker and Milk Bar. Together, they will have a frank discussion on what it takes to get hired, from an HR point of view. Tonight, 6:30pm ET, on Zoom.

NYC PICKS

Closer-Look Tour at the American Folk Art Museum
The American Folk Art Museum invites guests to an hour-long tour of “MULTITUDES,” led by gallery guides who will take participants through the current exhibition to experience different perspectives of the works on view. Free with registration. Today, 1:00pm. American Folk Art Museum, 2 Lincoln Square.

2022 MoCCA Arts Festival
New York’s largest comics and cartoon festival returns this weekend and MFA Visual Narrative and the RisoLAB will be there at Table A94. Student and alumni work will be available for purchase and Riso demos will take place throughout the weekend by artists in residence. Saturday, April 2 – Sunday, April 3. Admission is $18 per day, $25 for weekend tickets. Metropolitan Pavilion, 125 West 18th Street.

SVA Destinations: Barbuda
This June, experience a Caribbean paradise that few get to ever see. SVA Destinations Barbuda is a unique opportunity to experience the untouched natural beauty and resources of the island of Barbuda. Meet with the program leader Mohammid Walbrook (BFA 2016 Photography and Video) for this information session, including a guided Q&A. Tuesday, April 12, 6:00pm ET, on Zoom. Register here. Questions? Email Stefanie Joshua, program coordinator, at sjoshua@sva.edu.
