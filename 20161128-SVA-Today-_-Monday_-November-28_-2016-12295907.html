SVA Today
Monday, November 28, 2016

How Kanye West's Yeezus Is Influenced by Basquiat

A Q&A with MFA Fine Arts faculty member Genevieve Hyacinthe, who tomorrow will give a talk about the influence of prominent 1980s artist Jean-Michel Basquiat on Kanye's 2013 album Yeezus and its surrounding aesthetics.
EVENT

"SVA x Skowhegan"

An exhibition of work by 10 artists affiliated with both SVA and the Skowhegan School of Painting and Sculpture in Madison, Maine. Curated by Lauren Haynes, curator, contemporary art at Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art. Reception Thursday, December 8, 6:00 - 8:00pm; catalog launch with curator remarks Tuesday, January 17, 2017, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Through February 4, 2017. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
PRESS PICK
Artwork by Tom Koken, via Artdaily.

Tom Koken's Light-Inspired Garment District Exhibition

Artdaily on an exhibition of work by the BFA 1977 Media Arts graduate, on view at the Kaufman Arcade building, 132 West 36th Street, through January 6, 2017, as part of the Garment District Space for Public Art program.
NYC PICKS

NYBG Holiday Train Show

Model trains travel a half-mile of track in a display featuring recreations of more than 150 popular landmarks, such as the Statue of Liberty and the Brooklyn Bridge. Ticket prices vary. Through January 16, 2017. New York Botanical Garden, Enid A. Haupt Conservatory, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.

"Max Beckmann in New York"

A selection of works the artist created about or while living in New York City. Suggested admission. Through February 20, 2017. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Avenue.
