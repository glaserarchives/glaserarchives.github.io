SVA Today
Wednesday, May 18, 2016
2016 Commencement Speaker Carrie Mae Weems. Photograph by Jerry Klineberg, 2012.
Congratulations to the Class of 2016!
Visionary photographer, MacArthur Fellow and faculty member Carrie Mae Weems will deliver the keynote address at SVA’s 2016 commencement exercises, which begin today at 10:00am at the legendary Radio City Music Hall in New York City (watch the live stream here). SVA President David Rhodes will confer BFA, MA, MAT, MFA and MPS degrees on more than 1,100 degree candidates at the ceremony. #SVAcommencement2016
