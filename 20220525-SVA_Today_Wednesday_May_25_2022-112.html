NYC PICKS

Mobilizing Chinatown: Past and Present on Film
An evening of documentary film and discussion centering stories of repression, mobilization and resilience in Chinatown. Tickets: $15. Tonight, 6:30pm. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue at 103rd Street.

Enacting Central Brooklyn: A Theatrical Sharing of Community and History
A theatrical experience that gives voice to the lives of Central Brooklynites, past and present, and reflects on childhood, injustice, memory, police brutality, protection, generosity and the meaning of community. Tonight, 7:30pm. Center for Brooklyn History, 128 Pierrepont Street, Brooklyn.
