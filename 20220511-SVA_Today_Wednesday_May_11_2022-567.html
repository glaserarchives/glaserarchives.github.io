Commencement Is Coming!
The commencement ceremony for the class of 2022 will be held Sunday, May 22, 1:00pm, at Radio City Music Hall, with writer Roxane Gay as the keynote speaker. The event will be live-streamed on sva.edu and Facebook Live.
