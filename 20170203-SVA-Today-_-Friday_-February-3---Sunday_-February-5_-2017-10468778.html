SVA Today
Friday, February 3 - Sunday, February 5, 2017

MFA Illustration Double Feature

This weekend, MFA Illustration as Visual Essay presents "A Report to the Academy," an exhibition of student work based on Franz Kafka’s short story, and "War x Artifice," a solo exhibition of work by faculty member and department alumnus Riccardo Vecchio. Tomorrow, February 4, through Thursday, March 2. Reception: Monday, February 6, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
EVENTS

10:00am | SVA Study Abroad Information Session

Learn about all of SVA’s study abroad options, including International Exchange, SVA in Rome and SVA Destinations. Dr. Rebecca Ross, BA Graphic Design course leader of partner institution Central Saint Martins in London, will be there to discuss the institution and answer questions. 10:00am. 136 West 21st Street, Room 410F.

3:00pm | Agar Art Workshop and Contest

BFA Fine Arts presents this two-part, hands-on workshop where you will create your own work of agar art using microbes as "paint" and agar as a "canvas." Photos of the agar art created in this workshop are eligible for the American Society of Microbiology contest. Register here. Part 1, Today, 3:00 – 6:00pm; Part 2, Sunday, February 5, 3:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Nature and Technology Lab, 335 West 16th Street.

You Can't Stop the Beat

TAKE NOTE

Yoga and Meditation for Wellness and Creativity

MPS Art Therapy and SVA's Student Health and Counseling Services present this two-part workshop, which will introduce students to basic yoga and meditation skills to promote well-being and foster creativity. Yoga mats will be available. Participants will receive free passes to Laughing Lotus yoga studio. Open to students only. RSVP to Navah Steiner at nsteiner@sva.edu. Friday, February 17, and Friday, March 24, 3:15 – 4:45pm. 132 West 21st Street, 5th-floor project space.
