SVA Today
Wednesday, December 15, 2021

Remembering Milton Glaser: The Greatest Gems
From the fall edition of The Glaser Gazette, a special publication celebrating the legacy of the late designer and SVA faculty member Milton Glaser, archivist Beth Kleber highlights some little-known works from the Milton Glaser Design Study Center and Archives. The commemorative exhibition “SVA ❤️ Milton” is now on view at the SVA Gramercy Gallery and SVA Flatiron Project Space and Windows.
