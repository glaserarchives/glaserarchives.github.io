NYC PICK

Innovators in Exile: Concert

An evening of music written by composers who were exiled from Austria for their Jewish identity or connection to modernism after founding the International Society for Contemporary Music during World War II. Free. Tonight, April 20, 6:30 – 8:00pm. Center for Jewish History, 15 West 16th Street.

Re-Fest

An annual festival that brings artists, activists, scientists and technologists together to explore our role in reshaping the future through a group exhibition, performances and conversations that explore the question, “How can we prioritize ancestral knowledge and forgotten technologies in order to renew our relationship to creation and progress?” Through Saturday, April 29, with a free kick-off event happening tonight, Tonight, April 20, 6:30 – 9:00pm, La MaMa Galleria, 47 Great Jones Street.

Next Week | The Algorithmic State: Artificial Aesthetics with Lev Manovich

MA Curatorial Practice presents artist and writer Lev Manovich to discuss AI media transformation, drawing on the theoretical analysis being developed in his new book Artificial Aesthetics: Critical Guide to AI, Media, and Design, as well as his experience as an artist working with computers since 1984. Thursday, April 27, 12:00 – 1:00pm, online.
