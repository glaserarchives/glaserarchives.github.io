SVA Library Food for Fines

SVA Library is offering $2 off library fines for every canned or non-perishable food item brought in through Monday, May 1. All donations will be given to City Harvest. No ramen noodles or frozen food. SVA Library, 380 Second Avenue. 2nd floor.

MFA Design for Social Innovation Thesis Presentations

Members of MFA Design for Social Innovation's class of 2017 will present their thesis projects on the critical topics of today, including healthcare, climate change, education, language, elder care and more. RSVP here. Monday, May 1, 12:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. A reception will follow from 4:30 – 6:00pm at 136 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

MFA Art Writing Thesis Reading
