SVA Today | Your guide to everything
Tuesday, October 27, 2015
Photos by Yulia Gorbachenko (MPS 2010 Digital Photography) for Harper's Bazaar.
The Hot Five
October's five most talked about projects from SVA Portfolios include an illustrated apocalypse, Sunday vibes and fashion photography.
2:00pm: Smart Classroom Training
SVA Audio Visual Services presents an information session on how to use the College's "smart" classroom technology. Topics covered include classroom equipment, trouble-shooting techniques and technical support information. RSVP to rbarton@sva.edu. 136 West 21st Street, Room 830F.
6:00pm: Matthew Deleget
The artist, curator, writer and director of the Minus Space gallery in Brooklyn, talks about his work. Deleget’s work was recently featured in the 2014 Whitney Biennial, and he has shown his work in numerous international solo and group exhibitions. Presented by MFA Fine Arts. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
6:15pm: Song of Lahore
SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a screening of Academy Award-winner Sharmeen Obaid-Chinoy's Song of Lahore, a feature-length documentary that follows the members of Pakistan’s Sachal Jazz Ensemble, a group of master musicians who find international recognition after decades of being forced underground by dictators and religious extremists. Producer/director Andy Schocken will answer questions following the screening. Free with SVA ID (one guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
6:30pm: Marco Ferrari
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents visual researcher, spatial designer and Studio Folder co-founder Marco Ferrari discussing his recent work, including the exhibition "Italian Limes," which featured at the 2014 Venice Architecture Biennal. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
Press pick of the day
Alum Wins Best Composer of the Year Award
Deadline reports that Michael Giacchino (BFA 1990 Film and Video) was named Best Composer of the Year at this year's World Sound Track Awards for his work on Jurassic World, Inside Out and Tomorrowland.
NYC pick of the day
NewFest
The final day of screenings for NewFest, the annual LGBT film festival. Click through for a full schedule of screenings. Ticket prices and locations vary.
Take Note
Michael Bierut Tour
Designer, critic and educator Michael Bierut leads a tour of his retrospective exhibition "The Masters Series: Michael Bierut." Wednesday, October 28, 5:00pm, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
Art of the Prank
BFA Advertising and BFA Design present a special screening of Art of the Prank, a new documentary about the life of artist and legendary prankster Joey Skaggs (BFA 1982 Media Arts), who turned the media hoax into an art form. Skaggs and director Andrea Marini will answer questions following the screening. Wednesday, November 4, 7:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
Image of the day
An illustration from Lauren Redniss' (MFA 2000 Illustration as Visual Essay) Thunder & Lightning, which chronicles the diversity of the earth's weather patterns and was released today by Random House.
