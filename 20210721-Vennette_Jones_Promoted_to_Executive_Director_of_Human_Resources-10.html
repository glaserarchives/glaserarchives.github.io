Subject: Vennette Jones Promoted to Executive Director of Human Resources
From: SVA Info
Date: 7/21/21, 10:27 AM
To: allfaculty@sva.edu, allstaff@sva.edu



I am very pleased to announce the much-deserved promotion of Vennette Jones to the position of executive director of Human Resources, effective immediately.

 

Vennette began her career at SVA a little more than 15 years ago when she was hired as employment manager in May 2006. She was promoted to associate director in November 2007 and then to director in May 2013.

 

Vennette serves on the Emergency Management Committee, the Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Task Force, and now, on the Executive Directors’ Committee.

 

She has demonstrated the merits of this promotion through her tireless work and leadership abilities, which have been particularly evident during the pandemic. Vennette has streamlined many processes in HR through her strong knowledge and applications of technologies, which she will continue to apply in her new role. She will also bring a new level of energy and critical thinking to the position.

 

Vennette has a background in dance—she even danced at the Apollo Theater. She has a nine-year-old daughter named Skylar, who is also creative with a passion for art and music. Vennette is an activist holding strong beliefs in social justice and criminal justice reform.

 

Please join me in congratulating her.

 

After more than 19 years of service at SVA, Frank Agosta has decided to retire. We thank him for his tremendous contributions and efforts during his time at the College. We wish him well in the future.

 

 

Gary Shillet

CFO
