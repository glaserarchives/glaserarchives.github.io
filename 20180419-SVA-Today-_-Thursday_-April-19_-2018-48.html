SVA in Comic Book History

Vulture's list of the 100 most influential pages in comic book history includes many by SVA faculty and alumni, including Steve Ditko, Will Eisner, Klaus Janson, Harvey Kurtzman, Gary Panter, Joe Sinnott, Raina Telgemeier and Wally Wood.

6:00pm | Design for All

MFA Interaction Design presents Gabi Steele, a member of Open Style Lab and a data visualization engineer at WeWork, who will explore how creative professionals design for a broad audience and the implications of doing so. RSVP here. 6:00 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

6:00pm | MA Curatorial Practice Major Year-End Exhibition Reception

MA Curatorial Practice presents thesis exhibitions by its graduating fellows. Click here for a list of exhibitions. 6:00 – 9:00pm. Through Friday, May 4. Pfizer Building, 630 Flushing Avenue, Brooklyn.

Fran Leadon: Broadway

Architect and author Fran Leadon and New Yorker journalist and author Ben Gibberd discuss Leadon's new book Broadway: A History of New York City in Thirteen Miles. Tickets $5 – $35; purchase here. 7:30pm. Strand Bookstore, 828 Broadway.

American Landscape

This exhibition features sculpture, photography, painting and installation by artists Teresita Fernández, Catherine Opie, Tim Rollins & K.O.S. and Nari Ward. Through Saturday, May 5. Lehmann Maupin, 536 West 22nd Street.
