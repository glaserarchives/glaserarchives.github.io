ANNOUNCEMENTS

Submissions Wanted: All-Staff Meeting “Hot Rods and Clunkers” Pre-Show

There’s nothing finer than the experience of hitting the open road in your first car. Whether that car was brand new or a lemon, it still gave you newfound freedoms. For this year’s All-Staff Meeting pre-show, we would like to highlight your first automobile. Please send us the make, model, color of your first car and one memory about it. Was it a fast car or an old jalopy? Did it spend more time at the garage than on the road? We want to hear all about it.


Send your submissions to dhalm@sva.edu by Friday, August 4. Thank you in advance for participating.
