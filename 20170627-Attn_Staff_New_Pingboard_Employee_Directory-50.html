Human Resources and Administrative & Network Services is pleased to announce that SVA has partnered with Pingboard for our new employee directory!

Pingboard makes it easy to know who's who, who's where, and who does what.
  • Put a face to a name with the searchable directory
  • Access your team's contact info wherever you are with the iPhone and Android apps
  • Who knows your coworkers best? Play the fun Who's Who game on your phone to find out
  • Review a full listing of SVA staff and easily browse staff by location and department
You can access Pingboard in several convenient ways:
  • Via web browser go to directly to https://sva.pingboard.com, click 'Sign in with Google' and enter your MySVA credentials
  • From within SVA Gmail (webmail.sva.edu) click in the app grid on the upper right, click More, and select Pingboard 
  • From MySVA (my.sva.edu) click Employee Directory on the header
  • Download the Pingboard mobile app to your mobile device, enter your @sva.edu email address, click 'Sign in with Google' and enter your MySVA credentials if prompted
Additional features include:
  • After login, you can edit your Pingboard profile to link to other social profiles such as LinkedIn/Twitter/Facebook and choose which photo to use on your Pingboard profile
  • Easily email or call an employee from a staff member's profile card
  • Use the 'Ping' feature under 'three dots icon' to quickly email an employee without needing to launch an email client
