Welcome to the SVA Libraries

EVENTS

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Associate Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).

6:30pm | Visual Narrative Information Session

SVA Continuing Education presents an information session for the CE Visual Narrative program featuring information on visual narrative, Risograph and self-publishing. Tonight, September 8, 6:30 – 8:00pm, online.

Ongoing | “The Masters Series: Lynsey Addario”

An exhibition of work by Lynsey Addario, acclaimed photojournalist, MacArthur Genius Grant and Pulitzer Prize recipient, as part of the 32nd annual Masters Series Award and Exhibition. Curated by Maya Benton and Perri Hofmann. Through Saturday, October 29. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Ongoing | “The Health Poster Project”

An exhibition of posters by 24 students from BFA Illustration, MFA Illustration as Visual Essay and the 2022 Summer Illustration Residency, curated by BFA Comics and BFA Illustration Chair Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Through Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “MPS Fashion Photography Class of 2022”

MPS Fashion Photography presents an exhibition of works by the class of 2022. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “Dwelling in Motion”

A juried exhibition of works that analyze the constantly growing relationship between habitat and inhabitant. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Ongoing | “A Sticker History Exhibition”

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents “A Sticker History Exhibition: A Snapshot from 100 Years of Design Told Through Adhesive Materials,” an exhibition of stickers by various cultural creators from diverse backgrounds and eras curated by DB Burkeman and Stuck-Up. Through Thursday, September 29. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.


SVA IN THE PRESS

Thomas Holton

“Somewhere Between Portraiture and Story Telling”

Thomas Holton (MFA 2005 Photography, Video and Related Media) is interviewed about his work, inspiration and photography series, “The Lams of Ludlow Street,” for Lenscratch.

NYC PICK

New York Indie Shorts Awards

The New York Indie Shorts Awards return this year with more than 100 international films, networking opportunities, master classes, panels and Q&As. Receive a free ticket for a screening block by registering at FilmFreeway with the code NYCSTU22. Today, September 8, 1:00pm – Saturday, September 10, 10:00pm. Cinépolis Chelsea, 260 West 23rd Street.


ANNOUNCEMENTS

Saturday | Barbarian Screening

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Barbarian, starring Justin Long and Bill Skarsgard and directed by Zach Cregger (BFA 2004 Computer Art). Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Visit svatheatre.com for SVA Theatre's current COVID-19 guidelines, including information on masks and vaccination requirements. Saturday, September 10, 7:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.


Next Week | Mid-Autumn Festival Gathering 2022

Join the International Student Office, SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and the Chinese Student Organization for a Mid-Autumn Festival celebration gathering with dumplings, noodles and mooncake. Open to everyone in the SVA community. RSVP required; register here. Thursday, September 15, 6:00 – 7:30pm. 24th Street Residence, 340 East 24th Street, terrace.


Next Week | Thesis Screening 2022

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents its 2022 thesis screening, featuring lens- and screen-based media by five alumni from the class of 2022. Tuesday, September 13, 6:30 – 9:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
