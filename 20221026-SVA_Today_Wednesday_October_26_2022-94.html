Has War Changed, or Only War Photography?

The New York Times examines “The Masters Series: Lynsey Addario” alongside an exhibition of Robert Capa’s photography documenting the Spanish Civil War to show how our images of battle lost their aura of nobility.

Call for Submissions: “Mixed Bag” Zine Exhibition

SVA Library West is seeking zine submissions for its debut exhibition, “Mixed Bag.” SVA students are invited to submit a unique zine by Tuesday, November 1, via the online form here.
