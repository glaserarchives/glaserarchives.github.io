Save the Date: SVA Commencement 2023

SVA’s 48th annual commencement exercises will be held on Sunday, May 21, 1:00pm, at Radio City Music Hall. Stay tuned for updates!

Wednesday | The Inaugural Ralph Caplan Memorial Lecture

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents a program of presentations in memory of faculty member Ralph Caplan (1925 – 2020), including a keynote lecture, “TMI: The Purpose and Pleasure of the Personal,” by writer and design historian Eva Hagberg, and remarks by designers Ayse Birsel and Leah Caplan. The program will be followed by a reception, 6:00 – 7:00pm. Wednesday, December 14, 4:30 – 6:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Thursday | La Casa de Abuelita

La Bodega invites everyone to “Casa de Abuelita,” an evening of dominoes and coffee—hasta entonces y mucha suerte en los últimos exámenes del semester! Thursday, December 15, 6:30 – 8:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Thursday | VASA Town Hall: Semester Recap Edition

Connect with VASA officers and hear about what students did this semester, what to keep an eye out for next semester and get the answers to any questions you may have. RSVP and submit questions here by end-of-day today. Thursday, December 15, 1:30pm, via Instagram Live.

Thursday | DIY Stress Ball and Coffee To-Go

Join Holly Fu and Rebecca Amoah from SVA Student Health and Counseling Services for a cup of coffee and make your own custom stress ball to de-stress for finals. Thursday, December 15, 2:00 – 4:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Faculty: Book a New Museum Tour for Your Class

For the spring and summer 2023 semesters, SVA’s New Museum University Level membership allows select opportunities for free tours. There is one docent-led tour and two self-guided tours available for faculty to use for their classes. Please contact Miranda Pierce, mpierce1@sva.edu, for details and scheduling.
