3:00pm | Internship-for-Credit Information Session for Spring 2023

Learn how you can qualify and apply for the spring 2023 Internship-for-Credit Program. This overview will cover the online application system (Symplicity) in detail as well as student enrollment criteria, the responsibilities of both students and employers, how to find and apply for an internship, deadlines and procedures for the spring semester and the special requirements for international students. Attendance is strongly recommended for first-time applicants and required. If you are an international student, attendance at this session is a requirement before submitting your application. #SVAwesome

The late artist and educator Marshall Arisman founded the celebrated MFA Illustration as Visual Essay graduate program at SVA and served as its chair for 38 years. His fondest wish was to be able to accept the many gifted artists who apply but are unable to afford the tuition. The Marshall Arisman Scholarship Fund will make it possible for them to further their artistic futures. If you are interested in donating to the fund, a check may be mailed to Jeffrey Nesin, Visual Arts Foundation, 209 East 23rd Street, New York, NY, 10010, with a notation for the Marshall Arisman Scholarship Fund.
