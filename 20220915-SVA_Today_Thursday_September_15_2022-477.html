New Resource Trial: Fashion and Race

The SVA Library has begun a trial of the Fashion and Race Database that runs through Wednesday, October 12. Containing original essays, profiles and object analyses primarily authored by racialized and minoritized scholars and writers, the Fashion and Race Database provides a roadmap for lasting change in the fashion system, offering lessons and resources that diversify how we understand fashion. The database is an invaluable platform for those who agree that it’s time to challenge dominant narratives and harmful practices in fashion.
