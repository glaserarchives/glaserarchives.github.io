Events

2:00pm | Blizzard Entertainment Virtual Presentation and Art Contest
Learn more about the gaming industry and how your talents might fit in with SVA Career Development at a virtual industry visit with Blizzard Entertainment. Hear what it's like working on the World of Warcraft team and learn more about their 11th Annual World of Warcraft Student Art Contest. Register first on Eventbrite and then on the Blizzard Entertainment website. 2:00pm ET, online.

3:00pm | Come to VASA Fest
Interested in becoming a member of the VASA, starting a club or getting to know more about the student government? Drop by VASA Fest. There will be a clubs info session and a VASA elections info session for those interested in running for student government. Today, 3:00pm – 4:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

4:30pm | MFA Social Documentary Film In-Person Showcase at DOC NYC
SVA MFA Social Documentary Film's annual showcase of student work. "DOC NYC U: SVA" includes six new films from current students and recent graduates. The screening is a part of DOC NYC, America's largest documentary festival. This in-person screening will be followed by a Q&A with several members of the filmmaking teams. Today, 4:30pm. Cinépolis Chelsea, 260 West 23rd Street. Purchase tickets here.

6:00pm | MFA DSI Open House
MFA Design for Social Innovation offers students the unique opportunity to study design practices that directly respond to complex, global challenges. Join this in-person open house to connect with amazing and diverse program faculty, students and alumni, learn about their exciting work and see what an MFA in DSI can do for you. Tonight, 6:00 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 5th floor. RSVP here.

Last Chance: Adoration
Today is the last day to catch BFA Visual & Critical Studies’ “Adoration,” an exhibition of paintings and drawings by John-Michael Byrd and Wayde McIntosh, curated by the artists. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Saturday | MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Info Session
Learn about the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program, requirements and courses from faculty, staff and alumni at this virtual event. Register here for a Zoom link. Tomorrow, November 13, 10:00am ET.

Saturday | MFA Social Documentary Film Info Session
Interested in human stories? Connected to social justice issues? Explore graduate study in SVA’s MFA Social Documentary Film program! Attend this department information session to learn more about the program and its facilities, and to meet department chair Maro Chermayeff, faculty and alumni. Tomorrow, November 13, 11:00am – 1:00pm. SocDoc Department Theater, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Monday, November 15 | Faculty Book Club: Who Gets to Tell Whose Story?
President, founder, and lead artist of RAE Comics T.J. Sterling will be joining us as co-facilitator with MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox (MFA 2002 Illustration as Visual Essay). We will be discussing the short documentary Drawn Together: Comics, Diversity, and Stereotypes and the comic Your Black Friend, by Ben Passmore. Monday, November 15, 4:30pm ET, on Zoom.

Friday, November 19 | SVA Language Exchange Social
Come and join the first SVA Language Exchange Social on Zoom, Friday, November 19, 2:00pm ET! Holly from Student Health and Counseling Services and Will from the International Student Office will host an hour of fun activities and opportunities to connect with others through language learning and speaking. Reservations required; register here.

Friday, November 19 | WSVA Unplugged
SVA's acoustic live talent show is back and in person! Save the date for Friday, November 19, 7:00pm, in the SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Got songs to sing? Jokes to share? Musicians, comedians and performers heed our call and join us at this open mic event. Sign up to perform by Tuesday, November 16, 12:00am ET.

Apply to Be a Visual Opinion Intern
Visual Opinion is looking for interns! Send your resume, portfolio/website and a statement of interest to vo@sva.edu with the subject line “VO Intern Application.” Deadline to apply is Tuesday, November 30, 9:00am ET. Applicants must be located within the United States, be a full time matriculated student, have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be able to work in-office hours.

Commencement 2022 Is In-Person
SVA will conduct its commencement ceremony for the Class of 2022 in person at Radio City Music Hall, Sunday, May 22, 2022, 1:00pm ET. The event will be live-streamed onsva.edu, as well as on Facebook Live. A recap of last year's event, broken out by department, is available on YouTube.

Saturday | Go Through the Darkness at DOC NYCat DOC NYC
DOC NYC is screening a world premiere of Go Through The Darkness, a documentary by Yunhong Pu (MFA 2019 Social Documentary Film) about a blind boy named Guanglin and his journey through social and economic hardship as a highly skilled player at the ancient board game Go. Tomorrow, 7:10pm. Cinépolis Chelsea, 260 West 23rd Street.
