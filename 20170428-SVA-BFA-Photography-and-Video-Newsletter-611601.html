Gloria Steinem to be Keynote Speaker at SVA's Annual Commencement

Gloria Steinem, writer, lecturer, political activist, and feminist organizer, will be the keynote speaker at the college's 41st annual commencement. The ceremony will be held on Tuesday, May 9, 2017, at 1PM at Radio City Music Hall, located at 1260 Avenue of the Americas, New York. The event is ticketed and open to candidates and invited guests only; it will be streamed live at 
www.sva.edu/commencement.

Signe Pierce Featured on Playboy.com for Her Work 

Described as a mix of Kardashians and performance art "soaked in neon," alum Signe Pierce (2011) is one of Playboy's "The New Creatives," featured with an interview and images of her work.
Faculty Member Allen Frame Wins Rome Prize 

Influential faculty member and artist Allen Frame has won the prestigious Rome Prize for 2017-2018. The award, given to five individuals in the visual arts category, involves a residency at the American Academy of the Arts in Rome, and a generous stipend.
Red Hook Labs Open Call for Labs New Artists Exhibition

Red Hook Labs is holding an open call for the Labs New Artists exhibition, which will run from July 12 to July 23, 2017. Submit up to 20 photographs of new work. Deadline is June 1, 2017.

The New York Times covers artist Erwin Wurm's current exhibit at Lehmann Maupin gallery. Ethics demonstrated in geometrical order is on view until May 26, 2017.
Cindy Sherman's Works from 1981-2011 on View at Mnuchin Gallery

An exhibition of works by photographer Cindy Sherman spanning 30 years and focusing on three of her most acclaimed series is on view through June 10, 2017.

Remembering Faculty Member Jenny Williams

Beloved faculty member Jenny Wiiliams has died after a short illness. Since 1995, Jenny has taught a weekly critique class, and we have been honored by her insight and keen intelligence and inspired by her commitment and generosity. She had a vivid presence in our lives and will remain in our hearts. A memorial service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 St. on Sunday, May 7 at 2PM.

FADER interviews alum Kathy Shorr (1988) on her new monograph of gun violence survivors in the U.S. This photo series encompasses 100 victims, ages 8 to 80 years old.

Staff Introduction: Jiamei Liu

Jiamei Liu is the newest Photographic Studio Technician at the Hub. She is originally from Sichuan, China and has been studying and living in New York City for five years. She received her BFA in Photography and Video at The School of Visual Arts and is now working as a freelance photographer and videographer. Her work pursues the attachment between inner peace with the reality of the world.
