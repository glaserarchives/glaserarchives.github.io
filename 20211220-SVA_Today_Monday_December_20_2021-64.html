SVA IN THE PRESS

MPS Directing Faculty Member Christina Kallas’s Paris Is in Harlem to Premiere at Slamdance
MPS Directing faculty member Christina Kallas’s Paris Is in Harlem, a film about a shooting in a historic Harlem jazz bar, is one of 16 features that have been selected for the 2022 Slamdance International Film Festival Competition, from a pool of over 1,500 submissions. The festival will run in Park City, Utah, from Thursday, January 20, through Sunday, January 23.

Bronx Zoo Holiday Lights
