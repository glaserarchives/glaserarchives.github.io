SVA Highlights from Miami Art Week

A rundown of the College's presence, and its alumni's achievements, at this year's Miami art fairs.
EVENTS

Noon | MFA Computer Art Open Studios 2016

Noon - 7:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, 12th floor.

5:30pm | Holiday Bazaar

VASA's annual event features student work, zines, crafts, food and more. 5:30 - 9:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

7:00pm | TINY: The Life of Erin Blackwell Screening and Q&A with Director Martin Bell

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a screening of this documentary film, directed by Mary Ellen Mark and Martin Bell, followed by a Q&A with Bell. 7:00 - 9:00pm; RSVP here. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

Saturday | “NEP 2.0”

BFA Interior Design presents “The Never Ending Project (NEP) 2.0.,” an exhibition that brings together students of three institutions, the School of Visual Arts, Penninghen, Paris, and The American University of the Emirates, Dubai, to produce a collaborative project by meeting virtually and working around the clock, benefiting from the teams' different time zones. Tomorrow, December 10 - Thursday, January 12. Reception: Tuesday, December 13, 6:00 - 8:00pm. SVA Flatiron Projects Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Saturday | “On Sustainability”

BFA Interior Design presents an exhibition of student work focusing on the pillars of sustainability: environment, economics and equity. Tomorrow, December 10 - Thursday, January 12. Reception: Tuesday, December 13, 6:00 - 8:00pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.
PRESS PICK

Food Becomes Fashion

ACurator on "Our Appetite for Trends," a new photo series by Evelina Reinhart (MPS 2014 Digital Photography), which is part of "The Fun Food Show," now on view at Gallery 128, 128 Rivington Street.
NYC PICKS

New York Motorcycle Show

A full weekend of exhibitions, speakers, shows and more. Tickets start at $20; purchase here. Today, December 9, through Sunday, December 11. Jacob K. Javits Convention Center, 655 West 34th Street.

Artist’s Eye: Betty Tompkins on Marilyn Minter

Artist Betty Tompkins discusses MFA Fine Arts faculty member Marilyn Minter's current retrospective exhibition, “Marilyn Minter: Pretty/Dirty.” Free for SVA students, faculty and staff; general public suggested admission. Saturday, December 10, 2:00pm. Brooklyn Museum, 200 Eastern Parkway, Brooklyn.

Merry Tuba Christmas

Hundreds of tuba players gather on the Rockefeller Center ice-skating rink to lead a holiday singalong. Sunday, December 11, 3:30pm. Rockefeller Center, 49th and 50th Streets between Fifth and Sixth Avenues.
TAKE NOTE

No Water at 380 Second Avenue Tomorrow Morning, December 10

The management of 380 Second Avenue will be performing the cleaning and sanitizing of the building's water tank on Saturday, December 10, 7:00am - noon. During this time there will be no water in restrooms, drinking fountains, janitor sinks, etc. Water service will be restored shortly after noon.

BFA Fine Arts Open Studios

Monday, December 12, 6:00 - 9:00pm. 335 West 16th Street.

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue: Silence

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a screening of Martin Scorsese’s latest, starring Andrew Garfield, Adam Driver and Liam Neeson, followed by a Q&A with producer Randall Emmett (BFA 1994 Film and Video). Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Monday, December 12, 6:15pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

SVA in San Francisco Information Session

Learn more about the spring 2017 SVA Destinations program in San Francisco, through which participants visit companies such as Pixar and ILM; network with animators, effects artists and recruiters; and explore job and internship opportunities in the city. Refreshments will be served. Wednesday, December 14, 2:30pm. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 310.

Triple Riso: Dispatches from the RisoLAB

MFA Visual Narrative presents three RisoLAB artists-in-residence, Fawn Krieger, Harvey Redding and Thu Tran, presenting their own work, as well as projects completed during their time at the department's printmaking facility. Register here. Thursday, December 15, 7:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

The Academy of Motion Picture Arts & Sciences presents a screening of Rogue One: A Star Wars Story. Seating is extremely limited. Free with SVA ID (one guest only per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Friday, December 16th, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
