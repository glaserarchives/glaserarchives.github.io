March Exhibitions and Events

A new season is around the corner and SVA’s spring calendar kicks off with the annual BFA Photography and Video “Mentors” show, an exhibition of collaborative work by MPS Art Therapy students, a full-day symposium on AI and more.

EVENTS

9:00am | Curatorial Roundtable: Nirith Nelson

MA Curatorial Practice presents Nirith Nelson, a contemporary art curator, lecturer and the Landeau Family Senior Curator of Contemporary Art at The Israel Museum, Jerusalem. Today, March 8, 9:00 – 10:00am, online.

6:30pm | The Show Must Go On: New Rules of Broadway

Hear from writers, directors, producers and promoters about reopening Broadway after shows were dark for 18 months. Panelists share how they navigated this challenging time and where the opportunities are for young people who would like to break into the Broadway scene. Presented by the Center for Communication. Free and open to the public. Tonight, March 8, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

SVA IN THE PRESS

“Young, Gifted and IcoKnick”

Faculty member Sophia Victor (BFA 2010 Fine Arts) is featured in Forbes for her work in “Young, Gifted and IcoKnick,” a group show highlighting emerging black artists at The Knickerbocker Hotel. On view through Sunday, April 30.
