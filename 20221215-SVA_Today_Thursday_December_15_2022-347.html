#SVAwesome

Each year in its first semester, MPS Directing produces a “learning film” with the help of its faculty and students. A short film script is written, produced, directed, performed, edited and finished by professionals, with each student playing an important role in front of or behind the camera. This year’s film, Goodbye Jonathan’s Soul, is directed by MPS Directing Chair Bob Giraldi, and produced by department staff Cailin McFadden, Alistair McMeekin and faculty member Natasha Soto-Albors. The script is written by screenwriting instructors Natasha Soto-Albors and Jake Williamson. Via MPS Directing.
