EVENTS

2:00pm | Second Annual Apple Box Short Film Festival

MPS Directing presents its thesis film festival. This year, the festival will be broken into two two-hour screening blocks of eight films, with each screening block followed by a Q&A, a screening of the department’s “learning film,” Goodbye Jonathan’s Soul, and an awards presentation. Today, May 19, 2:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Saturday | SVA Premieres

BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects presents a screening of thesis films by the class of 2023. Tomorrow, May 20; doors open at 11:00am, screening starts at 12:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Sunday | Fast X Screening

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Fast X. Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Masks are encouraged, but not required. Sunday, May 21, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

NYC PICKS

Global Mashup: Ireland Meets Mexico

Artists representing elements of Irish and Mexican culture will each perform an individual set, then come together to present a cross-cultural musical performance. Student tickets: $15. Tonight, May 19; dance workshop at 7:00pm, concert at 8:00pm. Flushing Town Hall, 137-35 Northern Boulevard, Queens.

Site: Yizkor/King Manor

A site-specific performance installation created by artist Maya Ciarrocchi (MFA 2002 Computer Arts) that examines individual and collective experiences of displacement and loss through the lens of Yizkor. Free. Tonight, May 19, 8:00 – 9:30pm. King Manor Museum, 150-03 Jamaica Avenue, Queens.

Asian American Pacific Islanders Cultural Heritage Parade

Celebrate Asian American and Pacific Islander culture and heritage at New York City’s second annual AAPI parade. Sunday, May 21, 12:00pm. The parade will take place on Sixth Avenue, proceeding north from West 44th Street to West 55th Street.
