February 6 - February 12, 2017

PoD meets MoMA

Read about MFA Products of Design's one-of-a-kind partnership with MoMA Wholesale, through which students create products for the brand.

From Graffiti to Fashion

A Q&A with street artist Michael De Feo (BFA 1995 Graphic Design), who recently worked with J. Crew to design T-shirts and window displays for the clothing brand.

NYC Exhibition Highlights: TM Davy and Marcia Lippman

A look at two exhibitions of new work by SVA faculty Davy (BFA 2002 Illustration) and Lippman, both on view at Manhattan galleries.
IN THE PRESS

"A Blank Canvas and Free Rein"

The New York Times spotlights SVA's long-running subway poster campaign and the traveling exhibition "Underground Images."

Capturing the Women’s March

Alpha Universe on the striking photos MPS Digital Photography Chair Karin Eismann took at the Washington DC Women’s March in January.

You Can’t Stop the Beat

Artnet News on Kate Gilmore's (MFA 2002 Fine Arts) Beat, a feminist art performance, which is presented along with a series of paintings by Karen Heagle. On view through Sunday, February 19. On Stellar Rays, 213 Bowery.
EVENTS

Gillian Laub

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by the filmmaker and photographer whose most recent project, "Southern Rites," is the result of a decade exploring issues of racism in the American South. Part of the i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tuesday, February 7, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Brand New: Art and Commodity in New York, 1979 – 1989

MA Curatorial Practice presents a panel discussion led by SVA faculty member Gianni Jetzer, with renowned artist Julie Wachtel and Columbia University PhD candidate Leah Pires. RSVP here. Thursday, February 9, 6:30pm. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.
