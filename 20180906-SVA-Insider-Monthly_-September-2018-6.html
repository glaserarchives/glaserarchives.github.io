Health & Wellness

September is Pain Awareness Month; chronic pain is the nation’s primary cause of lost work days. Food choices can help reduce pain. Prevention.com has a list of 10 pain-fighting foods that can help ease your aches naturally. Research shows that managing pain through exercise also works. Recommended exercises include stretching, strengthening exercises (such as push-ups), walking, swimming or other light aerobic activities.

September is also Cholesterol Education Month. Remember the 3 Cs: “Check. Change. Control.” Use this calculator to take preventative measures to protect your heart and know your risks. Check the Cholesterol Guide for more.

Free Courses & Tutorials

Lynda.com features more than 1,500 software courses and tutorials and SVA provides unlimited access to all employees. See the latest updates, below.

Managing Your Career: Mid-Career

Many people experience a mid-career crisis; how do you pivot and achieve your career goals? Using the identity framework, Mid-Career helps you approach your next move strategically. Dive in and learn how to identify what success means for you and your work, and develop a plan to realize your true career goals.

Pitching Yourself

Need to pitch an idea, a project, or even yourself? This free course shares a go-to approach for selling your ideas, your story, and yourself with three easy steps: destination, backstory, and connecting the dots.

Great Customer Service

By becoming the type of customer who employees will go out of their way to serve, you can consistently get better service. Learn the essential skills you need to develop rapport, and go the extra mile to increase the likelihood of having a memorable service experience.
