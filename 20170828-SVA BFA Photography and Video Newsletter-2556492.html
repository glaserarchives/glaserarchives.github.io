SVA BFA Photography and Video Newsletter
Michael Bailey Gates and Olivia Locher on Fashionista's List of 16 Up-and-Coming Photographers to Know

Alums Michael Bailey Gates (2015, above) and Olivia Locher (2013) are included in this list of young photographers on Fashionista.com.
Billy Sullivan and Kathy Rudin at Ille Arts

Faculty member Billy Sullivan and Kathy Rudin are in Ille Arts upcoming exhibition, opening on Saturday, August 26, 2017.
Erin Carr and Lyle Ashton Harris Featured in Vice's 2017 Photo Issue

Alum Erin Carr (2017) selected Lyle Ashton Harris as her idol for Vice's Photo Issue, which features their work juxtaposed together, as well as the subjects they both explore.
Patricia Voulgaris Wins Lucie Foundation Scholarship

Alum Patricia Voulgaris (2013) was selected as the recipient of the Lucie Foundation's Photo Made scholarship, to create or continue a project with a conceptual narrative approach.
Eui-Jip Hwang Interviewed by i-D for His Series Examining Korean Beauty Ideals and Cosmetic Work

Alum Eui-Jip Hwang (2017) spoke with Vice's i-D about his recent works that explore the prevalence of cosmetic surgery in South Korea and how Western aesthetics have influenced the culture. 
Nima Chaichi Takes First Place in PDN's The Look Fashion and Beauty Competition

PhotoGlobal student and MPS alum Nima Chaichi has been selected as the first place winner for PDN's Editorial/Celebrity category in their 2017 The Look competition with his photograph, To Be or Not To Be.
Ryan Shorosky Featured by The New York Times for his Las Vegas Photographs

Alum Ryan Shorosky (2014) crossed the country in an 18-wheeler and documented the people he met along the way. The New York Times features an array of his Las Vegas portraits, with a cast including cowgirls, punks, teenage rodeo stars, and drag queens.
Taleen Dersdepanian Featured on Cultura Colectiva

Alum Taleen Dersdepanian (2015) is featured for her identity and beauty-driven photography that highlights the aspects of fashion photography beyond the commercial motivation of the industry.
Ken Shung Included in Arthouse 18 Group Exhibition

Faculty Ken Shung exhibited two triptychs of blues legends Willie Dixon and Muddy Waters at the Morrison Hotel Gallery as part of the Trendsetters in Triptychs exhibit in Los Angeles. View the work online here. 
Claire Christerson Exhibits Solo Show at Entrance Gallery

Alum Claire Christerson (2015) will be showing new paintings and sculptures at Entrance Gallery, located at 48 Ludlow Street in the lower space. The opening reception will take place on September 9, from 2-8PM.

Digital PhotoPro Explores the Possibility of 3D Printing and Photography

Artist Kirsten Kay Thoen speaks with Digital PhotoPro about how she has implemented 3D printing technology in conjunction with her photographic practice, working to create sculptural works that still rely on the printed image.
Natalie de Segonzac Featured on The Fader

Alum Natalie de Segonzac (2014) speaks with The Fader about the life-altering accident that left her paralyzed, how her practice has grown, and the importance of making art.
Joseph Maida's Things "R" Queer Punctuates Aperture's Recent Primer on The History of Food Photography

Faculty member Joseph Maida's ongoing photo series, Things "R" Queer, is featured in Aperture's new publication by Susan Bright, Feast for the Eyes, which documents the history of food photography.
Clay Patrick McBride's Post-Fight Photographs on ESPN

Faculty member Clay Patrick McBride photographed MMA fighters after their Bellator 180 bouts at Madison Square Garden. 
2017 Alumni Exhibiting at Photoville in DUMBO

For this year's Photoville, taking place from September 13-24, the department exhibition will includes work from Valeria Alonzo Leon, Sophie Brill, Sam Clarke, Stephanie Escobar, Jason Isolini, Nicholas Drew, Nicole Maroon, Ramn Martinez, Quinn Torrens, Anthony Urrea, Ana Zinn, and Eui Jip Hwang. Photoville is free and open to the public!
Logan Jackson and Roe Ethridge Featured in Vice's 2017 Photo Issue 

Alum Logan Jackson (2015) selected Mentor Roe Ethridge as his idol and Vice paired their photo collages, bringing new meaning to both bodies of work.
Aperture Honors Kwame Brathwaite, Zackary Drucker, and Inez & Vinoodh at 2017 Gala 

Alum Zackary Drucker (2005) will be honored with Kwame Brathwaite and Inez & Vinoodh at this year's Aperture Gala on October 30, 2017. 
World Photography Organisation Features Lauren Fleishman's The Lovers

Alum Lauren Fleishman (2001) talks with the World Photography Organsation about her series The Lovers, her time spent documenting couples who have been together for decades, and the concept of romantic history.
Carlos Jaramillo Selected as FOCUS photo l.a. Finalist

Alum Carlos Jaramillo (2008) has been selected as a finalist for this year's FOCUS photo l.a., and was included the exhibition at Elizabeth Houston Gallery in New York.
Anthony Geathers Profiled by Steve Madden

Alum Anthony Geathers (2016) profiled by Steve Madden in an article on the Steven Madden website "self-made men" in New York City.

TIME Looks at Game of Thrones' On-Set Photographers 

Amidst Game of Thrones' film and production crew, there are also two on-set photographers. TIME takes a look at their jobs and how they have to shoot on the fly to capture the scene when the other cameras are rolling.
Susan Meiselas on the Importance of Photo Books

This interview with photographer Susan Meiselas explores the book as a format for sharing photos, the difference between zines and monographs, and the inherent challenges of the medium.
