SVA IN THE PRESS

Brianna Toussaint

2022 John J. Nelson Award Winner

Third-year BFA Interior Design: Built Environments student Brianna Toussaint has been awarded the International Interior Design Association’s John J. Nelson Legacy Scholarship for her presentation on how she sees herself enacting change through interior design, which features her supportive housing project “Solace Home.”

NYC PICK

“No Existe un Mundo Poshuracán”

A major survey of contemporary Puerto Rican art on the fifth anniversary of Hurricane Maria, including work by SVA faculty member Miguel Luciano. Free with SVA ID. Through Thursday, April 23. Whitney Museum of American Art, 99 Gansevoort Street.

The SVA Destinations Paris Impressionists program has been approved to run this summer. Email Aziza Gaines, program coordinator, againes@sva.edu, for more information or questions.
