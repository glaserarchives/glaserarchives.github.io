And the Winners Are . . .
The SVA Alumni Society has announced the winners of this year's Alumni Scholarship Awards! Sixty-seven students will receive over $52,000 to fund their thesis or portfolio projects.
Events

Balance: Community, Care and Compassion
MPS Art Therapy presents an exhibition of work by students in the program and people who they work with at their internship sites. Curated by Liz DelliCarpini, MPS Art Therapy internship coordinator and faculty member. On view through Thursday, March 3. SVA Flatiron Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.

12:00pm | Room Selection Housing Tours
If you are a current SVA student who is interested in living in the 24th Street, Gramercy or Ludlow residences for the coming academic year, stop by for one of Residence Life's housing tours, taking place every day this week on a rolling basis from 12:00 – 4:00pm.

To connect with a tour of the 24th Street and Gramercy residences, visit the Residence Life office, 340 East 24th Street, between 12:00 – 4:00pm. To join a tour of the Ludlow Residence, stop by the security desk at 101 Ludlow Street during those hours. Be sure to complete and show your eligible Cleared4 badge on your phone to the security officer at either location. For more information, visit sva.edu/residencelife.

3:30pm | Orientation Leader Information Session
Curious about the Orientation Leader (OL) position? Join SVA Student Affairs for our first info session and learn more about the position and how to apply. Today, 3:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Can't make it? There will be another info session Wednesday, March 9, on Zoom. The OL application is currently open and can be found here. Application deadline: Wednesday, March 9, 12:00pm ET.

6:00pm | Resident Assistant Information Session
Interested in being an RA this summer or next academic year? Attend one of the mandatory information sessions this week to learn more about the position and receive the application materials. Attendance at one information session is mandatory to receive the application materials. All sessions will be hosted on Zoom.

"I Can Only Be Myself"

Returning Student Room Selection
Are you a current SVA student interested in living in College housing for the 2022 – 2023 year? Returning student room selection is taking place this week. Current residential students can find details about the process in their email. Eligible students who are interested in reserving housing for next year can email reslife@sva.edu or click here.

Thursday | In Their Own Words: The Legacy of Black and African American Artists
Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at SVA hosts a panel discussion featuring Michael Barclay lll (student, BFA Design), vice president of the SVA Black Student Union; Rob Fields, director of the Sugar Hill Children’s Museum of Art and Storytelling; Dr. Georgette Grier Key, director of the Eastville Historical Society; TJ Sterling, comics creator and entrepreneur; designer, consultant, writer and educator Michele Washington (MFA 2011 Design Criticism); and Storm Ascher (BFA 2018 Visual & Critical Studies), artist, curator and entrepreneur. Thursday, February 17, 5:30pm ET, online. Register here.

Next Week | Responsive Design on Editor X With Wix Playground Academy
Join SVA Career Development for a live site creation demo and workshop to help you drill down on responsive web design and adopt a responsive approach to future projects. Wix Playground Academy will cover building fluid sites that suit any screen size using Editor X, a drag-and-drop creation platform for web designers. This is an advanced-level workshop for design students as well as those who are already using or building a Wix website. A 60-minute interactive presentation will be followed by 30 minutes of Q&A and open discussion. SVA design majors who attend this workshop will be invited to apply for a website review with Wix designers. Tuesday, February 22, 3:00pm ET, online. Register here.
