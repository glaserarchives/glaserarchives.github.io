SVA Wins a Webby!

The College’s website—designed by SVA’s in-house design studio, the Visual Arts Press—has won a Webby Award for outstanding website and mobile site in the school/university category for the Webby People’s Voice Awards!

NYC PICK

What Black Is This, You Say? Symposium

An evening of readings, conversations and performance on the plurality, complexity and nuance of Black experiences. The program is held in dialogue with What Black Is This, You Say?, the long-term public artwork by Chicago-based artist Amanda Williams at the Storefront for Art and Architecture. COVID-19 Update

Masks are encouraged but not required in all SVA buildings and all SVA students, faculty and staff must still complete their daily health attestation via Cleared4 before entering any campus building.

  • •The current seven-day city case (transmission) rate can be found here.
  • •The current SVA seven-day community case (transmission) rate is 5.

If you test positive, upload the results of any COVID-19 test to Cleared4 via the link in your daily symptom checker. Look for an email from studenthealthalert@sva.edu or employee-health-alert@sva.edu. Begin quarantining for at least five days.
