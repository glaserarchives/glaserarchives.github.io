Senior Sinjun Strom Featured in Pink Things Magazine

Pink Things interviewed Sinjun Strom about what inspired her to become a photographer, her goals and how she makes every single part of her images. 
Mentor Lorraine O'Grady Featured in Anohni Music Video

Lorraine O'Grady lip-synched for musical artist, Anohni's music video Marrow for her recent Hopelessness tour. The video is now available on YouTube. 
Alumni Hannah Garrett Featured in Mind Breath Magazine

In 2014, alumni Elizabeth Scholnick (2011), launched Mind Breath Magazine "to create a physical element that incorporated artists from all walks of life." The latest issue features alumni Hannah Garrett (above, 2011).
Alum Brian Finke Photographed the Ms. Senior America Pageant 

Asked by Goodhousing, Alum Brian Finke (1996) photographed all the glitz and glamour of the parent to create a narrative for the annual pageant held in Atlantic City.
Senior Erin Carr Featured in Musée Magazine

Senior Erin Carr is this week's Musée Magazine's Woman Crush Wednesday, featuring her series The Privilege of Looking. 
Alum Anthony Geathers Photographs World Series of Fighting’s Middleweight Champion

On New Year's Eve, Alum Anthony Geathers (2016) photographed David Branch successfully defending his mixed martial arts title at Madison Square Garden.
Alum Tayler Smith's Photograph In The New York Times
 
In an article for Wells Fargo, Alum Taylor Smith's (2016) brightly colored photograph of a customer using a bank ATM, is used in the Money New York Times section. 
Photography Grants For New Visual Projects

Need to fund a new photography project? This site has gathered grants which will award money for new projects based on simple criteria.
Faculty Ellen Wallenstein Featured on Light Leaked

East Harlem Street Scenes, is a series Ellen Wallenstein created between 2014 and 2015 while she was an Artist-in-Residence at the Carter Burden/Leonard Covello Senior Center on East 109th Street. 
Faculty Elizabeth Bick Awarded the Rudin Prize 

Nominated by Shirin Neshat, Elizabeth Bick wins the Norton Museum of Art's 2016 Rudin Prize for Emerging Photographers.
SVA BFA Photography and Video Booth at photo LA

The SVA Photography and Video Department booth at photo L.A., Frame Element, curated by faculty member Penelope Umbrico featured the work of students and alumni: Bobby Doherty (2011), Bryan Krueger (2011), Calvin Rocchio (2012), Cameron Mcleod (2018), Carolina Cueva (2007), Christopher Cassock (2009), David Brandon Geeting (2011), Jessica Pittway (2016) + Tayler Smith (2016), Kayode Ojo (2012), Michelle Dunkley (2016), Molly Matalon (2014), Pat O'Malley (2014), Rachel Wark (2014), Ryan Halliwell (2016), Sebastian Arpaio (2016), Vincent Bezuidenhout (2014), Titian Yan (2006). 
PDN's Annual Photo Award Contest Now Open
 
Visit PDN to learn more about PDN's Photo Annual 2017. The deadline is February 3, 2017. The submission fee for students is $25. 
Faculty Marcia Lippman Exhibits at Nailya Alexander Gallery

In her most recent series, Painting: Photographs by Marcia Lippman, Faculty member Marcia Lippman explores the relationship between painting and photography. The exhibition will be on display January 26 - March 2, 2017. 
Alumni May Lin Le Goff and Efrem Zelony-Mindell Featured in Posture Magazine

Alumni May Lin Le Goff (2012) and Efrem Zelony-Mindell's (2011) collaboration is featured in both Posture Magazine's online and print editions. 
Alum Samantha Friend Featured in It's Nice That

It's Nice That interviewed alum Samantha Friend (2016) about her documentary style of photography, which she describes as isolated documentaries. 
Alum Lorna Simpson Exhibits at Alexandria Museum of Art

In this exhibition, Lorna Simpson (1985) along with eight other artist, tackle the dangerous stereotypes that have never defined them. 
Faculty Max Blagg Shares Poems in Tribeca Park

For the past couple years, poet Max Blagg has been handing out signed original poems in Tribeca park to mark the winter solstice. 
Alum Justin Aversano Participates in Polaroid Exhibition 

Alum Justin Aversano (2014) exhibits at The Storefront Project Gallery through February 26th.
