6:30pm | Plan 75 Screening and Director Q&A

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a screening of Plan 75, starring Japanese actress Chieko Baisho and directed by Chie Hayakawa (BFA 2001 Photography). Plan 75 tells the story of three ordinary citizens whose lives intersect in a near dystopian future where Japan’s government launches Plan 75, a program encouraging the elderly to terminate their own lives to relieve its rapidly aging population’s social and economic burdens. Watch the trailer here. There will be a Q&A with writer and director Hayakawa following the screening. Registration is required and attendees must present their SVA ID at the door (non-SVA guests are permitted if accompanied by an attendee with an SVA ID); RSVP here. Seating is limited. Tonight, April 19, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
