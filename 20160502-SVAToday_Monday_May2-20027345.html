5:30pm: Spring Print Slam
MFA Visual Narrative presents an exhibition and sale of prints and zines produced by continuing education students enrolled in its Risograph courses. 5:30 - 8:30pm, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Allan Weck (left) with actor Brandon Essig
Mixing Comedy and Horror in After Death
In NorthJersey.com, BFA Film student Allan Weck talks about his short film After Death, which is competing in this year's Dusty Film & Animation Festival. The festival begins Saturday, May 7, at the SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
NYC PICK OF THE DAY
Weegee's Bowery
An exhibition of photographs by the legendary press photographer that capture New York's Skid Row during the 1940s and '50s. Through Friday, August 5, ICP Gallery at Mana Contemporary, 888 Newark Avenue, 6th floor, Jersey City.
Slacker
A free screening of director Richard Linklater's 1991 film. 7:30pm, Café Grumpy, 193 Meserole Avenue, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE
Senior Week
VASA presents a week of activities for graduating students, with Sleep No More on Wednesday, May 11, Medieval Times on Thursday, May 12, and Six Flags Great Adventure on Friday, May 13. Tickets are $5 and are available beginning Tuesday, May 3, 9:00am, at the Student Engagement and Leadership Office, George Washington Residence, 23 Lexington Avenue, mezzanine.
DEAR DAVE, at Frieze
BFA Photography and Video presents DEAR DAVE, magazine at the Reading Room of Frieze New York, an annual festival that brings together 200 international galleries. A limited number of $10 tickets are available to students and persons under 25, for admittance on Thursday, May 5, and Friday, May 6. Regular student tickets are $29, $45 general admission. Thursday, May 5, through Sunday, May 8, Randall's Island Park.
Presenting Shakespeare: Much Ado About Posters
The Art Directors Club, in partnership with MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller and MFA Illustration as Visual Essay faculty member Mirko Ilić, presents an exhibition of posters inspired by Shakespeare's plays, featuring SVA faculty members Milton Glaser, Paula Scher and more. Reception Thursday, May 5, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Thursday, May 5, through Thursday, May 19, ADC Gallery, 106 West 29th Street.
Miki Kratsman
MA Curatorial Practice, Artis and the International Center of Photography present Israeli photographer Miki Kratsman in conversation with Maya Benton, artist and curator at ICP. MA Curatorial Practice Chair Steven Henry Madoff will provide introductory remarks. Monday, May 9, 6:30pm, 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
