3:30pm: Marcel Ophuls
The Oscar-winning French documentarian, best known for his epic films exploring the effects of war, in conversation with MFA Social Documentary Film faculty member Thom Powers. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
6:30pm: José Parlá
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents the artist, who will discuss recent projects, including a mural at One World Trade Center, his participation at the Havana Biennial and his current shows at the Bryce Wolkowitz Gallery and the Mary Boone Gallery. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

The Platypus Affiliated Society presents a Marxist reading group at SVA. Learn about the historical and intellectual roots of the Left and Marxism and their development to the present day. Group meetings every Thursday, 6:30 - 9:00pm, 335 West 16th Street, Room 519.
