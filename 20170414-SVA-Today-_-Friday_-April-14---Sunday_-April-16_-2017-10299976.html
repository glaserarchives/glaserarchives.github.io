EVENTS

1:00pm | Internship-for-Credit Information Session

This session will cover the online application system CareerLink, and important deadlines and procedures for internship-for-credit programs. 1:00 – 2:30pm. RSVP here. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

6:30pm | Art of Autism: Beyond Etiology

MPS Art Therapy presents a community lecture by Lukas Prokes, PsyD. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

8:00pm | The Fate of the Furious Screening

The Visual Effects Society presents a screening of The Fate of the Furious, featuring Vin Diesel and Michelle Rodriguez. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). 8:00 – 11:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

SVA Inspires

Shutterstock's list of "50 Inspirational Designers to Follow in 2017" includes MFA Design co-Chair Steven Heller, MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman, BFA Design faculty member Timothy Goodman (BFA 2007 Graphic Design) and James Victore (1985 Graphic Design).
NYC PICKS

My Entire High School Sinking into the Sea

Screenings of Dash Shaw's (BFA 2005 Illustration) feature film. Tonight's, tomorrow's and Sunday's 7:00pm screenings will be followed by Q&As with Dash Shaw, animator Jane Samborski and producer Kyle Martin. Schedule here. Tickets $12 – $15; purchase here. Metrograph, 7 Ludlow Street.

"Making Space: Women Artists and Postwar Abstraction"

An exhibition that highlights the achievements of women artists between the end of World War II and the start of the feminist movement. Free with SVA ID. Tomorrow, April 15 – Sunday, August 13. Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53rd Street.

Yoga at the Penthouse

A charitable fundraiser to benefit the Morgan Stanley Children’s Hospital of NewYork-Presbyterian, hosted by Nico Sforza (BFA 2014 Photography). All funds raised will go directly to the hospital. Tickets $30; purchase here. Saturday, April 15, 11:00am – 12:15pm. Go-Studios Penthouse, 318 West 39th Street.
