BFA PHOTOGRAPHY & VIDEO READING ROOM
September 26 - October 25, 2018
214 East 21st Street, 4th Floor Project Space

SVA BFA Photography & Video, in collaboration with the SVA Library,
presents a Reading Room of Senior Books and accompanying materials
including the texts and artist books that inspired their work.
This newsletter is your newsletter! It is a resource for our students, faculty,
alumni, and New York's greater photography & art communities.

To share content to be considered for the next newsletter,
email bfaphotography@sva.edu by the 15th of each month.
Printed Matter Features Joseph Maida at NYABF

Chair Joseph Maida's newest monograph, Born Free and Equal: The Story of Loyal-___________ Americans (Convoke, 2018), is a posthumous collaboration with Ansel Adams that has been selected by Printed Matter to be featured at the NYABF at MoMA PS1, from September 21 - 23.
Joseph Maida in The Brooklyn Rail

Chair Joseph Maida, included in Interventions at Yancey Richardson Gallery, is featured in the July/August 2018 issue of The Brooklyn Rail for his photograph, Untitled (Nude).
Elizabeth Bick at The Ogden Museum of Southern Art

Faculty member Elizabeth Bick will be included in the group exhibition New Southern Photography, at the Ogden Museum of Southern Art in New Orleans, LA. The show opens on October 6 and is on view through March 10, 2019.
Artist Talk: Allen Frame

Join faculty member Allen Frame on Friday, October 19, 7PM, for a talk about his practice, his new exhibition Innamorato, and selections from his tenure at the American Academy in Rome. This event will take place at the SVA Theatre, 333 W 23rd Street. This event is presented by BFA Photography & Video and is free and open to the public.
Queer Moving Image Review Features Alex Hovet

Faculty and staff member Alex Hovet has her piece, Counter-Charge, currently being screened on Queer Moving Image Review.
Barbara Pollack Hosts an Evening at PACE

Join faculty member Barbara Pollack for a book signing, discussion, and walkthrough of Zhang Xiaogang: Recent Works, on the occasion of her forthcoming book, Brand New Art from China: A Generation on the Rise. This event will take place on Tuesday, September 25, from 6-8PM.
Don't Take Pictures Features Abby Robinson 

Faculty member Abby Robinson is featured on Don't Take Pictures, a bi-annual photography magazine and online blog.
Hyperallergic Features Penelope Umbrico 

Faculty member Penelope Umbrico is featured on Hyperallergic for her work included in New Territory: Landscape Photography Today at the Denver Art Museum.
Jerry Vezzuso at Carter Burden Gallery

Faculty member Jerry Vezzuso's solo exhibition, All Inclusive, will be on view in Carter Burden's West Gallery. The opening reception will be held on September 20, 6-8PM, and the show will run through October 11, 2018.
Lisa Elmaleh Takes Over The Smithsonian's Instagram

Alum Lisa Elmaleh (2007) did an Instagram takeover for the Smithsonian Magazine's Instagram account. Her photographs were also included in the August issue of the magazine.
Corinne Jones at Situations Gallery

Alum Corinne Jones (1996) has a solo exhibition on view at Situations. Chamber of the Lost Sea is on view through October 14, 2018.
Before and After SVA: Amanda Louis

Alum Amanda Louis (2017) is featured on Before and After SVA discussing her job as a photo researcher at Trunk Archive and being a freelance photographer.
Star Montana in Aperture's Los Angeles Issue

Alum Star Montana (2013) has a photograph included in the recent Fall 2018 "Los Angeles" issue of Aperture.
Louise Palmberg Photographs for The New Hard Rock Hotel & Casino

Alum Louise Palmberg (2016) was hired by RadicalMedia to photograph iconic music memorabilia for the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in Atlantic City. Her photos and animations are now on view in the hotel lobby.
Stephanie Pfriender Stylander Releases New Book

Alum Stephanie Pfriender Stylander (1983) released her new book The Untamed Eye, which features portraits from Harper's Bazaar, Glamour, Interview Magazine, British GQ, including photos of icons such as Nicole Kidman, Heath Ledger, Kate Moss and many more.
Collier Schorr Photographs Agnès Varda for Interview Magazine

SVA alum and photographer Collier Schorr (1985) photographed 90-year-old Belgian artist and filmmaker Agnès Varda for Interview, who was also featured on the first ever cover of the magazine in 1969.
Mario Sorrenti in Conversation with Dennis Freedman

BFA Photography & Video and Phaidon present Mario Sorrenti in conversation with Dennis Freedman. This event will take place on Tuesday, October 9, 7PM, at the SVA Theatre, 333 W 23rd Street, and it is free and open to the public. A book signing and reception will follow.
Photoville Returns to NYC

Photoville returns to Brooklyn Bridge Park, running from September 13 - 23. This year's festival will feature over 65 shipping containers transformed into galleries and exhibition spaces.
13th Annual NY Art Book Fair at MoMA PS1

Printed Matter's 13th annual NYABF will be taking place on the weekend of September 21 - 23 at MoMA PS1.
Ryan McGinley at Team Gallery

Photographer Ryan McGinley's solo exhibition, Mirror, Mirror, is on view at Team Gallery through September 29, 2018.
Penumbra Foundation's Fall 2018 Artist Series

Take advantage of Penumbra's Artist Series, held a short walk from the BFA Photography & Photo building at their location at 36 E 30th Street. Free for students to attend.

The New York Times' Lens features a chilling glimpse into US border policy and the ongoing humanitarian fallout.
