EVENTS

12:00pm | How Do I Find an Internship? Tips for Planning a Successful Internship Search

Designed specifically for students planning to intern while at SVA, this interactive workshop will provide an overview of how to search for internships and what materials can assist you. Topics will go beyond writing a resume and cover letter: learn how to determine what kind of internship you want, create a search plan and a strong application, apply and follow-up appropriately, use virtual tools, build your network and more. Today, November 9, 12:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C. Registration is closed for this event.

6:00pm | “Was I Dreaming?” Reception

MA Curatorial Practice presents an exhibition examining dreams and memory through a visual narrative born from different mediums, curated by student Diana Colón. Tonight, 6:00 – 8:00pm. The exhibition runs through Wednesday, November 23. MA Curatorial Practice, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

Internship-for-Credit Information Session

Learn how you can qualify and apply for the spring 2023 Internship-for-Credit Program. This overview will cover the online application system (Symplicity) in detail as well as student enrollment criteria, the responsibilities of both students and employers, how to find and apply for an internship, deadlines and procedures for the spring semester and the special requirements for international students. Attendance is strongly recommended for first-time applicants and required. If you are an international student, attendance at this session is a requirement before submitting your application. Open to third-year, fourth-year and graduate students. This presentation is co-hosted by SVA Career Development and the International Student Office. Wednesday, November 16, 3:00 – 4:30pm, online.
