Network Aesthetics

MA Curatorial Practice presents a panel of researchers and producers in the expanding field of "network aesthetics," which explores artistic production and what it means to live, work and communicate under the sign of algorithmic, networked life. Tonight, 7:00 – 9:00pm. CP Project Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

6:00pm | Parentheses Lecture Series: Storytelling

A panel of leaders in user experience, design, creative arts therapy, writing and technology explore how creative professionals use stories, both their own and those of others, to influence the creation of experiences. RSVP here. 6:00 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

Agar Art: Painting with Bacteria Workshop

BFA Fine Arts' Bio Lab presents a workshop on creating art using microbes as "paint" and agar as a "canvas." No experience necessary. Photos of the art created in this workshop are eligible for the American Society of Microbiology's Agar Art Contest. Register here. Thursday, March 1, and Saturday, March 3, 3:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Bio Art Lab, 335 West 16th Street, 3rd floor.

Rise Screening and Q&A

Center for Communication and SVA Theatre present an advance screening of NBC's new drama Rise. After the screening, hear from executive producers Jason Katims and Flody Suarez about creating this youth-driven series. Free; RSVP here. Thursday, March 8, 2:30pm.
