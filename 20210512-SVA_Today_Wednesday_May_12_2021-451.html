Alumni Scholarship Winner Talya Alsberg
BFA Photography and Video student and Alumni Scholarship Award winner Talya Alsberg's thesis project, Record Keepers, is an archeological excavation of collective histories stored in a “timeless” library activated by each viewer. Read more about it in our Q&A with Alsberg here.
