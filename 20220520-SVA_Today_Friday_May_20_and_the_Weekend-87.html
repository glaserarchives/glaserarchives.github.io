Alexander Si (MFA 2021 Fine Arts) is featured as part of Art in America’s “New Talent: Artists to Watch.” The latest installment of Si’s curatorial project, “Steeped in Spilled Milk,” is currently on view at EFA Studios Gallery through Friday, June 10.
