TAKE NOTE

Black History Month Exhibition: Call for Submissions

SVA's Student Engagement and Leadership Office and Residence Life are planning a February 2017 exhibition featuring works by black and African American student-artists of SVA. Any interested students who identify as black, African American and/or of the African diaspora may submit as many pieces as they would like, in any medium of their choosing. Deadline Tuesday, December 20, 11:59am. Selections will be announced by Monday, January 9. To submit, click here. For more information, email Oliver Colbert, Coordinator of Student Engagement and Leadership, ocolbert@sva.edu.
