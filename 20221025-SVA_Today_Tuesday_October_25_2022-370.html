9:00am | Jan Wilker

Jan Wilker, co-founder of the New York City-based design studio Karlssonwilker, will be speaking about his work pioneering the genre of performance design. Wilker will join BFA Design faculty member Nada Ray’s Interdisciplinary Design class to discuss project PUBLIKUM Calendar 2005, produced in collaboration of Karlssonwilker and FIA Group, led by Nada Ray. Today, October 25, 9:00 – 11:00am. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 202.

5:30pm | “Becoming 2022 (New York Is a Friendly Town)” Reception

Join BFA Photography and Video for a reception celebrating its annual exhibition of visual conversations between distinguished program alumni and current students. #SVAwesome

Happy Diwali! Shreya Gupta (MFA 2017 Illustration as Visual Essay) shared yesterday, on the first day of the celebratory festival, that her new picture book, She Sang for India: How M.S. Subbulakshmi Used Her Voice for Change, will be out next month. The book tells the story of M.S. Subbulakshmi, a famous Carnatic singer who gained recognition worldwide and was invited by the United Nations to perform during a time when women were not allowed to sing in public in India. She sang for peace and unity and was a trailblazer. Via @svanyc.
