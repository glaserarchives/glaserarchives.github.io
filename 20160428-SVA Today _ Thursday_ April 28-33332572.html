Using the Power of Cooking to Fight Bullying
Over the next few weeks, SVA will be highlighting some of the many outstanding projects by 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winners. First up is MFA Design for Social Innovation student Ivan Boscariol on his Boiling Down program, which aims to reduce bullying and challenge gender norms through the power of cooking. Boscariol is one of 25 MFA Design for Social Innovation students who are presenting their thesis projects today from noon to 5:00pm at the SVA Theatre (more information here). To watch a live stream of the presentations, click here.

TAKE NOTE
Facade Work Enters Next Phase at 205, 209 East 23rd Street
Scaffolding is currently being installed on the west side of the building and installation will be completed by Thursday, May 5. Select facade demolition and removal are currently taking place on the rear and east wall of 209 and will continue for approximately two weeks, weather permitting. During this removal period there will be loud construction noise associated with the project Monday through Friday between the hours of 7:30am and 3:30pm, with occasional days until 5:30pm, and 9:00am to 5:00pm on Saturdays. In the front of 205 there is a container for debris removal which will be present for the duration of the project. Periodic updates will be provided in SVA Today in the coming weeks. For questions, please contact Richard Riccio, executive director of Facilities, 212.592.2156.
