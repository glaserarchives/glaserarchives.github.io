Dear SVA Community Member,

Starting Wednesday, May 3, all community members will need to re-enroll their personal mobile devices in order to access "SVA Secure WiFi." (Mobile devices owned and managed by the college will automatically be updated.)

On Wednesday, May 3, and dates thereafter, from off-campus—while not on an SVA network—go to wifi.sva.edu on your mobile device via your home wireless or 4G/5G/LTW cellular to initiate the process. If you are already on campus you will need to connect to the "SVA Wireless Registration" network first then go to wifi.sva.edu to start the installation.

For Apple devices, select "Sign In/JoinNow" and sign in with your myID credentials then run through the secure certificate installation process.

On Android devices, from step 1 select "Restart Setup" or from step 2 select "JoinNow" to reach the sign-in page for your myID credentials and start the secure certificate installation process.

General instructions for various mobile devices can be found here.

Note: Academic and Administrative departments that maintain private wireless networks with WPA2 authentication will have their passwords changed on Wednesday, May 3. Details regarding this update are being communicated separately with departments.  

Thank you,

Administrative & Network Services
