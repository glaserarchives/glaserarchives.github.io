Move With Heart

BFA Interior Design student Tina Kvelashvili has received the Angelo Donghia Foundation 2017 Student Scholarship Award for Interior Design for her design for a spa and fitness center focused on creating a more holistic approach to men’s health.

Summer HD Festival

Nine outdoor performances from the Met’s "Live in HD" series of cinema transmissions, plus a special pre-festival screening of Ingmar Bergman’s classic 1975 film version of Mozart’s The Magic Flute, on Friday, August 25. The festival runs from August 26 through September 4. Free. The Metropolitan Opera, 30 Lincoln Center Plaza.

New Database Trial: Safari

SVA Library has just begun a 30-day trial of the Safari database, an online resource to help boost your learning and give you tools to help in your school and everyday projects. Through the trial, SVA students, faculty and staff will have unlimited access to over 40,000 ebooks, videos, audio books and tutorials covering everything from computer topics to graphic design and photography. Create an account and Safari will provide you with specific recommendations that will assist you with content discovery aligned with your personal learning goals. Add the domain @safaribooksonline.com to your Address Book, Contacts or Safe Senders List. To access, click here, enter your name and SVA email, and follow the steps provided for you.

At the MPS Art Therapy 2017 Annual Conference, therapists from different modalities will discuss innovative methodologies that integrate creative arts therapies through presentations and a panel discussion. Free for SVA students, faculty, staff and alumni; RSVP here. Friday, September 8, 9:30am – 4:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
