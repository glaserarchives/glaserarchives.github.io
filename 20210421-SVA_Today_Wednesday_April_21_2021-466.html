ANNOUNCEMENTS

Tomorrow | AAPI Faculty/Staff Forum
Within the past year, members within the Asian and Asian American communities have been facing an increased number of instances of harassment, discrimination and physical violence. The DEI Office will be hosting a virtual forum tomorrow, April 22, 10:30am – 12:00pm, as a space for open conversation with our AAPI peers across campus about how they have been coping. Faculty and staff who are interested and who self-identify as AAPI can fill out this Google Form and register for the Zoom call at the end of the form.

Please note: As we begin to develop more inclusive campus-wide conversations and initiatives, there will be opportunities for allies to be involved and provide support.

Friday | Virtual Support Space for International Students: Self-Care Tips
Do you feel overwhelmed by schoolwork, finals, graduation and current events? Come take a break and join Will from the International Student Office and Holly from Student Health and Counseling Services to de-stress and connect with fellow international students! They will share stress management and self-care tips for finals week. Friday, April 23, 9:00am ET. RSVP here. Note: This will be their last session for the 2020-2021 academic year.
