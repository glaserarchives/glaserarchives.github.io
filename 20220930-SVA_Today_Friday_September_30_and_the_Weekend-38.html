ANNOUNCEMENTS

Deadline Today | Apply for ISO Cultural Partner Program

Do you want to be engaged in local communities by participating in community service projects? SVA’s International Student Office welcomes all students to apply for a two-semester long membership-based program, featuring New York City culture trips, a monthly discussion series and the opportunity to participate in and lead a community service project. It is a small, supportive group where you can connect with students from different majors and years. Program requirements and the application can be viewed here. Email Yoko Anderson, yanderson@sva.edu, with any questions. Deadline is today, September 30.

2022 Annual Safety and Security Reports

SVA is committed to the safety and security of all members of the College community. In accordance with the federal Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act of 1990, the Annual Security Report (ASR), the Annual Fire Safety Report (AFSR) and Campus Crime Statistics for this year are now available.


These reports contain statistics and institutional policies concerning fire safety, as well as information on campus security issues such as sexual assault and other matters. Copies of these reports can be accessed on the Health and Safety section of the SVA Consumer Information webpage here.


Contact SVA Institutional Research at 212.592.2829 with any questions regarding this information.
