Museum Benefits Spotlight: MoMA PS1
Take advantage of the long holiday weekend to catch up on the goings-on at MoMA PS1. The trip to Long Island City is well worth it for free admission to this hub for experimental art. Currently on view through Monday, March 7, 2016, is the fourth iteration of "Greater New York," highlighting artists from the New York metropolitan area. Including programs of film and performance, the exhibition fills the entire building with over 400 works by 157 artists. Click here for a full list of museum benefits available to SVA students, faculty and staff. MoMA PS1, 22-25 Jackson Avenue, Queens.
2:00pm: Smart Classroom Training
SVA Audio Visual Services presents an information session on how to use the College's "smart" classroom technology. Topics covered include classroom equipment, trouble-shooting techniques and technical support information. RSVP to rbarton@sva.edu. 136 West 21st Street, Room 830F.

Take Note
Caroline Woolard
MFA Fine Arts presents faculty member and interdisciplinary artist Caroline Woolard discussing her work supporting affordable spaces for cultural, community and small-business use in New York City. Tuesday, December 1, 6:00pm, 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
Caroline Sinders
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents IBM Watson researcher Caroline Sinders discussing her work investigating human decision-making in developing robotics software, and how the line between human and computer informs her understanding of how design shapes conversations happening online. Tuesday, December 1, 6:30pm, 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
Landon Nordeman
MPS Digital Photography presents photographer Landon Nordeman discussing his work, which has appeared in ESPN magazine, Fortune, National Geographic, The New Yorker, New York, Saveur, Time, Vogue and W. Tuesday, December 1, 7:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
Discount at Cooper Hewitt Design Museum Store
As part of SVA's membership program with the Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum, students, faculty and staff are eligible for a 20% discount on merchandise at the museum's store from December 4 - 6. Use the code "HOLIDAY2015" in person and online to receive the discount. Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum, 2 East 91st Street.
