Juana Valdés

Juana Valdés at Miami Beach

The city of Miami Beach has voted to acquire Colored Bone China Rags, an installation by Cuban artist Juana Valdés (MFA 1993 Fine Arts), shown at this year’s Art Basel fair. Via The Art Newspaper.

NYC PICK

Gingerbread Lane

An installation of over 1,500 homemade gingerbread houses, named the largest gingerbread village by Guinness Book of World Records. Free. Through Monday, January 16. Essex Market, 88 Essex Street.
