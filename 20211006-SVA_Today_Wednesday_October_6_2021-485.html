ANNOUNCEMENTS

Friday | FAB FRIDAY Talk with Posture Media
FAB FRIDAY by BFA Advertising and BFA Design is a weekly guest-lecture series set to inspire and educate with great design. This week's guests are the co-founders of Posture Media, Winter Mendelson and SVA alumnus Asher Torres.

In their presentation, "Posture Media in the Making: Surviving an Industry Pivot from Editorial to Marketing," they will share their personal career journey starting with launching an indie magazine that ultimately transitioned into a creative agency. They will also share their experience as nonbinary and transgender creatives in the advertising and marketing industries, and where they think the future of the industry is headed. Join this Friday, October 8, 3:30pm, on Zoom. Email bfaadvdes@sva.edu with any questions.
