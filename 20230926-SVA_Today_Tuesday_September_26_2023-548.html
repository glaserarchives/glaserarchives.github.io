Tuesday, September 26, 2023

Collier Schorr for Interview

Collier Schorr (BFA 1985 Communication Arts) photographs Adesuwa, Dev Hynes and Venus Williams in Pelagia Kolotouros’s Lacoste designs for Interview.

EVENTS

1:00pm | The Artists Roundtable: Mithu Sen (New Delhi)

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with artist Mithu Sen, whose work explores themes of gender, identity and the body often using humor and subversion to challenge traditional notions of femininity. Today, September 26, 1:00 – 2:00pm, online.

5:00pm | Book Launch: Roger Mader’s Purposeful Enterprise

MFA Interaction Design presents a talk, Q&A and signing for faculty member Roger Mader’s new book, Purposeful Enterprise—an exploration of how to establish a compelling purpose that galvanizes a team and drives performance. Tonight, September 26, 5:00 – 7:30pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street.

6:30pm | Nanfu Wang in Conversation With Amy Taubin

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a discussion with award-winning filmmaker Nanfu Wang and critic and faculty member Amy Taubin. Tonight, September 26, 6:30 – 8:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, ground floor.

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Rob Hann

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk with self-taught British-born photographer Rob Hann. Tonight, September 26, 7:00 – 8:30pm. RSVP for location.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Friday, September 29, 5:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Follow @svacommunityfridge on Instagram to stay up to date with future events.

Next Week | The Artists Roundtable: Beatrice Glow (New York)

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk with Beatrice Glow, a New York and Bay Area-based multidisciplinary artist working in service of public history and just futures. Tuesday, October 3, 5:00 – 6:00pm, online.

MFA Visual Narrative at MICE

MFA Visual Narrative will have a full display of student and alumni work for sale at Table 28 at MICE (Massachusetts Independent Comics Expo). Free admission. Saturday, September 30 – Sunday, October 1. Boston University Fuller Building, 808 Commonwealth Avenue, Boston.

SVA Yearbook 2024 Needs Your Photo!

The SVA Yearbook is requesting that all students graduating in 2024 submit a photo of themselves for inclusion in the yearbook. Check your SVA email for more details or scan the QR code on the yearbook posters posted around campus. Questions? Email yearbook@sva.edu.

#SVAwesome

This past weekend, MPS Digital Photography joined 2018 program alumnus Vicky Azcoitia as she painted her NYC DOT Art mural commission for the 191st Street Station tunnel with the help of volunteers. The mural celebrates Dominican Merengue dance as part of the heritage of the Washington Heights neighborhood. Via @sva_digitalphoto.
