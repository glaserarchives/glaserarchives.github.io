From the Glaser Archives: “I ♥ NY” Knockoffs

Curbed’s weekly roundup includes an item on the late designer and SVA faculty member Milton Glaser’s personal collection of imitations of his famous “I ♥ NY” logo. “Milton Glaser: Pop,” an exhibition of Glaser’s 1960s and ’70s work, is now on view at the SVA Gramercy Gallery.
