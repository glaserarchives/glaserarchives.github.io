Time to Sign Up for NYC Pride March 2023!

Calling all SVA LGBTQIA+ community members and allies! 

The Offices of Academic Affairs and Diversity, Equity and Inclusion are excited to announce that the Provost has secured our place for SVA students, faculty, alumni and staff to walk in the NYC Pride March 2023! Join us in uplifting each other to celebrate and further the affirmational and inclusive campus that is SVA. Together, let us protest and demonstrate against the recent spread of anti-LGBTQIA+ and anti-trans legislation currently moving through U.S. state houses!
This year’s NYC Pride March theme is “Strength in Solidarity.”
Let’s raise our voices, rise up and thrive!

The March date: Sunday, June 25, 2023.
