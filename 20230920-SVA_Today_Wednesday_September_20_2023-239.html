A Snapshot in Hip-Hop, Featuring Angel Ibañez

SVA Diversity, Equity and Inclusion presents a conversation with longtime SVA staff member, artist and photographer Angel Ibañez (1974 Illustration) about his 1986 photograph of the early rap group Whodini, which appears on the College’s fall 2023 subway poster celebrating the 50th anniversary of hip-hop. Ibañez attended a multi-day video shoot for Whodini, photographing the goings-on and in the process capturing a pivotal moment in hip-hop history that drew an array of notable artists, actors and musicians of the time. He will share his stories from the experience, as well as other select photographs from the series. Monday, October 2, 6:30 – 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
