Community Lecture Series:
Art Therapy at Sing Sing Prison
Friday, January 19, 2018
133 West 21st Street, Room 101C
6:30pm-8:00pm

Presenter is Jane Marks Hart.  The lecture will describe Jane's experience of conducting the first art therapy group for a men's maximum security prison.  Jane will share what she has learned about working with this population and how the uncluttered approach that she discovered could be adapted to work with many other populations.
