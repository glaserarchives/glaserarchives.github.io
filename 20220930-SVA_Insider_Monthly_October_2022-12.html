🎙️ Get to Know: Amir Ali, coordinator, Mail Processing and Office Services

Tell us a little about your history with SVA and your current role.

It's been five years since my journey at SVA began. Initially, I was hired as a summer temp for the Mail Processing and Office Services department. Now, as one of the department coordinators, I am responsible for creating and maintaining much of our on- and off-site inventory and internal departmental moves and relocations pertaining to capital projects.


Congratulations on being named a 2022 Distinguished Staff Award winner! What does it mean to you to win this award?

Thank you! It is indeed an honor for one's hard work to be recognized by their peers. This win reflects the collective sacrifice and dedication made by my team and the four other essential departments over the past two years during the pandemic: Security, Facilities, Residence Life and SVA Campus Store. I am privileged to work with them every day.


What challenges and successes did you have prior to winning this award? Is there anything that you've accomplished that you're particularly proud of?

The past two years have brought many challenges and changes to my department's role at the College. While many transitioned to remote work, we continued our on-campus, in-person presence. Our response to such a need has not been without some difficulty as we adapted to the ever-changing definition of our role and the new responsibilities that we have assumed.


I am most proud of the resilience of my co-workers. Together, we have accomplished—and continue to accomplish—even the most daunting tasks. What we once thought to be impossible is now possible.


What are you hoping to contribute as a member of the Distinguished Staff Advisory Group?

I'm always ready to serve SVA and its community. Hopefully, I can offer a new perspective that meets the evolving needs of our SVA community.

🤯 Free Courses and Tutorials

LinkedIn Learning’s online classes and tutorials provide opportunities to brush up on your old skills and explore entirely new ones.


Learning Infographic Design

To create a truly successful infographic, you must understand the power of visual communication. Amy Balliett, CEO and Founder of Killer Visual Strategies, shows you how to create engaging and successful infographics that stand out from the crowd.


How Design Makes Us Think

Great design does something extraordinary. But it isn’t easy to qualify. In this course, instructor Sean Adams shows you how an aesthetic cue really works, triggering a complex, multifaceted set of responses that resonates with viewers and users.


Creative Video Editing Techniques

Explore creative video editing techniques with a comprehensive study. Senior staff instructor Ashley Kennedy begins by covering the rules and conventions of video editing, setting the stage for an explanation of the role of editing in cinematic storytelling.
