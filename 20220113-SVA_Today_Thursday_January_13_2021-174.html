Charles Traub’s Tickety-Boo in Print
For Print, MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller writes about MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Chair Charles Traub’s new book, Tickety-Boo, which features over 200 smartphone photographs taken by Traub over the past four years.
Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 9:30 – 11:00am ET, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline). Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today for a one-hour session with Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross. She will be available on Zoom to field questions about job searching, interviewing and other career-related topics. Join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join. Today, 10:00 – 11:00am ET.

11:00am | Coffee Talk!
Join therapist Brett and nurse Abby of Student Health and Counseling Services for our first virtual Coffee Talk of the spring semester. Bring your coffee and questions about boosters, COVID-19, testing and any other health-related questions you may have. Tomorrow, 11:00am ET, online via Instagram Live.

5:30pm | MA/MAT Art Education Final Info Session
Art Education faculty and alumni will present an overview of the program, explaining coursework and answering any questions about requirements, portfolios, student teaching, schedules, teacher certification and the job market for K – 12 art teachers. This will be the final information session and will feature Art Education alumni Amanda Stefanese and Marie Antonelli. Tonight, 5:30 – 7:00pm ET, online. Register here.

6:30pm | Visual Narrative Information Session
What is visual narrative? What is a Risograph? How do you self-publish comics, zines, photo and artist’s books? Spend an evening with some of our Continuing Education Visual Narrative faculty and see their work and student work to find out. Tonight, 6:30 – 8:00pm ET, online. Register here.

7:00pm | Daredoodles with Tom Motley
Join cartooning instructor Tom Motley for a series of drawing dares derived from his courses Cartooning Basics, Figure Drawing for Cartoonists and Short Form Comics. This time, attendees will generate doodles and develop them into comic sequences. Tonight, 7:00 – 8:30pm ET, online. Register here.

“25th & Park” by Jerry Schatzberg
Fotografiska presents “25th & Park,” an exhibition showcasing renowned New York photographer Jerry Schatzberg’s studio from the 1960s, where he photographed icons like Bob Dylan, Jimi Hendrix and the Beatles. Through Saturday, March 5. Fotografiska, 281 Park Avenue South.
