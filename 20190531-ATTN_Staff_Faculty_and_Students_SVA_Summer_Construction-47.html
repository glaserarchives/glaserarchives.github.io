Every summer, SVA takes the opportunity to undertake construction projects in order to renew space, address deferred maintenance and implement energy initiatives. Please be aware that SVA has some major projects affecting access to various buildings as follows: 

Sidewalk Reconstructions: 
  • 205-207/209-213 East 23rd Street: Temporary walkways will be in place during the course of this project and for a period of approximately 2 weeks the area will be impacted by noise from jack-hammering in order to open up the sidewalk. 
  • 132–136 West 21st Street: Temporary walkways will be in place during the course of this project and for a period of approximately 2 weeks the area will be impacted by noise from jack-hammering in order to open up the sidewalk. 
  • 133/141 West 21st Street Infill of Sidewalk Hatches: Though not as extensive as the above two projects, sections of this sidewalk will be filled in with concrete. 
Building Facades: 
  • 17 Gramercy Park South AND 133/141 West 21st Street: A sidewalk bridge will be constructed in the front of the building. There will be some noise as crews work to chip out mortar and renew the brick work throughout the exterior of the building. 
  • 335 West 16th Street: Work will be performed on the west wall only in order to remove and replace damaged stucco. Noise should not be an issue at this location, though a small bridge will be installed on the west corner of the building for the duration of this project.
