SVA Today
Tuesday, April 19, 2016
Steven Heller Receives 2016 Carle Honor
MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller was named one of four 2016 Carle Honorees, who are recognized for their "instrumental role in making children’s books a vibrant and influential art and literary form in America."
Noon: John Dowdell on Carol
BFA Animation and BFA Film present colorist John Dowdell discussing his work on and screening clips from the Oscar-nominated film Carol. Open to SVA students and faculty. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 502.
3:30pm: Getting Un-Stuck and Finding Flow: How to Respond Creatively to Creative Blocks
Student Health and Counseling Services presents a workshop on methods for breaking through creative blocks. Open to SVA students. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
6:30pm: Mark Wasiuta: The Making of "Environmental Communications: Contact High"
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents Mark Wasiuta discussing "Environmental Communications: Contact High," the first major exhibition of work by the West Coast media collective Environmental Communications. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Collaborating for a Cause
Artsy interviews MFA Fine Arts faculty member Marilyn Minter about her collaboration with Miley Cyrus for Planned Parenthood.
NYC PICK OF THE DAY
Dru Donovan
The photographer discusses her work, which focuses on rituals of caregiving and issues surrounding illness. Admission is free for students, $5 for general public. 7:00pm, The Aperture Gallery and Bookstore, 547 West 27th Street, 4th floor.
TAKE NOTE
MPS Art Therapy Thesis Presentations
Graduating students present their thesis projects. Friday, April 22, 9:00am - 3:00pm, National Arts Club, 15 Gramercy Park South.
