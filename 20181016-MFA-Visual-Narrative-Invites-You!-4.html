MFA Visual Narrative Invites You! 

Thinking about applying? Informational events may be happening near you. Applications submitted by February 1st will be considered for partial scholarships. SVA housing grants given to all students for summer sessions.


If you are in New York City - even better! We have a full department information session on November 10th. Come and ask us anything. Really.
MFA Visual Narrative Info Session
Event Date: Saturday, November 10, 2018, 2:30 - 5:30pm
Location: The MFA Visual Narrative department will host this event at 136 West 21st Street, Room
 418-F, New York, NY 10011
Description: Applications are open! Have questions about the program? The process? Your portfolio? Don’t miss this unique opportunity for prospective students to speak with Chair, Nathan Fox, along with faculty, alumni and students to learn more about the program. We will also be giving demos on our Risograph printer facility in the RisoLAB.

New York City Graduate Portfolio Day
Event Date: Sunday, October 28, 2018, 12:00 - 4:00pm
Location: Parsons The New School for Design, University Center, 66 5th Avenue (First Floor Lobby), New York, NY 10003
Description: MFA Visual Narrative Director of Operations Joan McCabe, and distinguished alumni Jennifer Goldstick, will be here to answer any questions you may have about the program and application process. Graduate National Portfolio Days are specifically for individuals that are in progress or have already earned a Bachelor’s degree and are interested in MFA, MPS, and MA degrees. Prospective graduate students have the opportunity to meet with schools accredited by the National Association of Schools of Art and Design. No registration needed.
