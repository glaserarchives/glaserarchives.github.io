After School Special

A look at five of the movies that will be shown at this year's After School Special, SVA's alumni film and animation festival, running from today until Sunday, September 17, at the SVA Theatre. See the full lineup here.
EVENTS

Artists, Artisans and Museums in Mexico

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents anthropologist and arts advocate Gobi Stromberg and SVA Art History faculty member Carla Stellweg, who will discuss Mexico’s institutional approach to its vast tradition of popular arts and culture. 6:30 – 8:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Art of the Prank

To kick off the After School Special, SVA Theatre presents a screening of Art of the Prank, followed by a Q&A with the film's subject, Joey Skaggs (BFA 1982 Media Arts). Free. 7:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Continuing Remembrance

AM New York on a sculpture made from World Trade Center steel, the creation of which was the subject of the documentary Remembrance, directed by Nicolas Lopez (BFA 2017 Film) and Dusty Film & Animation Festival producer Annie Flocco.
NYC PICK

Tales from the Vault: Scrapbooks from a Changing Brooklyn

Two members of the Brooklyn Historical Society's library staff share stories from a collection of photographs and scrapbooks that capture Brooklyn's transition from rural to industrial town in the early 1900s. Tickets $5; purchase here. 6:30 – 8:30pm. Brooklyn Historical Society, 128 Pierrepont Street, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

RisoLab Basics Workshop

MFA Visual Narrative presents a free workshop with RisoLab artist-in-residence Alix Pentecost-Farren, who will teach the basics of printing on the Risograph. Required RSVP here. Tomorrow, September 12, 6:30pm. RisoLab, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.

NYC Local Elections

The primary elections for city mayor and other local government positions take place tomorrow, September 12. The general election takes place on Tuesday, November 7. Visit voting.nyc for more information, or visit sva.edu/studentaffairs and click the voter registration tab for access to resources and registration materials. Voters must be registered by Friday, October 13, in order to participate in the general election in November.
