Artist-Parent or Parent-Artist?

From the Spring 2017 Visual Arts Journal, a look at the challenges of pursuing a career in the arts while also raising a family. Attend SVA Career Development's panel discussion tonight with parent-artists Scott Alario, Qiana Mestrich, Anna Ogier-Bloomer and Leah Bedrosian Peterson to find out how they maintain their work-life balance. Tonight, 6:30 – 8:30pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.
EVENTS

1:00pm | Image Machines: A Screening Program, with Discussion by Grahame Weinbren

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents screenings of short international films. Program here. 1:00 – 3:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

3:00pm | Understanding OCD Workshop

SVA Student Health and Counseling presents this workshop, where students can learn about OCD, its subsets and cognitive-behavioral therapy. 3:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 23rd Street.

6:00pm | The Digital Revolution and the Future of Work

MFA Interaction Design presents senior editor and economics columnist at The Economist Ryan Avent, who will look at how technology is affecting the economy, what workers can expect in the future and what challenges society can expect to face as we adjust to a digital world. RSVP here. 6:00 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

6:30pm | Local Address: Fresh Milk Art Platform

MA Curatorial Practice presents a discussion among Annalee Davis, artist and founder of Fresh Milk Art Platform in the Caribbean, Erica Moiah James, assistant professor of History of Art and African American Studies at Yale University, and Mario Caro, a researcher and curator of contemporary indigenous art. RSVP here. 6:30 – 8:00pm. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

7:00pm | Print and Process: Greg Foley and Natalie Andrewson

MFA Visual Narrative's RisoLAB artists-in-residence will present their work, including projects completed during their residencies at the lab. RSVP here. 7:00 – 9:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

NYC PICK

Celia Gerard: "Ascent/Descent"

An exhibition of paintings and bronze and ceramic sculptures by BFA Design faculty member Celia Gerard. Reception tonight, 6:00 – 8:00pm. On view through Saturday, May 6. Sears-Peyton Gallery, 210 11th Avenue, Suite 802.
TAKE NOTE

Smurfs: The Lost Village in 3D

SVA’s Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Smurfs: The Lost Village, featuring the voices of Julia Roberts, Demi Lovato and Rainn Wilson. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Sunday, April 9, 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Faculty and Staff Lunch and Learn: The New York Times

A representative from The New York Times will go over how to access SVA's digital subscription to the Times, demonstrate the tools and features that come with the subscription and introduce the Times’ teaching resource, The Times InEducation. Pizza will be provided after the session. Thursday, April 13, noon. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
