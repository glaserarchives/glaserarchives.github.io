ANNOUNCEMENTS

Constitution Day 2023

Today is Constitution Day, a day of federal observance which commemorates the day in 1787 when the U.S. Constitution was signed at the Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia. To learn more about the U.S. Constitution visit the U.S. Senate website, or pick up a free pocket-sized edition at SVA Student Affairs, 340 East 24th Street.

All interested students are welcome to join certified yoga instructor Nurse Emily from SVA Student Health and Counseling Services for an hour of Vinyasa-based yoga. All levels are welcome. RSVP is required; register here. Wednesday, September 20, 3:00 – 4:00pm. MPS Art Therapy Gallery, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor. Questions? Email Nurse Emily at egoodman4@sva.edu.

Thursday – Sunday | After School Special

SVA Theatre presents the College’s annual alumni film and animation festival. Events include screenings, premieres and Q&As with industry insiders that offer behind-the-scenes looks at the world of television and cinema. SVA alumni will be present at Q&As throughout the week. All events are free and open to the public. Thursday, September 21 – Sunday, September 24. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
