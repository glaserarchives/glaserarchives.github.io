“Mining the Milton Glaser Archives”

Beth Kleber, the founding archivist of the Milton Glaser Design Study Center and Archives and SVA Archives, shared a few of the late designer and faculty member’s rare illustrations and never-produced commissions with Curbed ahead of “Milton Glaser: Pop,” an exhibition opening at the SVA Gramercy Gallery on Wednesday, May 17.

Artsy’s Best Booths of New York Art Week

Superposition, the “nomadic” gallery founded and run by Storm Ascher (BFA 2018 Visual & Critical Studies), is featured in Artsy’s list of highlights from New York Art Week 2023. Read an interview with Ascher from the fall/winter 2021 Visual Arts Journal here.

NYC PICK

“Now Is Better”

An exhibition of original lenticular prints and adapted historical paintings by SVA faculty member Stefan Sagmeister that visualize long-term statistics on global development. Through Friday, June 16. Patrick Parrish Gallery, 50 Lispenard Street.

#SVAwesome

In honor of NYC BID Day and NYCxDesign 2023, SVA and NYCxDesign unveiled “Art History 101: A Brush with Fashion,” a series of history-inspired installations by SVA students studying 3D design with Kevin O’Callaghan (BFA 1980 Media Arts). The installations are on view from 61st Street to 77th Street along Madison Avenue. Via Gotham To Go.
