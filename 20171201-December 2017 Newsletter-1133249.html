Community Lecture Series:
Concepts of Art and Play Therapy with Children Using Diverse Communication
Friday, December 8, 2017
133 West 21st Street, Room 101C
6:30pm-8:00pm

Sarah Valeri has ten years experience working with children with developmental delays and sensory impairments, as well as with children with histories of severe trauma. This work has inspired a strong respect for the use of more bodily and sensory focused communication. As a current candidate in the IPTAR Children's and Adolescent Program she combines psycho-dynamic theories within the practical aspects of communicating with  children through a wide range of  developmental levels.
