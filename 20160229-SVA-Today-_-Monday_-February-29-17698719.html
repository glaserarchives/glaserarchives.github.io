Be Nice, Be Visible, and Trust Your Gut!
SVA Alumni Affairs recently hosted a panel discussion on cultivating a life in the arts featuring Scott Bakal (BFA 1993 Illustration), Jenny Morgan (MFA 2008 Fine Arts), Kate Neckel (MFA 2002 Computer Art), Vincent Peone (BFA 2007 Film and Video), Annie Watt (BFA 1974 Media Arts) and Heidi Zito (MFA 2012 Photography, Video and Related Media). Check out their tips!
6:30pm: Cronies Screening
The Center for Communication and the SVA Theatre present a screening of Cronies, written and directed by Michael J. Larnell and executive produced by Spike Lee. A panel discussion will follow, featuring Larnell; Ro Reddick, the film's marketing and distribution strategist; and Miranda Sherman, director of external affairs, NYU Cinema Research Institute. At the SVA Theatre.
Press pick of the day
SVA Students Win Big in the Graphis New Talent Competition
The New Talent Annual for 2016 contains 186 entries dedicated to the work of BFA Advertising and BFA Design students, three times the number of next art and design school.

Take Note:
Filmmakers Dialogue Screening: City of God
SVA Filmmakers Dialogue invites you and a guest to attend their free preview screening of City of God and Q and A with Pulitzer Prize-winning food critic Jonathan Gold and director Laura Gabbert. Tuesday, March 1, 6:00pm sharp, SVA Theatre. Your guest (can be non-SVA) must arrive with you. Please be prepared to show your SVA ID at the door. Learn more about the film.
Filmmakers Dialogue Screening: The Wave
SVA Filmmakers Dialogue invites you and a guest to attend their free preview screening of the Norwegian disaster film, The Wave. Tuesday, March 1, 8:00pm, SVA Theatre. Your guest (can be non-SVA) must arrive with you. Please be prepared to show your SVA ID Card. Watch a preview.
