SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts August 22 - 28
FEATURES
Concept branding for Brooklyn Bridge Park by semifinalist and MFA Design student Mira Khandpur.

SVA Semifinalists in the Adobe Design Awards

SVA received 46 individual semifinal citations for the 2016 Adobe Design Achievement Awards, representing work by 15 graduate students and 12 undergraduates from five departments. The winners will be announced in September.

Improvisation and the Risk of Failure [Video]

Watch a clip of MFA Fine Arts faculty member Kara Rooney (MFA 2008 Art Writing and Criticism) discussing how improvisation is incorporated into her multidisciplinary performance work.
IN THE PRESS

Hoop Dreams

Time highlights the streetball photos of Anthony Geathers (BFA 2016 Photography and Video).

Designers for Good

AIGA interviews MFA Interaction Design Chair Liz Danzico, who talks about designing for social innovation.
EVENTS

DJ Set by Sol Nova (sOlnOva)

As part of "The Beat Goes On," DJ Sol Nova will play visceral, bass-laden, drum-heavy club constructions of contemporary electronic dance music, with a focus on music created by artists of the African diaspora. Organized by Elia Alba. Saturday, August 27, 4:00 - 6:00pm, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.
