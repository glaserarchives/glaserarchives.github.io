SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts August 8 - 14
FEATURES

SVA Short Takes with Jordan Matter [Video]

Watch photographer Jordan Matter discuss the 55 takes it took to get the perfect image of dancers leaping on Fifth Avenue, from his winter 2016 i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture, presented by MPS Digital Photography.

Celebrating the Movie Experience

BFA Film student Ameer Kazmi was named one of three finalists selected to create a 30-second spot celebrating the movie going experience, for a competition sponsored by The Coca-Cola Company and Regal Films.

SVA Alumni Win NYFA Fellowships

Eight SVA alumni were recently named recipients of the prestigious Artists' Fellowship, which is granted by the New York Foundation for the Arts (NYFA).
IN THE PRESS
Photograph by Nicole Bengiveno

Milton Glaser Still Hearts NY

The New York Times profiles the acting SVA chairman and faculty member, who designed the iconic "I Love NY" logo, and asks him what type of design he would create for the New York of 2016.

Finding Poetry in Sport Photography

Time highlights the portraits of Olympic athletes by Thomas Prior (BFA 2002 Photography).

Salvage Supperclub

NPR on the sustainable dinner parties hosted by Josh Treuhaft (MFA 2014 Design for Social Innovation), which raise awareness of food waste.
EVENTS
Photograph by MPS Digital Photography student Ping Wang

In Cylinder

A juried exhibition of work by SVA students and recent graduates that confronts different forms of identity and the challenges of the present moment. Organized by SVA Galleries.
