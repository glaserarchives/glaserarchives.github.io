Visual Opinion Call for Submissions and 2016 Calendar
Visual Opinion, SVA's student-run magazine, is accepting submissions on the theme of identity for its next issue. Students can submit original artwork (300 d.p.i. and 2,000 pixels on the longest side) to vo@sva.edu. The deadline is Wednesday, January 27. The Visual Opinion 2016 calendar is now available in building lobbies across campus.
