EVENTS

BFA Visual & Critical Studies Senior Show

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of work by its graduating class. Through Monday, May 15. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

6:30pm | ISO Cultural Partner Program Meet & Eat

SVA’s International Student Office Cultural Partner Program (CPP) hosts its last Meet & Eat, at which its members will present their group community service projects. 6:30 – 8:30pm. 24th Street Residence, 340 East 24th Street, media room. A reception will follow; Cuban food will be served and CPP applications for next year will be available.

Arsty on the covers that BFA Illustration faculty member Edel Rodriguez created for the German magazine Der Spiegel.
NYC PICKS

The Artist's Eye

A walkthrough of the exhibition "Exploratory Works: Drawings from the Department of Tropical Research Field Expeditions" with artist Alexis Rothman. Tickets $5; purchase here. 6:30 – 8:00pm. The Drawing Center, 35 Wooster Street.

"Ted Russell: Bob Dylan NYC 1961 – 1964"

An exhibition of early photographs by Ted Russell that capture Bob Dylan at key moments that established him as one of America’s greatest artists. Through Saturday, June 3. Steven Kasher Gallery, 515 West 26th Street.
TAKE NOTE

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 3D Screening

The Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 featuring Chris Pratt and Zoe Saldana. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Friday, May 12, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
