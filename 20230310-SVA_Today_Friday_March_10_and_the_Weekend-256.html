1:00pm | Ask Humanities and Sciences

It's time to register for the 2023 – 2024 academic year. Learn more about Humanities and Sciences courses and ask questions during the department’s open-hours event. Today, March 10, 1:00 – 2:00pm, online via Zoom.


There will be another open-hours session on Tuesday, March 14, 3:00 – 4:00pm, 310 East 22nd Street, Room 818G. You may also email Susan Kim at skim60@sva.edu to schedule an appointment.

2:30pm | NEDA Week “We’ve Got the Power” Exhibition

Join Student Health and Counseling Services and The Renfrew Center of New York at the SVA Library to recognize National Eating Disorder Awareness Week. Renfrew staff will be exhibiting a table with featured artwork from their former clients in recovery. Meet Renfrew and Student Health and Counseling Services staff, pick up resources and enjoy snacks. Today, March 10, 2:30 – 4:30pm. SVA Main Library, 380 Second Avenue, second floor.

3:15pm | New York International Children’s Film Festival Industry Forum

New York International Children’s Film Festival presents its 2023 Industry Forum, a series of exciting community conversations to celebrate, support and expand the children’s media field toward a more inclusive future. Register for free at nyicff.org/industry. Today, March 10, 3:15 –5:30pm. Chelsea Factory, 547 West 26th Street.
