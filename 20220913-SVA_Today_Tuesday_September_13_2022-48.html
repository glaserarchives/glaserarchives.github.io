Access Office Hours, Colleagues and Art in the New SVA Concourse

Beginning this fall, SVA faculty, students and staff will be able to access online face-to-face hours at multiple offices by visiting one website. The new SVA Concourse uses GatherTown, an immersive video-conferencing platform, to provide a virtual campus where you can walk your avatar to an SVA office and speak face-to-face with its staff.


Schedules for the participating offices are listed below, although more may be added later in the semester.


IT: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:00 – 3:00pm

SVA Library: Monday through Friday, 10:00 – 11:00am and 2:00 – 3:00pm

Office of Learning Technologies: Monday through Friday, 10:00 – 11:00am and 2:00 – 3:00pm

Student Affairs: Monday through Friday, 9:00am – 5:00pm


While in the Concourse, you can wander the space and discover SVA art and other historical artifacts from the SVA Archives. Don’t miss SVA’s Subway Series (down in the subway) and audio recordings from notable guest speakers such as Agnes Martin, Salvador Dalí and Mel Brooks, to name a few.


Access the SVA Concourse here. If you are new to the Concourse, watch a short video about logging into the space here.

Art Therapy In-Person or Over Zoom

MPS Art Therapy is offering free art therapy services to the SVA community. Helpful with stress and academic or personal issues, these confidential services are no-fee and facilitated by a second-year graduate student using art. Email Val Sereno at vsereno@sva.edu for more information.

Visual Opinion Is Hiring Interns

Visual Opinion interns are responsible for learning the overall production and distribution of each issue. Support the team with creative ideas and in all phases of production. All candidates must be: matriculated and full-time registered at SVA, located in the U.S., have a 3.0 GPA or higher and be proactive and organized. Please email your resume, statement of interest and portfolio or portfolio sample to vo@sva.edu. Applications close Monday, September 19, 5:00pm.

VASA Student Senator Application Is Open

Students are invited to apply to be a VASA Student Senator. Senators represent their major in interdepartmental collaborations and events. Get involved on campus and organize events for your peers; apply here. Contact senators@sva.edu with any questions.
