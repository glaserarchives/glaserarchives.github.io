ANNOUNCEMENTS

The Alumni Scholarship Awards Application Is Now Open!
Class of 2022: Do you need funding for your final projects? The SVA Alumni Society provides a wide variety of scholarships and awards to students at both the undergraduate and graduate levels in all majors, including the Alumni Scholarship Award program, established in 1999. Final-year undergraduate and graduate students are eligible to apply for Alumni Scholarship Awards to aid the completion of final projects. These cash awards are intended to cover project expenses and range from $500 to $2,000. In 2021, 59 projects received a total of $40,500 in funding.

The online application portal is now open. For further details and eligibility criteria and to apply, visit sva.edu/asa.
