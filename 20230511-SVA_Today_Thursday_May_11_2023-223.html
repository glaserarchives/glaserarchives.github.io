12:00pm | MFA Design for Social Innovation Thesis Showcase

MFA Design for Social Innovation presents its annual thesis show—this year, the class of 2023 thesis projects center community partners and collaborators to co-create for change. Today, May 11, 12:00 – 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

6:00pm | “The Yellow Wallpaper” Reception

An exhibition of artwork by 19 MFA Illustration as Visual Essay students based on Charlotte Perkins Gilman’s short story of the same name. Curated by department faculty member and BFA Comics and BFA Illustration Chair Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Reception: Tonight, May 11, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Monday, May 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

6:30pm | Summer Online Drawing Marathon with Peter Hristoff

NYC PICK

“The New Math: Geometric Plasticism, Ideal Visions + the Reductive Impulse” Reception

An exhibition of contemporary artists whose aims closely align with those of the transcendental painters, co-curated by Michael Gormley and SVA faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts) and featuring work by Ryan J. Brady (BFA 2012 Visual & Critical Studies), faculty member Kayla Gibbons (BFA 2011 Fine Arts), Kyra Husbands (BFA 2022 Visual & Critical Studies) and 2022 SVA Summer Residency artist Myke Karlowski. Reception: Tonight, May 11, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Saturday, June 10. Equity Gallery, 245 Broome Street.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

COVID-19 Updates

Starting today, May 11, SVA no longer requires proof of the COVID-19 vaccination and booster. You will no longer receive Cleared4 notifications about completing a daily health attestation to enter campus buildings—simply swipe your SVA ID at security desks. Masks are not required but are always welcome.


If you do test positive for COVID-19, you must continue to report it by emailing studenthealthalert@sva.edu or employee-health-alert@sva.edu. For more information, click here.
