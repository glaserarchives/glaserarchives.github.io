Monday, May 22, 2023

“Revisiting the Era of the It Girl”

Zackary Drucker’s (BFA 2005 Photography) new documentary looking back on the New York social scene of the late 2000s, Queenmaker: The Making of an It Girl, is featured in The Guardian.

EVENTS

Last Chance | “Selections From Thesis Projects”

An exhibition of work by 16 MFA Illustration as Visual Essay students from the class of 2023, curated by acting chair and thesis coordinator David Sandlin. Closes today, May 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Last Chance | “The Yellow Wallpaper”

An exhibition of artwork by 19 MFA Illustration as Visual Essay students based on Charlotte Perkins Gilman’s short story of the same name, curated by department faculty member and BFA Comics and BFA Illustration Chair Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Closes today, May 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

SVA IN THE PRESS

“Every Monster I Draw is Actually a Self-portrait”

Mu Pan (MFA 2007 Illustration as Visual Essay; BFA 2001 Illustration) talks about his provocative, large-scale paintings in an interview for The Not So Innocents Abroad.

NYC PICK

Inka Essenhigh at Miles McEnery Gallery

A solo exhibition of surreal paintings celebrating the natural world by Inka Essenhigh (MFA 1994 Fine Arts). Through Saturday, June 3. Miles McEnery Gallery, 511 West 22nd Street.

ANNOUNCEMENT

SVA Artist Residency Programs presents an exhibition of artwork by four program alumni exploring materials and techniques traditionally associated with crafts. Thursday, June 1, through Thursday, June 8. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21 Street.
