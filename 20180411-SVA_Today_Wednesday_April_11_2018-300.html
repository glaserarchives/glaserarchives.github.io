SVA Today
Wednesday, April 11, 2018

Short Film: "Just One Bite"

A film by MPS Directing alumni duo Amanda Alvich and Mika Orr is one of the five finalists in the Coca-Cola Regal Films' contest and is eligible to win this year's Fan Favorite Award.
EVENTS

2:00pm | Nicole Katz

MFA Products of Design presents a talk by Nicole Katz, the CEO of Paper Chase Press, a second-generation, family-run press and bindery in Los Angeles. Katz diversified Paper Chase Press through the development of its ecommerce platform, publishing imprint and collection of retail goods. Graduate students only. 2:00 – 4:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 7th floor.

3:00pm | Nacho Break for Sexual Assault Awareness Month

Take a nacho break with Arielle Kempler from Student Health and Counseling Services and Laurel Christy, SVA's Title IX Coordinator, and learn about Sexual Assault Awareness Month. 3:00 – 5:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

6:00pm | "The Haves and Have Nots" Reception

BFA Cartooning and BFA Illustration presents an exhibition of work by third-year students exploring themes of social injustice and class struggle. 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Saturday, April 21. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

6:00pm | MFA Design Venture 2018: Design and Discovery

MFA Design presents its thesis forum, where the 12 program graduates and design entrepreneurs will premiere their unique products. RSVP here. 6:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

"Shot With AR-15"

New York Daily News on these ads by fourth-year BFA Advertising students Ji Kim and Andy Koo, which aim to combat gun violence by mimicking a popular Apple iPhone ad campaign.
NYC PICK

Fresh Ground Pepper Festival

This festival offers a new way of thinking about arts presentation, featuring art experiments, work-in-progress showings, community work spaces, parades, workshops, board games, and more. Ticket prices vary; schedule here. Through Saturday, April 21. New Ohio Theatre, 154 Christopher Street.
TAKE NOTE

Searching for Sketchbooks

SVA Sketchbooks is a video series featuring the work of students and faculty from across the College. If you would like to have yours shown in a video, submit three or four JPEG images from your sketchbook to style@sva.edu by Monday, April 30, for consideration.

Writing Visual Culture in Cambridge, England

Become a better observer and interpreter by studying and writing about various visual media on this SVA Destinations trip to Cambridge, England, taking place from July 22 – August 5. Worth three Humanities and Sciences credits. Apply here by Monday, April 30. For more information, click here or email Laurie Johenning at ljohenning@sva.edu.
