Celebrate Veterans Day
Today is Veterans Day, dedicated to honoring the service and sacrifices of the veterans of the U.S. Armed Forces. SVA—which since its founding has served veterans looking to further their education under the G.I. Bill—is proud of its veteran students and alumni, and its long-running designation as a Military Friendly School. For more information about or to get involved with the SVA student-run Veteran Coalition of Arts organization, visit the VCA Facebook and Instagram pages.

The Scharf Schak Is Back
The Scharf Schak—a converted newspaper kiosk that sold art and designs by Kenny Scharf (BFA 1981 Fine Arts) that operated in Manhattan in the early 1990s—has been reimagined as a pop-up in Seoul, in a collaboration with the Baik Art gallery. Via The Korea Times.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Friday | VASA Fest
The Visual Arts Student Association (VASA) represents the interests and needs of students at SVA, supporting student clubs and student senators, as well as a variety of activities designed to help students interact with and meet their peers. Students who are interested in becoming a VASA member, starting a club or getting to know more about the student government are encouraged to drop by VASA Fest. There will also be a clubs info session, at 3:30pm, and a VASA elections info session, at 4:00pm, for those interested in running for student government. Friday, November 12, 3:00 – 4:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Monday, November 15 | Yoga for Artists: TLC for Hands, Wrists and Shoulders
Join Student Health and Counseling Services and instructor Melissa Boo for a free yoga class and learn stretches for hands, wrists and shoulders that help release tension, improve flexibility and increase range of motion. The best way for artists and creatives to avoid injury and pain is through prevention! Open to the entire SVA community. Monday, November 15, 11:00am – 12:00pm ET, online. RSVP required.

Friday, November 19 | WSVA Unplugged
SVA's acoustic live talent show is back and in person! Save the date for Friday, November 19, 7:00pm in the SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. Got songs to sing? Jokes to share? Musicians, comedians and performers heed our call and join us at this open mic event. Sign up to perform by Tuesday, November 16, 12:00am ET.

Visual Opinion Call for Submissions
