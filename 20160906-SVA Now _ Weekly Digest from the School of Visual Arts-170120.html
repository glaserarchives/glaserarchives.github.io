"The Beat Goes On" Performances

As part of "The Beat Goes On," Paul D. Miller (DJ Spooky) will perform in his listening room on Wednesday, September 7, 6:00 - 8:00pm, and Michael Paul Britto (Brittofied) will DJ on Thursday, September 8, 6:00 - 8:00pm, at the SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

"C, D, E, No F, G" Reception

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of work that investigates urban objects and their environment, contemplates the irrationality of reality, juxtaposes found and discarded objects with the body, and challenges perspective through the context of installation. Reception Thursday, September 8, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Through Wednesday, September 21, SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.

To a Point...
