Joseph Maida Contributes to Charlotte Cotton's Latest Publication

Chair Joseph Maida has contributed an essay entitled "Pictures without Words" on gender, language, and photography to Charlotte Cotton's new book Public Private, Secret: On Photography and the Configuration of Self, published by Aperture and ICP.
Elizabeth Bick and Isaac Diggs for Harper's

Faculty members Elizabeth Bick (left, detail) and Isaac Diggs (right, detail) are featured in July's Harper's Magazine, with photographs for an article on the decline of New York City due to gentrification.
Esopus Magazine Presents: John Edmonds and Marjon Carlos

Join faculty member John Edmonds and writer & editor Marjon Carlos as they discuss Edmonds’ most recent series, Tribe. The conversation will focus on race, representation, art history, and pop culture will take place at the SVA Library West, 133 W 21st Street, Lower Level, on September 7, 7PM.
Simons Finnerty for JW Anderson

Alum Simons Finnerty (2018), winner of JW Anderson's "YOUR PICTURE OUR FUTURE," has photographed their Autumn Winter 2018 Campaign.
Carlos Jaramillo at W83

Alum Carlos Jaramillo (2015) has an exhibition of photos documenting life in and around Lurigancho, a prison in Lima, Peru. Behind Bars is on view through September 23, 2018.
Justine Kurland at Higher Pictures

Alum Justine Kurland (1996) is in a two-person show at Higher Pictures with her late father, painter Bruce Kurland. Airless Spaces is on view through August 31, 2018.
Anne Lamb at Studio Uzi

Alum Anne Lamb (2011) was included in the recent group exhibition The Violators, a group exhibition featuring 15 queer artists whose works have been targeted by censorship on social media.
Jessica Pettway Artist Talk at Photoville NYC

Join alum Jessica Pettway (2016), one of the inaugural Lit List finalists, for a 30 minute artist talk at The Authority Collective’s container at Photoville NYC on Saturday, September 15, at 5:30PM.
Devin Yalkin in The New York Times

Alum Devin Yalkin (2010) has photographs included in this article about the never-ending war in the Middle East and its toll on a generation of soldiers.
Hyperallergic Features Francena Ottley

Current student Francena Ottley is featured on Hyperallergic's review of Constellations, an exhibition that was recently on view at SVA Gallery Chelsea.
Exhibit in The 214 East 21st Street Galleries!

Senior Books Have Arrived!

Students may pick up their books from Monday to Friday between the hours of 9AM and 5PM at 214 E 21st Street, Fifth Floor, Room 510-A. Students have until September 14, 2018, to pick up their book. Please contact Bina at galtera@sva.edu with any questions.
Apply for the Barbara Hammer Lesbian Experimental Filmmaking Grant

The Barbara Hammer Lesbian Experimental Filmmaking Grant is an annual grant that will be awarded to self-identified lesbians for making visionary moving-image art. The deadline to apply is October 1, 2018.
Feature Shoot Emerging Photographer Call for Entries

The Feature Shoot Emerging Photography Awards is an international photography competition for up-and-coming image-makers who are not yet represented by a gallery or agent. The deadline to apply is October 1, 2018.
Open Call - Stephen Shore Curates NYC Billboards for SaveArtSpace

SaveArtSpace and Stephen Shore are collaborating to curate artists' work onto NYC billboards. The deadline to apply is October 3, 2018.
