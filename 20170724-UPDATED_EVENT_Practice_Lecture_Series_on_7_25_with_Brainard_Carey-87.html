MFA Art Practice would like to announce a change to the Practice Lecture Series speaker lineup. Instead of Christopher Lew and Mia Locks, the speaker for tomorrow, July 25 will be Brainard Carey. As planned, the lecture will take place at 12:30pm, at 335 West 16th Street, Room 501H, and is free and open to the public. 

Brainard Carey was born in Manhattan and raised in Yonkers, NY, and is a conceptual artist and author with his wife, Delia Carey, who is also an author and artist. They live in New York City and New Haven with their son. Carey publishes an educational newsletter to develop the professional careers of artists and produces a number of online courses to do the same. He also interviews artists, curators, writers and architects for his ongoing Yale University radio series.
