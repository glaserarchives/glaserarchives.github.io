ANNOUNCEMENTS

Today | Bystander Intervention Training

An interactive training session that will give you the tools to intervene safely and effectively in online abuse using the nonprofit advocacy organization Right To Be’s “5 Ds” of bystander intervention. This training session will go over what online harassment is and how to identify abusive tactics, the severe impact online abuse can have and what concrete strategies can be used to distract, delegate, document, delay or directly intervene in a way that is safe, effective and centered on the needs of the person being targeted. Today, February 17, 4:00 – 5:00pm, online.

Next Week | Community Lecture Series: Crescendo

MPS Art Therapy presents a virtual presentation by Richard Lacayo, former Time art critic, on how artists like Francisco Goya, Henri Matisse, Edward Hopper and Louise Nevelson made some of their most profound work in the final decades of their lives. Friday, February 24, 6:00 – 7:30pm, online.
