Opens Today | “Milton Glaser: POP”

Steven Heller, design historian and co-chair of MFA Design; artist, designer and longtime SVA faculty member Mirko Ilić; and Beth Kleber, head of the Milton Glaser Design Study Center and Archives and the SVA Archives, present an exhibition based on Milton Glaser: POP, their book collecting work created by the late designer, illustrator and SVA faculty member Milton Glaser during the 1960s and ’70s. Through Monday, June 5. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
