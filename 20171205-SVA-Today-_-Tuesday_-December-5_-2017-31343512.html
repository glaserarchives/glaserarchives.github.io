Paris Impressionists: A Walking Tour

SVA Destinations presents a spring break trip to Paris. Refresh your creative palate with the masters of impressionist painting; explore Paris and its surrounding suburbs by walking in the footsteps of artists such as Degas, Monet, Van Gogh and Renoir; discover where these masters set their easels and visit the museums where their work is exhibited; and learn about the main characteristics of impressionist painting and how it changed art history. For more information, contact Aziza Gaines, program coordinator, at againes@sva.edu or 212.592.2070, or visit the SVA Destinations website.
