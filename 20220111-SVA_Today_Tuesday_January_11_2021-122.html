SVA Today
Tuesday, January 11, 2022

SVA's nurse, Abby Wohl, answers your questions regarding COVID-19 and other health-related concerns. Read below for the latest Q&A and use this link to see the question archive. You can also submit any questions you have here.

SVA is requiring a COVID-19 booster shot by Monday, January 24. However, I recently received my second dose of Sinovac (October 5). Can I still access SVA buildings on or after January 24?

SVA is only requiring COVID-19 booster shots by January 24 for all community members who are eligible for them, meaning:
  • it has been two months since your first dose of J & J; or
  • five months since your second dose of Pfizer; or
  • six months since your second dose of Moderna, Sinopharm BIBP, Sinovac, Covaxin, Covishield, AstraZeneca/Oxford, etc.
Since your last dose of Sinovac was given on October 5, you are not due to upload proof of your COVID-19 booster shot until Tuesday, April 5. Therefore, you can still access SVA buildings.
