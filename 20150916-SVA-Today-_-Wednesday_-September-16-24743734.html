The Making of "Taking the Plunge" [Video]
Go behind the scenes to watch BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects students make their Student Academy Award-winning film, Taking the Plunge. Chihyun Lee's film Zoe also won a Student Academy Award, and students receive their awards at a ceremony on Thursday, September 17.
7:00pm: Small Camera, Big Results: Professional iPhone Photography
Travel, portrait, stock and iPhone photographer Jack Hollingsworth discusses his technique along with the apps and accessories he uses to create his mobile photography. Presented by MPS Digital Photography. Wednesday, September 16, 7:00pm, 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.
