SVA Today
Wednesday, September 28
FEATURE
Installation view of "Transitions." Photo by Carla and Francis DiTommaso.

Art and Art Therapy in the Land of Van Gogh

A look at "Transitions," an exhibition at Saint-Paul de Mausole in Saint-Rémy, France, featuring work by MPS Digital Photography students and alumni and on view through October 25.
EVENTS

10:00am: Yearbook Photoshoot

Portrait sessions for the class of 2017. 10:00am - 2:00pm, 214 East 21st Street, Room 309.

5:45pm: One Love Workshop

One Love presents a workshop to raise awareness of domestic violence and relationship abuse. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

6:00pm: "The Secret: Shape of Destiny" Reception

MFA Fine Arts presents a collaborative exhibition of sculpture, painting and video created by students Scott Ferguson and Andrew Jilka, and curated by Zoe Frederick. Exhibition runs through Friday, October 14, SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Photo by Lauren Casselberry

Honoring Creative Leaders

The Louise Blouin Foundation recently honored Sarah Sze (MFA 1997 Fine Arts) at the Blouin Creative Leadership Summit for "expanding our understanding of aesthetics and the physical world around us."
NYC PICKS OF THE DAY

Pattinson x Cronenberg

Screenings of the collaborations between director David Cronenberg and actor Robert Pattinson. Tickets are $14. Screenings start at 4:30pm, BAM Rose Cinemas, 30 Lafayette Avenue.

Lisa Joy Performance

A performance by MFA Computer Art faculty member Benton C. Bainbridge, with interactive video synthesizer objects and single-channel digital media. RSVP required: rsvp@movingpictures.gallery. 7:00pm, Harvestworks, 596 Broadway, #602.
TAKE NOTE

BK BioReactor

The Visible Futures Lab presents an exhibition of work by artists-in-residence BK BioReactor documenting the collective's investigation into the microbiology of Brooklyn's Gowanus Canal, an EPA-designated Superfund site. Members of the group will discuss their work on Friday, September 30, 7:00pm. Friday, September 30, through Tuesday, October 25, Visible Futures Lab, 132 West 21st Street, 7th floor.
