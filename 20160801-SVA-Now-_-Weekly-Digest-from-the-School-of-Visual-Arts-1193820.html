SVA Now
A digest of news and events from the School of Visual Arts August 1 - 7
FEATURES
A poster for TypeCon, by BFA Design student Jens Marklund

The July Hot Five

The July roundup of the top five most discussed projects from SVA Portfolios features stellar rebranding work, illustrated ghosts and spirits, a backward idea that's brilliant, and more.

Meet Tomorrow's Design Leaders

Three BFA Design alumni were selected as 2016 New Visual Artists by Print. Read more about the designers' philosophies and inspirations.

After School Special 2016: Alumni Film & Animation Festival

For the third year in a row, SVA presents its multi-day festival featuring some of the College’s most accomplished film, television, animation and visual effects alumni, and will include screenings of Deadpool, Peanuts, Beauty and the Beast, and more. The festival takes place Thursday, September 15, through Sunday, September 18, at the SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Announcing Untitled (Gender and Representation) 2016

BFA Photography and Video present Untitled (Gender and Representation) 2016, a day-long symposium featuring Kate Bornstein, Zachary Drucker (BFA 2005 Photography), Grace Dunham, Zanele Muholi, Ethan James Green, Ron Gregg, M. Lamar, and more. The conference will take place Saturday, October 1, at the SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
IN THE PRESS
Freedom to Vote (and make it count), by MFA Illustration as Visual Essay faculty member Mirko Ilić

Designing for Democracy

Fast Company on "Thoughts on Democracy: Freedom to Vote 2016," an exhibition curated by MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller that opens at the Wolfsonian in Miami on August 26.

Partying with the 1%

Business Insider highlight photos from Privilege, a new photo book by BFA Photography and Video faculty member Jessica Craig-Martin that chronicles the social lives of the wealthy elite.

Video: Sharing Art on Social Media

In Dazed, Lorna Simpson (BFA 1982 Photography) discusses her work with members of the Art Hoe Collective.

Video: Road to Rio with Team USA Artist

Watch NBC New York's Bruce Beck talk with official Team USA artist Charles Fazzino (BFA 1977 Media Arts) about his 3D print for the Rio Olympics.
EVENTS
Portrait by MPS Art Therapy student Mahala Sacra

A Toothache

A juried exhibition of work by SVA students and a recent graduate that blurs the line between private and public life. Reception Wednesday, August 10, 6:00 - 8:00pm. Saturday, August 6, through Saturday, September 10, SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Summer Residency Exhibition

An exhibition of work by participants in SVA's 2016 Summer Residency programs. Thursday, August 4, 6:00 - 8:00pm, SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

LoopdeLoop

BFA Animation and BFA Film present LoopdeLoop, a bi-monthly animation festival and screening. The theme of this edition of LoopdeLoop is pranks. Monday, August 8, 7:00pm, 209 East 23rd Street, Room 502.
