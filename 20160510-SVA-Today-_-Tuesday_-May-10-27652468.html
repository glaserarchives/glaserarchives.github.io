And the Winner Is . . .
This year's Dusty Film & Animation Festival award winners are announced at tonight's ceremony, 6:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. RSVP to dustyawards@sva.edu. The ceremony will also stream live at dusty.sva.edu.

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by the award-winning documentary photographer. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Star Wars-007 Mash Up [Video]
Mashable highlights BFA Design student Kurt Rauffer's recreated title sequence for The Empire Strikes Back, which features James Bond-inspired graphics and an unused Radiohead song from the last Bond installment, Spectre.
NYC PICKS OF THE DAY
2016 Student Scholarship Competition
The Society of Illustrators presents an exhibition of work by over 200 illustration students, including many from SVA. A jury of professionals will select the most outstanding works. A reception and awards ceremony will take place on Friday, May 13, 6:00pm. Through Saturday, June 4, Society of Illustrators, 128 East 63rd Street.
Galaxy Was a Memoir, Earth Was a Souvenir
Artist Kimsooja discusses her work with MA Curatorial Practice Chair Steven Henry Madoff. 7:00pm, Cooper Union, 41 Cooper Square.
TAKE NOTE
Vital Signs: MA Design Research Festival
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents graduating students discussing their thesis projects. Commentary moderated by IDEO design researcher and BBC journalist Alex Gallafent. Click here for a full schedule. Tuesday, May 17, 2:00 - 6:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.
