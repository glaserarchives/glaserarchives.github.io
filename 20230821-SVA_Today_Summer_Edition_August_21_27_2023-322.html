SVA Celebrates Its Subway Posters Series With a Retrospective Exhibition

“Underground Images: A History,” a new exhibition showcasing the full collection of SVA’s iconic “subway series” posters, which have covered the walls of NYC transit stations for almost 75 years, opens next week.

A Tour Through 2023’s SVA Destinations

This summer, students reinvigorated their passion for art through SVA Destinations, the College’s travel-based, short-term study programs. Read all about it and see photos from visits to Arizona, Egypt, Southern France and more at the link above.

SVA IN THE PRESS

Charles Traub

“Public Beaches are Remarkably Democratic”

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Chair Charles Traub talks about the significance of public beaches in an interview for AnOther on the occasion of his new photo book, Remembrances of Summers’ Past.

“People Move Through a Constant Stream of Temporary Spaces”

Se Young Yim (MFA 2022 Fine Arts) talks to Woosik Choi (MFA 2022 Fine Arts) about “vulnerable physicality” and how the experience of temporarily living in various cities has influenced her artwork. Via Artefuse.

Charish’s Four Designers to Know

Adam Greco (BFA 2007 Interior Design) is featured in a Chairish roundup of “four designers to know.”

The Last Mermaid

Peter Ash Lee (MPS 2012 Fashion Photography; BFA 2009 Photography) is featured in AnOther for his project photographing the free divers of Jeju Island, soon to be published as a book, The Last Mermaid.

Reimagining the Works of Hans Holbein the Younger

Hyun Jung Lee (BFA 2022 Design), winner of a 2023 Graphis New Talent Gold Award, is featured in the Graphis blog for the identity system she created to capture the works of Renaissance artist Hans Holbein.

NYC PICKS

Monday Movie Nights at Bryant Park finishes this summer with a screening of Good Burger (1997). Free. Monday, August 21; food vendors open at 4:00pm, screening begins at 8:00pm. Bryant Park, Sixth Avenue between 40th and 42nd Streets.

“Water”

An installation solo show by Graciela Cassel (MFA 2014 Fine Arts) featuring a series of sculptures and video animations that explore our connection and reliance on waterways. Through Sunday, August 27. 405-B House, Colonels Row at Governors Island.

“Birkin”

A solo exhibition exploring Alexander Si’s (MFA 2021 Fine Arts) construction of an exact Birkin bag replica. Through Monday, September 4. Home Gallery, 291 Grand Street.

Create a Video/Animation, Win $500

SVA Theatre seeks a 10- to 20-second pre-roll video (animated or live action) to play before film screenings shown during this September’s After School Special 2023: SVA’s Alumni Film and Animation Festival.


Set the video to music that you have created yourself, own the copyright for, have obtained a license for, or is in the public domain, and please note its source when submitting. Download logos for the festival, SVA and SVA Theatre here. View examples of past winning videos here and here.


Upload submissions to Vimeo or YouTube, and send an email with a link to the video, your name, graduation year and program major to Adam Natale, director of SVA Theatre, at anatale@sva.edu, by Friday, August 25, 5:00pm. Email Natale with any questions. Final video format should be a digital file (e.g., QuickTime ProRes). The winner will receive $500.
