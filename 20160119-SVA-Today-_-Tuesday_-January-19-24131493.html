Call for Submissions to The Match Factory
The Match Factory, the Humanities and Sciences Department's biannual online literary magazine, is seeking submissions for its spring 2016 issue. Students, faculty and staff may submit poetry, short stories, film scripts, one-act plays, music or film reviews, essays, personal essays, memoirs or any other kind of writing. Submissions can be sent through the website's form or emailed to SVA faculty member and Match Factory editor Edwin Rivera at riveramatchfactory@gmail.com. The deadline for submissions is Friday, April 8. The fall 2015 issue is available online.
