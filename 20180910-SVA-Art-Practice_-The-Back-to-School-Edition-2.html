SEPTEMBER SVA ART PRACTICE NEWS

Our summer 2018 residency is over, but we're back to school this month, online, with fall semester classes. And our current MFA candidates, recent alumni and faculty are showing work all over.
Christine Stiver's (AP 2017) immersive mural installation, How Many Nipples Does a Horse Have? recently opened at the Baltimore-based project space, St. Charles. She also has a collaborative installation, Shadow Play, with Ben Quesnel (AP 2017), currently on view at the 4heads Governors Island Art Fair. 
Giulia Mangoni (AP 2018) will be showing new work at the Orto Botanico (Botanical Gardens) in Rome's Trastevere district, from September 15th - September 22nd. 
Nico Wheadon's review of The Racial Imaginary Institute: On Whiteness in the Brooklyn Rail highlighted work by Baseera Khan (AP Faculty).
