Events

This Week | SVA Library West Welcome Week
SVA Library West is hosting a week of video game-themed events during Welcome Week! Stop by for Mario Kart tournaments, Mortal Kombat movie screenings, displays of video game concept art and more. There will be prizes for winners and treats for those who drop in. See a full list of events here. Through Sunday, September 4. SVA Library West, 133 West 21st street.

Tomorrow | “The Health Poster Project”
An exhibition of posters by 24 students from BFA Illustration, MFA Illustration as Visual Essay and the 2022 Summer Illustration Residency, curated by BFA Illustration and BFA Comics Chair Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Tuesday, August 30 – Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Tomorrow | “MPS Fashion Photography Class of 2022”
MPS Fashion Photography presents an exhibition of artworks by the class of 2022. Tuesday, August 30 – Monday, September 19. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Tomorrow | Introduction to the Magic of Watercolor
SVA Continuing Education presents a watercolor workshop with faculty member Andrew Chang (MFA 1987 Illustration as Visual Essay), who will introduce the materials for watercolor painting and demonstrate a few simple techniques. Tuesday, August 30, 7:00 – 8:30pm, on Zoom.

Friday | “The Masters Series: Lynsey Addario”
An exhibition of work by Lynsey Addario, acclaimed photographer, MacArthur Genius Grant and Pulitzer Prize recipient, as part of the 32nd annual Masters Series Award and Exhibition. Curated by Maya Benton and Perri Hofmann. Friday, September 2 – Saturday, October 29. Reception and Award presentation: Thursday, September 8, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Friday | Come to the Health Fair!
Student Health and Counseling and Nurse Abby are excited to invite all SVA students to the Health and Wellness fair happening during Welcome Week. All SVA students are welcome! The fair will feature live yoga demos, health and wellness tips, health professionals to answer your questions, campus store giveaways and so much more! Friday, September 2, 1:00 – 3:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Saturday | Three Thousand Years of Longing Screening
SVA Theatre presents a screening of Three Thousand Years of Longing, starring Idris Elba and Tilda Swinton. Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Visit svatheatre.com for SVA Theatre’s current COVID-19 guidelines, including information on masks and vaccination requirements. Saturday, September 3, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Ongoing | “Dwelling in Motion”
A juried exhibition of works that analyze the constantly growing relationship between habitat and inhabitant. Through Monday, September 19. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

NYC AND ONLINE PICKS

“Wildflowers in the Concrete Jungle”
An online exhibition highlighting artwork by staff across Pace’s global locations, featuring work by Amanda Kopp (BFA 2011 Fine Arts). Through Saturday, September 17. Pace Gallery Online.

The Six Foot Platform
An experimental public art and performance residency program installed in Dumbo featuring Brooklyn-based artists. Through Monday, September 12. Located at the intersection of Washington Street and Water Street in Dumbo, Brooklyn.

Hester Street Fair on Labor Day Weekend
Hester Street Fair kicks off of Labor Day weekend with a roster of unique and buzz-worthy vendors, local food and a live DJ. Saturday, September 3, 11:00 am – 6:00pm. Pier 17, 89 South Street.

West Indian American Day Carnival
The largest Caribbean Carnival in North America returns to Brooklyn, marching down Eastern Parkway in celebration of Caribbean culture, arts, food, history, traditions and people. Monday, September 5, parade begins at 11:00am. The parade starts at Schenectady Avenue and ends at Grand Army Plaza, Brooklyn.

Rockaway Beach Bazaar
A bazaar on the beach featuring local food, pop-ups and live music. Free. Saturday, September 3 – Monday, September 5. Sand Shark Bar, Rockaway Bazaar, 9701 Shore Front Boulevard, Rockaway Beach, Queens.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Free Museum Admission with SVA ID Is Back!
As a benefit to students, faculty and staff, SVA maintains memberships at various museums. With your SVA ID, you can enjoy free and discounted access to the Brooklyn Museum, Museum of Modern Art, MoMA PS1, New Museum of Contemporary Art and Whitney Museum of American Art. For more details, visit the MyID dashboard > Department Resources Page > Museum Benefits, or click here. Contact Miranda Pierce at mpierce1@sva.edu or ext. 2305 with any questions.

Virtual Narcan Training with Nurse Abby
Join Nurse Abby Wohl from Student Health and Counseling Services for a virtual SVA staff and faculty Narcan training session to learn more about how to reduce risk and prevent an opioid overdose. As a Narcan trainee, you will receive your own Narcan kit. Register here by Tuesday, September 20, to attend the session on Wednesday, September 21. If you are unable to attend, additional trainings will be offered throughout the year. Email Wohl at awohl@sva.edu for more information.
