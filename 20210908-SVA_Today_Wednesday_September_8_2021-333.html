ANNOUNCEMENTS

Download the GoSVA Mobile App
The GoSVA mobile app is available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and Google Play Store for Android. This new mobile app provides on-the-go access to all SVA essential systems and information for students, faculty, staff and visiting guests. If you have the prior version of the GoSVA app installed on your device please uninstall it before installing this new version. More information and links to the app stores can be found at go.sva.edu. An installation guide is available at technology.sva.edu.

Monday | The Killing of Kenneth Chamberlain Screening
SVA Filmmakers Dialogue would like to invite you to join actor Frankie Faison, actor-producer Enrico Natale and writer-director David Midell for a free advance screening of The Killing of Kenneth Chamberlain on Monday, September 13, 6:30pm, at SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. RSVP here to attend. SVA ID cards and proof of vaccination must be shown for admittance; see full SVA Theatre COVID-19 protocols here.

Based on the true story of the events that led to the death of Kenneth Chamberlain Sr., The Killing of Kenneth Chamberlain tells the story of an elderly African American veteran with bipolar disorder, who was killed during a conflict with police officers who were dispatched to check on him.

Maya Lin: Ghost Forest
