Congrats, Class of 2021!
At SVA's 46th commencement exercises last night, President David Rhodes conferred degrees on some 1,000 graduates and artist and faculty member Marilyn Minter gave a forthright, inspiring speech. Read more about the ceremony here.

SVA Wins a Webby!
SVA’s Digital Viewbook won a Webby Award in the category of School/University and is a Webby Honoree in the Best Use Of Animation or Motion Graphics category! Read our Q&A with Eric Corriel, digital strategy director, to learn more about the innovative viewbook site.
EVENTS THIS WEEK

Tuesday | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Tuesday, May 25, will be the last day of SVA's International Student Office virtual drop-in hours for the 2020 – 2021 academic year. From 9:30 – 11:00am ET, students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link.

Individual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week. To schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

Tuesday | i3 Photo Lecture: David Maisel
David Maisel's photography, video and painting work focuses on power and the production of space by examining landscapes, objects and archives that are off-limits, quarantined or hidden from view. Join him for a Zoom lecture hosted by MPS Digital Photography. Tuesday, May 25, 7:00pm ET.

Wednesday | Faculty Book Club
For the next book club meeting on Wednesday, May 26, the group will discuss the documentary Through a Lens Darkly by Thomas Allen Harris (2014, 93 minutes), which is available for free on Kanopy through the SVA Library.

Join the Zoom meeting led by faculty member and SVA Continuing Education Associate Director Keren Moscovitch, 4:30 – 5:30pm ET. As a reminder, we invite all faculty to join us on Canvas for more readings and discussion, all of which can be done at your own pace.

Thursday | Career Development Virtual Office Hours
Join SVA Career Development's weekly virtual office hours Thursday, May 27, 3:00 – 4:00pm ET. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

Call for Submissions: In Solidarity More Than Ever
In support of the new federal anti-Asian hate crimes legislation, SVA is renewing its recent exhibition, "In Solidarity: SVA Celebrates Its Asian and Asian American Communities," with "In Solidarity More Than Ever."

SVA students are again invited to participate in this effort by submitting original artwork that supports and portrays the beauty, diversity and strength of Asian communities. They may also include a very brief artist statement on their piece and why they chose it for this initiative. Students can send a hi-res image (300 ppi) of their original work in any desired medium, along with their SVA ID, department affiliation and year of graduation to Bill Martino, wmartino@sva.edu.
