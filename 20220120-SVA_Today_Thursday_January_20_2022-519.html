Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 12:30 – 2:00pm ET, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today for a one-hour session with Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross. She will be available on Zoom to field questions about job searching, interviewing and other career-related topics. Join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join. Today, 10:00 – 11:00am ET.

12:00pm | The Creators of the Next documenta Speak
Join Farid Rakun of artist collective ruangrupa, Lara Khaldi from the documenta fifteen artistic team and documenta curatorial assistant and SVA alumnus Jasa McKenzie for a special conversation previewing documenta’s opening this June. Presented by MA Curatorial Practice. Today, 12:00 – 1:00pm ET, online. Register here.

IN TOWN AND ONLINE

INterSECTS
The New York Public Library presents “INterSECTS: Where Arthropods and Homo Sapiens Meet,” an exhibition by graphic novelist and BFA Cartooning faculty member Peter Kuper, featuring illustrations of various insects interacting with the library’s architectural spaces. Through Saturday, August 13. Stephen A. Schwarzman Building, 476 Fifth Avenue.

Call for Submissions: Lunar New Year Poster Competition
The International Student Office is calling for poster submissions with the theme of Lunar New Year or this coming year's zodiac animal, the tiger. All submitted posters will be showcased on the ISO Instagram page, and a winner will receive a $100 gift certificate. To participate, students must complete this form and email a poster as a JPEG to Yoko Anderson at yanderson@sva.edu by Wednesday, January 26. Open to all students.
