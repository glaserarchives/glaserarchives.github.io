EVENTS

4:00pm | Visual Development/Concept Art Workshop

SVA’s Women in Animation club invites students to an interactive presentation about assembling a visual development portfolio with tips on world building and environmental design. Participants are required to bring their own Wacom pens for use on the Cintiqs. Today, November 30, 4:00 – 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 601C.

6:00pm | SVA Graduate Admissions Alumni Panel

Prospective graduate students are invited to meet Graduate Admissions staff and hear from a panel of graduate alumni. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer 2023 or fall 2023 admission. Tonight, November 30, 6:00 – 8:00pm, online.

6:30pm | The Cyberfeminism Index With Mindy Seu at SVA Library West

SVA Library West presents a discussion with Mindy Seu about her project The Cyberfeminism Index—a digital collection of resources related to the umbrella term “cyberfeminism.” A presentation by Seu about the project will be followed by a conversation with BFA Design student Briana Rengifo, moderated by Barbara Calderón, SVA librarian. Tonight, November 30, 6:30 – 8:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Class of 2023: Don’t Forget to Apply for the Alumni Scholarship Awards!

Do you need funding for your final projects? Final-year undergraduate and graduate students are eligible to apply for Alumni Scholarship Awards to aid the completion of final projects. These cash awards are intended to cover project expenses and range from $500 to $2,000. In 2022, 67 projects were funded a total of $52,500.


The online application portal closes on Monday, December 12. Attend the final How to Apply Session on Zoom, Monday, December 5, 4:00pm; RSVP here.


For further details and eligibility criteria and to apply, visit sva.edu/asa.
