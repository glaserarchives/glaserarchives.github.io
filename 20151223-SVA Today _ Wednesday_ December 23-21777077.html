Closing Notice
Administrative offices will be closed from Thursday, December 24, through Sunday, January 3. Spring semester classes begin on Monday, January 11.
NYC Winter Recess Picks
Kwanzaa Celebration: Regeneration Night
The Apollo Theater's annual celebration features Abdel Salaam’s Forces of Nature Dance Theatre. Tickets start at $20. Sunday, December 27, 2:00pm and 7:30pm, Apollo Theater, 253 West 125th Street.
Good Riddance Day
Forget the past by shredding all unwelcome memories of 2015 and get ready to make a fresh start in 2016. Monday, December 28, noon - 1:00pm, Times Square.
New Year's Eve Fireworks
View the annual fireworks display from vantages along Grand Army Plaza, the Park's West Drive and Prospect Park West between Grand Army Plaza and 9th Street. Thursday, December 31, 11:00pm - midnight, Brooklyn.
Midnight Run 2015
Ring in the new year with a four-mile run along Central Park's "inner loop," featuring fireworks, two DJs and a sparkling cider halfway toast. $55 registration fee for New York Road Runners members through Wednesday, December 30. $60 on the day of the event. $60 and $65 for non-members, respectively. Thursday, December 31, 11:59pm, 72nd Street Transverse near Bethesda Terrace.
Berlin Metropolis: 1918 - 1933
An exhibition of approximately 300 works focused on the city's Weimar period. $20 general admission, $10 students and seniors. Through Monday, January 4. Neue Galerie, 1048 Fifth Avenue.
