Preventing the Flu

Flu season is here. To keep yourself and the SVA community healthy, remember to:

-Consider getting a flu shot; find locations here.
-Wash your hands regularly.
-Avoid touching your mouth, nose or eyes.
-Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke.
-Clean frequently touched surfaces.
-Support and encourage SVA students, staff and faculty who have a fever to stay home.

If you do get sick:
-Cover coughs and sneezes with tissues or by coughing into the inside of your elbow.
-Stay home from school/work for at least 24 hours after your fever is gone without the use of a fever-reducing medicine.

For more information, go to sva.edu/flu.
