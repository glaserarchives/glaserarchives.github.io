Subject: AAPI Faculty/Staff Forum—Thursday, April 22
From: SVA Info
Date: 4/19/21, 4:08 PM
To: allstaff@sva.edu, allfaculty@sva.edu

Dear Colleagues,


Within the past year, members within the Asian/Asian American communities have been facing an increased number of instances of harassment, discrimination and more recently, physical violence.  In light of the pandemic and not having met in-person, an open conversation to discuss with our AAPI peers across campus about how they have been coping is needed. 


On Thursday, April 22, the DEI Office will be hosting a virtual forum from 10:30am to 12pm. Interest for this forum has been demonstrated, and we would like to provide an opportunity for all of our AAPI colleagues to participate. Please note the following:


  • For this discussion, we would like to provide a brave space for discussion and free expression for our faculty and staff that self-identify as Asian or Asian American.


  • As we begin to develop more inclusive campus-wide conversations and initiatives, there will be opportunities for allies to be involved and provide support.  Please respect the confidentiality and privacy of this initial forum.  


If you are interested in participating in a discussion and/or have additional suggestions to address these issues, please fill out this Google Form and register for the Zoom call at the end of the form. Please note that this form is anonymous and you have to voluntarily share your contact information with us if you would like to participate in the forum.


Thank you!


Jarvis M. Watson, Ed.D.

Director of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

Office of DEI
