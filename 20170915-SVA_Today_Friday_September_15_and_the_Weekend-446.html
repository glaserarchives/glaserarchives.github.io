NYC PICKS

An event for dogs and dog-lovers with activities, live music, treats from Shake Shake and more. Free. Tomorrow, September 16, 11:00am – 2:00pm. Madison Square Park, 23rd Street at Broadway.

Taste of Gramercy Neighborhood

An opportunity to try the variety of food that can be found in Gramercy. Tickets $30 – $70; purchase here. Saturday, September 16, noon – 4:00pm. Irving Place between 17th and 18th streets.

Cartoon Network: 25 Years of Drawing on Creativity

An exhibition celebrating 25 years of Cartoon Network, where visitors can experience the creative process from sketch to screen, also featuring screenings of shows like Steven Universe and Adventure Time on weekends. Free. Tomorrow, September 16 – Sunday, October 8. The Paley Center for Media, 25 West 52nd Street.

The Feast of San Gennaro

A festival that aims to keep the spirit of Italian immigrants alive, featuring free musical entertainment, a variety of delicacies, restaurants and cafes and a world-famous cannoli-eating competition. Schedule here. Through Sunday, September 24. Little Italy, Manhattan.
TAKE NOTE

Constitution Day 2017

Sunday, September 17th is Constitution Day, a day of federal observance which commemorates the day in 1787 when the U.S. Constitution was signed at the Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia, PA. To learn more about the U.S. Constitution please visit the U.S. Senate website, or pick up a free pocket-sized Constitution at Student Affairs, 340 East 24th Street.
