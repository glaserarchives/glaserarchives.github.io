TAKE NOTE

SVAlert Test Today

At approximately 1:00pm today, there will be a test of SVAlert, the College's electronic notification system. All staff and faculty, along with students who have registered for SVAlert, will receive messages via the contact options that they are currently registered for. When you receive the test message, you do not need to take any action.

Students not registered for SVAlert need to sign up by logging into MySVA and clicking on the SVAlert image in the upper right. Given the possibility of future school closings, class cancellations or emergency situations, you are urged to register today. Please do not register parents or roommates without informing them first.

See the Music: The Evolution of Music and Video

MFA Social Documentary Film and MFA Visual Narrative present an advance screening of the new PBS series Soundbreaking, produced and directed by MFA Social Documentary Film Chair Maro Chermayeff. After the screening, former MTV executive Rob Barnett will talk with Chermayeff, Soundbreaking editor and MFA Social Documentary Film faculty member E. Donna Shepherd and Vashtie Kola (BFA 2004 Film and Video), who has directed music videos for Kendrick Lamar and Justin Bieber. Click headline to RSVP. Thursday, November 3, 7:00 - 9:00pm, 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.

Political Advertisements IX, 1952 - 2016

MFA Art Practice, MFA Computer Art, MFA Fine Arts and MFA Social Documentary Film present Political Advertisements, a film created in 1984 and updated every election cycle to record how politics and politicians are represented on TV. Michelle Goldberg, a writer at Slate, will moderate a talk on the film. Friday, November 4, 7:00 - 10:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
