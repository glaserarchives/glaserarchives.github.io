NOTICE

Masks On
SVA has a mask mandate in place for all students, faculty, staff and visitors. This means face coverings must be worn at all times inside every SVA building. Students are not permitted to eat anywhere on campus except at the two Moe's Cafe locations. This policy is in place to keep everyone safe and healthy. For more information, see sva.edu/reopening.
