Dylan Rose Rheingold

“The Myth of Agency Around Artists’ Signatures”

MFA Fine Arts student and 2023 Alumni Scholarship Award recipient Anoushka Bhalla interviews MFA 2022 Fine Arts alumni Xayvier Haughton and Dylan Rose Rheingold, faculty member Baseera Khan, and others for her essay in Hyperallergic about the history of artist signatures and why some are seeking to forgo the tradition.

NYC PICKS

Threads of Knowledge: Sarah Zapata and Julia Bryan-Wilson in Conversation

Fiber artist Sarah Zapata and art historian Julia Bryan-Wilson examine the work on view in the American Folk Art Museum’s “What That Quilt Knows About Me” exhibition, with a focus on textile aesthetics and politics. Free. Today, April 5, 6:00 – 7:15pm, online.

Passover Pop-up at the Met

In honor of Passover, join gallery curators at the Metropolitan Museum of Art for a Passover pop-up exhibition and talk featuring two illuminated Hebrew manuscripts that date to the 15th-century Italian renaissance. Pay-what-you-wish with student ID. Monday, April 10, 11:00am – 12:30pm. Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Avenue, Gallery 503.
