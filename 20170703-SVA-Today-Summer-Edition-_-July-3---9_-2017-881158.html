SVA Today
Monday, July 3 - Sunday, July 9, 2017

Cannes Future Lions

BFA Advertising students and recent alumni received the prestigious Future Lions prize, the official student award of the Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity, for their innovative ideas for Google and CNN.

June Hot Five

The top five most-discussed projects on SVA Portfolios this month include minty fresh photos, eye-catching animation work for American Gods, bikinis illustrated and more.

"Do More Than Is Expected"

Claudine Eriksson (BFA 2016 Design) talks about working with Paula Scher at Pentagram, the connections she made at SVA, the importance of clear visual and verbal communication and more.
THIS WEEK

Thursday: MAT Art Education Thesis Presentations

The MAT Art Education class of 2017 present their thesis projects. Thursday, July 6, 10:00am – 12:00pm, and 1:00 – 3:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, 4th floor.

Thursday: Open Studios: Painting and Illustration Summer Residencies

SVA Continuing Education and SVA Summer Residency Programs present their open studios, featuring work by participants in two Summer Residency Programs: Painting and Mixed Media, and Illustration and Visual Storytelling. Thursday, July 6, 6:00 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, 4th and 8th floors.

Friday: "Re-treat, Re-born, Re-venge, Re-position, Re-charge, Re-peat" Reception

An exhibition of thesis work by the MAT Art Education class of 2017. Through Saturday, July 8. Reception: Friday, July 7, 4:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Saturday: "And Then . . ."

MFA Visual Narrative presents an exhibition of thesis projects by its class of 2017. Saturday, July 8 – Monday, July 24. Reception: Friday, July 21, 6:00pm. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

NYC PICKS

Macy's Fireworks

The 41st annual Macy's Fourth of July Fireworks along the East River, accompanied by a score and music performances. Viewing locations here. Tuesday, July 4, beginning around 8:00pm.

"Pulverize the Patriarchy"

A group exhibition to benefit Planned Parenthood, curated by Crystelle Colucci (BFA 2017 Photography and Video) and featuring the work of BFA Photography and Video alumni and faculty members. Friday, July 7, 6:00 – 10:00pm. Chinatown Soup, 16 Orchard Street.

"American Visionary: John F. Kennedy’s Life and Times"

An exhibition on the private life and public trajectory of John F. Kennedy. On view through January 7, 2018. Tickets up to $20; purchase here. New-York Historical Society, 170 Central Park West.
TAKE NOTE

MPS Branding Thesis: Repositioning the American Dream

The MPS Branding class of 2017 will present the repositioning of the American Dream through the lenses of capitalism, immigration, technology, commerce and the pursuit of happiness. Tuesday, July 11, 11:00am – 5:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

"Astral America"

SVA Continuing Education and SVA Summer Residency Programs present an exhibition that addresses how the concepts of hyperreality and ethereality manifest in the contemporary notion of “America” as both location and ethos. Thursday, July 13 – Monday, July 31. Reception: Thursday, July 13, 6:00pm. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Save the Date: All-Staff Meeting, 8.24.17

Mark your calendars. The annual SVA All-Staff Meeting will be held on Thursday, August 24. Breakfast and Benefits Fair begin at 8:00am; program begins at 9:00am. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. More information to come.
