FEATURES
Stills from Political Advertisements IX, 1952 - 2016. Via ASU Art Museum.

From "Daisy" to "Make America Great Again": The Changing Nature of Political Advertisements

A Q&A with the artists behind Political Advertisements, an ever-evolving documentation of modern political advertising.

"See the Music" in PBS's Soundbreaking

A look at Soundbreaking, a documentary miniseries co-directed and produced by MFA Social Documentary Film Chair Maro Chermayeff, premiering Monday, November 14, on PBS.

Art School Can Be Scary, Hilarious [Slideshow]

A recap of the annual Halloween party hosted by Visual Arts Student Association, SVA's student government.
IN THE PRESS
Photo by Shen Wei, from the series "I Miss You Already." Via CNN.

The "Dreamy, Intimate" Work of Shen Wei

CNN profiles the MFA 2006 Photography, Video and Related Media graduate, whose work uses dreamlike settings and nude subjects, including himself, to create an otherworldly effect.

Yawnie, the Reverse Alarm Clock

Fast Company on a proposed alarm clock that tells users when to go to bed, created by MFA 2016 Products of Design graduates Nic Barajas and Ziyun Qi.

"The Science of Delicious"

Wired on a recent project by Brian Finke (BFA 1998 Photography), photographing the latest experiments in food science.

SVA Faculty Member Directs Le Tigre's "I'm With Her"

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media faculty member Laura Parnes helmed the video for the "electroclash" band's new, pro-Hillary Clinton single.

Image courtesy Marvin Heiferman.

Picturing Science

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media faculty members Marvin Heiferman and Oliver Wasow discuss Wasow's contribution to Heiferman's "Seeing Science" project, which investigates photography's influence and impact on the sciences. Click to RSVP. Wednesday, November 9, 1:00 - 3:00pm, 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

Design Systems and Material Design at Google

MFA Interaction Design presents Rob Giampietro, creative lead, and Bethany Fong, senior interaction designer and accessibility lead, discussing the company's approach to material design and design systems. Click to RSVP. Wednesday, November 9, 6:00 - 8:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, 3rd floor.

DOC NYC: DOC U SVA

A screening of shorts by students and graduates of MFA Social Documentary Film, presented as part of the DOC NYC festival. Tickets $10. Friday, November 11, 5:15 - 6:30pm, IFC Center, 323 Sixth Avenue.
