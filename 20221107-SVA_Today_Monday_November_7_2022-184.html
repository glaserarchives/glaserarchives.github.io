VASA Annual Holiday Bazaar Sign-up

Vendor applications are now open for VASA’s annual Holiday Bazaar. Join with fellow students to sell your artwork and other merchandise. Due to limited space this year, individual vendors will not be accepted. If you have a group that would like to sell its work collectively, have one person apply to represent your group.


Apply to be a vendor through the form here by Tuesday, November 15, 11:59pm. The bazaar will take place on Thursday, December 8, and Friday, December 9, 4:00 – 7:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.
