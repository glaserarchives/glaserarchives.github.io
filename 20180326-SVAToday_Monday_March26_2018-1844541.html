SVA Today
Monday, March 26, 2018

Joes@Moe's with VASA

In the latest Joes@Moe's video, host Zipeng Zhu (BFA 2013 Design) talks with the current Visual Arts Student Association officers, President Kenneth Varvel, Vice President Amy Fields, Treasurer Eugene Lee and Senate Chair Wega Chen.
PRESS PICK

"I Am Who I Am"

Ryan Pfluger (MFA 2007 Photography, Video and Related Media) photographed pop and country singer K.D. Lang for a recent New York Times article.
NYC PICKS

Pen Out Loud: A Wrinkle in Time

PEN America and the Strand Book Store celebrate unconventional literary heroes with readings from Madeleine L’Engle’s classic novel and related texts by various writers. Tickets $15; purchase here. 7:00pm. Strand Book Store, 828 Broadway.

Art by Liniers

Artist Liniers’s first major exhibition in the United States, which showcases more than 125 pieces of original artwork from his daily comic strip Macanudo and his graphic novels Good Night, Planet and The Big Wet Balloon alongside select process pieces and covers from The New Yorker. Through Saturday, May 5. Society of Illustrators, 128 East 63rd Street.

The Orchid Show

A showcase of thousands of dramatically displayed orchids in the Enid A. Haupt Conservatory, featuring a series of installations crafted by Daniel Ost. Ticket prices vary; purchase here. Through Sunday, April 22. New York Botanical Garden, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.
TAKE NOTE

Outside In with Director Lynn Shelton

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue invites you to join director Lynn Shelton (MFA 1995 Photography and Related Media) for a screening of her latest film Outside In, starring Edie Falco and Jay Duplass. A Q&A with Shelton will follow. Free with SVA ID (one guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Tomorrow, March 27, 6:15pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Dog Therapy

De-stress with New York Therapy Animals, presented by SVA Student Health and Counseling Services. Wednesday, March 28, 3:00 – 4:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 1022.

Visiting Artist: Nancy Shaver

BFA Fine Arts presents a talk by artist Nancy Shaver. Thursday, March 29, 7:00 – 9:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.

First Offerings: A Selection of First-Year Paintings

A selection of works by foundation painting students from BFA Cartooning, BFA Design, BFA Fine Art and BFA Illustration who are enrolled in the SVA Honors Program, curated by BFA Fine Arts faculty member Leigh Behnke and student Stella Song. Monday, April 2 – Friday, April 13. Reception: Thursday, April 5, 6:00pm. SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.
