Tonight! NYC Cinematic Premiere of Ursula von Rydingsvard: Into Her Own
Attend a free screening of this new documentary, which traces the story of the acclaimed sculptor and former SVA faculty member Ursula von Rydingsvard. Join the artist herself and director Daniel Traub (MFA 1998 Photography and Related Media) for a conversation following the film. 7:30pm. RSVP here. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
