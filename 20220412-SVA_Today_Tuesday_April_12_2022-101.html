SVA’s Guggenheim Fellowship Winners

Events

Today | Illuminating Interactions
BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects presents thesis video work by its graduating class. Through Monday, May 2. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

1:00pm | MFA Products of Design Spring 2022 Lecture Series: Pune Dracker
MFA Products of Design presents a guest lecture with SVA alumnus Pune Dracker (MA 2021 Design Research, Writing and Criticism). Dracker is a researcher, writer and social-justice warrior specializing in animal welfare. Today, 1:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 7th floor.

7:00pm | i3 Lecture: Joe Quint
A talk with documentary filmmaker and photographer Joe Quint as part of MPS Digital Photography’s i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Today, 7:00pm ET, on Zoom.

7:00pm | Millennium Film Journal: FACT/ARTIFACT Screening
MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a screening of rarely seen moving image works by distinguished filmmakers in celebration of the publication of Millennium Film Journal No. 74, in which the works are discussed. Today, 7:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Thursday | Love Letters to Survivors Art Event
April is Sexual Assault Awareness Month. SVA Student Health and Counseling Services, the College’s Title IX Office and New York City’s DOVE Program invite survivors and allies to show support for sexual-assault survivors and contribute to an art display of empowering art, affirmations and uplifting messages. To learn more about the #SurvivorLoveLetter movement, click here. Thursday, April 14, 2:00 – 4:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Saturday | Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore
SVA Theatre presents a screening of Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore. Free with SVA ID (one non-SVA guest permitted per SVA ID holder). RSVPs not accepted or required. Seating is first-come, first-served. Click here for SVA Theatre’s current COVID-19 guidelines, including information on masks and vaccination requirements. Saturday, April 16, 2:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | Yoga for Artists: Back, Shoulder and Headache Relief
Take a yoga break with SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and instructor Melissa Boo. This free, virtual class will focus on mind-body care. Learn poses that may help relieve stress and re-energize you during finals. Beginner-friendly and open to the entire SVA community. Tuesday, April 19, 11:00am ET. RSVP here.
