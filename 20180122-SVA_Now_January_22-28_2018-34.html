EVENTS

Chris Knight

Chris Knight's aesthetic combines an unconditional love of art history with technical virtuosity and an uncompromising eye for detail. MPS Digital Photography presents an illuminating talk with the noted photographer. Tuesday, January 23, 7:00 – 8:30pm.136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Ellen Handler Spitz: Art and Storytelling

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents a lecture by Ellen Handler Spitz exploring how brilliant visual art captures spectators and pulls them into stories with suspense, hope, terror and the artist’s own first-person take. Monday, January 22, 2018, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Temporary Services

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by Temporary Services, a.k.a. Brett Bloom and Marc Fischer, a duo that produces exhibitions, events, projects, and publications. Tuesday, January 23, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

MFA Visual Narrative Online Application Workshops with Nathan Fox

Join one of two upcoming Google Hangouts with MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox to find out more about the low-residency graduate program and its application process. For more information, click here. Monday, January 22, 7:00pm, and Wednesday, January 24, noon.

RisoLAB/Society of Illustrators Workshop

MFA Visual Narrative and Society of Illustrators hosts this two-day, bootcamp-style workshop on the Risograph machine, which combines the vibrant colors of silkscreening with the speed and automation of digital and offset printing. Taught by MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox. $175 for Society members; $200 for non-members. For more information and to sign up, click here. Saturday and Sunday, January 27 and 28, 10:00am - 4:00pm. SVA RisoLAB, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.
