9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Tuesday from 9:30 – 11:00am ET, SVA's International Student Office will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week. To schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

6:30pm | Animal, Vegetable, Mineral: 20 Questions for Ben Hagari
Artist and MFA Photography, Video and Related Media faculty member Ben Hagari specializes in film and video installation. This event will feature his film Fresh, followed by a discussion about its relevance to man’s relationship with plant life. 6:30 – 8:30pm ET. Join on Zoom.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

SVA COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Students, Faculty and Staff
Yesterday, in a letter to the SVA community, SVA President David Rhodes announced that all SVA students and employees will be required to show proof of a COVID-19 vaccination in order to attend in-person classes, study or work on SVA's campus for the fall 2021 semester. Eligibility information and vaccine appointment information are posted at sva.edu/reopening.

By Tuesday, June 15, you will receive information about how to submit the required documentation, as well as information on applying for a medical or religious exemption.

Requiring the vaccine, along with other measures SVA has put into place, such as wearing appropriate face coverings and social distancing, will help prevent the spread of the virus in the SVA community and help keep everyone safe when we return in the fall.

Visit sva.edu/reopening for updates.
