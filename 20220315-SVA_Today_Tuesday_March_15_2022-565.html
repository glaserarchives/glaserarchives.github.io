ANNOUNCEMENTS

Call for Entries: “Perevorot (Revolution) Illustrated”
SVA Galleries invites the SVA community to participate in “Perevorot (Revolution) Illustrated,” an international exhibition showing solidarity with the people of Ukraine, scheduled to be held in early April at multiple locations around the world, including the SVA Flatiron Windows. The exhibition protests the repression of Russian artists, journalists and citizens for voicing their opposition to the war in Ukraine. The SVA effort will be curated by Russian artist and curator Natasha Konyukova.

Submitted works (illustration, comics, photographs) may involve any interpretation of the idea of “revolution,” and should be completely wordless and black and white. Photographs may not contain documentary images of the war in Ukraine. The call for entries can be found here, or email art@white-lanes.ru. The deadline is Thursday, March 24.

Deadline Extension: Submit to the Humanities and Sciences Writing Contest
SVA Humanities and Sciences Writing Contest is open to undergraduates for submissions in poetry, short story, personal essay/memoir, critical essay and film script or play. Prizes in each category are $600, $300 and $100 for first, second and third places, respectively. Submissions should be sent to Edwin Rivera, erivera3@sva.edu, by no later than the extended deadline of Wednesday, April 6.
