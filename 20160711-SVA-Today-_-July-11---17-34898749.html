TAKE NOTE

After School Special 2016 Video Contest

The SVA Theatre is seeking a 10-20 second pre-roll video (animated or live-action) to play before screenings at this year's After School Special: SVA Alumni Film & Animation Festival, which will take place in September. Last year's winning entry by Silver Paul (BFA 2016 Animation) can be viewed here. Information about last year's festival can be found here. Logos for SVA, the festival, and the SVA Theatre can be found here. Submissions should be sent via email to SVA Theatre Director Adam Natale (anatale@sva.edu), and include your name, graduation year, SVA department and a link to the video on Vimeo or YouTube by 5:00pm on Friday, August 19. Final video format should be DCP or digital file (e.g. QuickTime ProRes). The winner will receive $500 and an invitation to the festival's VIP reception. For questions, contact anatale@sva.edu.
