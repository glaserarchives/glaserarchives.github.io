FEATURE

From the Classroom to Madison Avenue

Garments designed by BFA Design students are currently on view along Madison Avenue, from 57th to 79th Streets, in connection with New York Fashion Week. The designs, which originated in faculty member and alumnus Olga Mezhibovskaya's Basic Typography course, are part of the exhibition "The Language of Fashion," which runs through Sunday, September 18.
EVENTS

6:00pm: Graduate Student Reception

The SVA Library invites all graduate students to an informal reception to meet library staff and fellow students, as well as browse the library's collections. 6:00 - 7:30pm, SVA Library, 380 Second Avenue, 2nd floor.

6:00pm: "C, D, E, No F, G" Reception

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of work that investigates urban objects and their environment, contemplates the irrationality of reality, juxtaposes found and discarded objects with the body, and challenges perspective through the context of installation. Curated by faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts). 6:00 - 8:00pm, SVA Flatiron Project Space, 133/141 West 21st Street. Exhibition runs through Wednesday, September 21.

6:00pm: DJ Set by Michael Paul Britto (Brittofied)

As part of "The Beat Goes On," visual artist Michael Paul Britto (Brittofied) performs a DJ set. Organized by Elia Alba. 6:00 - 8:00pm, SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

7:30pm: "To a Point..." Reception

MA Curatorial Practice presents an exhibition that considers the politics of inclusion as it relates to spaces of cultural production. Curated by MA Curatorial Practice fellow Ikechukwu Casmir Onyewuenyi. 7:30 - 9:00pm, CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor. Exhibition runs through Sunday, September 25.

TAKE NOTE

Google Cardboard at the SVA Library

The SVA Library now offers Google Cardboard virtual reality headsets for in-library use. The viewers can be used with an Android device or iPhone and compatible apps. For questions, contact sva.libanswers.com.

A book signing and reception will follow. Monday, September 12, 7:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
