SVA Today
Tuesday, October 10, 2017
Image by Jane Hammond

Jane Hammond

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by artist Jane Hammond, who has spent over a decade creating a surreal series of photographic collages, as part of its i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tonight, 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
EVENTS

6:00pm | "Safe Spaces" Gallery Tour

MPS Art Therapy and New York City Health + Hospitals present an exhibition of artwork and music created by people incarcerated on Rikers Island, curated by MPS Art Therapy alumni Lesley Achitoff, ATR-BC, LCAT, and Robert Belgrod, LCAT (LP). 6:00 – 7:00pm. Through Thursday, October 26. MPS Art Therapy Project Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

6:30pm | Ana Miljacki, "Bad Copies"

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents critic and curator Ana Miljacki, who will examine copying, authorship and other ways we conceptualize the movement of ideas in architecture. RSVP here. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

6:30pm | Franklin Boyd

MFA Fine Arts presents department faculty member, lawyer, collector and entrepreneur Franklin Boyd, who will address the issue of determining the legal creator of an artwork in this age of casual collaborations and collectives. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
PRESS PICK

Michael Giacchino's Fave Cues

In celebration of Michael Giacchino's (BFA 1990 Film and Video) birthday today, Classic FM shared clips of the Academy Award-winning composer explaining his favorite cues from a few of his famous film scores.
NYC PICK

Magnetic City: Touring New York with Justin Davidson

New York’s architecture critic Justin Davidson shares his takes on how the ever-changing city shapes the lives of its inhabitants using his recent book, Magnetic City: A Walking Companion to New York. Tickets $5; purchase here. 6:30pm. Brooklyn Historical Society, 128 Pierrepont Street, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

Time to Get Your Flu Shot

The best way to protect yourself, the SVA community and those closest to you from getting the flu is getting vaccinated. For more information on ways to prevent the flu and other respiratory illnesses please visit sva.edu/flu.

Voter Registration

Tomorrow, October 11, volunteers from Dominicanos USA (DUSA) will be at the SVA Student Center to complete voter registrations. They will answer questions and help you complete and mail your registration. The deadline to register for voting in the November NYC general election is Friday, October 13. Noon – 3:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Jack Pierson and Steel Stillman

BFA Photography and Video and Dear Dave, magazine present artist Jack Pierson and artist and writer Steel Stillman, who will discuss Pierson's new book The Hungry Years and the crucial role that photography has played in his diverse artistic practice. Thursday, October 12, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

MFA Design for Social Innovation Open House

MFA Design for Social Innovation presents its open house, where you will learn about the program from current students, faculty and alumni. RSVP here. Friday, October 20, 6:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 5th floor.
