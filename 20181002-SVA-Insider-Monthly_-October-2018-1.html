Get To Know

Adam Krumm, Assistant Director, Residence Life, and one of this year's Distinguished Staff Award Winners

Tell us about yourself and your journey at SVA.

I've worked at SVA for more than nine years. Working with SVA students has blown my world wide open and I've been exposed to so much more than I could have ever imagined. I've seen many more exhibits than I ever thought I would in a lifetime, watched more animated TV shows, seen more movies, and explored more.

Congratulations on this amazing honor! What does it mean to you to win this award?

One thing that sets SVA aside from other workplaces is its celebration of its community members for their individuality and success. It came as a shock, but is such an honor. I hope that my time on the DSAG committee is spent celebrating the accomplishments and individual achievements of my colleagues.

What are your biggest challenges?

Keeping up with a fast-paced student population. Students absorb, process and output information at such a high volume and fast rate that I found myself having to balance administrative responsibilities as well as my interpersonal interactions with them. They have taught me so much, and are so exciting that I want to keep learning. I was proud of the RA Selection Process, Residence Life and Queer SVA's tables at Accepted Students Day (in April) and the Student Leadership Award recipients. The spirit of the student body always reminds me that I'm lucky I found this place.

What's one thing you love about working at SVA?

Working at SVA has been an adventure. I thought I would be here for a few years to get some experience after getting my MA and then move on. The universe had a different plan for me. There's a place for everyone here who truly wants to be part of something awesome.

FREE COURSES & TUTORIALS

Lynda.com features more than 1,500 software courses and tutorials and SVA provides unlimited access to all employees. See recently added courses below.

Side Hustle Strategies for Designers

There are a number of options for designers looking to generate extra monthly income, from selling fonts to making brushes for Photoshop. In this course, you can learn strategies that can help you build a side hustle and start generating passive income with your creative skills. Discover different side hustle models and learn real-world examples of successful side hustlers, along with tips to get you started.

Developing Your Team Members

When it comes to employee development, taking a one-size-fits-all approach can cause leaders to misuse their time and energy. In order to most efficiently develop your team, it's important to determine the type of training, coaching and guidance that each team member requires, so that you can be more intentional about helping them grow. This course shares how to inspire and grow your rising stars, motivate low performers, avoid leadership pitfalls, and more.

Creating Art with Your Desktop Scanner

This course focuses on scanning using a desktop scanner for both personal and professional creative purposes. Learn how to get started using and controlling scanning equipment, discover unconventional scanning techniques for making advanced compositions and see how to export your digital scans into a program like Photoshop to make the most of their visual impact.
