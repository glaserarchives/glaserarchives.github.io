Monday, October 24, 2022

Maithili Joshi

“I Took a Chance”

Maithili Joshi (BFA 2022 Illustration) talks about making picture books, compiling a portfolio and how lockdown refined her art for Creative Boom.

EVENTS

3:30pm | Responsive Design on Editor X with Wix Playground Academy

Join Wix for a live site-creation demo and workshop to help you drill down on web design and adopt a responsive approach to future projects. They’ll share their process on building fluid sites that behave beautifully across all screen sizes using Editor X. Today, October 24, 3:30pm, online.

5:00pm | Women in Animation Internship Panel

SVA’s Women in Animation club invites students to a panel on internships. Learn what it takes to get an internship and what studio life is like from current students who interned at Augenblick, Dreamworks, Framestore, House of Cool and Pixar. Tonight, October 24, 5:00 – 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 301C.

6:30pm | Holy Spider Screening

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a special preview screening of Holy Spider—a retelling of the case of a serial killer who targeted sex workers in Iran—directed by Ali Abbasi. There will be a Q&A with Abbasi following the screening. This screening is open to the SVA community and their guests. Free. Registration required; RSVP here. Tonight, October 24, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. Watch the trailer here.

SVA IN THE PRESS

Drew Friedman

“Diary of a Nine-Year-Old Underground Comix Fanatic”

Drew Friedman’s (BFA 1981 Cartooning) latest book, Maverix and Lunatix: Icons of Underground Comix, pays tribute to the underground comics creators of the ’70s through a series of portraits. Via Book & Film Globe.

NYC PICK

“To Have a Mother Means Milk”

A solo exhibition of work by Mu Pan (MFA 2007 Illustration as Visual Essay; BFA 2001 Illustration), exploring an epic story in a self-created, imaginative world. Through Saturday, November 5. Hashimoto Contemporary, 54 Ludlow Street.

Tomorrow, October 25, 6:00 pm, doors at 5:30 pm. Seating is limited; RSVP here. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

Tomorrow | The Son Screening

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a special preview screening of The Son, starring Hugh Jackman and Laura Dern. SVA ID cards required at the door. Seating is limited. Tuesday, October 25, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. Watch the trailer here.

#SVAwesome

Yesterday marked the last day of “Material Mixtape,” a group show at the New Collectors gallery featuring works by 12 MFA Fine Arts candidates at SVA. The participating students, taught by artist James O. Clark, presented work in a diverse range of mediums. Artists included Taylor S. Bernstein, Tom Hecht, Jingyao Huang, Rosie K. Kim, Nianxin Li, Silvia Muleo, Pei Ou, Yuli Aloni Primor, Camila Varon, Lu Xia, Sora Xu and Kaiqing Yu. Photographs by Jingyao Huang.
