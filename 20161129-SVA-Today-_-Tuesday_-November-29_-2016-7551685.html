SVA Today
Tuesday, November 29, 2016

November "Hot Five"

The top five most-discussed projects from SVA Portfolios this November include the opening credits animation for HBO's Westworld, a parody New Yorker cover and more.
EVENTS

6:00pm | Genevieve Hyacinthe

MFA Fine Arts presents department faculty member Hyacinthe discussing Kanye West's Yeezus-era aesthetics and how they were informed by Jean-Michel Basquiat's art. 6:00 - 8:00pm, 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

6:15pm | Harry Benson: Shoot First Screening and Q&A

SVA Filmmakers Dialogue presents a screening of this 2015 documentary about the photographer who covered The Beatles in their early years. Benson will take part in a Q&A following the film. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). 6:15pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

6:30pm | Sean Anderson

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents Anderson, an associate curator at The Museum of Modern Art, discussing the exhibition "Insecurities: Tracing Displacement and Shelter," on view at the museum through January 22. Register here. 6:30 - 8:30pm, 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

7:00pm | Yulia Gorbachenko

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by the program alumnus (2010) and fashion photographer. 7:00 - 8:30pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
