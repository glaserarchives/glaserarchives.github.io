Events

3:00pm | Remembering Ed Benguiat
The interment of longtime SVA faculty member and legendary type designer Ed Benguiat (1927 – 2020) will be held today at Arlington National Cemetery. In addition to teaching at SVA for almost 50 years and creating more than 600 typefaces, Benguiat served in the Army's Air Corps during World War II. Today, 3:00pm ET, on Zoom. Please mute your device when entering the call.

3:30pm | Fab Friday with Nick Le Messurier
Join this talk by New York-based creative Nick Le Messurier, who originally hails from Australia. Le Messurier started his career in typography and wayfinding before switching to advertising and fashion, and now works with such esteemed brands as Christian Dior, Bally, Cartier, Maison Margiela and Armani. Fab Friday is a weekly design inspiration guest lecture series hosted by BFA Advertising and BFA Design. Today, 3:30pm ET, on Zoom.

The Port Authority, together with New York Governor Kathy Hochul and Delta Air Lines, has partnered with the Queens Museum to commission new works from New York-based artists for LaGuardia Airport's Terminal C, including a piece by Mariam Ghani (MFA 2002 Photography and Related Media). NYC PICK

Celebrating the City
"Celebrating the City: Recent Photography Acquisitions from the Joy of Giving Something" juxtaposes work by some of the 20th century’s most important photographers with striking recent images by contemporary practitioners, including Jaime Permuth (MPS 2009 Digital Photography; MFA 1994 Photography and Related Media), Rong Xu (BFA 2012 Photography and Video) and BFA Photography and Video Chair Joseph Maida. Ongoing. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.
