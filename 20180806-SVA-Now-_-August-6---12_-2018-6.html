News and events from the School of Visual Arts

Before and After SVA: Jacob Kaplan

Jacob Kaplan (BFA 2017 Animation) talks about how he got his current job at Pixar and shares advice for incoming students.

July Hot Five

Last month's five most-discussed projects from SVA Portfolios include an eye-popping and vibrant silkscreen poster, a fantastically photographed salad cookbook and a well-known street fighter come to life.
IN THE PRESS

On Belonging

The New Yorker interviews illustrator Nora Krug (MFA 2004 Illustration as Visual Essay) about Belonging, her upcoming graphic memoir about German identity.

"Dreams of Fire and Starshine"

Hyperallergic on Delano Dunn's (MFA 2016 Fine Arts) multi-layed, mixed media solo show "Dreams of Fire and Starshine," on view through Friday, August 10, at Project for Empty Space in Newark, New Jersey.

Pet Prints by Broderpress

House Beautiful talks with printmaker and SVA faculty member Shannon Broder (MFA 2016 Fine Arts; BFA 2011 Visual & Critical Studies) about her Etsy shop, Broderpress.
EVENTS

Constellations

A juried exhibition of works by SVA students who utilized the multiplicities within identity to create personal yet socially engaged work. Through Saturday, August 11. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Summer Residency Program: Illustration and Photography and Video Final Exhibition

Residents from Illustration and Visual Storytelling and Photography and Video will present new work made during their time in the SVA Summer Residency Program. Wednesday, August 8, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

Summer Residency Program: Fine Arts and Painting and Mixed Media Final Exhibition

Residents from Fine Arts: Contemporary Practices and Painting and Mixed Media will present new work made during their time in the SVA Summer Residency Program. Thursday, August 9, 6:00 – 9:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, 8th and 9th floors.
