TAKE NOTE

Clay Patrick McBride

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by photographer and filmmaker Clay Patrick McBride, whose portraits of top athletes and musicians have appeared in numerous magazines. This talk is part of the i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tuesday, November 14, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

Brandstand: States of America

The MPS Branding Alumni Network presents its second annual Brandstand, which will examine the radical shifts in the meaning of "America" as a brand. Speakers: Charles Blow, Brian Collins, Jim Johnson, Paola Mendoza, Debbie Millman and Derek Thompson. RSVP here. Wednesday, November 15, 6:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Seeing Alcoholics Anonymous through Attachment Theory

MPS Art Therapy presents a lecture by Judith Jordan, PhD, LCSW, CASAC. Jordan will discuss her doctoral research, which showed that individuals who participate in Alcoholics Anonymous for 90 days can improve the quality of their interpersonal relationships. Friday, November 17, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Murder on the Orient Express

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Murder on the Orient Express, starring Johnny Depp and Penelope Cruz. Admission free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Friday, November 17, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
