EVENTS

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

3:00pm | VASA February Town Hall

Join your VASA officers at this semester’s first town hall on Instagram Live! Connect with VASA officers, stay up to date on events and get the answers to any questions you may have. Meet a few more student leaders across campus and learn about them and their roles. Today, February 16, 3:00pm, via Instagram Live.

3:30pm | HOPE Art Competition Exhibition

Join SVA Student Health and Counseling Services for the opening reception of the “Together, we can find HOPE” exhibition. Eighty-eight students submitted artwork for our suicide prevention campaign. Thirteen finalists were selected and their artwork will be showcased from today, February 16, through Wednesday, February 22, at the SVA Flatiron Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street. Student Health and Counseling Services will announce the Grand Prize and Fan Favorite winners at the reception. Finalists’ prizes will be sponsored by Student Health and Counseling Services and the SVA Campus Store. Light refreshments will be provided. Today, February 16, 3:30 – 5:00pm.

  • •“A Conciliatory Letter,” through Wednesday, March 1. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.
