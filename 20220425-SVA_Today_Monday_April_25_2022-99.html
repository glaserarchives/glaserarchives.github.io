Marshall Arisman, 1937 - 2022
The longtime chair and founder of MFA Illustration as Visual Essay; illustrator for The New York Times and Time magazine among others; graphic novelist; and painter with a singularly mystical and magical vision passed away on Friday, April 22. Read about his SVA career retrospective here.

#SVAWESOME
Since fall 2020, Marilyn Montúfar (BFA 2009 Photography) and Underground Writing have been collaborating on a photography series focused on mentoring and photographing Chicana and female migrant youth with heritage in Mexico. An exhibition featuring photographs and writings from the project, “Migrant Youth/Chicana Perspectives: Living in Multiple Spaces,” is currently on view at the Museum of Northwest Art’s Outside Inside Gallery. The photography is also featured in Remark, a new art book published by 4Culture gallery about love, persistence and a region rich in creativity. Photo Credit: Marilyn Montúfar.
