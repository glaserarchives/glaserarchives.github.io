As a global community, SVA Continuing Education invites a panel of artists, designers and cultural producers who will discuss language and art in the 21st century. 6:30 – 8:00pm ET. Register here to receive a Zoom link.

SOCIALLY DISTANCED PICK

Forget Me Not at Human Rights Watch Film Festival
Fighting for their son's right to an inclusive education, where children with disabilities would be taught alongside their classmates without disabilities, film director Olivier Bernier (BFA 2006 Film and Video) and his wife Hilda investigate the personal stories of disabled students and their parents in the U.S. in Forget Me Not. Purchase a ticket here. Watch the film live online tonight at 6:45pm ET, and register here to join the following Q&A at 8:30pm ET.

Tomorrow | Self/Centered: MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism Thesis Presentations
Presenting the independent thesis work of the nine outstanding graduates of MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism's class of 2021, who find common ground in their rigorous explorations of the self in relation to design, engaging subjects that span media, language, citizenship, accessibility, gender and race.
