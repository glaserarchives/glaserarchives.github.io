Events THIS WEEK

Tuesday | Flash Tips for Outdoor Photography with Joe Sinnott
Longer and sunnier days create opportunities and challenges for taking pictures during the summer months. Join SVA Continuing Education faculty member Joe Sinnott (BFA 1988 Photography) while he explains a variety of flash techniques you can use to improve your photographs. Tuesday, June 1, 6:30pm ET. Register here to join on Zoom.

Thursday | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Over the summer, office hours will take place each Thursday at 10:00am ET only, unless otherwise specified.

Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour this Thursday, June 3, 10:00am ET. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

Thursday | "Seed Score" Performance and Workshop
"Event scores" are performances that involve simple actions, ideas and objects from everyday life. SVA Continuing Education faculty member and visual artist Patricia Voulgaris (BFA 2013 Photography) will be performing from two specific event scores featured in Yoko Ono's book Grapefruit (1964), using everyday materials. Thursday, June 3, 5:30pm ET. Register here to join on Zoom.

SOCIALLY DISTANCED PICKS

Dana James: Something I Meant to Say
An exhibition of abstract compositions by Dana James (BFA 2008 Fine Arts). Join the opening reception on Thursday, June 3, 5:30 – 8:30pm ET. RSVP to rsvp@hollistaggart.com. Social distancing and masks required. On view through Friday, July 2. Hollis Taggart Gallery, 521 West 26th Street.

Perception of JI EUN
Through her research-based, extensively planned yet minimalist compositions, artist Ji Eun Lim (MAT 2019 Art Education) explores the contradictions of human personality and expression as they manifest in herself and others. See this work in her solo exhibition at One Art Space, 23 Warren Street, from June 3 – 6. Reception: Friday, June 4, 6:00 – 8:00pm ET. Register here.

PBS American Masters: Ballerina Boys
Watch the premiere of this episode of American Masters about Les Ballets Trockadero de Monte Carlo (The Trocks), an all-male company that for 45 years has offered audiences their passion for ballet classics mixed with exuberant comedy. MFA Social Documentary Film faculty E. Donna Shepherd and Mark Mandler were this episode's editor and sound recordist. Friday, June 4, 9:00pm ET.

First Saturday Lite: Still Here, Still Queer
Celebrate the resilience and joy of queer community this Pride Month at Brooklyn Museum's First Saturday Lite—a low-key, free outdoor afternoon amplifying culture and community. Read more about the lineup here. Saturday, June 5, 2:00 – 6:00pm ET. Brooklyn Museum Plaza, 200 Eastern Parkway, Brooklyn.
