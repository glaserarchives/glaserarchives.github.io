TAKE NOTE

Announcing the Spring 2017 Term Honors Recipients

A total of 622 students from SVA's 11 undergraduate majors have been awarded Term Honors for spring 2017, and the list of recipients is now available on MySVA. To be included, students must successfully complete 12 credits of coursework without any grades of incomplete, and earn a term GPA within the top 20% of all students in their major. Students whose term GPA is within the top 5% in their major receive High Term Honors. Congratulations to the honorees!
