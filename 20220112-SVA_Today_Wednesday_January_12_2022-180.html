January 2022 Exhibitions and Virtual Events
It is a new year at SVA, which means new exhibitions, events and workshops. While SVA campus buildings are currently closed to the public due to COVID-19 concerns, you can still attend an array of virtual lectures and workshops. Click here to view our list of exhibitions and virtual events for this month.

SVA IN THE PRESS

The New York Times’s 2021 Illustration Roundup
The 2021 illustration recap from The New York Times includes work or curation by Deanna Donegan (BFA 2012 Illustration), Tonya Douraghy (MFA 2010 Design), Karlotta Freier (MFA 2021 Illustration as Visual Essay), Jon Han (BFA 2009 Graphic Design), Ina Jang (MPS 2012 Fashion Photography; BFA 2012 Photography), Annie Jen (BFA 2013 Graphic Design), Jon Key (MA 2021 Design Research, Writing and Criticism), Jaedoo Lee (BFA 2016 Design), Simone Noronha (MFA 2014 Design), Tala Safie (MFA 2018 Design), Deena So'Oteh (MFA 2018 Illustration as Visual Essay), Zak Tebbal (BFA 2015 Design), Rachel Willey (BFA 2012 Graphic Design) and Shuhua Xiong (MFA 2019 Design).
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

The Schomburg Center's 10th Annual Black Comic Book Festival
The Black Comic Book Festival is returning this year for its 10th annual celebration of Black comic books and graphic novels, featuring panel discussions, workshops, cosplay showcases and work of creators from across the country. Through Saturday, January 15. Online. Register here for free.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Coffee Talk!
Join therapist Brett and nurse Abby of Student Health and Counseling Services for our first virtual Coffee Talk of the spring semester. Bring your coffee and questions about boosters, COVID-19, testing and any other health-related questions you may have. Tomorrow, 11:00am ET, online via Instagram Live.

Faculty Book Club
For the next book club meeting, we will discuss a short article and a podcast addressing the presence of white supremacy in artistic and therapeutic contexts, available on the Faculty Book Club Canvas page. Co-facilitated by MA/MAT Art Education chair Cathy Rosamond and MPS Art Therapy faculty member Liz Dellicarpini. As a reminder, all faculty are invited to join on Canvas for more readings and discussion, all of which can be done at your own pace. Wednesday, January 19, 4:30 – 5:30pm ET, on Zoom.

SVA Campus Store Closed This Week
The SVA Campus Store will be closed through Monday, January 17. Enjoy free shipping this week by using code "STAYSAFE" at checkout. We will reopen on Tuesday, January 18, from 10:00am – 3:00pm. Follow us on Instagram for updates on our hours of operation.
