NYC PICKS

Day of the Dead Festival

Celebrate the Day of the Dead tradition in New York City with art-making, live music and more. Today, October 28 – Sunday, October 30, 12:00 – 5:00pm. St. Mark’s Church in-the-Bowery, 131 East 10th Street.

Annual Halloween Festival and Great PUPkin Dog Costume Contest

Brooklyn’s largest dog costume contest returns. Free. Saturday, October 29, 12:00 – 1:30pm. Prison Ship Martyrs Monument, Fort Greene Park, Dekalb Avenue and South Portland Avenue, Brooklyn.

Saturday Afternoon Cartoons: A Haunted Halloween

SVA faculty member Tommy José Stathes hosts a Halloween-spirited screening of animated cartoons from the 1920s through the 1950s. Tickets: $17. Saturday, October 29, 1:40pm. Metrograph, 7 Ludlow Street.

Poetry &: Beginnings & Endings with Jon Sands and Riley Mulherkar

Award-winning poet and performer Jon Sands partners with acclaimed jazz trumpeter Riley Mulherkar for an intimate live set. Free. Saturday, October 29, 6:00pm. Dia Chelsea, 537 West 22nd Street.

SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Monday, November 28, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
