Remembering Bayard Rustin

The SVA Honors Program and Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Office present an exploration of Bayard Rustin’s legacy, who during his 60-year career as an activist and organizer formulated many of the civil rights movement’s strategies and organized the March on Washington. We’ll be joined by Rustin’s partner, Walter Nagele, and others for a film screening. To learn more in advance of the event, read an interview with civil rights movement veterans in The New York Times here. Tuesday, February 13, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
