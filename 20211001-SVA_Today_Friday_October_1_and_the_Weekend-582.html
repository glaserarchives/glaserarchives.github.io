ANNOUNCEMENTS

Saturday, October 2 | Critical Network Maintenance
SVA Network Services will be performing an emergency network maintenance Saturday, October 2, 11:00pm – Sunday, October 3, 3:00am. All network-connected services such as core campus building Internet access, Cisco phones and new wireless sessions initiated during the maintenance will be entirely offline for a minimum of two hours.

Additionally, services hosted on campus such as MyServices and IT WebHelpDesk will be inaccessible for the duration of the maintenance. This maintenance is intended to improve Internet bandwidth at our west side core campus facilities. Thank you for your patience in this matter.

Call for Student Submissions: Visual Opinion
Submit your artwork to the Visual Opinion today and get paid! VO is SVA's student-run magazine. The next edition's theme is "exposed." Deadline: Tuesday, October 5, 9:00am. Email your submission to vo@sva.edu with your name, the title of the piece, and your year and major. Please note that only students residing in the United States can submit.

"Stop the Spread!": Student Health and Counseling Services' Poster Competition
Student Health and Counseling Services (SH&CS) invites all current SVA students to take part in a poster competition to communicate health and wellness messaging on campus and prevent the spread of COVID-19 and the flu. The winner will receive a $50 gift card to the Campus Store and all entries will be considered to be used by SH&CS in social media and print campaigns. Check out the Google Form for more information and to submit your artwork. Competition ends on Monday, October 18, and the winner will be announced Thursday, October 21.

2021 Annual Safety and Security Reports
SVA is committed to the safety and security of all members of the College community. In accordance with the federal Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act of 1990, the Annual Safety and Security Report (ASSR), the Annual Fire Safety Report (AFSR) and the Campus Crime Statistics for this year are now available.

These reports contain statistics and institutional policies concerning fire safety, as well as information on campus security issues such as sexual assault and other matters. Copies of these reports can be accessed in the Health and Safety section of the SVA Consumer Information webpage.

Contact SVA Institutional Research at 212.592.2829 with any questions regarding this information.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

Tomorrow | Cartoon Carnival and Fleischer Tribute on TCM
This weekend, watch the world premiere of Cartoon Carnival, a new documentary about silent era animated films featuring interviews with various animation historians (including former SVA instructor Howard Beckerman), and see a spotlight on 1920s – '30s Fleischer Studios animation. Both features highlight restoration work by SVA faculty member Tommy José Stathes, a noted early animation archivist and historian. See it live on the Turner Classic Movies channel on Saturday, October 2, 8:00pm ET.

Jasper Johns: Mind/Mirror
This most comprehensive retrospective ever devoted to Jasper Johns's art features his most iconic works along with many others shown for the first time, comprising a broad range of paintings, drawings, prints, and sculptures from 1954 to today. Through February 2022. Whitney Museum of American Art, 99 Gansevoort Street.
