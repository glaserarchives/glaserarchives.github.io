News and events from the School of Visual Arts
SVA Now
News and events from the School of Visual Arts
August 7 - 13, 2017
By Debbie Millman.

11 Tips on Refreshing Your Creativity

With students on break and summer in full swing, we decided to check in with some of SVA’s department chairs for their tips on how to use the extra leisure time to recharge creatively.

Celebrating Dear Dave,

An interview with BFA Photography and Video Chair and MPS Fashion Photography Co-Chair Stephen Frailey, founder and editor-in-chief of Dear Dave, magazine, about the publication's milestone 25th issue.
IN THE PRESS

SVA Animator's Big Break

The Washington Post spotlights Ian Jones-Quartey (BFA 2006 Animation), whose new animated show OK K.O.! Let's Be Heroes! premiered on Cartoon Network last week.

Marshall Arisman's "Must-See" Retrospective

Crave lists MFA Illustration as Visual Essay Chair Marshall Arisman's upcoming retrospective as one of this month's "must-see" exhibitions. On view Saturday, August 19, through Saturday, September 30, at the SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Film Movement Checks into Hotel Salvation

Variety on how Shubhashish Bhutiani's (BFA 2013 Film and Video) award-winning film Hotel Salvation was picked up by New York-based distributor Film Movement.
EVENTS

Jonathas Nazareth: Land

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents "Land," an exhibition of recent works by Jonathas Nazareth (BFA 2014 Visual & Critical Studies) that look at the transitory systems that govern the fate of the once-permanent land. Through Friday, August 25. Reception: Tuesday, August 8, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Flatiron Projects Space, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

Summer Residency Exhibition and Open Studios

SVA Continuing Education and SVA Summer Residency Programs present an open studios and an exhibition featuring artists in the following Summer Residency Programs: Fine Art: Residency in Contemporary Practices, Illustration and Visual Storytelling, Painting and Mixed Media, and Photography. Thursday, August 10, 6:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery and 8th and 9th floors, 133/141 West 21st Street.
