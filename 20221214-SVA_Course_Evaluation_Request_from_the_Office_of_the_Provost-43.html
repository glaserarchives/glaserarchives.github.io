SVA Course Evaluation Request from the Office of the Provost

December 14, 2022

Dear Students,

Fall 2022 course evaluations went live on November 28, 2022, but we just learned that a change by our vendor led to some of you receiving email notifications from an unknown email address (notifications@watermarkinsights.com) which may have gone into your spam folder. Our apologies for any confusion this may have caused and going forward email reminders will be sent from olt@sva.edu.

I've asked your instructors to provide in-class time for you to complete the evaluation for their course. However, I also invite you to do so proactively within Canvas where you can access your course evaluations directly. Please log into Canvas from myid.sva.edu. Your course evaluations can be accessed from the "Student Course Evaluations" tab in the lefthand navigation menu. 

If you run into any technical difficulties completing their survey, please contact the Office of Learning Technologies at olt@sva.edu.

Thank you for your help with this important project! SVA greatly appreciates the valuable feedback it receives from students.

Best regards,

Christopher J. Cyphers, Ph.D.
Provost
