Call for Submissions: 2023 HOPE Art Competition

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services presents the 2023 HOPE Art Competition. The creator of the winning piece will win a prize of $500 and all of the finalist’s work will be showcased in the SVA Flatiron Windows exhibition space at 133/141 West 21st Street. This year’s theme is a suicide prevention campaign because together, we can find hope. The artwork will serve as a reminder to SVA students that #YouAreNotAlone and #YourLifeMatters. You can find more information here, along with submission guidelines. The submission deadline is Monday, February 6.

​​Call For Submissions: Honoring Turkish Heritage

SVA’s International Student Office calls for submissions of artwork that relates to Turkish culture or artwork from students with Turkish backgrounds. Open to all students! The deadline for submissions is Thursday, February 2. Submissions will be displayed in the ISO reception area as part of an exhibition running Monday, February 6, through Friday, March 31. Access the submission requirements and form here. Contact Chisato Takeyama, ctakeyama@sva.edu, with any questions or to schedule an artwork drop-off at ISO.
