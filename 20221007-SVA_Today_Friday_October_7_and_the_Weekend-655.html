EVENTS

10:00am | Yoga for Artists: Increasing Creativity and Improving Concentration

Join SVA Student Health and Counseling Services for a free virtual yoga class. Explore ways to get creativity flowing by focusing on the mind-body connection. All current students are welcome! No prior experience is needed. All you need is a flat surface and one to three soft squares (pillows, blankets or towels). RSVP required. Open to the entire SVA community. Today, October 7, 10:00 – 10:45am, online.

MPS Digital Photography presents an in-person info session by program alumni, faculty and staff, including a tour of the 2022 thesis exhibition, “Dot Dot Dot.” Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer 2023 or fall 2023 admission. Saturday, October 8, 3:00 – 5:00pm. 132 West 21st Street, Room 203E.

NYC PICKS

Panamanian Day Parade

The largest Panamanian Day Parade held outside of Panama returns for its 27th year, celebrating the country’s many leaders, educators, artists and celebrities. Tomorrow, October 8, 12:00pm. The parade begins at President Street and Classon Avenue, Brooklyn.

Hispanic Day Parade (Desfile de la Hispanidad)

The Hispanic Day Parade returns for its 56th year—21 countries participate in the parade, sharing their music, folklore and traditions with spectators. This year’s event will take place along Fifth Avenue from 44th to 77th Street. Sunday, October 9, 12:00pm.

“Dirty Work”

An exhibition of ceramics by contemporary artists living in New York City, including work by Anastasia Warren (BFA 2018 Visual & Critical Studies). Through Friday, October 21. Jane Hartsook Gallery, 16 Jones Street.

Cooper Hewitt National Design Week

The Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum is offering free admission and events during National Design Week, October 17 – October 23. Reserve your tickets in advance here. Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum, 2 East 91st Street.
