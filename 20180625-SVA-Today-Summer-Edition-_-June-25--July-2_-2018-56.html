RSVP here. Wednesday, June 27, 6:30 – 8:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Escher NYC

An exhibition that showcases the largest collection of artist MC Escher's original works and tells the story of his life and impact. There are also technological installments that allow visitors to "step into" his works, relativity rooms and black light rooms with his posters. At the door with SVA ID, student tickets are $15 everyday except Mondays, when they are $12. Purchase online here. Through February 2019. Industry City, 34 34th Street, Building 6, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

Announcing the Nominees for the 2018 Distinguished Staff Awards

The College's Distinguished Staff Advisory Group (DSAG) is pleased to announce the following nominees for the 2018 Distinguished Staff Awards:

Bibi Bacchus-Khan, Victor Davila, Maria Dubon, Bernard Gibson, Adam Krumm, Winnie Kwan, Jessica Jackson, Seth Lambert, Marko Manriquez, Wayde McIntosh, Anna Ogier-Bloomer, Michael Severance, Jennyfer Scott, Luis Soto, Tyson Skross, Jennifer Sturtz, Sharon Victory, Christopher Weatherstone and Emily Weiland.

The Distinguished Staff Awards recognize outstanding staff contributions to SVA and its mission. DSAG congratulates those nominated and recognizes their commendable efforts. The two award recipients will be announced on Thursday, August 23, at the 2018 All-Staff Meeting. Thank you to those individuals who took the time to write thoughtful comments in nomination of their colleagues.
