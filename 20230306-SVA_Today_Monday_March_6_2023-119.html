Into the Spiel: A Sondheim Megillah

Celebrate Purim with an original musical that transforms Sondheim’s Into the Woods into a comedic, modern telling of the story of Purim. Free. Today, March 6, 6:30 – 8:30pm. Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, 30 West 68th Street.

The Land We’re On: Living Lenapehoking

Join contributors to A Lenapehoking Anthology as they explore the personal journeys of people seeking welcome in their ancestral homeland while pushing back against erasure. Free. In-person and online. Today, March 6, 6:30 – 7:30pm. Stavros Niarchos Foundation Library, 7th Floor Event Center, 455 Fifth Avenue.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Thursday | Finding Your Voice: A Conversation With Scott Ross

MFA Computer Arts presents a discussion with Chair Terrence Masson and the founder of Digital Domain and former Senior Vice President of LucasArts Entertainment Scott Ross. Thursday, March 9, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Friday | New York International Children’s Film Festival Industry Forum

New York International Children’s Film Festival presents its 2023 Industry Forum, a series of exciting, community conversations to celebrate, support and expand the children’s media field toward a more inclusive future. Register for free at nyicff.org/industry. Friday, March 10, 3:15 –5:30pm. Chelsea Factory, 547 West 26th Street.

Next Week | How to Launch a Career in Video Games

The game industry is the fastest-growing area of media and entertainment. Whether you want to be a developer, producer, marketer or editor, find out how you can build your skills to pursue your passion in this thriving field. Companies represented on the panel include Avalanche Studios, Mokuni Games and T2. Free and open to the public. Wednesday, March 15, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | AI and the Lens and Screen Arts Spring Symposium
