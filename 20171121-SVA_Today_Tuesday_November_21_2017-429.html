SVA Today
Tuesday, November 21, 2017

Joes@Moe's: Steve Brodner

For the second installment of Joes@Moe's, host Zipeng Zhu (BFA 2013 Design) interviews BFA Illustration faculty member Steve Brodner about teaching, surviving as an illustrator and the intersection of illustration and politics.
EVENT

6:30pm | Elaine Byrne and Mario Marchese

MFA Fine Arts presents artist Elaine Byrne and magician Mario Marchese. Byrne will speak about her recent video installation featuring found footage of the magic trick of women being sawed in half and interviews with barrier-breaking magician Dorothy Dietrich. Marchese will then give a magic performance. A conversation will follow. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
PRESS PICK

An Airline for the Art World

Artnet News on Qinmin Liu's (MFA 2017 Art Practice) new airline Angelhaha, which was conceived as an artwork itself and offers one-way flights to art events.
NYC PICK

Festival of Life

David Zwirner presents two major concurrent exhibitions of recent work by Yayoi Kusama on view across three gallery spaces in New York: "Festival of Life" at 525 and 533 West 19th Street, and "Infinity Nets" at the recently opened space on 34 East 69th Street. Click here for more information. Through Saturday, December 16.

Outdoor Activities

An exhibition of previously unseen lithographs and etchings, as well as watercolors and collages by William Wegman. Through Wednesday, January 10. Picture Room, 117 Atlantic Avenue, Brooklyn.
