SVA Today
Tuesday, April 11, 2017

Dusty’s Special Guests

SVA will present Dusty Outstanding Alumni Awards to Academy Award-winning composer Michael Giacchino (BFA 1990 Film and Video); producer and founder of Titmouse, Shannon Prynoski (BFA 1994 Film and Video); and director/producer and founder of Titmouse, Chris Prynoski (BFA 1994 Animation) at this year's Dusty Awards.
EVENTS

1:00pm | Internship-for-Credit Information Session

This session will cover the online application system, CareerLink, and important deadlines and procedures for internship-for-credit programs. RSVP here. 1:00 – 2:30pm. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 502. Another session will take place Friday, April 14, 1:00pm. RSVP here. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

1:00pm | Venture17: Beyond Design

MFA Design presents its annual thesis forum "Venture17: Beyond Design," where 19 graduating students will showcase their innovative design enterprises. RSVP here. 1:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

6:00pm | "Beautiful Losers" Reception

BFA Cartooning and BFA Illustration present an exhibition of work by their third-year students who created projects that explore the theme of the tragic hero and heroine in literature. 6:00 – 8:00pm. On view through Saturday, April 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street.

6:15pm | Tommy’s Honour Screening

SVA Filmmaker’s Dialogue invites you to join director Jason Connery and actor Jack Lowden for a preview screening of Tommy’s Honour. Free with SVA ID (limit three guests per student, faculty and staff member). 6:15pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

7:00pm | Ethan Hill

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by portrait and celebrity photographer Ethan Hill. 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

TAKE NOTE

Artist-in-Residence Caroline Woolard: Idea Lab and Gallery Talk

The Visible Futures Lab presents an Idea Lab and a gallery talk by Caroline Woolard. The Idea Lab is a public workshop where you can meet other artists, designers and craftspeople who will participate in facilitated resource sharing. RSVP here. Tomorrow, April 12, 3:00 – 5:00pm. Later, Caroline will give a talk and guided tour of the work she developed during her 7-week residency at the Visible Futures Lab. Refreshments will follow. 6:00pm. Visible Futures Lab, 132 West 21st Street, 7th floor.

BFA Interior Design Thesis Reception

BFA Interior Design presents a student exhibition representing a wide range of projects and interests, each of which integrates conceptual thinking with programmatic layout, design and interior detailing to create an inspiring new place. Tuesday, April 18, 9:00am – 8:00pm. Affirmation Arts, 523 West 37th Street.

Rescheduled: David Gonzalez

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by award-winning writer, photographer and co-editor of The New York Times’ LensBlog. Tuesday, April 18, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

SVA on Kickstarter

SVA students, faculty and alumni can use Kickstarter, a funding platform, to raise money for creative projects. SVA Kickstarter is currently featuring active projects from MFA Interaction Design students. To add your project to SVA’s curated page, click here, set up your campaign and email the link to extrelations@sva.edu. If you are a student, please include your major.
