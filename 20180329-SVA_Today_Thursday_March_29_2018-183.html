News and events from around SVA
SVA Today
Thursday, March 29, 2018

"You and Your Private Journey"

Vice talks with painter Inka Essenhigh (MFA 1994 Fine Arts), whose work is currently on view at the Virginia Museum of Contemporary Art and will also be in two different New York galleries next month.
EVENTS

Noon | Yoga for Wellness and Creativity

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services presents this yoga workshop teaching skills to promote well-being and stress reduction. Yoga mats will be provided. SVA students only. RSVP not required. Email Christine Gilchrist at cgilchrist@sva.edu with any questions. Noon – 2:00pm. MPS Art Therapy Gallery Space, 132 West 21st Street, 5th floor.

2:30pm | LAIKA Information Session

A recruiter from LAIKA will discuss about how to apply for summer internships at the studio, what they look for in candidates and how to best prepare your reel and portfolio. Open to current SVA students. RSVP here. 2:30 – 4:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

7:00pm | Lucas Blalock and Steel Stillman

BFA Photography and Video and Dear Dave, present a conversation between artist Lucas Blalock and artist and writer Steel Stillman. 7:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

7:00pm | Nancy Shaver

BFA Fine Arts presents a talk by visiting artist Nancy Shaver. 7:00 – 9:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.
PRESS PICK

"Crafting a Student Short"

For Cartoon Brew, MFA Computer Arts student Devan Worth interviews two 2017 program graduates, Kirsten Cho and Ninaad Kulkarni, about their creative process, the making of their thesis films and their experiences during and after SVA.
NYC PICKS

Brian Finke: "Hip Hop Honeys"

An exhibition of behind-the-scenes photographs by Brian Finke (BFA 1998 Photography) of the professional models who act in supporting roles in the music videos of Kanye West, Jay-Z and other famous artists. Reception tonight, 6:00pm. On view through Saturday, May 12. ClampArt, 247 West 29th Street, ground floor.

Mod New York

Last chance to see this exhibition that traces the transformation of clothing between 1960 and 1973. Admission $12 – $18; purchase here. Through Sunday, April 1. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.
TAKE NOTE

Mindful Joint 2018: Contemporary Korean Art in Conversations

BFA Fine Arts presents Mindful Joint 2018, a two-day symposium that brings together artists, writers and curators from South Korea and the U.S. to break the generalized perception of Korea and understand the complexities of artists’ practices and cultural backgrounds. RSVP here. Thursday, April 5, 6:30 – 8:30pm, and Friday, April 6, 1:00 – 6:00pm. 335 West 16th Street, various floors.

MFA Products of Design and MoMA Design Store

Join MFA Products of Design at a reception celebrating their collaboration with the MoMA Design Store. RSVP required; if you would like to attend, email rsvp_momastore@moma.org. Thursday, April 5, 6:30 – 8:30pm. MoMA Design Store, 44 West 53rd Street.
