EVENTS

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-in Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).


Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

6:00pm | “Selections From Thesis Projects” Reception

An exhibition of work by 16 MFA Illustration as Visual Essay students from the class of 2023. Curated by acting chair and thesis coordinator David Sandlin. Reception: Tonight, May 4, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Monday, May 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Opens Today | “The Yellow Wallpaper”

An exhibition of artwork by 19 MFA Illustration as Visual Essay students based on Charlotte Perkins Gilman’s short story of the same name. Curated by department faculty member and BFA Comics and BFA Illustration Chair Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay). Reception: Thursday, May 11, 6:00 – 8:00pm. Through Monday, May 22. SVA Chelsea Gallery, 601 West 26th Street, 15th floor.

Last Chance | “Hide and Seek”

BFA 3D Animation and Visual Effects presents an interactive installation by student Mary Lindstrom. Closes today, May 4. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Last Chance | “Début 23”

An exhibition of multidisciplinary work by BFA Advertising and BFA Design students and recent alumni created during the 2022 – 2023 school year. Closes today, May 4. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.

NYC PICK

“Material Witness: Folk and Self-Taught Artists at Work”

An exhibition that explores how folk and self-taught artists learn with and through material engagement, often in ways that subvert conventional frameworks for artistic training. Free. Through Sunday, October 29. American Folk Art Museum, 2 Lincoln Square.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Career Development Virtual Office Hours This Month

Every Thursday in May, 10:00 – 11:00am, SVA Career Development will host its virtual office hour. A career counselor will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form on Thursday mornings to access a Zoom link to join.

All Staff Art Show

SVA staff are invited to participate in the 11th annual All Staff Art Show. Last year, over 45 works by talented staff were featured in the exhibition. Grab a piece from your collection or create something entirely new; all submissions are welcome. The deadline for submissions is Wednesday, May 31. To submit, click here.

Next Week | MFA Design for Social Innovation Thesis Showcase

MFA Design for Social Innovation presents its annual thesis show—this year, the class of 2023 thesis projects center community partners and collaborators to co-create for change. Thursday, May 11, 12:00 – 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Next Week | Summer Online Drawing Marathon with Peter Hristoff

SVA Continuing Education presents an online drawing marathon led by SVA faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts). Thursday, May 11, 6:30 – 9:00pm, online.
