SVA Today
Monday, April 11, 2016
The New SVA Subway Poster Has Arrived!
The latest SVA subway poster, designed by faculty member Viktor Koen (MFA 1992 Illustration as Visual Essay) with art direction by MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller, is a colorful tribute to the creative and productive spirit at the heart of the College. Debuting today, it will appear on subway platforms throughout the city until Sunday, June 5.
Opening: "Planting Season"
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of work by graduating students. Through Friday, April 29, VCS Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.
11:30am: BFA Computer Art Senior Thesis Presentations
BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects presents a screening of thesis films by graduating students for review by a jury of industry professionals. 11:30am - 3:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
6:30pm: SHEROES: She, Hero, Eros
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents artists Henrietta Mantooth, Joan Snyder and Betty Tompkins discussing their work and how the status of women has—and has not—changed in the art world over their 50-year careers. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
All-American Girls
It's Nice That features portraits from the series "Rodeo Girls" by Ilona Szwarc (BFA 2013 Photography).
NYC PICK OF THE DAY
Glitches in the Grass
A one-night-only collaborative exhibition by eight SVA students. 8:00 - 10:00pm, Rabbithole, 33 Washington Street, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE
Smeker
BFA Film presents a 30th anniversary screening of the 1986 Yugoslavian film, about a young man and his decision to leave behind a life on the streets for the army, only to be pulled back by his girlfriend. Filmmaker Zoran Amar will answer questions following the screening. Wednesday, April 27, 6:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
MFA Design for Social Innovation Thesis Presentations
MFA Design for Social Innovation presents graduating students discussing their thesis projects. Thursday, April 28, noon - 5:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
