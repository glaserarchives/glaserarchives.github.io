ANNOUNCEMENTS

Today | Anti-Asian/Asian American and Xenophobic Harassment Bystander Intervention Training

Nonprofit advocacy organization Right to Be hosts this free interactive training session that will give you the tools to intervene safely and effectively in anti-Asian/Asian American and xenophobic harassment using their “5 Ds” of bystander intervention. The session will cover the types of disrespect faced by Asians and Asian Americans—from microaggressions to violence—using a tool called the “spectrum of disrespect” and what concrete strategies can be used to distract, delegate, document, delay or directly intervene in a way that is safe, effective and centered on the needs of the person being targeted. Today, February 24, 2:00 – 3:00pm, online.
