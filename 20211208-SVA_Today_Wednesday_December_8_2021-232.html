SVA Today
Wednesday, December 8, 2021

Original Voices: A Discussion With the Indigenous Student Union
In recognition of Native American Heritage Month, SVA’s Diversity in 3D podcast honors those who identify as Native American and Indigenous. This episode provides a current perspective on the lived experiences of student leaders Lisa Marie Montgomery (BFA Design) and Kalani Van Meter (BFA Animation).
Events

2:00pm | Color Hour
Let's color together! Daniela and Abby from Student Health and Counseling Services are here to provide some virtual company and support while you work on a current art piece for finals or color something fun to de-stress and decompress. Chat about what’s on your mind and explore ways to deal with stress in healthy ways. Today, 2:00 – 3:00pm ET, online via Zoom.

6:00pm | Graduate Admissions Alumni Panel
SVA invites all prospective graduate students to meet Graduate Admissions staff and hear from a panel of graduate alumni. Gain insight into what it’s like to be a graduate student and make the most of the Manhattan campus experience. This panel will feature alumni from graduate programs such as MA/MAT Art Education, MPS Art Therapy and many more. SVA IN THE PRESS
Illustration by Jiayue Li (MFA 2019 Design). Via Creative Boom.

Jiayue Li in Creative Boom
Creative Boom showcases the thought-provoking illustrations of Jiayue Li (MFA 2019 Design), drawn predominantly with color pencils on textured paper. Though Li began drawing at a young age, she discovered her passion for creating visual narratives with graphic design during her time at SVA.
IN TOWN AND ONLINE

“The Shape of Things” by Carrie Mae Weems
“The Shape of Things,” by SVA’s 2016 commencement speaker and past faculty member Carrie Mae Weems, is a new series of large-scale installations incorporating projections of new and existing film footage, portraying modern-day turmoils through the form of a 19th-century carnival-like spectacle. Through Friday, December 31. Park Avenue Armory, 643 Park Avenue. Purchase tickets here.
ANNOUNCEMENTS

Last Chance: Apply for Alumni Scholarship Awards
Final-year undergraduate and graduate students are eligible to apply for Alumni Scholarship Awards to aid the completion of their final projects. The awards are intended to cover project expenses and range from $500 to $2,000. A cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher is required to qualify. The deadline to apply is tonight, 11:59pm ET. For guidelines on how to apply, click here. For more information on requirements, click here.

Health Tips for the Holidays
With the holiday season underway, it’s important to stay safe and healthy. Student Health and Counseling Services recommends all students to get both flu and COVID-19 booster shots, get tested for COVID-19 before and/or after attending holiday gatherings, and to stay home if they aren’t feeling well. Click here for more information on the flu vaccine, and here for more information on the booster. For vaccine locations in New York City, click here.
