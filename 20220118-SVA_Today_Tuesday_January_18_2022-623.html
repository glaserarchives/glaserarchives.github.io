ANNOUNCEMENTS

SVA's nurse, Abby Wohl, answers your questions about COVID-19 and other health-related concerns. Read below for the latest Q&A and click here for the question archive. Submit your questions here.

I heard that SVA is requiring weekly COVID-19 testing for all residential students. Will you accept rapid antigen test results, or only PCR?

In order to continue our mission to keep the entire community as safe as possible, yes, SVA is requiring all residential students to test weekly for COVID-19. While PCR test results are more sensitive:
  • we do acknowledge that some individuals may have recently recovered from COVID-19 and may get false positive PCR results;
  • they may have difficulty making an appointment for a PCR test during the current omicron surge; or
  • they may be told that the wait time for a result will be too long.
Since there is evidence to support rapid antigen testing in this setting, we will accept antigen test results as long as they have been administered by a reputable source (i.e., a pharmacy, lab, health-care provider or clinic) and contain two unique identifiers in the final result report (i.e., your name and date of birth) along with the test type, collection and result dates.

Please remember to upload your results to Cleared4 every week and to keep your important health-related questions coming!

Call for Submissions: Reflecting on the Legacy of Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.
In celebration of Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.’s birthday, Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at SVA is inviting submissions of work reflecting on Dr. King’s legacy and life’s work. This call is open to all students, faculty and staff; examples of work include written pieces, paintings, videos, photos, illustrations, etc. Select work may be included in programming and on social media for SVA’s observance of Black History month in February. For more information or to submit your work, click here.

If you have tested positive for COVID-19, you must report your status to SVA as soon as possible. Students should email student-covid-alert@sva.edu; staff should email employee-covid-alert@sva.edu.
