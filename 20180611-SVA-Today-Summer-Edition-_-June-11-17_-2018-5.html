Announcing the Spring 2018 Term Honors Recipients

A total of 662 students from SVA's 11 undergraduate majors have been awarded Term Honors for spring 2018, and the list of recipients is now available on MySVA. To be included, students must successfully complete 12 credits of coursework, without any grades of incomplete, and earn a term GPA within the top 20% of all students in their major department. Students whose term GPA is within the top 5% of all students in their major department receive High Term Honors. Congratulations to the honorees!
