Events

2:00pm | Virtual Support Space for International Students: Readjusting to Campus Life and Managing Pandemic Stress
Join the first Virtual Support Space for International Students of the academic year hosted by Cong (Holly) Fu from Student Health and Counseling Services and William Park from International Student Office today, 2:00 – 3:00pm. For this month's session, Amy Zhu, a psychiatric nurse practitioner from Graymatters, will join to discuss how to readjust to campus life and manage pandemic stress. RSVP here.

3:00pm | SVA Club Fair
Clubs are a fantastic way to make friends, discover new interests and get involved on campus. Explore some of SVA's 23 clubs at the club fair, today, 3:00 – 5:00pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Clubs that will be present today: Black Student Union, Chinese Students Organization, Dance Club, Fiber Arts Club, Gamer Guild, Indigenous Student Union, Korean International Student Union, La Bodega, Stickers at SVA, SVA Cos, Veterans Coalition of the Arts and Waffle Club. Questions? Email clubs@sva.edu.

3:00pm | 11th Annual SocDoc Thesis Showcase
The MFA Social Documentary Film class of 2021 will share their thesis films, which inspire and amaze—particularly since work on them was done through the global pandemic. Screenings will occur online with select showings in-person today starting at 3:00pm, at SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. Virtual director Q&As will also take place on Monday, September 20, 8:00am. View the full schedule here and RSVP here to receive streaming links and to attend any in-person screenings or online Q&As.

Saturday | SVA at Photoville
SVA Continuing Education and MPS Digital Photography will each present displays at New York City’s 2021 Photoville Festival, both located at Brooklyn Bridge Park, Pier 3. Click here to learn more.

Attend the Opening Day event tomorrow, September 18, 1:00 – 8:00pm, to see the debut of these SVA exhibitions, hear live music, participate in fun activities and eat at Brooklyn's Smorgasburg. The exhibitions will be on view through Wednesday, December 1.

Sunday | After School Special World Premiere: Love Is in the Legend

After School Special, SVA's alumni film and animation festival, kicks off this weekend with the world premiere of Love Is in the Legend, a documentary about the 1980s ball scene, followed by a Q&A with some of the film's subjects, as well as editor Yanni Feder (BFA 2002 Film and Video) and photographer Tina Paul (BFA 1982 Photography). Sunday, September 19, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. (Click here for SVA Theatre's COVID-19 protocols).

After School Special features daily screenings and events through Sunday, September 26. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. See the full festival schedule here.

Designpreneurs Hackathon: Info Session and Digital Design Workshop

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Constitution Day 2021
Today is Constitution Day, a day of federal observance which commemorates the day in 1787 when the U.S. Constitution was signed at the Constitutional Convention in Philadelphia. To learn more about the U.S. Constitution visit the U.S. Senate website, or pick up a free pocket-sized edition at Student Affairs, 340 East 24th Street.

Internship-for-Credit Program Fall Deadline Extended!
The application deadline for the fall 2021 Internship-for-Credit Program has been extended to Thursday, September 23, 11:59pm. No late applications will be accepted. All applications must be submitted through SVA’s CareerLink portal. Please review the Student Internship Guides under the Resources section of the internship webpage before applying.
