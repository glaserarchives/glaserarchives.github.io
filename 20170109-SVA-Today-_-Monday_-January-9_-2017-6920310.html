SVA Welcomes New MFA Computer Art Chair

Terrence Masson, an experienced educator, animation and visual effects artist and producer, has begun his tenure as chair of the MFA Computer Art Department.
EVENT

5:30pm | Blind Cinema

SVA Theatre and PS122 present Britt Hatzius' Blind Cinema, part of PS122's Coil 2017 Festival. In the darkness of a cinema space, you sit blindfolded as a child seated behind you describes a film that only they will see. Tickets are $20 and limited; purchase here. Waitlist tickets may be available an hour prior to each performance and a few free student tickets will be made available nightly; email svatheatre@sva.edu with the subject "Blind Cinema SVA" if interested. Nightly, Monday, January 9 – Thursday, January 12, 5:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Celebrity Ink

TAKE NOTE

ART NOIR: A Conversation between Jason Moran and Joan Jonas

MFA Fine Arts and ART NOIR present a conversation between Jason Moran, a jazz pianist, composer and performance artist, and Joan Jonas, a renowned multimedia artist. Tomorrow, January 10, 6:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Susan Bank

Call for Submissions: Lunar New Year Flyer Competition

SVA's International Student Office and Student and Health Counseling Services invite all students to submit a Lunar New Year flyer design celebrating the Year of the Rooster. Deadline is Thursday, January 19. Winner will receive $100. Email your flyer as a JPEG to Yoko Anderson at yanderson@sva.edu. There will be a Lunar New Year celebration on Monday, January 30, noon – 2:00pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

SVA in Rome and Avignon Information Session

Attend a brief information session about two of SVA Destinations' two-week summer programs: Underground Rome, where you can explore the most hidden layer of the city – the underground (May 14 – May 28); and Art History in Southern France, where you can draw, paint and photograph on location as you explore the region's art history (June 1 – June 14). Refreshments will be provided. Friday, January 20, 1:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 401C.
