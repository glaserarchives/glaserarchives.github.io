Dear SVA Staff and Faculty

 

In 2020, with our budget under unprecedented pressure at the height of the pandemic, SVA had to make the difficult decision to suspend 401k employer matching contributions. The plan allows for discretionary contributions if SVA is able to do so. Thanks to your efforts in controlling costs, this year SVA has made a discretionary contribution to the plan of $2,000,000. All staff and faculty who have active 401k accounts have received a discretionary contribution equivalent to 5.22% of their 2021 gross compensation. This contribution should appear on your June 30 statement from Voya. 

 

SVA will continue to monitor our economic situation, as it relates to the reinstatement of the match for the calendar year beginning January 1, 2023.

 

I appreciate your patience and your continued contribution to the college.

 

Best,
