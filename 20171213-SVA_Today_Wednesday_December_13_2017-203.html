Bodily

MA Curatorial Practice fellows John Elammar, María Sáenz and Andrea Valencia present a performance event with New York-based artists that will make visitors rethink the social and political dynamics of the body. Saturday, December 16, 7:00pm. CP Projects Space, 132 West 21st Street, 10th floor.

The Long Friday Screening and Discussion

Join MFA Design for Social Innovation for a screening of The Long Friday, a documentary-in-progress about the day 90% of women in Iceland walked off the job and out of their homes. Initially laughed off as "a huge joke" by many men, the strike laid the foundation for Iceland's status today as the world's most gender-equal country. A conversation with the strike's leaders will follow the screening. Tickets $12; purchase here. Wednesday, January 10, 6:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
