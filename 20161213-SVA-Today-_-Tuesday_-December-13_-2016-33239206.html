SVA Theatre presents a 3D screening of Passengers, directed by Morten Tyldum, who studied at SVA, and starring Jennifer Lawrence and Chris Pratt. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). Tuesday, December 20, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Call for Submissions: Humanities and Sciences Department's Annual Writing Contest

The Humanities and Sciences Department invites undergraduates to submit their original poetry, short fiction, personal essays, criticism and film scripts or plays. Winners will receive a cash prize, be published in The Match Factory, Humanities and Sciences' online magazine, and be considered for publication in Words, SVA's literary journal. Deadline is March 1; submit here. Winners will be announced on March 24.
