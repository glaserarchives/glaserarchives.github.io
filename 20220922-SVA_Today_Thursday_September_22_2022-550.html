NYC AND ONLINE PICKS

Reynier Leyva Novo: Methuselah

An online conversation and project demonstration celebrating the launch of the digital artwork by Reynier Leyva Novo that virtually reproduces the 6,000-mile transnational migratory journey of a single monarch butterfly, tracking its travel from southern Canada across the United States to Mexico. Free. Today, September 22, 7:00 – 8:30pm, online. The in-person exhibition opens Sunday, October 27, at El Museo del Barrio, 1230 Fifth Avenue.

We Gathered: 130 Years of Asian American Activism in New York City

A multidisciplinary celebration of over a century of Asian American activism in New York City featuring writers, artists, organizers and local politicians who will discuss activism across the Asian diaspora in New York City. Free. Thursday, September 22, 7:00 – 8:30pm. The Great Hall at Cooper Union, 7 East Seventh Street.
