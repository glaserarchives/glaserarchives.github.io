Doug Salati Wins 2023 Caldecott

The American Library Association has awarded SVA faculty member Doug Salati’s (MFA 2014 Illustration as Visual Essay) Hot Dog its top picture book honor, the Randolph Caldecott Medal.

Returning Student Room Selection 2023 – 2024

Want to live in SVA housing for the 2023 – 2024 academic year? Returning Student Room Selection is the process by which eligible returning students can select a space in one of the available SVA residence halls for the academic year. The 2023 – 2024 on-campus housing options for returning students will be in the 24th Street and Ludlow residences. For more information about Returning Student Room Selection, visit here or email reslife@sva.edu.
