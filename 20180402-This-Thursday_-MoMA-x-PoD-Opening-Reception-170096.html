Greetings all,

As you may know, for the past four years the students of our Products of Design Masters program have been working with The Museum of Modern Art's Wholesale Division to create new objects—manufactured and produced by MoMA. (See blog stories here, here, and here.)

To celebrate this ongoing partnership, we wanted to invite you to the opening reception of the launch of 6 new objects at MoMA's flagship midtown store. (This event also kicks off the month-long April window displays of "MoMA x Products of Design" in BOTH the midtown and SoHo locations!)

If you're in NYC, please come celebrate a great evening of design with us. Here are the details:

Event: MoMA x PoD Reception
Date: Thursday, April 5, 6:30–8:30pm
Location: MoMA Design Store, Midtown. 44 West 53rd St, New York City

Please RSVP to rsvp_momastore@moma.org with "SVA Celebration" in the subject line!

And of course, please do feel welcome to bring a friend, loved one, workmate, student, or MoMA fan to the party. Design and refreshments for all!

Looking forward to seeing you there,
The MFA Products of Design team
