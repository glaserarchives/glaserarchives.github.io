SVA Today
July 18 - 24
FEATURES
Julie Klausner and Billy Eichner. Photo courtesy of Hulu.

Julie Klausner of Difficult People on Creativity

The MFA Illustration as Visual Essay alumnus and star of the Hulu series shares her thoughts as the second season is released.

SVA at Annecy in Five Photos

SVA had a big presence this year at Annecy, the world's largest international animation film festival. Angie Wojak, director of Career Development at the College, moderated a panel and SVA alumni presented two films.

Cover Stories: Alex Mar's Witches of America

Cover designer Rachel Willey (BFA 2012 Graphic Design) and author Alex Mar talk about the cover for Witches of America, which was named a Notable Book of 2015 by The New York Times Book Review.
THIS WEEK

Tuesday (7/19): Lunchtime Lecture Series: Dread Scott in Conversation with Ron English

MFA Art Practice presents a conversation between artist Dread Scott, whose work incorporates performance, photography, screen-printing, video installation and painting, and street artist Ron English, whose work explores brand imagery and advertising. 12:30pm, 335 West 16th Street, Room 501H.

Tuesday (7/19): MFA Visual Narrative Thesis Presentations

Graduating MFA Visual Narrative students present their thesis projects, which are on view in "And Then..." 6:00 - 9:00pm, 209 East 23rd Street, 3rd-floor amphitheater.

Wednesday (7/20): Looking: The Movie

The SVA Theatre and NewFest present a screening of the feature film follow-up to the HBO series. Free with SVA ID (limit one guest per student, faculty and staff member). RSVP with code "SVANF." A reception will precede the screening at 6:30pm. 7:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Thursday (7/21): Summer Residency Open Studios: Sculpture, Installation and New Media Art

6:00 - 8:00pm, 335 West 16th Street, lower level through 2nd floor.

Thursday (7/21): "Trading Places" Reception

An exhibition of work by MFA Art Practice students. 6:00 - 8:00pm, SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street. Exhibition runs through Saturday, July 30.

Friday (7/22): "And Then..." Reception

An exhibition of thesis projects by members of the MFA Visual Narrative class of 2016, curated by faculty members Jonathon Rosen and Ed Valentine. 6:00 - 8:00pm, SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street. Exhibition runs through Tuesday, July 26.

Saturday (7/23): Star Trek Beyond in 3D

The Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Star Trek Beyond, starring Chris Pine, Simon Pegg, Zachary Quinto and Zoe Saldana. Members of the film's visual effects team will answer questions via Skype following the screening. Free with SVA ID (one guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). 3:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Sunday (7/24): Ghostbusters in 3D

The Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Ghostbusters, starring Kristen Wiig, Melissa McCarthy, Kate McKinnon and Leslie Jones. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). 3:00pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
