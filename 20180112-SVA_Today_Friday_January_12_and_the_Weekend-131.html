NYC PICKS

King in New York

Commemorating the 50th anniversary of the death of Martin Luther King Jr., this exhibition traces the civil rights leader’s encounters with New York from the 1950s until his assassination in 1968. Tickets $12 – $18; purchase here. Through June 2018. On Monday, January 15, 11:00am – 2:00pm, attend the museum's corresponding family program, free with admission. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.

Laura Owens at the Whitney

Call for Submissions Extended: Black History Month Exhibition

SVA's Residence Life and Student Engagement and Leadership offices are hosting a February 2018 exhibition featuring works by black and African American student-artists of SVA. Any interested students who identify as black, African American and/or of the African diaspora may submit as many pieces as they would like, in any medium of their choosing. Deadline extended to Monday, January 22, 11:59am. Selections will be announced by Wednesday, January 24. To submit, click here. For more information, email Cullen Rude at crude@sva.edu.
