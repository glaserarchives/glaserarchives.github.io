Megan Bowker (BFA 2016 Design), Enle Li (BFA 2015 Design), Xinmei Liu (MFA 2020 Illustration as Visual Essay) and Jenny Lucia Mascia (BFA 2018 Design) have been named finalists in The One Club for Creativity’s Young Guns 20 competition.

NYC PICK

Ancient Mali and the Modern World: Manuscripts, Archives and Uncomfortable Truths

A talk exploring how the Malian manuscripts can help rewrite dominant histories against the backdrop of prevailing Western narratives, as well as the stakes of preservation, the political suppression of collective memory and African knowledge and who holds the right to structure an archive. Tonight, September 29, 7:00 – 8:30pm. Brooklyn Central Library, Dweck Center, 10 Grand Army Plaza, Brooklyn.

ISO Gallery: Honoring Hispanic Heritage

SVA’s International Student Office calls for submissions of artwork that relates to Hispanic culture or artwork from students with Hispanic backgrounds. The deadline for submissions is Wednesday, October 5. Submissions will be displayed in the ISO reception area as part of an exhibition running from Wednesday, October 12, to Wednesday, November 9. Click here to access the submission requirements and form. Contact Chisato Takeyama at ctakeyama@sva.edu with any questions or to schedule artwork drop-off at ISO.
