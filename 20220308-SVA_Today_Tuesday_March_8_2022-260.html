ANNOUNCEMENTS

SVA Stands in Solidarity With Asian/AAPI Communities
SVA strongly condemns the recent hate-based acts of violence and harassment against Asian and AAPI community members in New York City and elsewhere in the country. Racism, bigotry, discrimination and harassment are neither tolerated nor accepted in the College’s community.

Students seeking support are encouraged to reach out to our Student Health and Counseling Services. Current undergraduate and graduate students may request an online or in-person appointment with one of SVA’s licensed therapists by filling out this form or emailing health@sva.edu. Short-term counseling services are available free of cost, even if you’ve waived SVA’s student health insurance plan. Spring 2022 appointments are between 9:00am and 3:00pm ET, Monday through Friday, and a Mandarin-speaking therapist is available.

To read the full statement on recent events and for additional resources, visit the College-Wide Communications section of sva.edu/dei.
