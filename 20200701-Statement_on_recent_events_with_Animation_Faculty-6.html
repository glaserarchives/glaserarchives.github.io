Greetings:

Last week I sent to the BFA Animation students the following email regarding the termination of former faculty member Donald Poynter.  At the urging of numerous students from other departments, I am sharing that email with the entire SVA student body.

I will also take the opportunity of this email to acknowledge the list that has been circulating concerning allegations about inappropriate behavior from other members of the faculty, both past and present.  I want to assure everyone that the information contained in these allegations is taken very seriously, but I’m not at liberty to comment on the allegations or any possible action taken in response to specific allegations.

Sincerely,


Christopher J. Cyphers
Provost
June 25, 2020

Greetings:

Amidst a growing list of complaints against SVA faculty member Donald Poynter by SVA students for behavior that is at odds with the College’s mission and not in keeping with our core values, Mr. Poynter has been terminated, effective immediately.

Every member of the SVA community should know that the College does not tolerate in any way acts of discrimination or harassment, and that every complaint made by any member of the SVA community is thoroughly investigated and acted upon in an appropriate fashion.

No student, faculty and staff member, alumni or guests of the College should feel they do not have recourse if they feel they are the victim of discrimination or harassment.

Claims made on social media to the contrary, SVA has taken a firm stance against discrimination and harassment, investigated all complaints, and, where these complaints can be substantiated, taken disciplinary action.

SVA’s Policy Against Sex-Based Discrimination, Harassment and Sexual Misconduct can be found at  sva.edu/students/sexual-misconduct.


Sincerely,


Christopher J. Cyphers
Provost
