SVA Today | Your guide to everything
Monday, December 14, 2015
Meet Molly Heintz
The MFA Design Criticism alumnus was named co-chair of MFA Design Research, Writing and Criticism earlier this year. SVA Close Up spoke to Heintz about her first semester in the role and her plans for the future.
5:30pm: Holiday Print Slam
MFA Visual Narrative presents an exhibition and sale of books, zines and print editions produced by students of the inaugural RisoLab continuing education course. 5:30 - 8:30pm, RisoLab, 136 West 21st Street, 11th floor.
6:00pm: BFA Fine Arts Open Studios Reception
BFA Fine Arts presents students showing recent work in their studios. 6:00 - 9:00pm, 335 West 16th Street, 2nd - 5th floors.
7:00pm: Edward Scissorhands
The SVA Theatre presents a 25th anniversary screening of Tim Burton's Edward Scissorhands (1990), starring Johnny Depp, Winona Ryder and Dianne Wiest. Free and open to the public. Searing is first-come, first-served. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
Press pick of the day
Slow Fashion in Charlotte
The Charlotte Observer profiles MFA Design for Social Innovation alumnus Stephen Bernasconi on the new trends making the North Carolina city a "slow fashion" destination.
NYC pick of the day
Store Front II: A History Preserved
Photographers Karla and James Murray give an illustrated lecture based on their book that documents the changing face of New York City streets since the '90s. Admission is free. 6:30pm, Mid-Manhattan Library, 455 Fifth Avenue.
Take Note
Declare a Chosen or Preferred Name for SVA Records
Students, faculty and staff can now declare a chosen or preferred name to appear on all official College records. The declaration form can be found in the Downloads column on the right-hand side of the Registrar page. Once completed, submit the form to the Registrar for processing.
