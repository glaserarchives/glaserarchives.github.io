Tonight | Alexis Rockman at SVA
Join Career Development and MFA Fine Arts for a virtual talk by painter Alexis Rockman (BFA 1985 Fine Arts) about his career and artistic practice. A native New Yorker, Rockman often depicts the effects of climate change on future landscapes and the biodiversity crisis in his work. Join on Zoom. 6:00 – 7:00pm.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Friday | AAPI Faculty/Staff Forum
Members of the Asian and Asian American communities continue to face harassment, discrimination and physical violence. On Friday, October 15, 11:00am – 12:30pm ET, faculty and staff who self-identify as AAPI are invited to join SVA Diversity, Equity and Inclusion for a virtual forum to connect on these or other topics for peer support. Some of you may have participated in April; new members are welcome. If you are interested in participating, please fill out this Google Form to register. Zoom information will follow.

If you are unable to participate, you can still fill out the Google Form to indicate your interest in future opportunities and to be included in future correspondence.

Deadline Approaching | "Stop the Spread!": Student Health and Counseling Services' Poster Competition
Student Health and Counseling Services (SH&CS) invites all current SVA students to take part in a poster competition to communicate health and wellness messaging on campus and prevent the spread of COVID-19 and the flu. The winner will receive a $50 gift card to the Campus Store and all entries will be considered to be used by SH&CS in social media and print campaigns. Check out the Google Form for more information and to submit your artwork. Competition ends on Monday, October 18, and the winner will be announced Thursday, October 21.

Next Week | How Do I Find an Internship? Tips for a Successful Internship Search
Designed specifically for students planning to intern while at SVA, this interactive workshop will provide an overview of how to search for internships and what materials can assist you. Presented by Career Development. Tuesday, October 19, 3:00 – 4:00pm. Register here to join on Zoom.
