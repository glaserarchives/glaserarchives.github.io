Join us for an Hour of Reflection on Our Day of Healing


Dear SVA Community,

We invite the campus (students, faculty, and staff) to join us on Wednesday, April 19, at 2pm in the  Beatrice at the SVA Theatre for an Hour of Reflection on our Day of Healing. As a society, we have witnessed a series of traumatic events, such as police brutality, gun violence, natural disasters, and the systematic attack on civil rights of BIPOC, AAPI, and LGBTQIA+ communities.  In our personal and professional spaces, we have either overtly expressed our concern and/or we have dealt with these collective situations in silence.  

In response to these issues, the DEI Office will host a Day of Healing where we bring the campus community together to take a necessary pause to recognize the magnitude of these events.  We have invited a licensed therapist Dr. Archandria Owens and other campus leaders to create a physical and virtual space to contextualize sources of these traumas and how they manifest within our daily lives.  Dr. Owens will provide healthy strategies to assist us with learning how to heal individually, and how to heal collectively as a campus.  

You have the option of attending in person or via live stream. If you RSVP for virtual attendance the Zoom webinar link will be sent to you closer to the date of the event. Additionally, we will provide a time for Dr. Owens to address questions from the audience.  During the event, you will have the opportunity to ask Dr. Owens questions.  Due to time and additional factors, we can not guarantee that all questions will be answered, but we will do our best to accommodate you.  Space will be limited in the Beatrice Theatre, so please RSVP by April 17. If you need any special accommodations, please email us at diversity@sva.edu.  

We look forward to seeing you all. Be well.


Jarvis M. Watson, Ed.D.
Director
Office of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion
