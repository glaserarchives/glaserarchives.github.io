Human Resources

Direct Deposit

Directly depositing your check into your bank account is the fastest, easiest and safest way to get paid. We strongly encourage everyone to take advantage of it. Contact Tristan Roque at troque@sva.edu to enroll.


SVA Health Insurance

Cigna has expanded plan coverage to support the well-being of its customers as follows:

  • Waiver of cost-sharing and co-payments of COVID-19 testing, office visits, virtual care and treatment through May 31, 2020.

  • Expanded telehealth services for in and out-of-network providers.  There are several ways you can connect with a doctor by phone or video to discuss symptoms related to COVID-19:

    1. Call or email your primary care provider.

    2. Connect with a board-certified doctor by phone or online 24/7 by visiting myCigna.com and selecting the “Connect Now” button. 

    3. Call MDLIVE at 888.726.3171 or Amwell at 855.667.9722.

  • 24-hour COVID-19 toll-free help line (1.866.912.1687), where you can speak with behavioral health clinicians for one-on-one support.

Employee Assistance Program

We are all trying to adjust to this “new normal” and the anxiety that comes with it. Cigna’s Behavior Health and Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is ready to help. To contact them, call 800.274.7603.

 

Harassment, Discrimination, and Sexual Misconduct

The Title IX Office is available to support and respond to incidents of harassment, discrimination and sexual misconduct.  If you are aware of students who need support for domestic violence or any kind of sexual violence, please refer them to Laurel Christy, Title IX Coordinator at lchristy@sva.edu.  There is an online form available for reports of Harassment, Discrimination, or Sexual Misconduct.  Please complete this form if you are aware of incidents or have any concerns about students in the SVA community.

 

Office of Learning Technologies Resources
The Office of Learning Technologies (OLT) has incorporated online office hours into its "new normal." Typically OLT supports faculty and students with adopting best practices for online teaching and learning. However, many of these processes translate to remote workers as well. Connect face-to-face (on-screen) with an OLT staff member if you have questions related to improving your online communications and workflows.


OLT online office hours will run Monday through Friday, from 10:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and will remain at this link for the remainder of this spring 2020 semester. We encourage you to click on the link and then bookmark it for future reference. If you have never joined a Zoom video conference before, then you will be prompted to download the Zoom app, but can also simply join through your web browser.

You can also leave OLT an email or voicemail and they will respond to you as quickly as possible, olt@sva.edu and 212.592.2313.

 

Information Technology Resources

Access your work tools from a web browser by going to https://myid.sva.edu. Available apps include Google products such as Gmail, Calendar, Docs and Drive, Zoom for video conferencing and Grammarly, an AI-powered writing assistant. In addition to these resources, IT has created a virtual space to house the following desktop apps: Adobe Acrobat DC, Microsoft Word/Excel/Powerpoint, and Perceptive Content. 

 

Links to WebAdvisor, Kronos, Canvas, Pingboard (employee directory), Linkedin Learning (training portal), and many more are also available via your myID dashboard.

 

For access to Colleague applications and department file shares, please use the VMWare Horizon icon in your myID dashboard to access your virtual workspace. Access to Colleague is restricted. 

 

Coming soon: Office 365, for access to cloud-based Word, Excel, and Powerpoint and also download Microsoft Office for home use. An announcement will be made in the next two weeks.

 

Please contact helpdesk@sva.edu if you have any questions or issues accessing any of these resources.
