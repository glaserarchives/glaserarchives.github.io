Monday, February 8, 2016
"Normal" in L.A. with Wyatt Mills
The BFA Fine Arts alumnus discusses his artistic influences and working in Los Angeles. His current solo show, "Normal," is on view at L.A.'s Project Gallery through March 6.
8:00pm: Taxi Driver
The SVA Theatre presents a 40th anniversary screening of Martin Scorsese's Taxi Driver (1976), starring Robert De Niro, Jodie Foster, Cybill Shepherd and Harvey Keitel. Free and open to the public. Seating is first-come, first-served. Doors open approximately 30 - 45 minutes prior to showtime. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
Press pick of the day
Strong Is Beautiful [Video]
Good Morning America featured Pantene's "Strong Is Beautiful" Super Bowl campaign, which tasked NFL dads with doing their daughters' hair. Emily Pracher (BFA 2015 Advertising), of Grey Advertising, served as art director for the project.

The Artist's Journal
BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents an exhibition of small-scale paintings that investigate personal iconography, narrative and color theory. Thursday, February 11, through Sunday, March 13. Reception Thursday, February 11, 6:00pm. VCS Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.
