Go Beyond Design at Venture17: Thesis Forum

We warmly welcome our colleagues in the design and business communities, the press, and the interested public to join us on April 11th at the SVA Theatre for a venture beyond design, and into the boundless potential of design entrepreneurship.

Nineteen graduating students will present their innovative design enterprises, each a product of intensive coursework, research, and consultation, and elevated by the program’s central focus on the thoughtful leverage of visual and entrepreneurial strategy.

Register to attend this free event on Eventbrite.

This event will be broadcast live at http://design.sva.edu/venture17 on the day of the show beginning at 1:00 PM.

You can participate online by tweeting your questions #venture17

Tuesday, April 11, 2017

Doors open at 12:30 PM. Event starts promptly at 1:00 PM.
