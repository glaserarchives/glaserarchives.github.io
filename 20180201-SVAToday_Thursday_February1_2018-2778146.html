"Underground Images" in the Netherlands

SVA's subway posters continue their worldwide tour with two shows in the Netherlands, at two of the most publicly trafficked sites the exhibition has had on its five-year journey thus far.

Announcing the Fall 2017 Term Honors Recipients

A total of 654 students from SVA's 11 undergraduate majors have been awarded Term Honors for fall 2017, and the list of recipients is now available on MySVA. To be included, students must successfully complete 12 credits of coursework, without any grades of incomplete, and earn a term GPA within the top 20% of all students in their major department. Students whose term GPA is within the top 5% of all students in their major department receive High Term Honors. Congratulations to the honorees!
