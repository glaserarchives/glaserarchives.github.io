Spring Break in LA

A rundown of last week's SVA Destinations trip in Los Angeles, where students visited various Hollywood studios, including Walt Disney Animation Studios and Cartoon Network.
EVENTS

6:30pm | Creative Arts Therapies for the Military Population

As part of its Community Lecture Series, MPS Art Therapy presents Melissa Walker, ATR, who will discuss the evolution of the Healing Arts Program at the National Intrepid Center of Excellence and Walter Reed National Military Medical Center, both in Bethesda, Maryland. Tonight, 6:30 – 8:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

7:00pm | Kaleidoscope Word and Multimedia Variety Show

SVA Humanities and Sciences presents a mini art fest of spoken word, visual art, music and dance performed by SVA students, alumni and faculty members. Tonight, 7:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

8:00pm | Titmouse Five-Second Animation Night

SVA Theatre and Titmouse present Five-Second Animation Night, an annual event for which Titmouse animators create whatever goodies have been floating around in their minds all year. Tickets $5 – $10; purchase here. Tonight, 8:00 – 10:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

The Weatherman: Exclusive Preview

Entertainment Weekly shares a look inside MFA Visual Narrative Chair Nathan Fox and writer Jody LeHeup's forthcoming sci-fi comic The Weatherman.
NYC PICKS

Irish Arts Center Book Day

Since 2011, Irish Arts Center staff and volunteers have distributed tens of thousands of free books to New Yorkers of all backgrounds every St. Patrick's Day. Pick one up a book at one of the pop-up stands across the five boroughs: locations here. Tomorrow, March 17, 9:00am.

St. Patrick's Day Parade

Watch the world's oldest and largest St. Patrick's Day Parade, marching up Fifth Avenue from 44th Street to 79th Street. Starts at 11:00am.

Paint and Donate

The Potter's Wheel presents Empty Bowl, a event where participants paint and donate a bowl for charity. Tickets $5; purchase here. Sunday, March 18, 2:00pm. Kew Gardens, 120-33 83rd Avenue, Queens.
TAKE NOTE

2018 VASA Candidates Meet and Greet

Meet this year's Visual Arts Student Association candidates as they discuss their platforms and answer students' questions. Monday, March 19, 3:30pm. SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street.

Updated Date and Location – Phil Patton Lecture: Natasha Jen

Note: This lecture was originally slated for Wednesday, March 21, at 136 West 21st Street.

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents a lecture by award-winning designer and SVA alumnus Natasha Jen, held in memory of department faculty member Phil Patton. Wednesday, March 28, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Isle of Dogs Screening

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Wes Anderson's new stop-motion animated film Isle of Dogs, with a special introduction by one of the film's producers, Jeremy Dawson (MFA 1993 Photography, Video and Related Media). Admission is free with SVA ID (one guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Thursday, March 22, 7:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
