3:30pm | Wellness Wednesdays: Panel Discussion on the Overturning of Roe v. Wade

SVA Diversity, Equity and Inclusion, in collaboration with Student Health and Counseling Services, presents a discussion on the recent Supreme Court decision to overturn Roe v. Wade, and how to understand your reproductive rights and access SVA resources. This discussion will be moderated by Jarvis M. Watson, director of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion. Panelists include Arielle Kempler, associate director, Student Health and Counseling Services; Abby Wohl, nurse, Student Health and Counseling Services; and Gabriela Andrade and Morgan Cousins of Planned Parenthood of Greater New York Action Fund. Today, October 12, 3:30pm, online.

The Alumni Scholarship Awards Application Is Now Open!

Class of 2023: Do you need funding for your final projects? Final-year undergraduate and graduate students are eligible to apply for Alumni Scholarship Awards to aid the completion of their final projects. These cash awards are intended to cover project expenses and range from $500 to $2,000. In 2022, 67 projects were funded a total of $52,500. The online application portal is now open. For further details and eligibility criteria and to apply, visit sva.edu/asa.
