Ramie Ahmed

Preserving LGBTQ Beauty

BFA Photography and Video student Ramie Ahmed talks about the importance of documenting life within the LGBTQ community and his mentorship with artist Clifford Prince King in an interview for Paper. Ahmed’s work is on view at “Mentors 2023” through Monday, March 27, at the SVA Chelsea Gallery.

EVENTS

5:00pm | Curatorial Roundtable: Marina Reyes Franco

MA Curatorial Practice presents a talk by Marina Reyes Franco, curator at the Museo de Arte Contemporáneo de Puerto Rico (MAC). Today, March 22, 5:00 – 6:00pm, online.

5:30pm | “Cultivating Oneself” Reception

An exhibition of painting, photography, digital drawing and sculpture exploring the construction and upkeep of identity by eight SVA students selected by a jury of their peers. Reception: Tonight, March 22, 5:30 – 7:30pm. Wednesday, March 22, through Monday, April 10. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street, ground floor.

6:00pm | Making the Cut at Pixar With Bobbie O’Steen

MFA Computer Arts presents a talk with Bobbie O’Steen on her book Making the Cut at Pixar: The Art of Editing Animation. Attend for an in-person look behind the scenes of Pixar and the art of film editing. Tonight, March 22, 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 301C.

SVA IN THE PRESS

“I Find Freedom in Constraints”
