Apply Now for Fall 2017 - Take a Tour

Fall 2017 Admissions deadline is right around the corner!

Gain the creative skills to tell compelling true stories that reflect our world. Students direct several documentaries with mentorship from our established filmmaker faculty, and are introduced to New York City's robust documentary film and tv community. Alumni have won Student Academy Awards and Emmys, and been distributed worldwide.

SocDoc will not only teach you how to tell a great story - we will guide you toward a career in the thriving documentary industry. Get in touch for a personal tour or info session.

Alumni Profile: Amitabh Joshi

Tashi’s Turbine, an uplifting tale of a small village’s attempt to harness the renewable enery of the wind, will be rebroadcast on PBS World Channel on Sunday, January 29. This film is directed by Amitabh Joshi (and his is SocDoc Thesis!) and is produced by Erik Spink, both current faculty and SocDoc alumni (2013).

Amitabh talks with us about the film and his career as a filmmaker in this short interview with current SocDoc student, James Stuart.

Briefly...

SocDoc is representing at Sundance Film Festival this week! Producing faculty Beth Levison is there with her doc 32 Pills, a recipient of a Sundance Documentary Fund Grant; Mudflow, directed by SocDoc alumnus Sasha Friedlander and Oscar-winner Cynthia Wade also received that grant. Rancher, Farmer, Fisherman, directed by Susan Froemke & John Hoffman premieres at the festival and has two of our faculty on the team – Story Editor Deborah Dickson and DP Bob Richman. Here’s to a great week in Park City!

Our fingers are tightly crossed for the Oscar nominations announcement on Tuesday – two SocDoc faculty-produced films are on the list: Cinematography faculty Kirsten Johnson’s Cameraperson, and Life, Animated – Directing faculty Richard Hankin is consulting editor on the film and alumnus Lindsay Richardson is Associate Producer.

On a political tip: Alumni Sasha Friedlander & Jenni Morello directed a #WhyIMarch video for the Women’s March. Peace & Solidarity to all marching for equality this weekend. ❤️️
