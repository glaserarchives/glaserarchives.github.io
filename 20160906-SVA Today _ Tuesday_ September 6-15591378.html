SVA Library West Reopens

Beginning today, SVA Library West (133/141 West 21st Street, lower level) resumes regular hours, Monday through Friday, 11:00am - 4:00pm. This satellite location features books, movies, video games and magazines from our main collection, as well as a group study room, computers, printers, a book scanner and an on-site librarian for consultation. Items can be sent between the two SVA Library locations for next day pickup.
