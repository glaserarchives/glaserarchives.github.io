ANNOUNCEMENTS

SVA Galleries Call for Entries

Want to exhibit at SVA? Applications are now open for matriculated students in any major, working in any medium. All entries will be reviewed by a select student jury. Accepted entries will be included in a series of group exhibitions in 2023. The deadline for submissions is Monday, November 28, 11:59 ET. Submit your application here.

Apply for ISO Cultural Partner Program

Do you want to be engaged in local communities by participating in community service projects? SVA’s International Student Office welcomes all students to apply for a two-semester long membership-based program, featuring New York City culture trips, a monthly discussion series and the opportunity to participate in and lead a community service project. It is a small, supportive group where you can connect with students from different majors and years. Program requirements and the application can be viewed here. Email Yoko Anderson, yanderson@sva.edu, with any questions.
