EVENTS

Faculty and Staff Appreciation Week

SVA Campus Store presents Faculty and Staff Appreciation week through Friday, April 20. Stop in for double discounts on all computers (purchased on April 20 only) and choose one free accessory. Spin the prize wheel for up to 25% off SVA-branded merchandise. All shoppers will receive a free gift and be entered into a weekly raffle for great prizes. Stop by the store before the event to set up your SVA Campus Store Technology Loan and recycle your old tech with Encore! for a gift card toward a future purchase. Email campusstore@sva.edu for more information. SVA Campus Store, 207 East 23rd Street.

Food for Fines at SVA Library

Need to clear your library fines in order to graduate or check materials out? Every canned or nonperishable food item brought into the SVA Library reduces your fines by $2. Peanut butter, beans and canned tuna are always in demand; no ramen, frozen food or perishables. All donations will be given to City Harvest. Through Monday, April 30. SVA Library, 380 Second Avenue, 2nd floor.

BFA Photography and Video Senior Show

BFA Photography and Video presents a show featuring work by its class of 2018. Through Tuesday, May 8. Reception: Sunday, May 6, 5:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, 2nd, 5th and 6th floors.

NYC PICK

Peanuts Global Artist Collective Murals

Starting today, view Peanuts-themed murals by seven contemporary artists, including Kenny Scharf (BFA 1981 Fine Arts), on the streets of the Hudson Square neighborhood. Plan a walking tour starting at the Children's Museum of the Arts using its provided map. Children's Museum of the Arts, 103 Charlton Street.
TAKE NOTE

Be Featured in SVA Style for the Chance to Win $50

SVA Style is looking to feature the personal objects, ephemera, knick-knacks and other items that students and faculty collect that inspire them. Email a description of your object(s) to style@sva.edu and SVA Style will reach out to get your items photographed. Time is of the essence, so submit ASAP. Students whose items are photographed will be entered for a chance to win a $50 Apple/iTunes gift certificate.

Spotlight on Broadway

Center for Communication presents a panel about working on Broadway with the Tony-winning producer of Spring Awakening Tom Hulce, executive producer of SpongeBob SquarePants: The Broadway Musical Susan Vargo and Rick Miramontez, head of Broadway's O&M PR firm. Free with SVA ID and RSVP. Monday, April 30, 6:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

MFA Products of Design Thesis Presentations

Join MFA Products of Design as its 17 graduating students each present their yearlong thesis work, comprised of research, artifacts, services, experiences and platforms. RSVP here. Thursday, May 3, 11:00am – 5:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
