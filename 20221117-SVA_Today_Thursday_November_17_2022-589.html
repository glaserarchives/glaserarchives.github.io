EVENTS

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour

Join SVA Career Development’s virtual office hour today, 10:00am. During the one-hour session, Associate Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

1:00pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours

Every Thursday, 1:00 – 2:30pm, SVA’s International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to “International Student Office Staff” headline).

2:30pm | VASA Town Hall

Connect with VASA officers and receive answers to any questions you may have at this semester’s first Town Hall meeting. Today, November 17, 2:30pm, via Instagram Live at @vasa.sva.

3:00pm | Mid-Autumn Narcan Training

Join Nurse Abby of Student Health and Counseling Services for a special gratitude-filled virtual Narcan training. All current SVA students, staff and faculty are invited. We will review the risks and prevention strategies related to opioid overdoses and learn how to reverse their effects with Narcan. Registrants will then be sent their own Narcan kit, which is good for two years. Additional trainings will continue to be offered throughout the year. Today, November 17, 3:00pm, online.

5:00pm | MFA Computer Arts Fall 2022 Open Studios

MFA Computer Arts presents its fall 2022 open studios, bringing together multidisciplinary projects from current students in a range of mediums including video, animation, design and interactive installations. Tonight, November 17, 5:00 – 8:00pm. MFA Computer Arts, 133/141 West 21st Street, 12th floor.

6:00pm | MPS Branding “Ask Me Anything” Session

MPS Branding presents an online “ask me anything” session with faculty, staff and alumni. Tonight, November 17, 6:00 – 7:00pm, online.

6:00pm | “Mixed Bag” Reception

SVA Library West presents its debut zine exhibition, “Mixed Bag,” featuring student work curated by SVA Library West student workers Gem Davison and Briana Rengifo. On view through Thursday, January 5, 2023. Opening reception: Tonight, November 17, 6:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

Last Chance | “(Be) Social Actors”

MA Curatorial Practice presents an exhibition of work about the concept of “the public domain,” curated by program student Zezhou Wang. Closes today, November 17. Pfizer Building, 630 Flushing Avenue, Brooklyn.

NYC PICK

Printing the Future: The Riso Revolution

An exhibition curated and organized by Panayiotis Terzis (MFA 2015 Fine Arts; BFA 2006 Illustration), co-founder and manager of the SVA RisoLAB, featuring work by more than 250 artists showcasing various book and print objects from across the globe. Through Thursday, December 22. Opening reception tonight, November 17, 6:00 – 8:00pm. There will also be an accompanying panel discussion moderated by Terzis on Saturday, November 19, 4:00 – 6:00pm. dieFirma, 32A Cooper Square.
