News and events from the School of Visual Arts
SVA Now
February 20 - February 26, 2017

Rising Star

Writer and filmmaker Shubhashish Bhutiani (BFA 2013 Film and Video) made it onto Forbes India’s "30 Under 30" list.

Truth Over Fiction

A Q&A with SVA faculty member Kathy Brew, who is curating the Museum of Modern Art's annual nonfiction film festival, Doc Fortnight. Through Sunday, February 26. Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53rd Street.
IN THE PRESS

"Black is Beautiful"

Daniel Fishel (MFA 2011 Illustration as Visual Essay) illustrated six colorful artworks for Bloomberg to celebrate Black History Month.

Predicting Trump?

Williamette Week interviews Bill Plympton (1969 Cartooning) about working with cartoonist Jim Lujan on their animated film Revengeance.

Sundance's "Dose of Humor"

Paste interviews BFA Film faculty member James Strouse about his new comedy starring Jessica Williams and Keith Stanfield, which screened at the 2017 Sundance Film Festival.
EVENTS

Jasmine Wahi

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by faculty member Jasmine Wahi, who curates international exhibitions dealing with issues of cultural identity, intersectionality and female empowerment. Tuesday, February 21, 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Thomas Roma

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by photographer and two-time Guggenheim Fellowship recipient Thomas Roma as part of its i3: Images, Ideas, Inspiration lecture series. Tuesday, February 21, 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
