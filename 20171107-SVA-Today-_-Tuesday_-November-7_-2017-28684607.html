Thor: Ragnarok

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Thor: Ragnarok, starring Chris Hemsworth and Cate Blanchett. Admission is free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
EVENTS

6:30pm | Anna Ogier-Bloomer

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by artist, photographer and faculty member Anna Ogier-Bloomer (MPS 2017 Digital Photography). 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

6:30pm | MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism Open House

Hear from program faculty, students and alumni about the ways in which they engage with design research and writing, and how they put it into practice in the workplace. Doors open at 6:00pm; presentations begin at 6:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
PRESS PICK

Scharf's NYC Show

Blouin Artinfo on "Inner and Outer Space," an exhibition of new paintings by Kenny Scharf (BFA 1981 Fine Arts), on view through Friday, December 22, at Deitch Projects, 18 Wooster Street.
NYC PICKS

Trains, Tunnels and Trees

A talk by historian Jill Jonnes about the past, present and future growth of New York City's infrastructure, followed by a discussion with architect and Columbia University professor Vishaan Chakrabarti. Tickets $20 – $30; purchase here. 6:30pm. Museum of the City of New York, 1220 Fifth Avenue.

Your Good Taste Is Showing

An exhibition of paintings and portraiture by Robin F. Williams. Through Saturday, November 11. PPOW Gallery, 535 West 22nd Street.
TAKE NOTE

Art and Activism: Framing the Political Debate

Join Young Professionals of the Americas and Americas Quarterly (AQ) as they discuss the meeting point of visual art and activism with BFA Illustration faculty member Edel Rodriguez and Lucia Cuba, two of AQ’s top 5 art activists. SVA students, faculty and staff must email bbintrim@as-coa.org today to register for free. Tomorrow, November 8, 6:30pm. Americas Society, 680 Park Avenue.

MFA Visual Narrative Information Session

MFA Visual Narrative presents an information session, where you will learn about the program and meet faculty, alumni and current students. RSVP here. Saturday, November 11. Presentation and Q&A, 4:00 – 6:00pm; demonstration in the RisoLAB, 6:00 – 7:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F, or tune in online at mfavn.sva.edu.

Emotional First Aid: Recognizing and Responding to Students in Distress

SVA Student Health and Counseling Services clinicians Christine Gilchrist and Navah Steiner present a workshop on recognizing and identifying signs that a student is struggling and what you can do to help. Through interactive methods, you will practice ways to respond more effectively to a student in crisis and leave this workshop feeling more competent and confident communicating with students in distress. Breakfast will be served. RSVP to Navah at nsteiner@sva.edu. Wednesday, November 15, 9:30 – 11:30am. 340 East 24th Street, board room.
