Subject: [URGENT] Back up your Adobe Creative Cloud before Wednesday, August 18
From: SVA Info
Date: 8/11/21, 12:45 PM
To: allstaff@sva.edu, allstudents@sva.edu, allfaculty@sva.edu

SVA Community,

If you have any documents or media stored online in your Adobe Creative Cloud account, please download and back them up before Wednesday, August 18.

On the evening of Wednesday, August 18, we will be migrating the account structure for all Adobe Creative Cloud products in order to synchronize the Adobe user accounts with the myID master account lists.

This process will purge all existing user accounts in SVA's Adobe Creative Cloud enterprise account and recreate them. Any content you have stored in the 100GB of available Creative Cloud storage will be deleted. PLEASE BACKUP YOUR DATA to an alternative storage location such as Google Drive, Microsoft One Drive, or local storage on your computer.

Access to the Adobe Creative Cloud products will be restored for user accounts by the morning of Saturday, August 21. Since the account synchronization is changing there may be some users who find they are not able to access Adobe Creative Cloud applications after the migration is complete. If this occurs please contact the Help Desk at helpdesk@sva.edu and note the issue with your Creative Cloud account.
  • Local settings and preferences per product should not be affected since those are tied to your local user account on your computer.
  • Your username and password for Adobe Creative Cloud will remain the same: your myID credentials.
To review your assets stored on the Adobe Creative Cloud:
  • Visit assets.adobe.com/cloud-documents
  • Or click “Your Work” at creativecloud.adobe.com
  • To learn more, click here.
Since web-view of Creative Cloud assets only allows individual file download, it is best to sync the files to your local computer via the Creative Cloud Desktop App. To learn more, click here.

Thank you for your patience during this migration.

-Administrative & Network Services
