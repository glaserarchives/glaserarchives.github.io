SVA Today
Thursday, December 9, 2021

“SVA ❤️ Milton” and the Legacy of Milton Glaser
SVA presents “SVA ❤️ Milton: The Legacy of Milton Glaser,” a new exhibition exploring the late design giant and faculty member’s life and career, with a companion exhibition featuring videos and notable Glaser quotes viewable from the sidewalk outside the 133/141 West 21st Street building. Through Saturday, January 15. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street, and SVA Flatiron Project Space and Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today for a one-hour session with Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross. She will be available on Zoom to field questions about job searching, interviewing and other career-related topics. Join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join. Today, 10:00 – 11:00am ET.

Tomorrow | Designing a Voice with Jennet Liaw
Join the Asian American Student Union (AASU) for a chat with designer, illustrator and superstar creative Jennet Liaw, who will discuss her journey as an AAPI designer in the creative industry navigating the worlds of fashion, entertainment and social activism. Liaw has worked with clients such as Nike, Adidas, Adobe and Netflix, as well as creating merchandise collaborations with Jubilee, Merch Aid and Hate Is A Virus. Friday, December 10, 4:30pm ET, on Zoom. Join the call here.
