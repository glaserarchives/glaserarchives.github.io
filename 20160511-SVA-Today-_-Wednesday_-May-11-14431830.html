Proficiency Exam Dates 2016 - 2017
The SVA Writing Resource Center has published the dates for upcoming proficiency exams. Students who matriculated in fall 2015 will not be able to register for Humanities and Sciences electives until they pass the exam. Current students who have not yet completed the requirement are urged to take the exam in August. Click through for the full schedule. For more information, call 212.592.2576.
Library Adds Harper's Bazaar Database
The SVA Library has acquired access to the Harper's Bazaar database, a searchable archive of every article, advertisement and cover published by the magazine since 1867. Please note that the archive presently covers from 2015 to 1969, with some coverage to 1962, and will be completely digitized by the end of 2016. The database can be accessed from the library's A – Z Databases page.
