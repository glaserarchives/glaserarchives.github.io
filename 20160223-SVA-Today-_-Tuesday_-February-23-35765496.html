Study Abroad in Rome, Paris, Kyoto and Beyond
Information sessions for the SVA in Rome and Semester Abroad programs begin this week. Read more about the opportunities for students to study abroad next spring.
6:00pm: Accra Shepp
MFA Fine Arts presents photographer Accra Shepp discussing his work, which is centered on how the natural environment influences human interactions, and international protest movements. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
6:30pm: Sloane Crosley
MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents essayist and novelist Sloane Crosley in conversation with department faculty member, writer and filmmaker Adam Harrison Levy. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.
7:00pm: Muriel Hasbun
MPS Digital Photography presents artist Muriel Hasbun discussing how her work reflects an intergenerational, transnational and transcultural vision. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
7:00pm: Let's Talk: Curating Your Life in the Arts | Alumni Panel Discussion
SVA Alumni Affairs presents a panel of six alumni discussing how their careers as illustrators, fine artists, photographers and filmmakers have evolved since graduating from SVA. Panelists include Scott Bakal (BFA 1993 Illustration), Jenny Morgan (MFA 2008 Fine Arts), Kate Neckel (MFA 2002 Computer Art), Vincent Peone (BFA 2007 Film and Video), Annie Watt (BFA 1974 Media Arts) and Heidi Zito (MFA 2012 Photography, Video and Related Media). SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Take Note
Eve Mosher
MFA Fine Arts presents faculty member, artist and interventionist Eve Mosher discussing her use of public works to raise awareness of issues such as the environment, public/private space use, history of place and the urban ecosystem. Tuesday, March 1, 6:00pm, 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
Auditions for April Fools' Ball
VASA is looking for unique acts to perform at this year's April Fools' Ball, such as dance, theater, performance art, burlesque, drag and more. Auditions will be held on Thursday, March 3, and Friday, March 4, 5:00 - 7:00pm, SVA Student Center, 217 East 23rd Street. To apply, contact vasa@sva.edu. The April Fools' Ball will take place on Friday, April 1, 7:30pm, The Box, 189 Chrystie Street.
