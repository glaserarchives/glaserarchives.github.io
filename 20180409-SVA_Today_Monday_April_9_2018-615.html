News and events from around SVA
SVA Today
Monday, April 9, 2018
By Jade Doskow

Alumni Art Shows in NYC

From sculptures to illustrations, hip-hop photography to surrealist paintings, SVA alumni are adding some amazing work to the New York art scene.
EVENT

6:30pm | Dissolving Borders: Art, Counter Surveillance and DIY Technology

BFA Visual & Critical Studies presents a panel on artistic research and hacking in anticipation of More Art's upcoming dual-city commissions on the global refugee and immigration crises. Panelists include artists Krzysztof Wodiczko and Nadav Assor and drone expert Andy Trench. 6:30 – 8:30pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.
PRESS PICK

Products of Design at the MoMA Design Store

Core77 explores the ongoing SVA MFA Products of Design collaboration with MoMA Wholesale. Be sure to check out the window displays at the MoMA Design Stores in Midtown and Soho, which feature the products created to date.
NYC PICK

Yvan Alagbé: Yellow Negroes and Other Imaginary Creatures

Alternative comic artist Yvan Alagbé discusses his work in comics with art director and author Kristen Radtke, in celebration of the release of his story collection Yellow Negroes and Other Imaginary Creatures in English. Tickets $5 – $22.95; purchase here. 7:30pm. Strand Bookstore, 828 Broadway.
TAKE NOTE

Poetic Algorithms

A group of works created by the first-year MFA Computer Arts students exploring the possibilities of technology and how it impacts the way we see the world. Tuesday, April 10 – Thursday, April 12. Reception: Thursday, April 12, 6:00pm. Highline Loft, 508 West 26th Street, Loft 5G.

MFA Design Venture 18: Design and Discovery

MFA Design presents its thesis forum, where the 12 program graduates and design entrepreneurs will premiere their unique products. RSVP here. Wednesday, April 11, 6:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

PoV: Persistence of Vision

BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects presents an exhibition that seeks to peel back the curtain and show what the fourth-year students' process of creation is like. Saturday, April 14 – Saturday, May 5. Reception: Thursday, May 3, 6:30pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Be Featured in SVA Style for the Chance to Win $50

SVA Style is looking to feature the personal objects, ephemera, knick-knacks and other items that students and faculty collect that inspire them. Email a description of your object(s) to style@sva.edu and SVA Style will reach out to get your items photographed. Time is of the essence, so submit ASAP. Students whose items are photographed will be entered for a chance to win a $50 Apple/iTunes gift certificate.

Faculty and Staff Appreciation Week

SVA Campus Store presents Faculty and Staff Appreciation week from Monday, April 16 – Friday, April 20. Stop in for double discounts on all computers (purchased on April 20 only) and choose one free accessory. Spin the prize wheel for up to 25% off SVA-branded merchandise. All shoppers will receive a free gift and be entered into a weekly raffle for great prizes. Stop by the store before the event to set up your SVA Campus Store Technology Loan and recycle your old tech with Encore! for a gift card towards a future purchase. Email campusstore@sva.edu for more information. SVA Campus Store, 207 East 23rd Street.
