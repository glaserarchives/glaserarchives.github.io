News and events from around SVA
SVA Today
July 2 – 8, 2018

SVA Subway Series Hall of Fame: James McMullan

The latest SVA Subway Series video features former faculty member and 2012 Masters Series honoree James McMullan, who taught illustration at SVA for over 30 years.

June Hot Five

The top five most-discussed projects on SVA Portfolios last month include an interactive website for musician Pharrell Williams, a set of abstract illustrations and a packaging design for the soundtrack for the Netflix series Marvel: The Defenders.
THIS WEEK

Tuesday | Emma Sulkowicz

MFA Art Practice presents a talk by its artist-in-residence Emma Sulkowicz, the artist known for her Columbia University thesis, Mattress Performance (Carry That Weight). Tuesday, July 3, 12:30 – 2:00pm. 335 West 16th Street, Room 501H.

Tuesday and Thursday | MAT Art Education Thesis Presentations

Graduating students of MAT Art Education present their thesis projects. Tuesday, July 3, 10:00am – 3:00pm, and Thursdsay, July 5, 11:00am – 1:00pm. 132 West 21st Street, 4th floor.

Thursday | Summer Residency Program Open Studios: Fine Arts, Illustration and Painting

SVA Summer Residency Programs presents a one-night exhibition of work from residents in Fine Arts: Residency in Contemporary Practices, Painting and Mixed Media, and Illustration and Visual Storytelling. Thursday, July 5, 6:00 – 9:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, 1st, 4th, 8th and 9th floors.

Friday | "Dimensional Whateverism" Reception

MAT Art Education presents an exhibition of thesis work by its class of 2018, exploring the abstract concept of dimensionality. Friday, July 6, 4:00 – 6:00pm. Through Saturday, July 7. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Friday | The First Purge

SVA Theatre presents a screening of The First Purge. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. Friday, July 6, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Haring Mural Revealed

Hyperallergic on an Amsterdam mural painted by alumnus Keith Haring in 1986 that was recently revealed after being covered by insulation panels for almost 30 years.
NYC PICKS

Macy's Fireworks

The 42nd annual Macy's Fourth of July Fireworks along the East River, accompanied by a score and music performances. Viewing locations here. Wednesday, July 4, beginning around 8:00pm. Happy Independence Day!

French Restaurant Week

Through Sunday, July 15, French restaurants in New York City are offering promotional prix fixe meals in honor of the French Revolution.

Pop of Color

A color-focused exhibition curated by SVA staff member Dan Halm (MFA 2001 Illustration as Visual Essay; BFA 1994 Illustration) and featuring work by Matthew Keff (BFA 2007 Film and Video), Ken Lavey (BFA 2015 Film and Video), Adehla Lee (MFA 2011 Fine Arts) and SVA staff member Miranda Pierce. Through Saturday, July 14. HERE, 145 Sixth Avenue.
