Events

9:30am | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Tuesday from 9:30 – 11:00am ET, SVA's International Student Office will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week. To schedule a virtual appointment with an ISO advisor, click here. Email iso@sva.edu with any questions.

12:00pm | SVA in LA Summer 2021 Information Session for Animation Students
Feeling ready to travel again? Spend a week in Los Angeles (July 18 – 24), visit film and animation studios in the Hollywood area and meet directly (yes, no Zoom!) with some of the leading film and animation artists who create the work we see on the big screen and TV. Learn more here. Email Sal Petrosino at spetronsino@sva.edu with any questions. Note: Trip details, guests and studio visits are subject to change. Join the info session, focusing on the animation industry aspects of the trip, today, 12:00 – 1:00pm ET, on Zoom.

Alyssa Grella: Brand Steward
Design Quarterly profiles Alyssa Grella (BFA 2007 Interior Design), principal and co-owner of Studio Grella Design and president of the American Society of Interior Designers' New York Metro chapter.

Rapture: A Queer Taste for Color, Texture and Decorative Pattern
Featuring the work of SVA faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts), this exhibition stages a full-on display of the avenging transcendence of gaiety as a proven tactic for disarming oppression. You can view the showroom online here or visit in person at New York Artists Equity Association Inc., 245 Broome Street, New York.

Join BFA Animation on Twitch for a celebration and screening of thesis projects by its class of 2021! On both Saturday, May 8, and Sunday, May 9, screenings take place from 6:00 – 9:00/9:30pm ET.

Let's Talk With Luz Batista
A talk about life after SVA followed by a Q&A with storyboard artist from Disney Television Animation, Luz Batista (BFA 2014 Animation). Tickets are free.

Drag Queen Bingo with 'Shuga Cain'

Save the Date | Metamorphosis: MFA Interaction Design Thesis Festival Premiere
Join MFA Interaction Design for a celebration of work from its class of 2021. In this free and virtual forum, hear the graduates share their thesis work—work that connects the present to the future, bounding us forward into what is pragmatic and possible for the human experience. Friday, May 21, 10:00am – 2:00pm ET. Preview the projects and RSVP on the Thesis Festival site.
