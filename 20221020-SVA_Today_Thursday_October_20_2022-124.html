SVA Wins Big at The Rookies

The online design platform The Rookies has named SVA the #1 2D Animation School in the World! The BFA Animation program won the 2D Animation Rookie of the Year Award, and BFA Animation student Brooke Burnett’s film, The Spellening, won 2D Animation Film of the Year.

Abolition Geography Panel Discussion

Free; RSVP here. Tonight, October 20, 7:00 – 9:00pm. Great Hall at the Cooper Union, 7 East Seventh Street, between Third and Fourth Avenues.

Free Access to VIEW Conference 2022

The VIEW Conference is the premiere international event in Italy on computer graphics, interactive techniques, digital cinema, 2D/3D animation, VR and AR, gaming and VFX. Explore the new digital frontier through lectures, meetings, tributes, exhibits, screenings and demo presentations. Free; register here to watch all live-streamed sessions. Through tomorrow, October 21, online.
