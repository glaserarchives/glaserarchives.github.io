TAKE NOTE

Wafaa Bilal

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by the Iraqi-born performance and interactive artist. Tomorrow, January 31, 6:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

Rome/Avignon SVA Destinations Information Session

Learn more about two of SVA Destinations' two-week summer programs: Underground Rome, where you can explore the city's most hidden layer—the underground (May 14 – May 28); and Art History in Southern France, where you can draw, paint and photograph on location while exploring the region's art history (June 1 – June 14). Refreshments will be provided. Wednesday, February 1, 1:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 402C.

Agar Art Workshop and Contest

BFA Fine Arts presents this two-part, hands-on workshop where you will create your own work of agar art using microbes as "paint" and agar as a "canvas." Photos of the agar art created in this workshop are eligible for the American Society of Microbiology contest. Register here. Part 1, Friday, February 3, 3:00 – 6:00pm; Part 2, Sunday, February 5, 3:00 – 6:00pm. SVA Nature and Technology Lab, 335 West 16th Street.

Call for Submissions: 2017 HOPE Art Competition

Student Health and Counseling Services invites SVA students to submit a piece for the HOPE Competition and Exhibition that expresses their identity, aspects of identity and/or the intersection of various identities. Submission forms are available at sva.edu/health or can be picked up at Student Health and Counseling Services, 340 East 24th Street, ground floor. Deadline is Monday, February 6, 4:00pm. Finalists’ work will be on view Thursday, February 16 – Saturday, February 18, at the SVA Flatiron Windows, 133/141 West 21st Street. First, second and third place winners will be announced at a reception on Friday, February 17, 5:30 – 6:30pm, at the SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Arthur Jafa Film Screening and Conversation with Amy Taubin

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a screening of two films by Jafa: Dreams Are Colder Than Death (2013) and Love Is the Message, the Message Is Death (2016). Following the screening, he will talk with critic and faculty member Amy Taubin. RSVP here. Tuesday, February 7, 7:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

The Match Factory's Latest Issue Is Online

SVA's Humanities and Sciences Department presents the fall 2016 issue of its online magazine, The Match Factory. The content varies from film criticism by Tom Yoannidis and Etna Ozbek, cartoons by Amber Ross, poetry from faculty member Kristin Wolfe, to art by Sydney Kaye. Work is now being considered for the spring issue; submission deadline is Saturday, April 1.

Titmouse Five-Second Animation Night

SVA Theatre presents Five-Second Animation Night, where Titmouse artists and animators present the animated goodies that have been floating around in their minds all year. Tickets $5 – $10; purchase here. Friday, March 3, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
