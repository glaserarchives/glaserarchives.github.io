Tuesday: "Land" Reception

Thursday: Summer Residency Exhibition and Open Studios

SVA Continuing Education and SVA Summer Residency Programs present open studios and an exhibition featuring artists in the following residency programs: Fine Art: Residency in Contemporary Practices, Illustration and Visual Storytelling, Painting and Mixed Media, and Photography. Thursday, August 10, 6:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery and 8th and 9th floors, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Thursday: Wind River Screening

The Visual Effects Society presents a screening of Wind River, starring Jeremy Renner and Elizabeth Olsen. Admission is free with SVA ID (three guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). Thursday, August 10, 8:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

TAKE NOTE

SVAlert System Test

On Friday, August 11, at approximately 1:00pm, there will be a test of SVAlert, the College's electronic notification system. All students, faculty and staff will receive messages via selected contact options. When you receive the test message, you do not need to take any action.

All students, faculty and staff are automatically enrolled in SVAlert. You are encouraged to update your preferences and verify your contact information by logging on to my.sva.edu. Click on the SVAlert icon to review and then click "Submit."

Create Marquees for SVA Theatre

SVA Theatre is looking for a student to create 12 video animations that will be shown throughout the year on its marquee facing 23rd Street. You will work with SVA Theatre staff to determine ideas and a timeline for completion. Compensation will be $1,000. Knowledge of Adobe After Effects is preferable, but work can be completed using alternative software. Thousands view the marquee daily, so it's a great way for the public to see your work. If interested, email Adam Natale at anatale@sva.edu.
