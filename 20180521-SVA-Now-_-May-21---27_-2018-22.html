SVA Subway Series Win Graphis Poster Awards

Four SVA Subway Series campaigns have won top honors in the 2019 Graphis Poster Annual. Winners include SVA's Acting Chairman of the Board Milton Glaser and BFA Illustration faculty Chris Buzelli, Edel Rodriguez and Yuko Shimizu (MFA 2003 Illustration as Visual Essay).

Read Maya Lin's Full 2018 SVA Commencement Speech

"Art can be a reflection of our time, or it can pose a new way of looking at our world." Relive the entire address.
IN THE PRESS

Essenhigh's "Full Embrace of Enchantment"

Hyperallergic on the paintings of Inka Essenhigh (MFA 1994 Fine Arts), which are on view through Friday, May 25, at Miles McEnery Gallery, 525 West 22nd Street.

Debbie Millman on Design, Art and Life

Breed interviews MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman about her past work and experiences, her podcast, and more.

"A Slipstream in Digital Space"

Artsy rates Liu Shiyuan (MFA 2012 Photography, Video and Related Media) as one of its 15 artists on the rise.
EVENTS

Steel Stillman with Matthew Connors and Sam Contis

Artists Matthew Connors and Sam Contis in conversation with the artist and writer Steel Stillman, discussing their recent bodies of work, which are currently featured in "Being: New Photography 2018" at The Museum of Modern Art. Monday, May 21, 7:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.

Trembling Mountain Screening with Kesang Tseten

MFA Social Documentary Film presents a screening of the documentary Trembling Mountain with the film's director Kesang Tseten. Free; RSVP to Amitabh Joshi at ajoshi@mfasocdoc.com. Tuesday, May 22, 6:30pm. SocDoc Auditorium, 136 West 21st Street, ground floor.

"Catalysts in the Morning Dew" Reception

A juried exhibition featuring illustrations, paintings, photographs and mixed-media collages that are inspired by socio-political themes throughout the world. Thursday, May 24, 6:00pm. Through Tuesday, June 5. SVA Gramercy Gallery, 209 East 23rd Street.
