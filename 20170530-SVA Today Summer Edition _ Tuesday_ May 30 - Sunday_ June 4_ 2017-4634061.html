TAKE NOTE

Library Computer Lab Renovations

The SVA Library computer lab is being renovated to accommodate additional iMacs and new Cintiq tablets. The work is anticipated to take about four weeks to complete. During this time there will be some excessive noise in the library and limited computing services available; 14 iMacs, one flatbed scanner and a black-and-white printer have been set up in the library reading room as a makeshift computer lab during the renovation. Please note that there will be no color printing capabilities while the renovation takes place. Once renovations are complete, the computer lab will have a new layout with 40 iMacs, four Cintiq tablets, two flatbed scanners, one book scanner, and color and black-and-white printers.
