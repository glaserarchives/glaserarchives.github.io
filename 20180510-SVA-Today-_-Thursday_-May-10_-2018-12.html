SVA Today
Thursday, May 10, 2018

Top Honor for Gail Anderson

A Q&A with Creative Director for the Visual Arts Press Gail Anderson, who is the recipient of the 2018 National Design Awards' Lifetime Achievement Award, presented by Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum.
EVENT

6:00pm | The Project Class Screening

Six short films by students in the Project Class of 2018, an undergraduate honors class in the BFA Advertising and BFA Design programs taught by Bob Giraldi, chair of MPS Directing, Jason Cicci and Patti Greaney. RSVP here. 6:00 – 9:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Photo of a Kiss Wins Big

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media faculty member Elinor Carucci's image of a kiss, which accompanied a much talked-about short story in The New Yorker last December, is featured in PDN's Photo Annual 2018.
NYC PICKS

Head to Toe with Mirko Ilić and Steve Heller

A conversation between SVA faculty members Mirko Ilić and Steve Heller about their new book, Head to Toe: The Nude in Graphic Design. 6:00pm. Rizzoli Bookstore, 1133 Broadway.

Heavenly Bodies: Fashion and the Catholic Imagination

The Costume Institute's spring 2018 exhibition features a dialogue between fashion and medieval art. Free with museum admission. Through October 2018. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Avenue, and the Met Cloisters, 99 Margaret Corbin Drive.

AFA Educating America Tour

Alzheimer's Foundation of America presents a free Alzheimer's and caregiver educational conference, which will provide Alzheimer's-related education and services, tips and strategies and information on local resources, and will feature a talk by Christine Gilchrist, associate director of SVA Student Health and Counseling Services. Open to family and professional caregivers, individuals living with Alzheimer's disease and the general public. Register here. Friday, May 18, 9:00am – 2:00pm. Crowne Plaza Times Square Hotel, 1605 Broadway.
