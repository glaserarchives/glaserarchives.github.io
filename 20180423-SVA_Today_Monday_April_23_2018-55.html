Renew Your Access to The New York Times Online

All SVA faculty and staff with an sva.edu college email address can sign up for unlimited access to nytimes.com by visiting nytimes.com/passes. Every year, faculty and staff have to renew their pass to access SVA's institutional subscription; current users should receive renewal emails based on when they first signed up. Follow the directions in the renewal email to reactivate your pass. If you haven't received an email, visit nytimes.com/passes and follow the link at the bottom that says "Already have an account? Log in here." This will reset your account for another calendar year of access.
