Upcoming Scheduled Fire Drills﻿

SVA Facilities Management will be conducting fire drills as per New York City compliance tomorrow, September 28. A representative from Global Security will walk through each location with a member of SVA Security and Facilities. The fire drills will begin on the top floors and proceed down until all floors have completed the exercise. A short briefing will take place on each floor during the walkthrough; depending on occupancy, some locations will have a briefing in the lobby. The schedule is included below:

  • •17 Gramercy Park South, 8:00am
  • •132 West 21st Street, 9:30am
  • •136 West 21st Street, 10:30am
  • •133/141 West 21st Street, 11:30am

Deadline Friday | Apply for ISO Cultural Partner Program

Do you want to be engaged in local communities by participating in community service projects? SVA’s International Student Office welcomes all students to apply for a two-semester long membership-based program, featuring New York City culture trips, a monthly discussion series and the opportunity to participate in and lead a community service project. It is a small, supportive group where you can connect with students from different majors and years. Program requirements and the application can be viewed here. Email Yoko Anderson, yanderson@sva.edu, with any questions. Deadline is Friday, September 30.
