SVA Today

Escape NYC This Summer

SVA Destinations offers programs around the world that span a range of artistic disciplines and can help stimulate your creativity this summer.
EVENTS

2:00pm | SVA in Cannes Information Session

Come learn about this SVA Destinations program, taking place at the Cannes Film Festival in France this May. 2:00pm. 209 East 23rd Street, Room 502.

6:30pm | A Critical Reader for Critical Times

MA Design Research, Writing and Criticism presents a live reading of texts that listeners may find particularly resonant, comforting or empowering during these uncertain times. RSVP here. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 136 West 21st Street, 2nd floor.

6:30pm | Joseph DeLappe

MFA Fine Arts presents a talk by artist Joseph DeLappe. 6:30 – 8:00pm. 133/141 West 21st Street, Room 101C.

7:00pm | Juan Giraldo

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk by photographer Juan Giraldo, as part of its i3: Images, Ideas and Inspiration lecture series. 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.

9:00pm | Red Sparrow

SVA Theatre presents a screening of Red Sparrow, starring Jennifer Lawrence. Free with SVA ID (three guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). RSVPs not accepted or required. 9:00 – 11:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Women for Women on Instagram

Scroll In on BFA Illustration students Tara Anand and Ellie Lee, whose inspired Instagram project helps women stop criticizing members of their own gender.
NYC PICKS

A Conversation with Erwin Redl

Erwin Redl (MFA 1995 Computer Art), the creator of the Madison Square Park art installation Whiteout, will discuss with author Lawrence Weschler the opportunities and challenges inherent in realizing such a project. Free; RSVP here. 6:00pm. Austrian Cultural Forum, 11 East 52nd Street.

Hudson Valley Restaurant Week
Celebrating fine dining just outside of NYC at an affordable price. Three-course lunch and dinner from $22.95 – $32.95. Through Sunday, March 25. See participating restaurants here.

Using Computer Vision, Data Visualization and Artificial Intelligence to Explore Global Photography

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents a workshop with theorist of digital culture Lev Manovich on the way technology expands the possibilities of photography. Register here. Tuesday, March 20, 6:00pm. 214 East 21st Street, Room 120.

Saturday Church
