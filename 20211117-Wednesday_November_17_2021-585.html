12:00pm | Natural and Cultural Heritage of the Future
Presented by MA Curatorial Practice, join members of Not An Alternative—a nonprofit art collective that initiated The Natural History Museum traveling pop-up museum—to ask how new interpretations of cultural and environmental heritage can arise from connecting climate justice and land rights movements to the history of museums and the legacy of colonialism. Today, 12:00 – 1:00pm ET, online. Register here.

6:00pm | Graduate Admissions Alumni Panel
SVA invites all prospective graduate students to join us this fall to meet Graduate Admissions staff and hear from a panel of graduate alumni. Gain insight into what it’s like to be a graduate student and make the most of the Manhattan campus experience. This panel will feature alumni from graduate programs such as MFA Art Practice, MPS Branding and many more. Attendees will receive an $80 application fee waiver if they apply for summer 2022 or fall 2022 admission. Tonight, 6:00pm ET, online. Register here.

Visual Opinion Interns
Visual Opinion is looking for interns! Send your resume, portfolio/website and a statement of interest to vo@sva.edu with the subject line “VO Intern Application.” Deadline to apply is Tuesday, November 30, 9:00am ET.
