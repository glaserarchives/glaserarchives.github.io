10:00am | Yoga for Artists: Back, Shoulder and Headache Relief

Take a break with SVA Student Health and Counseling Services and yoga instructor Melissa Boo! Participate in a free virtual yoga class focusing on emotional and physical tension relief. Learn poses that may help reduce back, shoulder and head pain while reducing other stress symptoms. Join us for our last yoga class of the semester and stress less for finals. Beginner-friendly! Open to all SVA students, staff and faculty. RSVP required. Today, December 2, 10:00am.
