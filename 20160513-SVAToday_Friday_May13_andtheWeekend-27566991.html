SVA Today
Friday, May 13, 2016
Documentary Follows Blind Soccer Players in China
MFA Social Documentary Film student and 2016 Alumni Scholarship Award winner Anqi Cai talks about her thesis film, Blind Soccer, which follows a group of blind soccer players in China as they train for a national tournament.
Announcing the SVA Makers Market
On Saturday, June 11, at Metropolitan Pavilion, SVA Alumni Affairs and External Relations present a fair that celebrates the entrepreneurial, do-it-yourself spirit of SVA's alumni. Alumni-made products such as apparel and accessories, furniture, jewelry, housewares and decorative objects, stationery and self-published books, magazines and comics will be for sale.
6:30pm: MFA Art Writing Thesis Presentations
Until 8:30pm. 132 West 21st Street, 6th floor.
PRESS PICK OF THE DAY
Going Minimal
Business Insider asked MFA Design Co-Chair Steven Heller and MPS Branding Chair Debbie Millman their opinions on the new Instagram logo.

Society of Illustrators' Student Scholarship Reception
The Society of Illustrators presents a scholarship competition and exhibition featuring work by over 200 students, including many from SVA. Reception and awards ceremony tonight at 7:00pm; doors open at 6:00pm. Through Saturday, June 4, Society of Illustrators, 128 East 63rd Street.

TAKE NOTE
2016 Distinguished Staff Awards
Each year at the All-Staff Meeting, SVA recognizes outstanding staff contributions to the College with the presentation of two Distinguished Staff Awards. Each award includes a cash prize of $5,000. Details and nomination forms can be found at staffawards.sva.edu. The deadline for nominations is Friday, May 20, 5:00pm.
The Graduate Show: MFA IxD Festival
Graduating MFA Interaction Design students present work that connects ideas to the human experience, with prototypes available for play and exploration. The event will stream live here. Tuesday, May 17, 10:00am - 3:30pm, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
Jessica Jones and Love
The SVA Theatre and the SAG-AFTRA Foundation present screenings of episodes from Netflix's Jessica Jones (6:00pm) and Love (8:00pm). Jessica Jones' Krysten Ritter and Love's Gillian Jacobs will answer questions after their respective screenings. Free with SVA ID (one guest limit per student, faculty and staff member). Tuesday, May 17, SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
NY Photo Salon: Christine Callahan, Jay Maisel and Deepanjan Mukhopadhyay
MPS Digital Photography presents photographers Christine Callahan (BFA 1992 Photography), Jay Maisel and Deepanjan Mukhopadhyay. Each will answer questions following their 20-minute presentations. Thursday, May 19, 7:00pm, 136 West 21st Street, Room 418F.
