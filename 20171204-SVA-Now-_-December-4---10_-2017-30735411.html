SVA at Miami Art Week

A look at some of the many SVA alumni, students and faculty who are showing work and taking part in the fairs and activities of Miami Art Week, which kicks off today.

A "Street Smart" Designer

BFA Design faculty member Timothy Goodman (BFA 2007 Graphic Design) on how New York City has influenced his art and the mural he created for "Street Smart: The Intersection of Art and Design in the City."

Podcast Pros' Favorite Shows

SVA's podcast experts share their top picks for art, design and cultural audio programming.

November Hot Five

The top five most-discussed projects on SVA Portfolios last month include futuristic motion graphics, portrait photographs and vintage illustrations for Entertainment Weekly.
IN THE PRESS

A Painting Power Couple

Hyperallergic talks with Inka Essenhigh and Steve Mumford (both MFA 1994 Fine Arts), both of whose work "can be at once an invitation, and a provocation."

"Using My Camera as a Therapist"

PetaPixel on the talk that Ryan Pfluger (MFA 2007 Photography, Video and Related Media) recently gave at TEDxPasadena about how his camera saved him from loneliness.

Capturing America in 2017

The Week on BFA Photography and Video's exhibition "2017 America," which features "artfully direct" images by high school students from all over the U.S.

"A Reinterpreter of Land Art"

Art News on Erwin Redl's (MFA 1995 Computer Art) Whiteout, an installation of 900 floating, choreographed lights in Manhattan's Madison Square Park.
