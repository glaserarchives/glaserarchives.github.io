SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133 West 21st Street

Community Lecture Series:
Art Therapy with Military Populations
Friday, February 23, 2018
133 West 21st Street, Room 101C
6:30pm-8:00pm

Paula Howie will discuss her book Art Therapy with Military Populations, which provides an in-depth overview of both the theoretical and historical bases of art therapy with these groups.  It also chronicles the latest trends in treatment and the continued expansion of treatment settings.
