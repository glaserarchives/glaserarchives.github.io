My Art Portfolio: Then and Now
The latest installment of the SVA YouTube series "My Art Portfolio" features fourth-year student Yoko Chen (BFA Film), who talks about the progression of her work from when she applied to SVA to now. To see more of Chen's work, check out her website and her Instagram, @yoko_chen150.
Events

The Booster Bus Is Here
All eligible students, faculty and staff who need a COVID-19 vaccine booster can get one at a booster bus, which is hosted on campus by SVA Student Health and Counseling Services. The bus will be parked outside the SVA Student Center at 217 East 23rd Street, today, 9:00am – 6:00pm. Appointments are not necessary and eligible recipients can receive a $100 incentive.

Be sure to bring proof of prior vaccination and proof of ID (i.e. government-issued driver's license or passport). For questions about booster eligibility, click here. Remember to upload your booster verification into Cleared4.

10:00am | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour today, 10:00am ET. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

12:00pm | The Public Seminars: Raqs Media Collective—The Unknown as an Enchanted Limit
MA Curatorial Practice presents the first of four public seminar sessions, featuring Monica Narula and Shuddhabrata Sengupta, founding members of the Delhi-based Raqs Media Collective, whose practice encompasses artistic production, curating and publishing around the world. Today, 12:00pm ET, on Zoom. Register here.

12:30pm | ISO Virtual Drop-In Hours
Every Thursday, 12:30 – 2:00pm ET, SVA's International Student Office (ISO) will be offering virtual drop-in hours, during which students can meet with an ISO advisor on Zoom without an appointment. Fill out this form during drop-in hours to receive a Zoom link. Find more details on how to sign up here (scroll to "International Student Office Staff" headline).

Individual virtual appointments with ISO advisors are available throughout the week; to schedule one, click here. Email iso@sva.edu if you have any questions.

5:00pm | How to Respond to Anti-Asian/American Harassment
SVA Student Health and Counseling Services; Diversity, Equity and Inclusion; and the International Student Office present this free, one-hour interactive training, hosted by Hollaback, on how to safely intervene when witnessing or experiencing street harassment. Today, 5:00pm ET, online. Register here.
