EVENTS

12:00pm | Fear and Sensitivity in Large-Scale Exhibitions

MA Curatorial Practice presents curator Maria Lind, art historian and critic Terry Smith, curator Claire Tancons and Anselm Franke, head of visual art and film at HKW in Berlin, for a discussion on some of the polarizing debates we’ve seen this summer around large exhibitions such as documenta fifteen and the Berlin Biennale. Today, October 11, 12:00 – 1:30pm, online.

6:00pm | “In Their Own Words” Dialogue Series: Afro-Latino Creatives

In honor of Hispanic Heritage Month, SVA Diversity, Equity and Inclusion presents a moderated discussion with four Afro-Latino creatives. Tonight, October 11, 6:00pm, online. RSVP here.

7:00pm | i3 Photo Lecture: Jeff Chien-Hsing Liao

MPS Digital Photography presents a talk with Jeff Chien-Hsing Liao (MFA 2005 Photography and Related Media), a Taiwanese photographer who immigrated to the U.S. in 1999 at the age of 22. Tonight, October 11, 7:00 – 8:30pm. 136 West 21st Street. RSVPs required; register here.

NYC PICK

Just Above Midtown: Changing Spaces

An exhibition presenting artists and artworks previously shown at JAM, an incubator of Black avant-garde art of the 1970s and ’80 featuring work by Janet O. Henry (1965 Illustration), Jorge L. Rodriguez (BFA 1976 Fine Arts) and Rosemary Mayer (1969 Fine Arts). Free with SVA ID. Through February 18, 2023. Museum of Modern Art, 11 West 53 Street.

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Tomorrow | Wellness Wednesdays: Panel Discussion on the Overturning of Roe v. Wade

SVA Diversity, Equity and Inclusion, in collaboration with Student Health and Counseling Services, presents a discussion on the recent Supreme Court decision to overturn Roe v. Wade, and how to understand your reproductive rights and access SVA resources. This discussion will be moderated by Jarvis M. Watson, director of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion. Panelists include Arielle Kempler, associate director, Student Health and Counseling Services; Abby Wohl, nurse, Student Health and Counseling Services; and Gabriela Andrade and Morgan Cousins of Planned Parenthood of Greater New York Action Fund. Tomorrow, October 12, 3:30pm, online.

Virtual Narcan Training with Nurse Abby

Join Nurse Abby Wohl from Student Health and Counseling Services for a virtual SVA staff and faculty Narcan training session to learn more about how to reduce risk and prevent an opioid overdose. As a Narcan trainee, you will receive your own Narcan kit. If you are unable to attend, additional training will be offered throughout the year. Tuesday, October 18, 2:00 – 3:00pm, online.
