Friday, May 21, and Commencement Weekend

SVA Commencement, Past and Present
Renowned visual artist and longtime MFA Fine Arts faculty member Marilyn Minter will give the keynote speech at SVA's 46th annual commencement exercises this weekend. Click here to learn more about Minter and for some highlights from past commencements.

Watch the 2021 SVA Commencement Exercises beginning Sunday, May 23, 7:00pm ET, at sva.edu/commencement or facebook.com/SchoolOfVisualArts.
Events

10:00am | Metamorphosis: MFA Interaction Design Thesis Festival Premiere
The 2021 MFA Interaction Design thesis festival is a celebration of work by the graduates of the program. Click here to join the online thesis festival premiere today, 10:00am – 2:00pm ET.

8:00pm | SVA Short Film Fest 2021: Directors Q&A
Head to svashortfilmfest.com to view the narrative short films from the MPS Directing class of 2021, then join the Q&A with the films' directors and see who wins best picture! 8:00pm ET. Register here.

Now on View | Animation Celebration
Don't miss this exhibition of the animated thesis films by the BFA Animation class of 2021! Curated by BFA Animation staff and faculty, the exhibition is on view through Thursday, June 3, online at galleries.sva.edu and in the SVA Flatiron Gallery windows, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Saturday | Before We Begin, Again
In this exhibition of their thesis work, selected MFA Computer Arts 2021 graduates reconsider histories and memories, while creating space for possible worlds that have yet to exist. On view from tomorrow, May 22, through Saturday, June 12, online at galleries.sva.edu.

Saturday | Faculty and Staff Appreciation at SVA Campus Store
From tomorrow, May 22 – Wednesday, May 26, use promo code SF21 for 25% off all SVA branded merchandise, an additional $50 off iMacs and MacBook Pros and $25 off MacBook Airs and iPad Pros—plus a free tech gift! Shop at svacampusstore.com.
SVA IN THE PRESS

Sarah Sze at Storm King
The New York Times talks with artist Sarah Sze (MFA 1997 Fine Arts) about the production of her upcoming installations—Fallen Sky and Fifth Season—at Storm King Art Center in New Winsdor, New York.
SOCIALLY DISTANCED PICK

The Deans' Table—Compassionate Leadership: Facing Crisis with Heart
A panel of leaders at several NYC design schools, including BFA Interior Design: Built Environments Chair Carol Bentel, will discuss guiding staff, administration, faculty and students through drastic change and uncertainty. Presented by Design Pavilion. Watch here today at 2:00pm.
