SVA Today
Friday, April 13, and the Weekend

Talking with Lynn Shelton

Director Lynn Shelton (MFA 1995 Photography and Related Media) discusses her new film Outside In and shares advice for young filmmakers.
EVENTS

4:00pm | Tabletop Game Night

SVA Library and the Cards and Dice club present a Friday the 13th-inspired tabletop game night. Free popcorn and soda for all players. 4:00 – 8:00pm. SVA Library West, 133/141 West 21st Street, lower level.

7:00pm | MFA Photography, Video and Related Media Spring Salon

MFA Photography, Video and Related Media presents this opportunity to see the diverse range of photography, video and moving image artwork being produced by students in the department. 7:00 – 10:00pm. 214 East 21st Street.

Saturday | PoV: Persistence of Vision

BFA Computer Art, Computer Animation and Visual Effects presents an exhibition that seeks to peel back the curtain and show what the fourth-year students' process of creation is like. Saturday, April 14 – Saturday, May 5. Reception: Thursday, May 3, 6:30pm. SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street.

Sunday | My Fair Lady

A screening of the classic 1964 film, with an introduction by a special guest who will discuss the upcoming stage revival. Tickets $5; purchase here by clicking "Book Now." Sunday, April 15, 3:00pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

Lessons from an Interior Designer

The Kitchn shares six lessons that HGTV host and interior designer Genevieve Gorder (BFA 1998 Graphic Design) learned while renovating her own kitchen.
NYC PICKS

Orchid Evenings

Stroll through Belgian floral artist David Ost's "The Orchid Show" while enjoying music and performances. Tickets $28 – $38; purchase here. Tonight and tomorrow, 6:30 – 9:30pm. New York Botanical Garden, 2900 Southern Boulevard, Bronx.

Fashion in Film Festival

This festival explores the connections between fashion, cinema and time and asks what concrete manifestations of time fashion and clothing enable. Schedule here. Through Sunday, April 22. Museum of the Moving Image, 36-01 35 Avenue, Queens.

The Senses: Design Beyond Vision

Explore experimental works and practical solutions designed to inspire wonder and new ways of accessing our world. Free with SVA ID. Through October 2018. Cooper Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museum, 2 East 91st Street.
TAKE NOTE

Be the Change You Want to See in 2018

92Y presents former Army captain and Missouri’s 39th secretary of state, founder of Let America Vote and CNN contributor Jason Kander, artist, poet and author Cleo Wade and Symone Sanders, Bernie Sanders' former campaign press secretary, who will discuss how to make an impact on society. Get $20 tickets when you sign up for 92Y's XYZ group, or purchase full-price tickets here. Friday, April 20, 7:00pm. 92nd Street Y, 1395 Lexington Avenue.
