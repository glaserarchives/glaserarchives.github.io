Next Stop: Philadelphia

"Underground Images," SVA’s traveling exhibition of its iconic, faculty-designed subway posters, is being hosted by the Philadelphia chapter of AIGA and opens today.
EVENT

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 Screening

The Visual Effects Society presents a 3D screening of Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, featuring Chris Pratt and Zoe Saldana. Free with SVA ID (two guest limit per SVA student, faculty and staff member). 8:00 – 10:30pm. SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street.
PRESS PICK

"The Best of All Revolutions"

Harper's Bazaar on the best quotes from Gloria Steinem's speech at SVA's 2017 Commencement Exercises.
NYC PICKS

An Evening with the New Negress Film Society

A series of screenings of short films by black women filmmakers, followed by a Q&A with members of the New Negress Film Society. Tickets $9 – $11. Tonight, 7:30pm. Anthology Film Archives, 32 Second Avenue.

Smorgasburg

An open-air food market with over 100 local vendors. Open Saturdays, 11:00am – 6:00pm, 90 Kent Avenue, Brooklyn, and Sundays, 11:00am – 6:00pm, Prospect Park Breeze Hill, Brooklyn.
TAKE NOTE

SVA Today Summer Edition Begins Monday

SVA Today will go weekly for the summer months, beginning on Monday, May 15. The weekly summer edition will include all the features of SVA Today. The daily edition will resume the first day of the fall 2017 semester.

Correction

An article about the 2017 Commencement Exercises in yesterday's edition of SVA Today misstated the number of degree candidates. The correct figure is 1,170, not 1,700.
