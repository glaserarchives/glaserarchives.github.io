CAMPUS ACCESS UPDATE

Guide to Cleared4 for SVA Students, Staff and Faculty
As of Monday, August 23, all students, staff and faculty have access to Cleared4, a health-screening platform that also stores your vaccine record. Cleared4 enables SVA to easily monitor any COVID-19 symptoms and helps us quickly identify others who might have been exposed if someone has symptoms. Look for an email from Cleared4 to help you get started.

Each day before coming to campus, you will need to complete a Cleared4 health survey via a link sent by email or text. You can also access your link through the Cleared4 tile in your myID dashboard. Click here for more guidelines and FAQs.

Events this week

Wednesday | Summer Drawing Triathlon
This event combines three approaches to drawing in an intense three-hour session. Using wet and dry mediums with references from life, art history and imagination, participants will create over 100 drawings in one evening, led by visual artist and SVA faculty member Peter Hristoff (BFA 1981 Fine Arts). Presented by SVA Continuing Education and BFA Visual & Critical Studies. RSVP here to join on Zoom. Wednesday, August 25, 6:00 – 9:00pm ET.

Thursday | Career Development Virtual Office Hour
Join SVA Career Development's virtual office hour this Thursday, August 26, 10:00am ET. During the one-hour session, Assistant Director of Career Development Tricia Ross will be available on Zoom to field questions about your job search, an upcoming interview and other career-related topics. No waiting room—you can jump in, join the conversation and share with your classmates and fellow alumni. Fill out this form during office hours to receive a Zoom link to join.

Thursday | Mobile Vaccination Clinic Outside of SVA Theatre
SVA invites students in need of a COVID-19 vaccination to visit the NYC Mobile Vaccination bus for their first dose of the Pfizer vaccine. Thursday, August 26, 9:00am – 3:00pm, outside the SVA Theatre, 333 West 23rd Street. Vaccinations are free. No insurance is required and no appointments are necessary. Live interpretation services are available. Participants are eligible for NYC's various vaccine incentives. Email student-covid-alert@sva.edu with any questions.

Last Chance | Create a Video/Animation, Win $500
SVA Theatre seeks a 10 – 20 second pre-roll video (animated or live action) to play before film screenings shown during this September's After School Special 2021: SVA's Alumni Film and Animation Festival.

Set the video to music that you have created yourself, own the copyright for, have obtained a license for, or is in the public domain, and please note its source when submitting. Download logos for the festival, SVA and SVA Theatre here. View examples of past winning videos here and here.

Upload submissions to Vimeo or YouTube, and send an email with a link to the video, your name, graduation year and program major to Adam Natale, director of SVA Theatre, at anatale@sva.edu, by this Friday, August 27, 5:00pm ET. Questions may also be directed to Natale. Final video format should be a digital file (e.g., QuickTime ProRes). The winner will receive $500.
