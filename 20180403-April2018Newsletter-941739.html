Spring Forward
Boys Hope Girls Hope NY
Exhibition: April 4-April 14
Reception: April 13, 5:00-7:30pm
132 West 21st Street, 5th Floor Gallery

Navigating self-awareness and a need for change can be a daunting and intimate process.  Through mixed-media self portraiture, performance arts, and collaborative installation scholars of Boys Hope Girls Hope New York will articulate this conscious movement forward in a contained, shared, and creative space.

Contact arttherapy@sva.edu to make an appointment for viewing hours.
